## Lyrics

Rama Rama Jaya Raghukula Tilaka
Rajeeva Lochana Ram
Eshwaramba Sutha Jaya Jaga Vandana
Ratnakara Kula Deepa (3)

## Meaning

Worship the lotus-eyed Lord Rama, who is the auspicious symbol of the Raghu dynasty. He is verily Lord Sathya Sai, the son of Mother Eshwaramba and is the glory of the Ratnakara dynasty.
