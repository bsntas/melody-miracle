## Lyrics

Madhava Mohana Murari
Madhusudhana Hey Muralidhara [ Madhava Mohana ... ]
Govinda Ananda Dayaka
Gopala Giridhari Sri Hari
Sri Hari Sai Sri Hari

## Meaning

Glory to the sweet and enchanting Lord Krishna, the slayer of demon Madhu and the one who holds the flute. He held the Govardhana mountain aloft and grants Supreme Bliss. Lord Hari (Vishnu) is none other than Lord Sai.
