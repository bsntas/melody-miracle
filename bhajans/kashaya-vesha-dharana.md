## Lyrics

Kashaya Vesha Dharana
Kali Yuga Avatara Radha Ramana [ Kashaya Vesha ... ]
Kunja Vihari Sri Sathya Sai
Dwarakamayi Narayana
Chithravathi Teera Narayana Hari

## Meaning

Glory be to the incarnations of Kali Age, Shirdi Sai and Sathya Sai who has donned the ochre robe. Lord Krishna, the beloved of Radha and who resides in the gardens has reincarnated as Sai. Dwarakamayi was Shirdi Sai's abode while Sathya Sai has chosen the banks of Chitravathi.
