## Lyrics

Prem Bhakthi Jagao Man Mey
Aao Merey Hruday Mey Baba
Tum Ho Deena Janon Ke Baba
Tum Ho Bhaktha Janon Ke Sai [ Aao Merey ... ]

## Meaning

Oh Lord Sai Baba! Welcome to my heart and please awake within me the love and devotion for Thee. You are the support of those who are meek. You are the dear Sai of the devoted.
