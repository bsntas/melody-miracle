## Lyrics

Raghupathey Sri Rama Chandra Raghava Daya Nidhey (2)
Pashupathey Sri Parvatheesha Shankara Sadashiva (2)
Madhava Sri Radhikesha Govinda Harey Krishna (2)

## Meaning

O Rama, Lord of the Raghus, who is still and shining as the moon, You are the Ocean of Mercy. O Lord Shiva, the beloved of Mother Parvathi, You are ever unchanging and the Source of Goodness. Oh Krishna, the Splendorous Lord of Radha, You are the protector of our souls and the remover of sorrows. Oh Lord Sai, You are Lord Rama, Lord Shiva and Lord Krishna.
