## Lyrics

Gananayakam Sai Gananayakam
Sadananda Sundara Gananayakam
Vara Dayakam Sai Shubha Dayakam
Parthi Pureeshwara Gananayakam

## Meaning

Salutations to You, O Lord Ganesha, the Lord of the Ganaas (Demigods), You are eternally blissful and most beautiful. You bestow various boons and confer all auspiciousness. O Lord of Lords, You reside at Parthi as Lord Sai.
