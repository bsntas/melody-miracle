## Meaning

Salutations to the Supreme Lord, Sai, who protects all those who surrender to Him. Salutations to the gentle and pleasing Lord whose abode is Puttaparthi, on the banks of river Chitravathi. The sages and the noblest of men surrender to You, O Sai, You are the source and sustenance for all the three worlds. Victory unto Thee, O Lord Sathya Sai, the protector and sustainer of all devotees and the one who bears the burden of all those who take refuge in You.
