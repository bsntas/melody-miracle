## Lyrics

Jaya Murali Madhava Shourie
Parama Pavana Pandari Natha
Paramatma Hey Purandara Vittala
Sri Ranga (Sai) Natha Hrudayanta Ranga

## Meaning

Chant the different names of the Lord Krishna, Madhava the valorous one, Murali with the Flute in His Hand, Pandari Natha the Lord of Pandharpur. He is Vittala, the Supreme Consciousness and the highly sacred one and Lord of Purandara and Ranganatha, the indweller of the Heart.
