## Lyrics

Amba Parameshwari Akhilandeshwari
Adi Parashakthi Palaya Maam
Sri Bhuvaneshwari Raja Rajeshwari
Ananda Roopini Palaya Maam

## Meaning

O Divine Mother of the Universe (Bhuvaneshwari, Raja Rajeshwari) and the Consort of Lord Shiva, the all-powerful one, You are bliss personified, we pray to You to protect us.
