## Lyrics

Murali Gaana Lola Nanda Gopa Bala Raavo Raavo Radha Lola Radha Lola
Treta Yugamuna Sita Ramudai
Dwapara Yugamuna Radha Loludai
Geetanu Drapina Taraka Naamudai
Yuga Yuga Manduna Kaguroopa Dariyai

## Meaning

Welcome O Lord Krishna, Nanda's Son, the beloved of Radha. In the Age of Treta, You incarnated as Rama. In the Age of Dwapara, You enchanted Radha and taught the Sacred Hymn of Bhagavad Geetha. You are the liberator of humanity and in every Age You are the most beautiful one to look at.
