## Lyrics

Padmanabha Narayana
Parthi Vasa Narayana (2)
Narayana Namo (Sai) Narayana (3)

## Meaning

Salutations to the Lord of all beings, Lord Narayana, from whose navel springs the Lotus of Creation. He is none other than Lord Sai who resides at Puttaparthi.
