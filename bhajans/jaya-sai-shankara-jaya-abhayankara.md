## Meaning

Glory to Lord Sai who is verily Lord Shankara (Shiva) who bestows freedom from fear, the one whose other half is 'Amba' (Parvathi). He is also known as Hara, He holds the river Ganga and crescent moon on His head and sits on tiger skin.
