## Lyrics

Bhajorey Bhai Sai Ram
Sai Sab Ka Hai Bhagawan [ Bhajorey Bhai ... ]
Ram Hi Sai Krishna Hi Sai
Yuga Avatara Narayana Sai [ Sai Sab Ka ... ]

## Meaning

Let us chant the name Sai Ram who is the Lord of all. He is Lord Rama, Lord Krishna and Lord Narayana who has descended in the Kali Yuga.
