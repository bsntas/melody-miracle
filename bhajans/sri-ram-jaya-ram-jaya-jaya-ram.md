## Lyrics

Sri Ram Jaya Ram Jaya Jaya Ram
Raghupathi Raghava Raja Ram
Dasharatha Nandana Sri Raghu Ram
Parthi Pureeshwara Sai Ram [ Raghupathi Raghava ... ]

## Meaning

Victory to Lord Rama, the descendant of the Raghu clan, the son of Dasharatha. Victory to Lord Sai, Lord of Parthi, who is none other than Lord Rama.
