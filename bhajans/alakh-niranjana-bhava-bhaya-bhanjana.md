## Lyrics

Alakh Niranjana Bhava Bhaya Bhanjana Narayan Narayan
Narayan Narayan Narayan Sriman Narayan (2)

## Meaning

O Lord Narayana, the one who is free of all blemishes and the remover of fear and ignorance in this world, we chant Your Holy Name.
