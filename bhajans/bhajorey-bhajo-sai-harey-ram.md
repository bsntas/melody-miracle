## Lyrics

Bhajorey Bhajo Sai Harey Ram
Govinda Gopala Sai Harey Ram
Madhava Mohana Sai Harey Ram (Hey) [ Bhajorey Bhajo ... ]
Sri Madhusudhana Bhava Bhaya Bhanjana
Bharey Premanand Se Bolo Ram Harey Ram
Govinda Gopala Sai Harey Ram [ Madhava Mohana ... ]

## Meaning

Chant the holy name and sing the glory of Lord Sai, Govinda, Gopala, Madhava and Madhusudhana (names of Lord Krishna). O Lord, You remove the fear of birth and death. Chant the names of the Lord filled with love and bliss.
