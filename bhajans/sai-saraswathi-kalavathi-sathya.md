## Lyrics

Sai Saraswathi Kalavathi (Sathya)
Sakala Shastra Vikasini [ Sai Saraswathi ... ]
Bhaava Raga Laya Tala Vinodini (3)
Parthi Nivasini Bharati Brahmani

## Meaning

Lord Sai who is residing in Parthi is none other than Goddess Saraswathi, the one who is personification of all types of Arts and the one who enjoys the Art of Music.
