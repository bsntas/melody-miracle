## Lyrics

Allah Bhajo Maulah Bhajo Hey Allah Sai Bhagawan

## Meaning

Chant the divine names of the Lord - Allah, Rama, Krishna, Guru Nanak, Buddha, Zohrashtra, Mahavir, Shirdi Sai and Sathya Sai.
