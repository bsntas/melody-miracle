## Lyrics

Vittala Hari Vittala (3)
Panduranga (Purandara) Vittaley Hari Narayana
Hari Narayana Bhajo Narayana
Sai Narayana Sathya Narayana

## Meaning

Sing the glory of Hari Vittala, also known as Panduranga Vittala, Purandara Vittala, Sai Narayana and Sathya Narayana.
