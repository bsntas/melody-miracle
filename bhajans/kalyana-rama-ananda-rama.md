## Lyrics

Kalyana Rama Ananda Rama
Rama Sri Rama Jaya Rama [ Kalyana Rama ... ]
Avatara Sai Avatara Rama
Poorna Avatara Sathya Sai Rama

## Meaning

Salutations to Lord Rama, who confers bliss and causes the welfare of the world. Salutations to Lord Rama the incarnation and Sathya Sai Rama who has come down with all the facets of an incarnation.
