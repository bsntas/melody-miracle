## Meaning

Worship Mother Kali, who destroys evil and wears a garland of skulls; Worship Mother Saraswathi, Goddess of knowledge and wisdom, from whom the Vedas emanated; Worship the Supreme Mother Gowri, who confers auspiciousness and is the savior of the Universe.
