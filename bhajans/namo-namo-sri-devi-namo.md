## Lyrics

Namo Namo Sri Devi Namo
Shritha Jana Palini Sai Namo
Chinmaya Roopini Devi Namo
Sri Partheeshwari Sai Namo

## Meaning

Salutations to the Divine Mother Sai, the Goddess of Parthi, the embodiment of pure consciousness and the protector of all those to surrender to Her.
