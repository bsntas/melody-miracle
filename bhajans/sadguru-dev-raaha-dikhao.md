## Lyrics

Sadguru Dev Raaha Dikhao Prabhu

Tum Ho Merey Prana Daata

## Meaning

O Lord Sai, our Divine Preceptor, show us the path to liberation, You are our life giver as well as the one who bestows all prosperity.
