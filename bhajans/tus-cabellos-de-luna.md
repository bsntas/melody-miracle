## Lyrics

Tus cabellos de luna, manantial de agua pura,
Se convierten en Ganges, bañando tu figura
Shiva, Shiva, Shiva, Shiva, Sathya Sai
Voy limpiando mi mente, respiro lentamente,
Y contemplo en mi frente tu sin par hermosura.
Shiva, Shiva, Shiva, Shiva, Sathya Sai
Déjame adorarte, déjame venerarte
Sólo quiero extasiarme en tu fresca ternura.
Shiva, Shiva, Shiva, Shiva, Sathya Sai

## Meaning

O Lord Shiva, the spring of pure water on Your moon-decorated hair became Ganges by bathing Your figure. Glory to Thee, O Lord Shiva and Lord Sathya Sai. My mind is cleansed and my breath slows down when admiring Your unmatched beauty face-to-face. O Lord Sathya Sai who is verily Lord Shiva, let me worship You, let me venerate You. I just want to thrill in Your fresh tenderness.
