## Lyrics

Radhey Mukunda Murari Govinda
Govinda Govinda Ananda (3) [ Radhey Mukunda ... ]
Giridhari Giridhari Ananda (3)

## Meaning

Chant the names of the Lord Krishna, the Lord of Radha, Mukunda - bestower of liberation, Murari, who slayed the demon Mura, Govinda, the protector of the innocent and Giridhari, who held aloft the mountain. Sing repeatedly the names of Radhey Mukunda, Govinda, Giridhari, the real source of Bliss.
