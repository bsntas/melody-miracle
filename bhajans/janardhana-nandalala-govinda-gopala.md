## Lyrics

Janardhana Nandalala Govinda Gopala
Harey Krishna Harey Krishna
Krishna Krishna Gopala
Sai Krishna Gopala

## Meaning

Chant the names of Lord Govinda, Gopala, Krishna and Sai. O Lord Krishna, You are the son of Nanda and the one who induces people to do good actions.
