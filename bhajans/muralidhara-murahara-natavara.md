## Lyrics

Muralidhara Murahara Natavara
Gopi Jana Priya Giridhara
Brindavana Sanchara Jagadodhara
Yashoda Bala Nanda Kumara

## Meaning

Salutations to you Lord Krishna, son of Nanda and Yashoda, holding flute in hand, you are the supreme dancer whom the Gopis adore, you stroll in Brindavan and up lift the humanity from miseries.
