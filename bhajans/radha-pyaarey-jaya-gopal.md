## Lyrics

Radha Pyaarey Jaya Gopal
Radha Madhava Jaya Gopal
Brindavan Ke Jaya Gopal
Murali Manohara Jaya Gopal
Jaya Gopal Jaya Gopal (4)

## Meaning

Victory to Lord Krishna, the universal Protector and the beloved of Radha. Victory to Lord Gopal of Brindavan, the one who enchants with the Divine Flute.
