## Lyrics

Jai Hari Bol Jai Sita Ram Gopi Gopala Bhajo Radhey Shyam
Harey Ram Ram Ram Ghana Shyam Shyam Shyam
Sri Raghu Nandana Sri Rama Daasharathey Jaya Raghu Rama
Nanda Kishora Navaneetha Chora Brindavana Govinda Lala [ Harey Ram Ram ... ]

## Meaning

Victory to Lord Hari, Lord Rama, chant the name of Lord Gopala. Lord Rama belongs to the Raghu dynasty and is the dear son of King Dasharatha. Lord Krishna is the dear son of Nanda. He is the one who stole butter from houses of Gopis and moved about amongst the thick bushes of Brindavan.
