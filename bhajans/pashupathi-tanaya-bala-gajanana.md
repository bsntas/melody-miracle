## Lyrics

Pashupathi Tanaya Bala Gajanana
Tum Ho Vighna Vinasha Ganesha
Hey Shiva Nandana Bala Gajanana
Vidya Buddhi Pradatha
Mangala Kara Hey Mangala Kara Hey
Sundara Sai Ganesha Ganesha

## Meaning

Chant the name of the darling son of Lord Shiva (Pashupathi), the elephant-faced Lord Ganesha, the destroyer of obstacles. O Son of Lord Shiva, You are the bestower of wisdom (spiritual and intellectual). Chant the name of the charming Lord Sai Ganesha, who grants auspiciousness.
