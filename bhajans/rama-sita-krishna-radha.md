## Lyrics

Rama Sita Krishna Radha Vitthala Rakhumayi Jai Jai Vittala Rakhumayi (2)
Surdas Prabhu Karuna Sagara Vittala Rakhumayi
Meera Ke Prabhu Giridhara Naagara Vittala Rakhumayi
Sai Natha Prabhu Gaana Manohara Vittala Rakhumayi Jai Jai Vittala Rakhumayi
Vittala Vittala Vittala Jai Jai (3)
Vittala Jai Jai Panduranga Jai Jai
Vittala Vittala Vittala Jai Jai

## Meaning

Victory to Lords Rama, Krishna and Vitthala whose praises were sung by Surdas and Meera. Victory to Lord Sai who is pleased with music and who is none other than Vitthala.
