## Meaning

Chant the mantra 'Om Namah Shivaya' and bow to Lord Shiva who is the embodiment of auspiciousness, health and prosperity, the beloved of Gowri, and the one who holds the Ganga in His tresses. We bow down to Him, who is the Lord of Kailasa, who resides in Gangotri (origin of Ganga in Himalayas). He is verily our Lord Sainath.
