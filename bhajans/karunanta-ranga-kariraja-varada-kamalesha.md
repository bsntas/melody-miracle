## Lyrics

Karunanta Ranga Kariraja Varada Kamalesha Sri Sai Rama
Omkara Rama Prashanthi Rama
Parabrahma Sathya Sai Rama
Kamalesha Sri Sai Rama

## Meaning

Glory to the lotus-eyed and compassionate Lord Sai Rama, the one who saved the elephant king Gajendra, as Lord Narayana. He is the embodiment of Om and the Supreme Lord whose abode is Prashanthi Nilayam at Puttaparthi.
