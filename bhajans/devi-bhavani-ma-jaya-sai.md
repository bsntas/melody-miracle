## Lyrics

Devi Bhavani Ma Jaya Sai Bhavani Ma
Daya Karo Sai Ma Krupa Karo Sai Ma
Jaya Ma Jaya Ma Jaya Devi Bhavani Ma
Jaya Parthi Nivasini Ma Jaya Sai Bhavani Ma

## Meaning

Victory to Mother Sai, the destroyer of the cycle of birth and death. O Mother Sai, bestow on us Your mercy and compassion. O Mother, the resident of Parthi, victory to Thee.
