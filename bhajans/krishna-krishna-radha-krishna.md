## Lyrics

Krishna Krishna Radha Krishna
Govinda Gopala Venu Krishna
Mana Mohana Krishna Madhusudhana Krishna
Murari Krishna Mukunda Krishna

## Meaning

Chant the name of Lord Krishna, the one with flute with His consort Radha, the Charmer who slayed the demons Madhu and Mura.
