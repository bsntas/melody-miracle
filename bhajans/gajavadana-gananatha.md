## Lyrics

Gajavadana Gananatha
Gowri Tanaya Dayamaya [ Gajavadana ... ]
Bhuvanaadhara Pranava Swaroopa
Palaya Palaya Parthi Pureesha

## Meaning

O Lord Ganesha, the elephant-faced Lord, the son of Mother Parvathi, You are extremely compassionate. You are the one whose is the very form of the Pranava (Om) and who is the basis of the entire creation. Protect us, O Sai, the Lord of Parthi, You are verily Lord Ganesha.
