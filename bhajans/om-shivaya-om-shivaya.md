## Lyrics

Om Shivaya Om Shivaya
Shambho Shankara Om Shivaya (3)

## Meaning

Bow down to Lord Shiva who gives happiness and prosperity and who is the bestower of good. We offer humble prayers to Lord Shankara who grants auspiciousness and Lord Shiva who grants sustenance to be submerged in universal consciousness.
