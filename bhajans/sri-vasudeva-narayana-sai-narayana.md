## Lyrics

Sri Vasudeva Narayana Sai Narayana
Janardhana Hey Madhusudhana Sai Narayana
Govardhanodhara Gopalana Sai Narayana
Hari Om Hari Om Hari Om Narayana

## Meaning

Worship and chant the Holy names of Lord Krishna - Vasudeva, Janardhana, Madhusudhana and Narayana who as Gopala had lifted  the Govardhana mountain to protect His devotees. Lord Sai is verily Lord Narayana.
