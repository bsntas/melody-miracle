## Lyrics

Nanda Ke Lal Bhajo Murali Gopala
Radhey Shyam Radhey Shyam
Giridhari Shyam Bhajo Gopi Gopala
Meera Ke Natha Prabhu Hey Nandalala [ Radhey Shyam ... ]

## Meaning

Sing the glory of the child of Nanda, Lord Krishna, the protector of cows, who plays the flute. We pray to You, the blue colored Lord of cowherd girl Radha; You lifted the mountain Govardhana. Sing the glory of the Lord of the cowherds, the Lord of the great devotee saint Meera and the beloved son of Nanda.
