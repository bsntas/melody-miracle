## Lyrics

Vittala Bhajo Sai Vittala Bhajo (2)
Pandari Natha Panduranga Vittala Bhajo
Parthi Vasa Sai Deva Vittala Bhajo

## Meaning

Sing the glory of the Lord Vittala, also known as Panduranga, the Lord of Pandharpur and who is none other than Lord Sai residing in Parthi.
