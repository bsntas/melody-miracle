## Lyrics

Jaya Panduranga Jaya Vittala
Jagadodhara Hari Vittala
Pandari Natha Jaya Hari Vittala
Purandara Vittala Parama Dayala

## Meaning

Victory to Lord Vittala, the one standing on a brick, the redeemer of the Universe, Lord of Pandharpur, the Indweller and the one who is supremely compassionate.
