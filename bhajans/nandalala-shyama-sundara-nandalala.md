## Lyrics

Nandalala Shyama Sundara Nandalala
Kaustubha Dhari Vanamala Dhara Shyamala Madana Gopala
Radhey Shyam Radhey Shyam Sai Shyam Sai Shyam
Radha Madhava Madana Gopala
Mathura Natha Gopala
Parthi Nandana Hrudaya Sanjeevana
Shyamala Madana Gopala [ Radhey Shyam ... ]

## Meaning

This song praises Lord Sai Krishna as the one who is most charming, the one who wears the jewel Koustubha, one who is the beloved of Radha, the Lord of Mathura and the one who resides in the hearts of His devotees.
