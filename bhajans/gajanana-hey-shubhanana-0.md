## Lyrics

Gajanana Hey Shubhanana
Parama Niranjana Gajavadana
Vakratunda Dhara Vighna Vinasha
Pranava Swaroopa Parthi Pureesha
Siddhi Vinayaka Paahi Gajanana [ Parama Niranjana ... ]

## Meaning

Oh elephant-faced Lord Gajanana, You confer auspiciousness, You are supremely pure, Oh the one with a curved trunk, the destroyer of all the obstacles, grant us victory. Oh Lord, Your form is that of the primordial Om (Pranava) and You are none other than the Lord of Parthi. Oh the all powerful one, protect us Your devotees.
