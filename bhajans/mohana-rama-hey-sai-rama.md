## Lyrics

Mohana Rama Hey Sai Rama
Pavana Naama Prashanthi Rama [ Mohana Rama ... ]
Karuna Nidhey Prabhu Karunya Rama
Bhaktha Jana Priya Palita Rama
Parthi Pureeshwara Hey Sai Rama (2)

## Meaning

The enchanting Rama is our Sai Rama. The blessed name of Prashanthi Rama, the Compassionate Lord who is full of mercy, is the beloved of the devotees.
