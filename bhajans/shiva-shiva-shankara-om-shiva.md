## Meaning

Chant the name of Lord Shiva who is accompanied by Divine Mother Gowri (Parvathi) also known as Uma and Shakthi. Chant the name of Neelakanta (another name for Shiva as His throat looks blue after swallowing poison for the sake of Gods during their fight for nectar with demons) who is verily Lord Sai.
