## Meaning

Victory to Lord Rama of the Raghu clan, who is the very life of Mother Janaki (Sita), the one who is the Lord of the Earth and beauty personified. Glory be to the one who is the descendant of the Solar dynasty and who rooted out the demonic forces to protect the devotees.
