## Lyrics

Hey Nandalala Gopala
(Ghana) Shyama Gopal Sai Gopal [ Hey Nandalala ... ]

## Meaning

Sing the Glory of Lord Gopala (Krishna) who is of dark complexion, the dear son of Nanda and the one who protected everyone by holding the Govardhana mountain with His little finger.
