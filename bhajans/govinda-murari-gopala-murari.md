## Lyrics

Govinda Murari Gopala Murari
Vittala Sri Hari Panduranga
Hrdaya Vihari Hey Giridhari
Murahara Shourie Panduranga

## Meaning

Glory be to Lord Govinda, the protector of cows (Veda, senses), who slayed the demon Mura. Glory to Lord Vittala who stands on a brick (or the idol of knowledge) in the city of Pandharpur and to Lord Giridhari, who lifted the mountain and who resides in our hearts.
