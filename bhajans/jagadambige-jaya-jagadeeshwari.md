## Lyrics

Jagadambige Jaya Jagadeeshwari
Taye Parashakthi Parameshwari [ Jagadambige Jaya ... ]
Maye Maha Sai Maheshwari
Niye Vinai Tirkkum Vishweshwari
Unaye Sharanadainden Karunakari
Arulvaye Ananda Lahari Gowri

## Meaning

Glory be to the Mother of the Universe, Mother of Lord Ganesh and Subrahmanya; Glory to the Mother worshipped as Uma, Durga, Shakti and the consort of Lord Shiva. O Lord Sai, the Mother of all, grant us as our wishes.
