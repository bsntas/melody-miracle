## Lyrics

Jaya Jagat Janani Jagat Palini
Sai Bhavani Ma (Sathya)
Paapa Vinashini Parthi Nivasini
Suramuni Vandini Ma (3) [ Sai Bhavani Ma ]

## Meaning

Salutations to the Mother Sai Bhavani, who is ever compassionate, the one who destroys sins, One who dwells in Parthi and worshipped by sages and Holy men.
