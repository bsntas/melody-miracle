## Lyrics

Kaalatheethaya Siddhi Roopaya Yogeshwaraya Namo
Jagat Vitaya Vishwa Roopaya
(Sathya) Sayeeshwaraya Namo
Om Namah Shivaya Shivaya Namah Om (5)

## Meaning

We bow to the Lord of Yogis, Lord Sai who is beyond time and who is the embodiment of supreme power. We bow to Lord Sai, the provider of the world, the one who has the Universe as His form, who is verily Lord Shiva and the embodiment of Om.
