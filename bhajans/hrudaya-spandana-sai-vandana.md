## Lyrics

Hrudaya Spandana Sai Vandana
Gaana Lola Prabhu Sai
Baajath Taal Mrudanga Veena
Muraliya Madhura Vadya Manjira
Ananda Mangala Sai

## Meaning

Let us offer our prayerful salutations to Lord Sai with every beat of our heart. He is the lover of music. Various sweet musical instruments such as Mrudang, Veena, the Flute and the Manjira resound in His glory. May we be engrossed in the auspicious bliss of chanting the Holy name of Lord Sai.
