## Meaning

Glory to the Lord Narayana, the one who is resting on the Celestial Serpent Adisesha and the one who is the personification of serenity. He is the lotus-eyed one who is the beloved of Goddess of wealth (Lakshmi). He is none other than Lord Sai of Parthi.
