## Lyrics

Aao Gopala Giridhari
Aao Aao Antaryami
Aao Aao Ananda Sai [ Aao Gopala ... ]
Aao Aao Hrudaya Nivasi
Aao Aao (Pra) Shanthi Nivasi

## Meaning

Welcome Lord Gopala (Krishna), the one who lifted the Govardhana hill, the indweller of our hearts. Welcome, O blissful Lord Sai, Lord of Prasanthi, You are verily Lord Krishna.
