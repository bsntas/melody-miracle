## Meaning

Victory to Lord Sathya Sai, the incarnation of Supreme Love and the complete manifestation of everything. He is the incarnation of this Kali Age who had earlier incarnated as Lord Rama, descendant of Raghu dynasty and as the dark complexioned Lord Krishna.
