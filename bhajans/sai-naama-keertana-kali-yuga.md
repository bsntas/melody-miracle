## Meaning

Singing the glory of Lord Sai is the penance for Kali Yuga. He is verily Lord Krishna, the redeemer of the universe. He is the one who slayed the demons Mura and Madhu. He is the enchanter with the peacock feather onHis crown. He is the dark blue-hued one who is the savior of all and who is the mischievous darling son of Nanda.
