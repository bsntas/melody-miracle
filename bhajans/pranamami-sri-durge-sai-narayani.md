## Lyrics

Pranamami Sri Durge Sai Narayani
Jaya Jagadambe Devi Bhavani [ Pranamami Sri ... ]
Jaya Mahishasura Samhaarini
Mahamaya Dukha Haarini

## Meaning

We offer our obeisance to You, Mother Durga (Sister of Vishnu), Jagad Ambe (the Mother of the Universe) and Devi Bhavani, the destroyer of the cycle of birth and death, the destroyer of the demon Mahishasura (ego) and the remover of miseries and the vast illusion or Maya (ignorance).
