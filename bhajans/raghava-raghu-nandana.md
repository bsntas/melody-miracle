## Lyrics

Raghava Raghu Nandana
Madhava Madhusudhana [ Raghava Raghu ... ]
Rajeeva Dala Lochana Shyama Bansi Mohana
Donon Hi Naam Hai Anand Dham (2)

## Meaning

Sing the glory of Raghava, the scion of Raghu dynasty, Lord Rama. He is also the sweet Lord Krishna who slayed demonic force Madhu. He is the lotus-eyed and dark-hued one, who enchants us with His flute. Both the names, Ram and Shyam grant liberation and bliss.
