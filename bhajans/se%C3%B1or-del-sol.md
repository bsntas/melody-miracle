## Lyrics

Señor del sol, fuente de amor
Señor Shiva, transfórmame, destruye aquello que me impede ver
Señor Shiva, que la verdad sea el camino a la libertad

## Meaning

Lord of the Sun, source of Love. Lord Shiva, transform me, destroy what prevents me from seeing. Lord Shiva, may the Truth be the way to freedom.
