## Lyrics

Shankaram Bhajey Shankaram Bhajey
Parthi Vasa Sai Deva Shankaram Bhajey
Sadgurum Bhajey Sadgurum Bhajey
Satchidananda Guru Sadgurum Bhajey

## Meaning

Chant the name of Lord Sai who resides at Parthi, who is none other than Lord Shankara (Shiva). Chant the name of the Universal Preceptor who is the very embodiment of bliss.
