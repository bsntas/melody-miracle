## Lyrics

Hari Hari Govinda Narayana
Sri Hari Bol Jaya Hari Bol [ Hari Hari ... ]
Harey Rama Harey Krishna Prashanthi Raja Ram
Sai Ram Sai Ram Sai Ram Bolo

## Meaning

Sing the Glory of the Lord Hari who is also known by different names Govinda, Narayana, Rama, Krishna. Sing the praises of Sai Ram who is the Divine Master of Prashanthi Nilayam.
