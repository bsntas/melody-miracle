## Lyrics

Karuna Samudra Sri Rama
Kausalya Tanaya Sri Rama
Sharanagatha Priya Sai Rama
Sharanam Sharanam Sai Rama

## Meaning

Oh Rama the son of Kausalya, you are verily the ocean of compassion. Oh Sai Rama, you love those who have surrendered to you and hence I thus surrender myself.
