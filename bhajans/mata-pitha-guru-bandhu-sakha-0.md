## Lyrics

Mata Pitha Guru Bandhu Sakha Hari
Narayana Sai Narayana (2)
Allah Tum Ho Yesu Tum Ho
Buddha Zohrashtra Mahavir Tum Ho
Sab Ka Malik Ek Hai Bhagawan [ Narayana Sai ]

## Meaning

Oh Lord Sai Narayana you are the Mother, Father, Guru, Kith and Kin. You are none other than Allah, Jesus, Buddha, Zohrashtra, Mahavir and the Lord of the entire humanity.
