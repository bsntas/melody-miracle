## Lyrics

Sheetala Charanam Komala Charanam, Manjula Charanam Mama Guru Charanam
Mrudula Charanam Anugraha Charanam
Sadguru Charanam Sada Smarami
Prema Daata Sai Guru Natha
Pavana Charanam Sada Bhajami

## Meaning

The Feet of my Divine Preceptor Sai are cool and soft, are tender to touch and are full of grace. Let us all meditate and offer our prayers to these holy Feet, which always shower love and grace.
