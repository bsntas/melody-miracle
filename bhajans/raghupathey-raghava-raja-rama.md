## Meaning

Pray and chant the name of Lord Rama, the handsome Prince of King Dasharatha and Mother Kausalya. Victory to Lord Rama, the Saviour of the downtrodden and the Lord of Sita. Victory be to You, O Raghava, the Lord of Raghu dynasty, the one with a captivating form and of dark complexion.
