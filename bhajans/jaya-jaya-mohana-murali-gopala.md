## Lyrics

Jaya Jaya Mohana Murali Gopala
Jaya Giridhara Brindavana Bala [ Jaya Jaya Mohana ... ]
Jaya Hey Madhava Ghana Ghana Neela (2)
Jaya Meera Prabhu Radhey Gopala

## Meaning

Chant the names of the charming Gopala who plays on the Flute, the lad of Brindavan who surprised everyone by lifting the Mountain on His little finger. Sing in Praise of Madhava of the complexion of deep blue clouds and dear Lord of Meera.
