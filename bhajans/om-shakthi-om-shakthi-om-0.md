## Meaning

Shakti is the power behind the three worlds. It is creative, sustaining, and destructive power inherent in Brahma, Vishnu, and Shiva. It is also the power of action and inaction, of divine will and of truth. All pervading, primeval and unlimited, it is the inherent power of Sai of Shirdi and Parthi.
