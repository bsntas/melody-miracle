## Lyrics

Samba Sadashiva Sayeeshwara
Gangadhara Hara Gowri Shiva [ Samba ... ]
Narthana Sundara Natana Manohara
Natya Priyakara Nataneshwara (2)

## Meaning

Oh Lord Sayeeshwara you are none other than Lord Shiva also called by other names like Gangadhara, Gowri Shiva and Nataneshwara who performs the beautiful Cosmic dance.
