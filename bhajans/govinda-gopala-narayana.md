## Meaning

Glory to Lord Govinda (Vishnu) who is known by many names - Gopala, the protector of the cows, Narayana, the infinite, auspicious one, Madhava, the one whose is as sweet as Madhu or honey and Hari. Glory to Lord Sai who is verily Lord Narayana.
