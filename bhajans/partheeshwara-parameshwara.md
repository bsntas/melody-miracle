## Lyrics

Partheeshwara Parameshwara
Pavana Naama Sayeeshwara
Jagadeeshwara Hrudayeshwara
Jagadodhara Jatadhara [ Pavana Naama ... ]

## Meaning

Sing the glorious name of Lord Sai, the incarnation of Parameshwara (Shiva). Sing the beautiful name of He who is the Lord of the Universe and the one who resides in our hearts, the Sustainer of Creation, the long-tressed Lord with the glorious name.
