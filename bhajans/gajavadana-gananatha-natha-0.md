## Lyrics

Gajavadana Gananatha Natha
Gowri Vara Tanaya Gunaalaya [ Gajavadana ... ]
Vidya Dayaka Buddhi Pradayaka
Siddhi Vinayaka Hey Shubha Dayaka

## Meaning

Glory to the elephant-faced Lord Ganesha, the Lord of the demigods and the beloved son of Mother Gowri. O Lord, Thou art extremely merciful. You bestow Knowledge and Wisdom. You grant auspiciousness and gifts.
