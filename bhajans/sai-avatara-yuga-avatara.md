## Meaning

O Supreme Lord Sai, O Incarnation of this Kali Age, You are the protector of the meek, the remover of danger. You are Brahma (Creator), Vishnu (Sustainer) and Maheshwara (Destroyer of evil) and the beloved Sai of devotees. Chant the name of the Lord of Lords, Lord Sathya Sai, Prema Sai, who loves all true religions.
