## Lyrics

Bala Gopal Jai Jai Sai Shyam
Prem Bharo Dil Mey Hamarey Sai Ram
Hamarey Sai Ram Badey Pyaarey Sai Ram [ Prem Bharo... ]
Hum Bhakthon Ke Tum Ek Sai Ram
Tumharey Jagat Mey Bada Pyaara Pyaara Naam
Sangh Raho Har Dum Hamarey Sai Ram
Sangh Raho Hamarey Sai Ram

## Meaning

Victory to Sai Shyama, the Infant Gopala, our Beloved Lord. Saturate our hearts with Divine Love and be with us in every moment of our life. Your dear name and glory engulfs the entire world.
