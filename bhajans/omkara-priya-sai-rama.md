## Meaning

We pray to Sai Rama, who loves the Omkar (Primordial sound), who is always worshipped by pious souls, who is the embodiment of peace, who could grant self realization, the one who loves devotees who are immersed in the divine consciousness. He is the embodiment of divine music and dance.
