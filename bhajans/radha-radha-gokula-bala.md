## Lyrics

Radha Radha Gokula Bala 
Nanda Nanda Mukunda Gopala 
Navaneetha Chora Shyama Gopala 
Radha Madhava Raasa Vilola

## Meaning

Sing the glory of Lord Krishna, also known as Gopala, the beloved son of Nanda. Glory to the dark-hued child who grew up in Gokula, who loved to steal butter. He is one who danced the 'Raasa Leela' with the Gopikas and who is the beloved of Radha.
