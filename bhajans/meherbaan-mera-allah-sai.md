## Lyrics

Meherbaan Mera Allah Sai
Allah Sai Maulah Sai [ Meherbaan Mera ... ]
Tum Ho Yesu Buddha Sai

## Meaning

O Lord Sai, You are the most compassionate Allah. You are Jesus, Buddha, Zohrashtra, Mahavira, Vahey Guru and Guru Nanak. Glory to Thee.
