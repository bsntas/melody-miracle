## Lyrics

Karuna Sindhu Dasharatha Nandana Parthi Pureeshwara Ram
Prema Swaroopa Prashanthi Niketana Maruti Sevitha Ram
Ahalyodharaka Rajeeva Lochana Raghukula Bhooshana Ram

## Meaning

Glory to the compassionate, lotus-eyed Lord Rama, the son of Dasharatha and the one who was served by Maruti (Hanuman). Lord Rama redeemed Ahalya of her curse. He is verily the Lord of Parthi, Lord Sai, the embodiment of Love who resides at Prashanthi Nilayam.
