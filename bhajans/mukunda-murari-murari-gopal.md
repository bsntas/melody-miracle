## Meaning

O Lord Krishna, darling son of Nanda, You are the slayer of demon Mura, You are the effulgent light of the Atma, You are Truth, Auspiciousness and Beauty. You are the embodiment of beauty, You are none other than Lord Sai who resides in Prashanthi.
