## Lyrics

Radhey Govinda Jai Jai Radhey Gopala
Deena Natha Govinda Anatha Natha Gopala [ Radhey Govinda ... ]
Radha Lola Govinda Raasa Vilola Gopala
Govinda Jaya Govinda Gopala Sai Gopala

## Meaning

Victory to Lord Gopala, the Lord of Radha. You are the Lord of the meek and those who have no support. Oh Lord of Radha! You revel in the cosmic dance with the Gopis. Victory to Lord Govinda. Victory to Lord Gopala, the protector and sustainer of all beings and who is one other than Lord Sai.
