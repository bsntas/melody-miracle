## Meaning

Glory to the supremely blissful Lord Govinda, Gopala, Mukunda, Keshava, Madhava, Krishna and Rama. Glory to the lotus-faced Gopala (protector of all beings) who is the very embodiment of Truth, Awareness and Bliss and who is verily Lord Sai.
