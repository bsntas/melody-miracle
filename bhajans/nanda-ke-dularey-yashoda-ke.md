## Lyrics

Nanda Ke Dularey Yashoda Ke Pyaarey
Govinda Murarey Deenon Ke Saharey [ Nanda Ke ... ]
Karuna Sagara Giridhara Naagara
Murali Manohara Shyama (Sai) Murarey

## Meaning

O Lord Krishna, the beloved child of Nanda and Yashoda, O the protector of cows, the supporter of the downtrodden, You are the ocean of compassion who lifted the mountain Govardhana, You are the one who enchants with the sweet music of the flute, the one with a dark complexion like the rain cloud and the one who killed the demon Mura.
