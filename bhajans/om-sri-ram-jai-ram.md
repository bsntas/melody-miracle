## Lyrics

Om Sri Ram Jai Ram Jai Jai Ram (4)
Sita Ram Sita Ram Sita Ram
Radhey Shyam Radhey Shyam Radhey Shyam

## Meaning

Victory to Lord Rama, the Lord of Sita and Shyam, the beloved of Radha. Chant the name of the Lord Sri Rama, Sita Ram and Radhey Shyam.
