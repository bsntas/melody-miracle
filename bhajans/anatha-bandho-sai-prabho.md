## Lyrics

Anatha Bandho Sai Prabho
Aparadha Kshama Karo Sai Prabho
Jeeva Yatra Mey Saath Raho Tum
Prem Bhakthi Hum Ko Deejo
Hrudaya Mandir Se Math Jao
Charanamrutha Seva Deejo Prabhu

## Meaning

Oh Lord Sai! You are the protector and kin of the destitute. Kindly forgive our misdeeds and be with us in our journey of life. Bless us with Divine Love and devotion and be constantly in our thoughts and our hearts, grant us the opportunity to serve Your Lotus feet.
