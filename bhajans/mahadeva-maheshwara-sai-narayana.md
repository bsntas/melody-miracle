## Lyrics

Mahadeva Maheshwara Sai Narayana
Natanagara Dukha Bhanjana Sai Narayana
Karunakara Akhileshwara Sai Narayana
Allah Ho Tum Mahavir Yesu Sai Narayana
Hrudayeshwara Partheeshwara Sai Narayana

## Meaning

Glory to Lord Sai, the Lord of all worlds, the epitome of compassion, the destroyer of sin, skillful dancer and the Lord of Lords. He is verily Lord Shiva, Narayana, Allah, Mahavir and Yesu. He is the indweller and the Lord of Parthi.
