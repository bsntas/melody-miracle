## Meaning

Glory to Thee, Lord Sai, Lord of Puttaparthi, You destroy the cycle of birth and death. Glory to Lord Shiva, the Lord of the Universe. You are the conqueror of Death and You reside on the snow-clad mountain. O enchanting one, You are the remover of the mind's attachment. O Sathya Sai, You are full of effulgence and luster.
