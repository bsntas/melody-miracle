## Lyrics

Jaya Rama Raghu Rama
Ranadheera Sukumara [ Jaya Rama ... ]
Raghupathi Raghava Raja Rama
Rajeeva Lochana Rama Rama

## Meaning

Victory to the lotus-eyed Lord Rama, the mighty and valorous yet so tender natured, the reputed one who is the crown jewel of the Raghu clan.
