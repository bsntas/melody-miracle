## Lyrics

Jeevan Jyothi Jalao Kanhaiya
Jo Kuch Mera Sab Hai Tera [ Jeevan Jyothi ... ]
Daya Karo Mujhey Hey Sai Shyama
Sharan Lagalo Mujhey Krishna Kanhaiya
Hey Anatha Natha Sri Sai Natha

## Meaning

Light the Lamp of my Life, O Young Krishna, I owe everything to You. O Lord, accept me into Your fold and bless me with Your Grace. You are none other than Lord Sai, the refuge of the forlorn.
