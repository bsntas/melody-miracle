## Meaning

Glory to Lord Sathya Sai of Parthi who is Love incarnate and is verily Lord Rama, Krishna, Hari and Hara (Shiva). He is the very embodiment of the Supreme Atma and of Truth and Love. He is verily Lord Sai who incarnated at Shirdi.
