## Lyrics

Ramam Bhajo Raghu Ramam Bhajo
Raghukula Tilakam Ramam Bhajo [ Ramam Bhajo ... ]
Dasharatha Nandana Ramam Bhajo
Dashamukha Mardhana Ramam Bhajo

## Meaning

Sing the name of Rama, who is the star of the Raghu clan, who is beloved son of Dasharatha and who slayed the ten-headed demon, Ravana.
