## Lyrics

Guruvayurpura Sri Hari Krishna Narayana Gopal
Mukunda Madhava Muralidhari Narayana Gopal
Narayana Gopal Sai Narayana Gopal (2)
Govardhana Giridhari Murari Narayana Gopal [ Mukunda Madhava ... ]

## Meaning

Chant the names of the Lord of Guruvayur, Lord Hari, Krishna, Narayana, Gopal, Madhava, Muralidhara, Mukunda and Sai Narayana. Chant the name of Krishna who lifted the Govardhana mountain.
