## Lyrics

Giridhara Gopala Sai Giridhara Gopala
Parthi Pureeshwara Sai Gopala [ Giridhara Gopala ... ]
Meera Ke Prabhu Giridhara Naagara
Paahi Maam Ghana Shyam O Shyama (2)

## Meaning

O Lord Sai Gopala, the lifter of the Govardhana mountain, the Lord of Meera, You are verily Lord Sai, the Lord of Parthi. O Lord, kindly protect me.
