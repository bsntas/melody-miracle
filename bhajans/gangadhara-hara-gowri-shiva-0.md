## Meaning

Glory to Lord Shiva, the one who has the river Ganges on His head and the consort of Gowri. Glory to Lord Sai Shiva, the auspicious one who confers happiness and prosperity. Glory to Thee, the Lord of the world, the greatest Lord who resided as Sai in Shirdi and is now residing in Parthi. Glory to Lord Sai who liberates the world.
