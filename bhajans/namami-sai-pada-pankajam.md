## Lyrics

Namami Sai Pada Pankajam
Smarami Sai Naamamrutham
Bhajami Sai Charanambhujam
Karomi Sai Sankeertanam

## Meaning

Let us worship the Lotus Feet of Lord Sai, meditate upon the nectar of His Name, sing the glory of His Divine Lotus Feet and perform the singing of His glory.
