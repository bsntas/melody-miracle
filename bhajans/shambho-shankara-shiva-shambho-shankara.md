## Lyrics

Shambho Shankara Shiva Shambho Shankara
Shambho Shankara Samba Sadashiva Shambho Shankara
Parthi Nayaka Paramesha Paahi Maam (2) [ Shambho Shankara ... ]

## Meaning

Salutations to Lord Sai, the Lord of Puttaparthi, who is none other than Lord Shiva also glorified as Shambho, Shankara, Sadashiva and Paramesha.
