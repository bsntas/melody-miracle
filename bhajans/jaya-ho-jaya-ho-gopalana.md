## Lyrics

Jaya Ho Jaya Ho Gopalana
Jaya Ho Jaya Ho Narayana
Jaya Ho Jaya Ho Janardhana
Jaya Ho Jaya Ho Niranjana

## Meaning

Victory to Lord Krishna who fosters cowherds, victory to the one whom man is a part of, victory to the one who uplifts humanity and the one who is faultless and pure.
