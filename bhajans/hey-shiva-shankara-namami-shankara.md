## Lyrics

Hey Shiva Shankara Namami Shankara Shiva Shankara Shambho
Hey Girija Pathi Bhavani Shankara
Shiva Shankara Shambho (3)

## Meaning

Prostrations to Lord Shiva or Shankara, the One who bestows auspiciousness. He is the Lord of Mother Girija who is also known as Bhavani.
