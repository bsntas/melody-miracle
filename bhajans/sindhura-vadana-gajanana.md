## Meaning

Worship the elephant-faced Lord Gajanana, the destroyer of obstacles and bestower of spiritual wisdom. Glory to the valorous son of Mother Parvathi (Gowri) and Lord Shiva. Glory to Lord Sai of Parthi who is verily Lord Ganesha.
