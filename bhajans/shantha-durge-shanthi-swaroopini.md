## Lyrics

Shantha Durge Shanthi Swaroopini
Shyamala Komala Mana Mohini [ Shantha Durge ... ]
Adi Anadi Anantha Swaroopini
Moksha Pradayini Narayani (2)

## Meaning

This song extols Mother Durga who has a peaceful appearance, attractive dark complexioned soft features, who is the embodiment of a form that is all pervading without beginning and end and the one who liberates us from the cycle of birth and death.
