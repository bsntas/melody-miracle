## Meaning

Chant the divine names of Lord Narayana and Lord Krishna such as Gopala and Govinda, the beloved of Nanda and the one who bestows liberation to the devotees. O Lord, Your glory has been extolled in the Vedas. Victory to You, O Lord Narayana.
