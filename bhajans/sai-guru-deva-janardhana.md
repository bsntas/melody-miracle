## Meaning

Glory to Lord Sai, the Divine Preceptor, who is none other than Lord Krishna (Janardhana), who is reclining on the serpent Adi Sesha as Lord Narayana. O slayer of the demon Madhu, You are the destroyer of the cycle of birth and death. Praise be to Lord Sai who is our Guru, we bow at His Feet.
