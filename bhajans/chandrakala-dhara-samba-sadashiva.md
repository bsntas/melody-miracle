## Lyrics

Chandrakala Dhara Samba Sadashiva
Shambho Mahadeva (Shiva) (3)
Om Namah Shivaya Shivaya Namah Om (2)

## Meaning

Lord Shiva, who is eternal and unchanging, wears the crescent moon on His head as a decoration. The Lord of the Lords is the bestower of good. Let us chant the mantra "Om Namah Shivaya" which means, "I bow to Lord Shiva".
