## Lyrics

Sab Ka Malik Ek Hai Sai Tum Ho Bhagawan
Sarva Dharma Priya Ram Parthi Pureeshwara Ram
Manava Seva Madhava Sab Ko Seva Karo
Bhajan Bina Sukh Shanthi Nahin Sai Bhajan Karo
Prem Se Sab Ko Seva Karo Sai Bhajan Karo

## Meaning

O Lord Sai, You are the Lord of all. You foster all religions, O Lord of Puttaparthi. Service to mankind is service to God, so serve everyone. There is no happiness or peace without singing the glory of God. Serve all with love and sing the glory of Lord Sai.
