## Lyrics

Jaya Partheesha Jaya Paramesha
Jaya Gowreesha Jaya Gangadhara
Jatadhara Jaya Sai Shankara
Vijayee Bhava Tava Sharanam Dehi
Sharanam Dehi

## Meaning

Victory to Lord Shiva, Lord of Gowri, who bears Mother Ganges in His matted locks, who is the personification of Supreme Consciousness, who is Sai Shankara, the Lord of Parthi. O Lord, I sing Your praises and seek Your protection.
