## Lyrics

Mana Mohana Muralidhari Jaya Radhey Govinda
Jaya Yamuna Teera Vihari Jaya Radhey Govinda
Jaya Brindavana Sanchari Jaya Radhey Govinda
Jaya Meera Prabhu Giridhari Jaya Radhey Govinda
Jaya Brindavana Sanchari Jaya Radhey Govinda [ Jaya Yamuna ... ]

## Meaning

Glory be to Lord Krishna (Govinda) who is a delight to the Mind, the player of Flute, who moves about on the banks of Yamuna river and Brindavan, and who is the Lord of Meera.
