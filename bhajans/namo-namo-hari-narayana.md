## Meaning

I bow down to Lord Sai who is the incarnation of Lord Vishnu - the ultimate abode of all creation. He is the form of the lion-faced Lord Narasimha. He is the form of divine music. He is the Lord of Puttaparthi. He is the one who cleanses us of our sins.
