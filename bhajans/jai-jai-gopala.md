## Lyrics

Jai Jai Gopala
Sai Gopala Shyama Gopala [ Jai Jai ... ]
Hey Nandalala Gopi Lola Gokula Bala
Brindavana Sanchara [ Sai Gopala ... ]

## Meaning

Victory to Lord Gopala (Krishna) whose consort is Radha. Victory to the son of Nanda, to the one who is endearing to the Gopis and who moved about in Brindavan.
