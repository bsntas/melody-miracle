## Lyrics

Bhagawan Bhagawan Patitha Pavana Ram
Daya Karo Daya Karo Daya Karo Bhagawan
Patitha Pavana Ram ( Sai )
Deena Dayalu Parama Krupalu
Raksha Karo Bhagawan
Sai Krupa Karo Bhagawan

## Meaning

O Lord Sai, chanting Your name purifies the downtrodden. O the ever merciful and compassionate Lord, please shower Your grace and protect us.
