## Meaning

Victory to the Divine Preceptor Sai who is none other than Lord Narayana and Lord Mahadeva. Glory to the one who delights in soulful music and dance. O Lord Sai, You are both the Form and Formless divinity and the Incarnation of God in its Totality. You are the Lord of Parthi.
