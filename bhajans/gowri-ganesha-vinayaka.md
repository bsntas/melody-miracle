## Lyrics

Gowri Ganesha Vinayaka (3)
Shuklambara Dhara Natha Gajanana
Sri Ganaraya Vinayaka
Gajavadana Jaya Ganapathi Vandana
Mangala Murthy Gajanana (2)

## Meaning

Victory to the son of Gowri, Lord Ganesha, who is adorned with white garment and who bestows auspiciousness. Victory to Thee, O Lord of the demigods, we offers our obeisance to You.
