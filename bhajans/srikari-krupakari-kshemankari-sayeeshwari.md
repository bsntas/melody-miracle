## Meaning

Glory be to the beautiful, compassionate and all pervading goddess Parvathi, the consort of Shankara (Shiva), the one who bestows auspiciousness, who looks after the welfare of devotees, who bestows fearlessness and who is fond of the 'Tandava' dance.
