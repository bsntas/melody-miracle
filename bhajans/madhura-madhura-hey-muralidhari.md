## Lyrics

Madhura Madhura Hey Muralidhari
Murahara Shourie Hey Giridhari
Parthi Vihari Hrudaya Vihari
Hari Hari Sri Hari Sai Murari

## Meaning

Salutations to Lord Hari who incarnated as Lord Krishna, the sweet one with the flute who destroyed the demon Mura,, the one who gives bliss, the resident of every heart and who is verily Lord Sai, the resident of Parthi.
