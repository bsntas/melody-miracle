## Lyrics

Mukunda Madhava Karivarada Sri Sathya Sai Ram
Govinda Keshava Sri Hari Roopa Sathya Sai Ram
Mathsya Kurma Varaha Narahari Dashavatari Ram
Parthi Vasa Palaya Maam Prabho Sathya Sai Ram

## Meaning

O Lord Sathya Sai, You were the Kari Varada (Lord Narayana, the saviour of the elephant from the clutches of crocodile), Mukunda, Madhava, Govinda, Keshava (various names of Lord Krishna), You are none other than the Ten Incarnations of Lord Vishnu such as, Matsya (Fish), Kurma (Tortoise), Varaha (Boar), Narahari (Narasimha). I pray to You O Lord Sathya Sai to protect me.
