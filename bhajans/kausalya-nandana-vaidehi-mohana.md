## Meaning

The lotus-eyed Lord Rama, son of Kausalya and enchanter of Vaidehi (Sita) is the incarnation of the Treta age. Salutations to the bow-wielding Rama who was worshipped by His brother Bharata. Lord Rama, the uprooter of the demon clan, now dwells at Parthi as Sai.
