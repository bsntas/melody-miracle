## Meaning

Chant the names of Lord Sai, the Divine Preceptor, Lord Govinda, Gopala (names of Lord Krishna). Chant the names of  Lord Sai of Shirdi, Parthi and Prema Sai who are all the embodiments of Love and the very incarnation of the Lord Supreme.
