## Lyrics

Manuva Bolo Radhey Radhey Radhey Radhey Shyam Naam
Ram Ram Ram Bolo Ram Sita Ram Naam [ Manuva Bolo ... ]
Ranga Ranga Ranga Bolo Panduranga Baba Naam
Parthi Vasa Sai Deva Panduranga Baba Naam
Tukaram Namdeva Bhaktha Sakha Baba Naam
Panduranga Panduranga Panduranga Baba Naam

## Meaning

Meditate on the various names of the Lord such as Shyam (Krishna), Ram and Panduranga, who is none other than our Sai, Lord of Parthi. The very same Lord was the friend of Saints like Tukaram and Namdev.
