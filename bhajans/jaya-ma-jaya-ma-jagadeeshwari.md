## Lyrics

Jaya Ma Jaya Ma Jagadeeshwari Sai Ma
Jagadeeshwari Maheshwari Sayeeshwari Sai Ma
Jaya Ma Jaya Ma, Jagadeeshwari Sai Ma 
Jagadeeshwari Maheshwari Sayeeshwari Sai Ma (2)

## Meaning

Victory to Mother Sai who is the Goddess of the Universe. She is the Supreme One and is known as Mother Sayeeshwari.
