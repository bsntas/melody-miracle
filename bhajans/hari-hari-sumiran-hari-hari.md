## Lyrics

Hari Hari Sumiran Hari Hari Bhajo Man
Hari Hi Tero Tan Man Aur Dhan [ Hari Hari ... ]
Tulsidas Bhajey Ramam Ramam
Meera Daasi Bhaja Govindam
Hari Bin Jeevan Kaisa Jeevan
Hari Charanan Mey Arpan Kar Man

## Meaning

O Mind, meditate upon the name of Lord Hari, who is your body, mind and wealth. Lord Hari's devotees Tulasidas sang Rama's name and Meera sang Govinda's name (Krishna). This life without Hari is not worth it. O Mind, surrender at the feet of Hari.
