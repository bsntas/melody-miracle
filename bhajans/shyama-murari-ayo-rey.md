## Meaning

Sing in ecstasy of the arrival of Lord Krishna who is none other than Lord Sai. Lord Krishna is also called Shyama (dark-hued) and Murari (slayer of the demon Mura). Wearing the yellow cloth is a characteristic of Lord Krishna's attire.
