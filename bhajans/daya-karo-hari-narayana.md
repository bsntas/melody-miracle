## Lyrics

Daya Karo Hari Narayana
Krupa Karo Hey Jaga Vandana [ Daya Karo ... ]
Bhavatheetha Bhagya Vidhata
Deena Natha Anatha Ke Natha

## Meaning

O Lord Hari Narayana, bestow on us Your mercy, You are worshipped by the whole world. You are the Lord of the destitute and the helpless; You are the saviour from worldly miseries and the bestower of auspiciousness.
