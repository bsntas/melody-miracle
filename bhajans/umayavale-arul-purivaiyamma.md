## Lyrics

Umayavale Arul Purivai Amma
Omkara Roopini Akhilanda Nayaki
Adiyargal Vinai Tirkkum Adi Parasakti [ Umayavale Arul ... ]
Nan Seida Pizhaigalellam Poruppai Amma
Jagadambige Shivaranjani

## Meaning

Grace us O Goddess Uma! Ruler of the Universe! You are the personification of Aum; You are the divine energy, and the destroyer of sins. Forgive us, all our past errors, O Mother of the Universe, consort of Shiva.
