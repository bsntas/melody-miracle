## Meaning

Glory to Lord Rama, the hero of the Raghu dynasty. Glory to Ramachandra, the illustrious warrior prince. He is verily the heavenly abode, the goal of our life. He is the Lord of Raghu dynasty, the Lord of all worlds. He is our very own relative, the sea of mercy and compassion. Sing the glory of Lord Rama.
