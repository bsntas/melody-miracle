## Lyrics

Hey Vishwa Pala Gopala
Hey Deena Pala Gopala
Meera Ke Prabhu Giridhara Bala
Giridhara Bala Sai Gopala

## Meaning

Sing the glory of Lord Gopala who sustains the whole Universe and the protector of the downtrodden. He held aloft the Govardhana Mountain and is the Lord of Meera. He is verily Lord Sai.
