## Lyrics

Govindethi Sada Snanam
Govindethi Sada Japam
Govindethi Sada Dhyanam
Sada Govinda Keertanam

## Meaning

Always immerse (wash sins away) yourself in the name of Lord Govinda. Always chant, meditate and sing on the name of Govinda.
