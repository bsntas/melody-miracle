## Lyrics

Parthi Pureesha Sai Deva
Sthira Chara Vishwa Vidhata Natha
Deena Dayala Anatha Natha
Bhagawantha Hey Dhrata (2) [ Sthira Chara ... ]

## Meaning

Lord Sai of Parthi is the eternal protector of the movable and immovable world and the entire Universe. He is the savior of the forsaken and fallen.
