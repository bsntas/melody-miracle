## Lyrics

Deena Dayala Hari Parama Dayala
Radha Ramana Hari Govinda Gopala
Madhura Manohara Krishna Gopala
Radha Ramana Hari Govinda Gopala
Meera Ke Prabhu Giridhara Bala
Brindavana Ghana Madana Gopala

## Meaning

Chant the many names of the supremely merciful, sweet and beautiful Lord Krishna - Hari, Govinda, Gopala. He is the one who liftted the Govardhana mountain and is the Lord of Meera.
