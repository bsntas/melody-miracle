## Lyrics

Beda Paar Karo Merey Sai
Uddhar Karo Merey Baba [ Beda Paar ... ]
Allah Eshwar Sai Baba

Reham Nazar Karo Sai Baba
Daya Karo Sai Krupa Karo

## Meaning

O Lord Sai, uplift and help us cross the ocean of life and death. You are Allah, Eshwar and Shirdi Baba. Shower Your mercy, Compassion and Grace on us.
