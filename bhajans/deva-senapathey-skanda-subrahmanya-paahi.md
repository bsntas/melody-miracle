## Lyrics

Deva Senapathey Skanda Subrahmanya Paahi Maam

Subrahmanya Shanmukha Natha
Sai Natha Karuna Sindho [ Paahi Parameshwara ... ]

## Meaning

O Lord Subrahmanya (son of Shiva), the chief of the battalion of Gods (Devas), protect us. O Skanda, the Supreme God, protect us. Protect us, O Lord with six faces, You have incarnated as Lord Sai, the embodiment of kindness.
