## Lyrics

Jaya Jaya Jaya Jaya Ma
Jagat Janani Shubha Karini [ (Ma) Jaya Jaya ... ]
Jagadodharini Sai Ma
Jagat Paripalini Parvathi Ma
Jagat Janani Shubha Karini

## Meaning

We offer our obeisance to our Universal Mother who confers all that is sublime and auspicious. The Uplifter, the Administrator and the Mother of the Universe that is Parvathi, is also Mother Sai.
