## Lyrics

Raghuveera Ranadheera Rama Rama Ram
Rakumayi Vittala Shyama Shyama Shyam [ Raghuveera ... ]
Sukumara Sundara Rama Rama Ram
Shirdeesha Partheesha (Sayeesha) Rama Rama Ram

## Meaning

Let us sing the glories of Lord Rama, the champion of Raghu dynasty, the one who is mighty and valorous. Chant the name of Lord Vittala, the dark hued one and His consort Rakumayi. Victory to the good, young and handsome Rama, who is verily Sai, the Lord of Shirdi and Parthi.
