## Lyrics

Eshwaramba Nandana [ Parthi Pureeshwara ... ]

## Meaning

Glory be to Lord Rama, son of Dasharatha and the consort of Mother Sita. Glory to Lord Krishna, the dark-hued son of Vasudeva and the beloved of Radha. Glory to the very embodiment of Rama and Krishna, Lord Sai of Puttaparthi, son of Mother Eshwaramba.
