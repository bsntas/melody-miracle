## Lyrics

Sharanam Sharanam Sayeesha Sharanam
Sri Guru Mama Guru Sadguru Sai Guru [ Sharanam Sharanam ... ]
Parthi Pureeshwara Sai Gopalam
Shanthi Nivasam Guru Deva Sharanam (Pra)

## Meaning

Surrender to our Lord Sai who is our Divine Preceptor and whose abode is Prashanthi Nilayam in Parthi.
