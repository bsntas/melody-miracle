## Lyrics

Radhey Krishna Govinda Bol Manuva
Sri Hari Madhava Govinda Bol [ Radhey Krishna ... ]
Nanda Ananda Shyama Govinda
Pandari Natha Panduranga Bhajorey

## Meaning

O Mind, chant the names of Krishna, the blissful, dark-hued son of Nanda. Chant the names of Govinda, Madhava and Hari, the Lord of Lakshmi and the name of Panduranga, the Lord of Pandharpur.
