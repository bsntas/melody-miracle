## Meaning

O mind, chant the name of Lord Rama and Krishna. Victory to Lord Rama, the Jewel of the Raghu dynasty. Victory to the dark-hued Lord Krishna, Lord of Radha. Chant the Mantra, 'Harey Ram Harey Krishna'.
