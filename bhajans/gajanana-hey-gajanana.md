## Lyrics

## Meaning

Glory to Lord Gajanana (Ganesha), beloved son of Mother Gowri (Parvathi). O Son of Shiva, You are revered by the whole world, You are the one who bestows knowledge and intelligence, You are the supreme one who has the mouse as Your vehicle. You are Lord Sai, Lord of Puttaparthi.
