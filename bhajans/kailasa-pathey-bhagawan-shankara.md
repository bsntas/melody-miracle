## Lyrics

Kailasa Pathey Bhagawan Shankara
Ayodhya Pathi Sri Rama Raghuvara
Mathura Pathey Sri Krishna Natavara
Parthi Pathey Sai Ram Shankara

## Meaning

Shankara is the Lord of Kailasa. Sri Rama belongs to Raghu lineage and is the Lord of Ayodhya. Krishna the supreme actor is the Lord of Mathura and Sai Shankara is the Lord of Parthi.
