## Lyrics

Giridhara Govinda Gopala Ghana Shyama Nandalala
Mana Mohana Madhusudhana Hey Madhava [ Giridhara Govinda ... ]
Damodara Janardhana Muralidhara Mana Mohana
Namo Sai Narayanaya Namo Sathya Narayanaya
Namo Sai Narayanaya

## Meaning

Chant the various names of Lord Krishna, the divine flutist and the beautiful son of Nanda. Lord Krishna with a dark complexion is the slayer of the demon Madhu. Salutations to the Lord Sai who is verily Narayana.
