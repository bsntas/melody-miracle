## Lyrics

Jaya Narayana Sathya Narayana

Sri Krishna Govinda Sai Narayana

## Meaning

Victory to Lord Krishna also known as Keshava, Madhava, Govinda, Mukunda. Glory to Lord Krishna who is Lord Narayana. Lord Sathya Sai is none other than Lord Krishna.
