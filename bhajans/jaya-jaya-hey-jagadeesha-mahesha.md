## Lyrics

Jaya Jaya Hey Jagadeesha Mahesha
Jaya Jaya Hey Kailasa Vasa [ Jaya Jaya Hey Jagadeesha ... ]

Jaya Hey Shambho Sai Natha
Jaya Jaya Jaya Hey Anatha Natha

## Meaning

Victory to the Universal Lord Mahesha (Shiva), who dwells at Mountain Kailasa and who is the beloved Lord of Mother Gowri (Parvathi). He is the protector of the destitute and is none other than Lord Sai.
