## Lyrics

Karuna Sagara Hey Nandalala
Murali Manohara Shyama Gopala [ Karuna Sagara ... ]
Giridhara Naagara Sai Gopala
Meera Ke Prabhu Shyama Gopala

## Meaning

Glory to the son of Nanda, Lord Krishna (Gopala), who is an ocean of mercy, plays lilting tunes with the Flute, dark-complexioned, the one who lifted the Govardhana mountain and the Lord of Meera.
