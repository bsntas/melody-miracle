## Lyrics

Ajanu Bahum Aravinda Netram

Prabhu Sai Ramam Manasa Smarami (2)

## Meaning

Meditate on the form of Lord Rama, whose abode is the heart of devotees, whose arms extend upto His knees and whose eyes are like the blooming lotus. Meditate on Lord Sai Rama, the Dweller of Parthi, which is verily the Heaven on Earth.
