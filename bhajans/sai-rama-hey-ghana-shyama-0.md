## Lyrics

Sai Rama Hey Ghana Shyama
Parama Dayaghana Atma Rama [ Sai Rama ... ]
Shantha Saguna Hari Hara Paramatma
Bhaktha Samrakshaka Dayabhi Rama
Patitha Pavana Sathya Sai Rama
Sharanam Sharanam Hey Sai Rama

## Meaning

Salutations to Lord Sai Rama, You are the most compassionate, the virtuous one who safeguards the devotees and the one who uplifts the downtrodden. O Lord, we seek Your protection.
