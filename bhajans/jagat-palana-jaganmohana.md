## Lyrics

Jagat Palana Jaganmohana
Sundara Sai Gopalana
Munijana Sevitha Madhusudhana [ Jagat Palana ... ]
Keshava Govinda Janardhana
Sridhara Madhava Damodara
Achyutha Anantha Narayana
Sri Sai Krishna Narayana (2) [ Munijana Sevitha ... ]

## Meaning

Sing the various names of the Lord Krishna, Gopala, Keshava, Govinda, Janardhana, Sridhara, Madhava, Damodara, Achyutha, Anantha, Narayana, and Madhusudhana. He is the nourisher and captivator of the Universe, the one who is handsome and served by saints, who has incarnated as Lord Sai.
