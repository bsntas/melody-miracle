## Lyrics

Hey Madhava Hey Madhusudhana
Damodara Hey Muralidhara [ Hey Madhava ... ]
Mana Mohana Hey Yadu Nandana
Deenavana Bhava Bhaya Bhanjana

## Meaning

Glory be to the Lord Madhava, who vanquished demon Madhu and who holds the Flute in His hands. Lord Damodara removes the fear of repetitive births, has an Enchanting Form and the source of succor to the underprivileged.
