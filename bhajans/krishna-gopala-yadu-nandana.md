## Lyrics

Krishna Gopala Yadu Nandana
Venu Vilolana Mana Mohana [ Krishna Gopala ... ]
Mora Mukuta Gokula Ramana
Ghana Shyama Ananda Govinda
Krishna Murari Madhusudhana

## Meaning

Sing the Glory of God by chanting His various names. Krishna, Gopala, Yadu Nandana, Ghana Shyama, Govinda, Ananda and Murari. Krishna delights in playing the Flute, is enchanting in form with a peacock plumage on His head and the chieftain of Gokul.
