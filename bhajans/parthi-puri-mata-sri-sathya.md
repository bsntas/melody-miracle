## Lyrics

Parthi Puri Mata Sri Sathya Sai Mata
Daya Karo Mata Mujhey Krupa Karo Mata
Merey Janam Janam Saathi Merey Jeevan Ke Aadhaar
Terey Charanon Ke Pyaasi Mujhey Darshan Do Mata

## Meaning

O Mother Sathya Sai of Puttaparthi, please have mercy and show compassion on me. You are the Eternal Companion and the very basis of my life. I thirst for the vision of Thy Lotus Feet, please grant me Your Divine Darshan, O Mother Sai.
