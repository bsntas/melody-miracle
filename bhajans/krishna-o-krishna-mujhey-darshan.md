## Meaning

Hey Krishna! Please grant Your Divine vision (darshan) to me. O blue-complexioned Lord Krishna, the Destroyer of the demon Mura, You lifted the mountain and enchanted everyone with Your Flute music. O Lord Sathya Sai, You are my beloved Lord Krishna, please listen to my heart’s yearnings. Come O Krishna, please come now and grant me Your darshan; I am longing for Your Divine Presence.
