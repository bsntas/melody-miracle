## Lyrics

Raghu Nandana Raghava Ram Harey
Hey Janaki Jeevana Ram Harey
Hey Rajeeva Lochana Ram Harey
Sri Rama Harey Sai Rama Harey (5)

## Meaning

Worship Lord Rama of Raghu Dynasty. Worship the lotus-eyed Lord Rama, who is the very life-breath of Mother Sita. Sai Rama who is verily Lord Rama, we sing Your praises.
