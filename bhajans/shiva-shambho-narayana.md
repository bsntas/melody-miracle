## Lyrics

Shiva Shambho Narayana
Parthi Vasa Jagadeeshwara [ Shiva Shambho ... ]
Shirdi Vasa Jagadeeshwara
Sathya Sai Shiva Shankara

## Meaning

We pray to our Lord Sai who is the embodiment of Lord Shiva and Lord Narayana who has come down to earth as the Lord of Parthi and Shirdi and the Lord of all the worlds. We surrender to you Oh Lord Sathya Sai Shiva Shankara.
