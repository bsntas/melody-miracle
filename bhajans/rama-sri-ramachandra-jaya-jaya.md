## Meaning

Pray to Lord Rama, who shines like the full moon, the compassionate, the attractive and the auspicious one who wields the bow, Kodanda . He is the one who protects those who surrender at His Lotus Feet. Pray to Lord Rama, the victorious heavenly warrior.
