## Lyrics

Omkara Roopa Sri Sai Natha
Sadguru Sri Sathya Sai Harey
Allah Yesu Eshwar Tum Ho
Gautama Buddha Mahavir Ho
Zohrashtra Guru Grantha Nanak Tum Ho
Ram Rahim Sai Krishna Karim

## Meaning

Pray to Lord Sathya Sai who is the embodiment of Omkara; who is the True Guru, the one who removes darkness and gives enlightenment; He is verily Allah, Yesu, Eshwar, Buddha, Mahavir, Zohrashtra, Guru Nanak, Ram, Rahim and Krishna.
