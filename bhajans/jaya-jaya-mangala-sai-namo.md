## Lyrics

Jaya Jaya Mangala Sai Namo
Jaya Partheeshwara Sai Namo [ Jaya Jaya ... ]
Shiva Shaktheeshwara Sai Namo

## Meaning

Prostrations to Lord Sai, the Lord of Parthi, the embodiment of Shiva and Shakthi (Matter and Energy) who also dwells at Shaila Mountains.
