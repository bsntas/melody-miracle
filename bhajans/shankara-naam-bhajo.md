## Lyrics

Shankara Naam Bhajo
Shankara Shankara Naam Bhajo (3) [ Shankara Naam ... ]
Peetambara Dhara Sai Shankara
Chandrakala Dhara Gowri Manohara

## Meaning

Chant the name of Lord Shankara (Shiva) the consort of Gowri. Lord Sai, the one who adorns the yellow dress (Peetambara) is verily Lord Shiva who has the crescent Moon on the head.
