## Meaning

Glory be to Lord Krishna, the one who vanquished the demon Madhu. Glory to the charmer who moves about in Brindavan. Praise the one who dwells in Shirdi and Parthi as Lord Sai. Repeat the names of Radhey Shyam and Sairam.
