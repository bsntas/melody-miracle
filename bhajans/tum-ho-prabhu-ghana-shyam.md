## Lyrics

Tum Ho Prabhu Ghana Shyam O Sai
Parthi Vihari Shyama Murari [ Tum Ho ... ]
Meera Ke Prabhu Giridhara Naagara
Natavara Sundara Shyama Murari
Charana Kamala Balihaari Murari

## Meaning

O Lord Sai, You are verily Lord Krishna, the one with the dark complexion of a rain cloud, who slayed the demon Mura. You reside at Puttaparthi. You are the supreme actor, the most beautiful Lord who lifted the Govardhana hill. You are the Lord of Meera. We surrender at Thy Lotus feet, O Lord.
