## Lyrics

Sri Ranga Harey Vittala Sai Ranga Harey Vittala
Hey Daya Nidhey Vittala Hey Krupa Nidhey Vittala

## Meaning

Glory to Lord Vittala who is all compassionate and merciful. Salutations to Lord Sathya Sai who is verily Lord Vittala.
