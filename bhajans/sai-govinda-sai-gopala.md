## Meaning

Oh Lord Sai Govinda! Oh Lord Sai Gopala! Without Thee, there is no one to lead us to the light (of wisdom) and to remove the darkness (of ignorance). Oh Lord Sai Govinda! I pray to Thee to kindly accept me at Your Lotus feet. Oh Lord Sai Gopala! Kindly install Thyself in the temple of my mind and heart.
