## Meaning

Victory to Lord Rama, the one with the famous bow, Kodanda. Victory to Him, the bestower of auspiciousness. Victory to Him, the beloved of Vaidehi (Mother Sita). Victory to Lord Rama, the crown Prince, the one with the sweet form and who resides in our hearts, Victory to Lord Sai who is verily Lord Rama.
