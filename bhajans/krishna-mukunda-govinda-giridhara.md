## Lyrics

Krishna Mukunda Govinda Giridhara
Murali Mohana Narayana
Parthi Purandara Narayana (2)
Madhava Sri Hari Mandara Giridhari
Govinda Murahara Narayana (Harey) [ Parthi Purandara ...) ]

## Meaning

Chant the names of Lord Krishna, Mukunda, Govinda, Giridhara, Murali, Narayana, Madhava, Hari, Murahara. Lord Sai is none other than Lord Krishna.
