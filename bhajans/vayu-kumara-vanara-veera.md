## Lyrics

Vayu Kumara Vanara Veera
Ati Balavanta Hanumantha
Kesari Nandana Anjani Putra
Jaya Ho Jaya Ho Pavana Kumara (2)
Jaya Ho Jaya Ho Pavana Kumara (2)

## Meaning

O Lord Hanuman, the son of Kesari and Anjana, You are the most powerful. Victory to You, O blessed son of the Wind God (born to Kesari and Anjana out of boon from the Lord of Wind).
