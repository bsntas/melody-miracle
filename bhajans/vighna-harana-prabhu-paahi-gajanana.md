## Lyrics

Vighna Harana Prabhu Paahi Gajanana
Parvathi Nandana Parama Dayaghana
Nithya Niranjana Bhava Bhaya Bhanjana
Pashupathi Tanaya Hey Jagavandana
Lambodhara Hey Mooshika Vahana [ Parvathi Nandana ... ]

## Meaning

Protect us, O elephant-faced Lord Ganesha, the remover of obstacles, the most compassionate and the ever pure son of Mother Parvathi. O the son of Shiva, who is worshipped by the whole world, the one with a large stomach and the one who has the mouse as the vehicle, we pray to You to remove our fear of worldly existence.
