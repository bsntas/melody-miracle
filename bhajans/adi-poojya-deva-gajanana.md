## Lyrics

Adi Poojya Deva Gajanana
Gowrivara Tanaya Shubhanana [ Adi Poojya ... ]
Vighna Vinashaka Siddhi Vinayaka
Jaya Jagavandana Tava Charanam

## Meaning

At the outset, worship Lord Ganesha, son of Mother Gowri who bestows good fortune. He destroys difficulties and grants success. The entire Universe worships His Feet.
