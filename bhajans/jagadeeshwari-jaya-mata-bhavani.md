## Lyrics

Jagadeeshwari Jaya Mata Bhavani

Hey Shiva Shankari Triloka Palini
Sharanagatha Paripalini Mata

## Meaning

Mother Bhavani is the Goddess of the entire universe. She sustains the three worlds, compassionate and beautiful. She is the consort of Lord Shiva and takes complete care of all who surrender to Her.
