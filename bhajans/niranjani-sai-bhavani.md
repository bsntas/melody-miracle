## Meaning

Salutations to the blemishless Mother Sai, the creator and bestower of life. The universal mother is the source of bliss divine. She is the combination of Durga (power) and Lakshmi (wealth), Narayani (all-round welfare) and Hamsa vahini (Saraswathi - education), Kamalasana (seated on the Lotus bloom). We bow to the mother who is everything for us.
