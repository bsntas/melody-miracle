## Meaning

O Lord Sai Baba, son of Eshwaramba, creator of the universe, dweller of Puttaparthi, noble Lord and Teacher, O lotus-eyed one, indweller of devotees' hearts, O Sai Krishna, incarnation of this age, bestow Thy Grace on us, make us merge in You.
