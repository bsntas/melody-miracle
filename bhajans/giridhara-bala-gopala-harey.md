## Lyrics

Giridhara Bala Gopala (Harey)
Govinda Govinda Gopala [ Giridhara Bala ... ]
Gopi Lola Gopala
Govardhanodhari Gopala

## Meaning

Glory to Lord Krishna, the cowherd boy who danced with the Gopis and the one who lifted the Govardhana mountain.
