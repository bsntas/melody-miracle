## Lyrics

Govinda Govinda Bhaja Mana Radhey Govinda
Radha Lola Govinda Raasa Vilola Gopala
Krishna Keshava Harey Madhava Govinda Jaya Govinda
Harey Murarey Sai Murarey Govinda Jaya Govinda
Govinda Jaya Govinda Govinda Jaya Govinda (2)

## Meaning

Let us chant the divine name of Lord Govinda (Krishna) reverberating in the mind of Radha, whose beloved is Lord Krishna who is engrossed in the divine sports with the Gopikas. O Lord, You are the destroyer of demon Keshi. Victory to You O Sai who is none other than Govinda.
