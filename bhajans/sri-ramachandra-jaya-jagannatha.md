## Lyrics

Sri Ramachandra Jaya Jagannatha
Sita Pathey Jaya Raghu Natha
Patitha Pavana Deena Natha
Deena Natha Deena Natha [ Patitha Pavana ... ]
Parthi Pureeshwara Sai Natha [ Sita Pathey ... ]

## Meaning

Worship Lord Rama, who is the Lord of the whole world and of the Raghu Dynasty, the consort of Sita, the one who sanctifies the sinners and who is verily the Lord of Parthi - Lord Sai.
