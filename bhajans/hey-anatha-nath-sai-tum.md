## Lyrics

Hey Anatha Nath Sai Tum Ho Antaryami
Tum Ho Merey Bhagawan
Deena Dukha Bhanjana Sahara Do Mujhey
O Merey Sai Bhagawan
Hrudaya Mandir Mey Aao Sai Merey
Deejo Krupa Vardaan [ Tum Ho ... ]

## Meaning

Oh Sai! You are verily my Lord, for You are the indweller and champion of distraught and helpless. Oh Lord! Take up my cause for You are the one who destroys the sorrows of the forlorn. Enter the temple of my heart and shower Your blessings.
