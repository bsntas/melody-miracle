## Lyrics

Govinda Madhava Gopala Keshava (3)
Krishna Krishna Sai Krishna
Krishna Krishna Gopala
Gokula Bala Gopi Ramana
Mana Mohana Madhusudhana Jaya Madhava Jaya Keshava

## Meaning

Glory to Thee O Lord Krishna who is known by many names - Govinda, Madhava, Gopala and Keshava. You are the adorable child of Gokula and the Lord of Gopis. You are the one who enchants and attracts, the slayer of demon Madhu. Victory to Lord Sai who is none other than Lord Krishna.
