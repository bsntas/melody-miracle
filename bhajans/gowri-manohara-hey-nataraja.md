## Lyrics

Gowri Manohara Hey Nataraja
Shiva Shiva Shankara Hey Shiva Raja
Pavana Charitha Paahi Mahesha
Paahi Mahesha Hey Jagadeesha
Karunamaya Prabho Hey Jagadeesha

## Meaning

Glory to Thee, Lord Nataraja (dancing Shiva), the beloved of Gowri, You are the supreme God who grants us auspiciousness. Those who extol You merge in divinity. Protect us, oh Lord Mahesha, the compassionate Lord of the universe.
