## Lyrics

Krishna Murari Bhava Bhaya Haari Hey Giridhari Gopal
Parthi Vihari Hrudaya Vihari Bhaktha Sakha Bhagawan
Bhaktha Sakha Bhagawan ( Sai ) (2)

## Meaning

The slayer of demon Murari also destroys the fear of the cycle of birth and death, the resident of Parthi is the true friend of his devotees.
