## Lyrics

Raghava Raghuvara Rama Bhajo
Mandara Giridhara Shyama Bhajo [ Raghava Raghuvara ... ]
Tulasidas Prabho Rama Bhajo
Meera Mohana Shyam Bhajo
Raghupathi Raghava Rama Bhajo Murali Mohana Shyama Bhajo
Nanda Nandana Shyama Bhajo Dasharatha Nandana Rama Bhajo 
Sai Sundara Naama Bhajo

## Meaning

Sing the praises of Lord Rama (Raghava) of Raghu lineage, the Lord of Saint Tulasidas, the son of Dasharatha and of Shyama (Lord Krishna), who lifted the Mandara mountain, the Lord of Meera, the one who delights everyone with lilting music of His Flute, the son of Nanda. Praise our beautiful (Sundara) Lord Sai.
