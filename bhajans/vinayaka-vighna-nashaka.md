## Lyrics

Vinayaka Vighna Nashaka
Anatha Rakshaka Ananda Dayaka
Uma Maheshwara Hey Shiva Nandana

## Meaning

Salutations to Lord Vinayaka, the destroyer of obstacles, protector of the downtrodden, who bestows happiness and who is the son of Goddess Uma (Parvathi's another name) and Lord Shiva.
