## Lyrics

Jayatu Shiva Natana Shiva

Bhasma Vibhushitha Phaala Shashidhara

Pinaka Vaibhava Sai Shankara

## Meaning

Sing the glories of the Lord of Dance, Lord Shiva, the Universal and Sovereign Lord. Shiva has the Sacred Ash smeared all over His body, the Crescent Moon on His forehead, has brown-colored eyes, charms Mother Ganges and holds the Bow (Pinaka) in His hand. Lord Sai is none other than Lord Shiva.
