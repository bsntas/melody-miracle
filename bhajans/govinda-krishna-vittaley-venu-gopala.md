## Lyrics

Govinda Krishna Vittaley Venu Gopala Krishna Vittaley
Ranga Ranga Vittaley Sri Panduranga Vittaley
Govinda Krishna Vittaley Venu Gopala Krishna Vittaley [ Ranga Ranga ... ]

## Meaning

Sing the Glory of Govinda, Krishna, Vittala. Sing the Glory of Venugopala, the one who plays the flute. O Krishna, You are Panduranga who is the Lord of Pandari.
