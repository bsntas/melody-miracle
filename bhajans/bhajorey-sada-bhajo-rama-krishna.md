## Lyrics

Bhajorey Sada Bhajo Rama Krishna Govinda
Bhajo Hari Deena Bandhu Krishna Rama Govinda
Bhajorey Anatha Natha Sai Rama Govinda [ Bhajo Hari ... ]

## Meaning

Always chant the name of Lord Rama, Krishna Govinda and Sai Ram. The Lord's name support and shelter the meek and is the friend of the weak.
