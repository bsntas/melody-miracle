## Lyrics

Rama Krishna Tum Ho Jaya Ram Jaya Ram
Sai Krishna Tum Ho Sai Ram Sai Ram
Yesu Pitha Tum Ho Hey Ram Hey Ram
Allah Eshwar Ho Allah Ho Akbar

## Meaning

O Lord Sai You are everybody - Rama, Krishna, Jesus, Allah, Eshwar (Shiva) and Shirdi Sai.
