## Lyrics

Gowri Vara Gangadhara
Shambho Shiva Bimbadhara [ Gowri Vara ... ]
Vyagrambaradhara Jyothi Prakasha
Pavana Naama Harey Paramesha
Om Namah Shivaya Shivaya Namah Om (2)

## Meaning

Salutation to Shiva, the embodiment of Aum, who is the consort of Gowri. You are adorned with the Ganges on the head and the crescent moon, wearing the tiger skin as your attire. You are the supreme God of effulgence and the saviour of all.
