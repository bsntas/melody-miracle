## Lyrics

Sri Raghu Nandana Janaki Jeevana
Rama Rama Rama Sai (Ram)
Alakh Niranjana Asura Nikhandana
Rajeeva Lochana Bhava Bhaya Bhanjana [ Rama Rama ... ]

## Meaning

Worship Lord Rama, who is the consort of Janaki (Sita), who is always pure, the slayer of demons, the one with eyes like Lotus and who destroys the fear of the materialistic world.
