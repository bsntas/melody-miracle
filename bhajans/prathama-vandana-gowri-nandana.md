## Lyrics

Prathama Vandana Gowri Nandana
Hey Shiva Nandana Paahi Gajanana [ Prathama Vandana ... ]
Eka Dantha Gunavanta Vinayaka
Vighna Harana Shubha Mangala Charana
Pranava Swaroopa Paahi ( Sai ) Gajanana

## Meaning

Foremost obeisance to Lord Ganesh, the beloved Prince of Lord Shiva and Mother Gowri. Oh Lord Sai Gajanana, the single-tusked One of noble character, You are the remover of obstacles, the bestower of auspiciousness. We bow to You, the embodiment of Pranava (Omkar).
