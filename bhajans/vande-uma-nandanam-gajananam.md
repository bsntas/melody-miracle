## Lyrics

Vande Uma Nandanam Gajananam
Suramuni Sevitha Sumukha Gajananam [ Vande Uma ... ]
Vidya Dayaka Buddhi Pradayaka
Siddhi Vinayaka Paahi Gajananam

## Meaning

Salutations to Lord Gajanana, the son of Uma and the one who is worshipped by sages, who is the giver of knowledge, intellect and boons.
