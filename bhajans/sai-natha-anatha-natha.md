## Meaning

Glory to Lord Sai, the Lord of Parthi, who is the Lord and Protector of the destitute. He is verily Lord Shankara(Shiva) who is adorned with the crescent Moon on His head, who rides the Nandi (Divine Bull) and who is Lord of the meek and innocent.
