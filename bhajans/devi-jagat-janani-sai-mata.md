## Meaning

O Mother Sai, You are the Mother to the entire world, You are the form of Pranava (Primordial Sound, Om) and You are the very life. O Embodiment of wisdom, You grant us the knowledge and wisdom. O Daughter of Dhaksha (Goddess Parvathi), You are the most beautiful one in all the three worlds. You are Bhagawathi, Bharati and Narayani. O Goddess of the entire world, You enchant the whole world. O Mother Sathya Sai, You are the embodiment of compassion.
