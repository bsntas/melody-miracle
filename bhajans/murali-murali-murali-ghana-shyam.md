## Lyrics

Murali Murali Murali Ghana Shyam
Mohana Murali Radhey (Sai) Shyam [ Murali Murali ... ]
Kanakambara Dhara Karuna Sagara
Meera Ke Prabhu Giridhara Naagara
Parthi Pureeshwara Sai Shyam

## Meaning

Salutations to Lord Krishna (Shyam) who plays enchanting music on the Flute (Murali). He is the beloved of Radha, He adorns the golden colored cloth, He is compassion personified, He is the Lord of His greatest devotee Meera, He saved the cowherds by deftly lifting the Govardhana Mountain. He is none other than Lord Sai residing in Parthi.
