## Lyrics

Abhaya Pradayini Ananda Roopini
Sai Mata Sathya Sai Mata
Himagiri Nandini Bhava Bhaya Bhanjani
Jaya Jagat Janani Devi Bhavani
Parthi Pureeshwari Sai Janani

## Meaning

Mother Sai is the embodiment of Truth, the one who bestows protection from fear and bliss personified. She is verily Mother Parvathi, the daughter of Daksha (Ice Mountain) and destroyer of worldly miseries. Victory to this Universal Mother Bhavani, the Goddess of Parthi.
