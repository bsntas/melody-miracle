## Lyrics

Gajavadana Ganeshwara
Suramuni Sevitha Vighna Vinasha [ Gajavadana … ]
Shwethambhara Dhara Pranava Swaroopa
Mangala Dayaka Paapa Vinashaka
Partheeshwaraya Sai Ganesha

## Meaning

Glory to the Lord of the Ganas, Lord Ganesha, the elephant-faced one, the destroyer of obstacles and who is worshipped by the sages and noble people. Glory to one clad in white, the very embodiment of Om, who bestows auspiciousness and destroys our sins. Salutations to the Lord of Parthi who is verily Lord Ganesha.
