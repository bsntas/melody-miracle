## Lyrics

Kanhaiya Teri Bansi Bajey Merey Lal
Nis Din Mein Bhajoon Tero Naam [ Kanhaiya Teri ... ]

Gopala Gopala
Govinda Govinda Govinda Gopala
Sai Krishna Sai Ram

## Meaning

Play Your enchanting flute, dear Lord Krishna, while I chant Your name everyday. O Lord Gopala (Krishna), the beloved son of Nanda, You are Govinda, the protector of cows and verily Sai Krishna and Sai Rama.
