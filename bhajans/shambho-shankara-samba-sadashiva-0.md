## Meaning

Glory to Lord Shankara (Shiva), the auspicious one who confers prosperity, the one who is eternally blissful, the consort of Amba (Parvathi) and the destroyer of evil traits. Glory to the greatest God who holds the sacred river Ganga in His matted locks and the one who pleased by dance. Salutations to the Lord of Parthi, Lord Sai, who is the verily Lord Shiva.
