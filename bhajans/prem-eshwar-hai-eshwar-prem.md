Koi Bhi Naam Japorey Manuva Eshwar Prem Hai (Manuva)

## Meaning

God is Love; Love is God. Lord Sai is the indweller of the heart and is the sustainer of each heart beat, which repeats that God is Love. Oh Mind! worship any name of the Lord - Rama or Krishna, Rahim, Zohrashtra, Jesus or Nanak, as all names and forms are love and only love.
