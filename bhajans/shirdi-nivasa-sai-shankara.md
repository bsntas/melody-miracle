## Lyrics

Shirdi Nivasa Sai Shankara
Parthi Nivasa Sai Shankara
Bhaavatheetha Bhaava Gochara
Kaalatheetha Kaala Bhairava
Jai Jagadeeshwara Omkareshwara (5)

## Meaning

Glory to Lord Sai Shankara who is the embodiment of Shirdi Baba and Parthi Baba, and who is beyond time and imagination. Victory to Lord Sai, the Lord of the world and who is the very embodiment of Om.
