## Meaning

Glory to You Lord Sai Baba, the Embodiment of Truth. Glory to You, Lord of Shirdi and Parthi, the embodiment and protector of Truth. Glory to the Supreme Lord who is Omnipresent and who bestows auspiciousness and Grace.
