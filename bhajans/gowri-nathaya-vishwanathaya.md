## Lyrics

Gowri Nathaya Vishwanathaya
Sai Nathaya Namah Shivaya
Gunatheethaya Gunadharaya [ Sai Nathaya ... ]
Kaala Kaalaya Kaalatheethaya
Kaala Roopaya Namah Shivaya
Prema Roopaya Parthi Vasaya [ Sai Nathaya ... ]
Om Namah Shivaya Sai Shivaya (4)

## Meaning

Obeisance to Lord Shiva, who is the consort of Gowri and the Lord of the universe. You are Sai Natha who embodies all attributes yet attributeless. You are the embodiment of time, beyond time and the Lord of time. You are the resident of Parthi whose form is Love.
