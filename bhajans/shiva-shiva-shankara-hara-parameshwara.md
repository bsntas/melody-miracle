## Lyrics

Shiva Shiva Shankara Hara Parameshwara
Bala Gangadhara Sai
Shiva Shiva Shankara Parthi Pureeshwara
Nata Shiva Shankara Sai [ Bala Gangadhara ... ]

## Meaning

Worship Shiva who is also known as Hara, Shankara, Gangadhara, Parameshwara and Nata Shiva Shankara (the one who performs Cosmic dance). He is also Lord of Parthi.
