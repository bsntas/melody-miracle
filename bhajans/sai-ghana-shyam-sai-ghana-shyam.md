## Lyrics

Sai Ghana Shyam Sai Ghana Shyam

## Meaning

Sing the praises of Lord Krishna who is also known as Govinda and Gopala, who is of deep blue complexion, holding the Flute in His hands. He is none other than our beloved Sai.
