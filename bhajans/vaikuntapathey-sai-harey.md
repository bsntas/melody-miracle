## Lyrics

Vaikuntapathey Sai Harey
Vaijayanti Mala Dhari [ Vaikuntapathey Sai ... ]
Hari Govinda Hari Gopala (3)
Vara Godavari (Chithravathi) Teera Vihari

## Meaning

Glory to Lord Sai who is verily Lord Narayana of Vaikunta, who wears the Vaijayanti garland (a necklace worn by Lord Narayana as a sign of victory). He is Lord Hari and Govinda who resides on the banks of river Godavari and on the banks of the river Chithravathi as Lord Sai.
