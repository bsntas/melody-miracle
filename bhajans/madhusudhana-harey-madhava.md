## Lyrics

Madhusudhana Harey Madhava

Janardhana Jagat Palana
Deenavana Dukha Bhanjana
Aravinda Lochana Ananda Naam [ Sri Vasudeva ... ]

## Meaning

Chant the names of Lord Krishna - Madhusudhana, Madhava, Vasudeva, Janardhana, Hari, Keshava, Narayana and Govinda. Chant the names of the one who charms the Mind, the motivator of people, the sustainer of the Universe, the destroyer of sorrows, the one who is lotus-eyed and whose name itself is Bliss.
