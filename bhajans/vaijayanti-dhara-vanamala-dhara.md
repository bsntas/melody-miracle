## Lyrics

Vaijayanti Dhara Vanamala Dhara
Shankha Chakra Dhara Peetambara Dhara [ Vaijayanti Dhara ... ]
Sri Kara Purandara Giridhara Naagara
Muralidhara Ghana Shyama Manohara

## Meaning

Glory to the dark-hued Lord Krishna who wears a garland of flowers and plays the enchanting flute. Wearing the golden robe, He is Lord Vishnu who holds the Discus (time) and the Conch (the sound Om). He is the Indweller who confers auspiciousness.
