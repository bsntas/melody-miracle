Eshwara Allah Terey Naam
Sab Ko Sanmati Do Bhagawan

## Meaning

Chant the name of Lord Sai Ram who resides in Parthi. He is Lord Rama, the consort of Mother Sita. He is the noblest of the Raghu dynasty who uplifts the sinners. He is none other than Lord Shiva and Allah. Oh Lord, bless us with wisdom.
