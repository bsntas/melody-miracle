## Lyrics

Mandara Mala Dhara Jaya Gowri Shankara
Hara Hara Paahi Trinetra
Gangadhara Bimbadhara Chandramouli Shankara [ Mandara Mala Dhara ... ]
Vamadeva Mahadeva Shiva Shiva Shiva Shankara
Sathya Dharma Shanthi Prema Sathya Sai Shankara

## Meaning

Victory to Lord Shankara, the consort of Gowri, who adorns the garland of 'Mandara' flowers. Victory to the benevolent three-eyed one who wears the Ganga and the moon on His crown of hair. Victory to the God of Gods, Lord Shiva. He is none other than our Sathya Sai who is the embodiment of Truth, Righteousness, Peace and Love.
