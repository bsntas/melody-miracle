## Lyrics

Shambhu Kumara Gowri Tanaya
Gajanana Sadaya Jaya Jaya
Varada Ganesha Duritha Vinasha
Pranavakaara Natha Sadaya [ Gajanana Sadaya ... ]

## Meaning

Salutations to Lord Ganesha, the son of Lord Shiva (Shambhu) and Mother Parvathi (Gowri), the one who grants boons, destroys obstacles and who is personification of the Primordial sound "Om".
