## Lyrics

Sridhara Sundara Radhikeshwara
Gaana Priyakara Murali Manohara [ Sridhara Sundara ]
Natavara Giridhara Gopikeshwara
Sayeeshwara Sathya Sayeeshwara

## Meaning

The beautiful Lord Vishnu - who is the Lord of Lakshmi and adored by Radha, the Lord who is fond of music and beautiful to look at with his flute, who is the supreme actor and dancer, the Lord of the Gopis, one who lifted the Govardhana Mountain, He is none other than our Lord Sathya Sayeeshwara.
