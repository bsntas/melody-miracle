## Lyrics

Gajanana Hey Shubhanana
Gowri Manohara Priya Nandana
Pashupathi Tanaya Gajanana
Parama Niranjana Shubhanana (Hey)

## Meaning

O elephant-faced Lord Ganesha, You are the loving son of Mother Gowri and the beloved son of Lord Shiva, who is the Lord of all creatures. You are the Supreme Lord, pure and free of all blemishes and the bestower of auspiciousness.
