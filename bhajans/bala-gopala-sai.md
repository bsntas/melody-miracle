## Lyrics

Bala Gopala Sai Bala Gopala
Devaki Nandana Gopala [ Bala Gopala ... ]
Vasudeva Nandana Gopala
Yashoda Nandana Gopala
Nanda Gopala Ananda Gopala
Sai Gopala Sathya Sai Gopala

## Meaning

Chant the names of Lord Gopala (Krishna). Gopala is the dear son of Mother Devaki and Vasudeva and fostered by Mother Yashoda and Nanda. He is the one who confers bliss. He is verily Lord Sathya Sai.
