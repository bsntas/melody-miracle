## Lyrics

Guru Nanak Ji Ki Jai Jai Kaar
Jo Boley So Hoyey Nihaal
Allah (Maulah) Sai Lelo Salaam
Salaam Salaam Lakhon (Mera) Salaam
Yesu Pitha Prabhu Sai Ram
Buddha Zohrashtra Mahavira Naam [ Salaam Salaam ... ]

## Meaning

Glory to Guru Nanak; whoever sings thus, that person becomes liberated. Accept my salutations, O Lord Sai, You are Allah; Accept these ten million salutations of mine; O Sai, You are Father Jesus, our Lord and You are Buddha, Zohrashtra and Mahavira.
