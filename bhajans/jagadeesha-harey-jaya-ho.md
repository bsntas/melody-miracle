## Meaning

Victory to Lord Sai, the Lord and Redeemer of the Universe. Lord Sathya Sai is the very embodiment of Allah, Jesus Christ, Nanak, Buddha, Mahavira and Zohrashtra. Sai is the indweller and embodies Supreme Peace.
