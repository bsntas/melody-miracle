## Meaning

Oh Mother Sai! You are that Pure Consciousness, Mother of the entire universe and the originator of the Primordial sound 'Om' from which this Universe has come into being. Oh Mother! You are superior even to the best, the embodiment of Matter and Energy principle and the ever-blissful.
