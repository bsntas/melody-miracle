## Lyrics

## Meaning

O blue-complexioned Lord Krishna, beloved of Radha, You are the destroyer of the demon Mura. Victory to You Lord Sai, You are verily Lord Krishna, the one who bestows liberation. Victory to the blissful son of Nanda, Lord Govinda, the protector of all beings.
