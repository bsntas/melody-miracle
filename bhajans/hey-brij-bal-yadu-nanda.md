## Lyrics

Hey Brij Bal Yadu Nanda Gopal
Shyama Sundara Mohana Gopal [ Hey Brij ... ]
Yashoda Ke Bal Yadu Nanda Gopal
Gopal Gopal Gokula Bal

## Meaning

Oh Lord Gopala, You are the son of Nanda and Yashoda and of the Yadava lineage. You are the one who moved about in the thick bushes of Brindavan, is of dark complexion and attractive to behold.
