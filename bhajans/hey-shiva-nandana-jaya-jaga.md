## Lyrics

Hey Shiva Nandana Jaya Jaga Vandana
Jaya Gowri Sutha Paahi Gajanana
Parvathi Nandana Parama Daya Ghana
Paahi Prabho Maam Paahi Gajanana

## Meaning

O Lord Ganesha, the son of Lord Shiva and Mother Gowri, the entire Universe bows down to You in reverence. Save me, O the elephant-faced son of Mother Parvathi, for You are the one with Supreme compassion.
