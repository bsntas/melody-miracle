## Meaning

Lord Sai, You are Allah and Jesus. Everything visible is Your own form. You are the Lord of Mecca and Madina as well as Mohammad Nabi. Incarnated previously as Buddha, Mahavir, Zoroaster and Jesus, You are the boundless ocean of compassion and the Lord who blesses the faithful.
