## Lyrics

Vibhuti Sundara Sai Nath
Sai Nath Sai Nath Sathya Sai Nath
Gangadhara Hara Samba Sadashiva 
Sai Nath Sathya Sai Nath [ Sai Nath ... ]

## Meaning

Chant the name of Lord Sathya Sai who is none other than Lord Shiva who is covered in sacred ash and whose head is adorned with the pious river Ganga.
