## Lyrics

Jaya Devi Durga Gowri Shankari Parvathi
Bhuvana Mohini Lalita Lakshmi Kalavathi [ Jaya Devi ... ]

Veda Mata Vidya Dayini Bharati
Hamsa Vahini Veena Paani Saraswathi

## Meaning

Voctory to Mother Durga who is also known as Gowri, Shankari, Parvathi and Bhagawathi. She is also Lakshmi, the Goddess of wealth, known as Kamala and Narayani. She is also Mother Saraswathi, the Goddess of Knowledge and the Mother of Vedas. She holds the Veena, has the Swan as her vehicle, and is also known as Bharati.
