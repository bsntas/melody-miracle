## Lyrics

Guru Deva Jaya Deva

Pranava Swaroopa Devadi Deva

## Meaning

Victory to our Divine Preceptor and God of Gods, Lord Sai. We surrender to Thee, O Lord, who is the embodiment of Om, the Lord of the Universe and the bestower of wisdom.
