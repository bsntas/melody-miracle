## Lyrics

Koi Boley Hari Hari Koi Boley Ram Ram
Koi Boley Durga Kaali Koi Boley Sai Ma
Koi Boley Narayana Allah Bismillah
Koi Boley Nanak Buddha Mahavira
Sadhu Sant Fakira Jaane Sab Hai Tero Naam
Bol Man Allah Malik Bol Man Sai Ram

## Meaning

Some may call God as Hari while some others may call Ram. Some may say Durga Kaali but some others Sai Ma. If Hindus call Him Narayana, Muslims call Allah Bismillah and yet some others Nanak, Buddha and Mahavir. Saints, Sages and Fakirs (Muslim Saints) know that the One God is known by different names.
