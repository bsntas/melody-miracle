## Lyrics

Jaya Kausalya Nandana Ram Paramananda Ananda Ram
Jaya Shirdeesha Partheesha Ram Jaya Prashanthi Sayeesha Ram [ Jaya Kausalya ... ]
Mangala Pavana Naam Jaya Sundara Vaidehi Ram
Sundara Sayeesha Ram ( Jaya )

## Meaning

Glory to the son of Kausalya, One of Supreme Bliss, who dwells in Shirdi and Prashanthi Nilayam in Parthi. The Name 'Ram' is supremely Auspicious and He is the beloved of Mother Vaidehi (Sita).
