## Lyrics

Daya Karo Mujhey Krishna ( Sai ) Gopala [ Darsha Dikhao ... ]
Devaki Nandana Deena Dayala
Mathura Natha Madana Gopala
Sai Narayana Sada Krupala [ Daya Karo ... ]

## Meaning

O Lord Gopala (Krishna), the beloved son of Nanda, grant me Your Vision and shower Your compassion on me. O Lord, the loving son of Mother Devaki, You are the compassionate Lord of the meek and weak, the Lord of Mathura and the most beautiful one. O the ever-compassionate Lord Sai, You are none other than Lord Krishna and Narayana. Shower Your mercy and grace on me.
