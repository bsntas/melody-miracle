## Lyrics

Atma Jyothi Namo Paramatma Jyothi Namo
Akhanda Jyothi Namo Mama Jeevana Jyothi Namo
Antaryami Namo Sri Sadguru Sai Namo

## Meaning

Bow to the omnipresent light, the light of God within. Bow to the never-ending light, which is the very essence of life. Bow to the indwelling true Guru, Lord Sai.
