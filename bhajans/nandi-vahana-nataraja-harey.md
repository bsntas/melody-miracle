## Meaning

O Lord Shiva who rides the divine bull Nandi, You are the Lord of cosmic dance. You are the one who is adorned with the crescent moon on the head. O Lord Sai Baba, the Lord of Puttaparthi, You are the very incarnation of Lord Shiva. You free us of all bondage. Glory be to Thee.
