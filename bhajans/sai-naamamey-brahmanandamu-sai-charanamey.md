## Meaning

Glory to Lord Sai - The chanting of that name confers on all of us supreme Joy, the Lotus Feet confer divine bliss, the vision of the very form of Sri Sathya Sai is blissful to the eyes that see Him He is determined to provide us joy with his miraculous love His subtle and graceful form bestows on us eternal Love and Joy.
