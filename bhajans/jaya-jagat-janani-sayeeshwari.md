## Meaning

Victory to Mother Sai, Goddess of the universe. She is verily Saraswathi, Goddess of learning, the one who holds the Veena and the Holy scriptures. Victory to the Goddess of wealth, Lakshmi, the consort of Lord Madhusudhana (Krishna). Mother Sai is the very embodiment of the Primordial sound 'Om' and the sounds 'Hreem' and 'Sreem'.
