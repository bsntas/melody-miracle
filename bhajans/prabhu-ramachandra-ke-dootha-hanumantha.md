## Lyrics

Prabhu Ramachandra Ke Dootha Hanumantha Anjaneya

## Meaning

Lord Hanumantha Oh! Anjaneya, Son of Anjana Devi and Pavana (Wind God); The messenger of Lord Ramachandra and one of Mighty Strength.
