## Lyrics

Gangadhara Hara Gangadhara Hara Kailasa Natha Prabhu Shankara
Bhava Nasha Bhava Bandha Vimochana
Sathya Sai Shiva Shankara

## Meaning

We praise the Lord Sathya Sai Shiva Shankara-The One who is Auspicious, Captivating - The One who bears the Ganges on his head, One who is the Lord of the mountain 'Kailasa' and the One who liberates us from the bondage of the world and prevents us from taking birth again and again.
