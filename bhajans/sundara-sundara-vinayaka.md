## Lyrics

Sundara Sundara Vinayaka
Shubha Mangala Dayaka Vinayaka
Vighna Koti Hara Vimala Gajanana
Sakala Vighna Hara Sai Gajanana
Eshwari Nandana Parthi Gajanana
Jai Jai Ganesha Jai Sri Ganesha (4)

## Meaning

Victory to Lord Ganesha who is very beautiful, who bestows everything good and auspicious. He is the remover of millions of obstacles that come in our way. He is the elephant-faced son of Parvathi and is verily Lord Sai who resides in Parthi.
