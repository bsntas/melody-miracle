## Lyrics

Aao Aao Nandalala
Sundara Shyama Gopala [ Aao Aao ... ]
Tum Hi Ho Bansidhari Shyama
Ayodhya Pathi Sita Rama
Parthi Baba Karuna Nidhan
Maakhan Chora Yashoda Bala

## Meaning

Welcome O Nandalala (Krishna), You are the beautiful, dark-hued one with the divine flute. You are Lord Rama of Ayodhya, the Lord of Sita. You are Lord Sai of Parthi, the compassionate one. You are also the butter-stealing son of Mother Yashoda.
