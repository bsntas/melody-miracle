## Lyrics

Sita Pathey Rama Jaya Shyamala Komala Rama
Veda Gamanutha Vimala Charitha
Vathathmaja Priya Rama
Varija Netra Rama

## Meaning

Salutations to You, O Lord Rama, the consort of Mother Sita. With Your dark complexion and soft nature, You are the personification of the essence of Vedas. You are the one with blemishless character and adored by Hanuman (Vathathmaja). You are most beautiful with lotus-shaped eyes.
