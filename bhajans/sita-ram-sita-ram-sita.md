## Lyrics

Sita Ram Sita Ram Sita Ram Radhey Shyam Radhey Shyam Radhey Shyam
Dasharatha Nandana Janaki Jeevana
Raghukula Bhooshana Jaya Jaya Ram
Jaya Jaya Ram (Ram) Jaya Sai Ram

## Meaning

Victory to Lord Rama, who is the crown jewel of Raghu Dynasty, who is the consort of Sita and the son of Dasharatha.
