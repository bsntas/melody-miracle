## Meaning

Glory to Shiva, the ultimate dancer, who mesmerizes one and all with his cosmic dance. Lord Shiva, who has no parallel to his dance and his dancing style. When his devotees sing his glory incessantly, he is enchanted. We bow to Shiva, the Lord of Parvathi, Hara Hara to bestow eternal bliss on his devotees.
