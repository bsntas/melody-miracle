## Lyrics

Kasturi Ranga Vittala Kamaneeya Ranga Vittala [ Panduranga ... ]

Partheesha Ranga Vittala Jaya Sai Ranga Vittala

## Meaning

Victory to the Lord Panduranga Vittala, the shining one who attracts like the fragrance of musk. He is the protector of all beings who is full of mercy. He is the Lord of Parthi, Sai, the director of the play of life.
