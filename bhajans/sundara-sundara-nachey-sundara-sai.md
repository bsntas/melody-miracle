## Lyrics

Sundara Sundara Nachey Sundara Sai Ghana Shyam
Nacherey Sai Sundara Shyam
Rhumaka Jhumaka Jhum Ghungaroo Baajey Nachey Ghana Shyam
Nachey Natavara Sai Sundara Merey Sai Ram
Nachey Nachey Man Mandir Mey Merey Sai Ram [ Nacherey ... ]

## Meaning

O the most beautiful, dark-complexioned Lord Krishna, it is a treat for the eyes and ears when the You dance with the anklets jingling on Thy little feet, which go in rhythm to the tune. O Lord, please dance in our hearts the same way for the song of our life.
