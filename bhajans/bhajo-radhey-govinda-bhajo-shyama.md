## Lyrics

Bhajo Radhey Govinda Bhajo Shyama Gopal
Shyama Gopal Bhajo Murali Gopal [ Bhajo Radhey ... ]
Keshava Narahari Shyama Gopal
Madhava Murahara Murali Gopal

## Meaning

Worship by chanting the many names of the Lord Krishna - Govinda, Gopala, Murahara, Shyama, Keshava and Madhava.
