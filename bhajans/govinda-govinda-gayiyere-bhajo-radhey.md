## Lyrics

Govinda Govinda Gayiyere Bhajo Radhey Gopala Krishna Gayiye
Murali Manohara Gayiyere Bhajo Nanda Ke Lal Prabhu Gayiye
Giridhari Giridhari Gayiyere Maayi Meera Ke Nath Prabhu Gayiye (2)

## Meaning

Sing the name of Lord Krishna - Govinda, Gopala and worship the Lord of Radha. Sing the name of the handsome flute player and worship the Lord, the son of Nanda. Sing the name of the Lord of Mother Meera.
