## Lyrics

Sita Ram Naam Bhajo
Madhura Madhura Sai Naam Bhajo
Radhey Shyam Naam Bhajo [ Madhura Madhura ... ]

## Meaning

Sing the Name and Glory of Lord Rama (Sita Ram), Lord Krishna (Radhey Shyam) and Lord Sai whose names are the sweetest of all.
