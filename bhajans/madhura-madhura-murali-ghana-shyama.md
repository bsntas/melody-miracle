## Lyrics

Madhura Madhura Murali Ghana Shyama
Mathuradhipathey Radhey Shyama
Surdas Prabhu Hey Giridhari
Meera Ke Prabhu Hrudaya Vihari

## Meaning

Glory to the dark-complexioned Krishna, Lord of Mathura, the one who resides in everyone's heart and the Lord of Surdas and Meera.
