## Lyrics

Ayodhya Vasi Ram Ram Ram Dasharatha Nandana Ram
Patitha Pavana Janaki Jeevana Sita Mohana Ram

## Meaning

Chant the name of Rama, the Lord of Ayodhya, the Son of Dasharatha and the consort of Sita and whose name purifies the deprived.
