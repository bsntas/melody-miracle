## Meaning

Chant the name of the easy-to-please Lord of the Universe, Lord Shiva, who grants all that is asked for without any question, the Lord who resides on the mountain, the Lord of Mother Uma, the one who destroys the cycle of birth and death. The Lord of the three worlds who has the Holy river Ganga in His matted hair is verily Lord Sainath.
