## Meaning

O Lord Ganesha, the Lord of all Ganaas (Demigods), O Lord of Parthi, You relieve us of all anxieties and sadness. O the Son of Shiva (the Lord of Kailasa), who is the embodiment of purity and the Lord of the destitute, O bestower of all the siddhis and prowess, the one whose face radiates goodness, O sustainer of the whole world and the abode of all peace, glory be to Thee.
