## Lyrics

Madhuvana Murali Guna Gao
Madhusudhana Hari Naam Japo [ Madhuvana Murali ... ]
Vraja Gopi Priya Radhey Shyam Naam
Raghupathi Raghava Raja Ram Naam
Hrudaya Vihari Sri Sathya Sai Ram
Gowri Manohara Shankara Naam Bhajo

## Meaning

Sing the glory of Lord Krishna, the one with the Flute, who moves about in the forest and who is the slayer of demon Madhu. Chant the name of Shyam, the beloved Radha and the Gopis of Brindavan. Chant the name of Lord Rama of the Raghu dynasty and Lord Shankara (Shiva) who charms Mother Gowri and the name of Lord Sathya Sai who resides in everyone's heart.
