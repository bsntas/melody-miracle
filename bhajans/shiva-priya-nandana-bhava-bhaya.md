## Lyrics

Shiva Priya Nandana Bhava Bhaya Bhanjana
Gowri Priya Sutha Om Shadanana [ Shiva Priya ... ]
Hey Mayurasana Smitha Vadana
Sindhura Vadana Shubha Charana
Shanmukha Natha Subrahmanya (Sathya Sai) Deva

## Meaning

We pray to Lord Shanmukha who is the son of Lord Shiva and Mother Gowri and the one who removes all fears of the materialistic world, the one who sits on a peacock and whose face is charming, the one whose face is of reddish-brown color and whose Holy Feet is worshipped by all. He is none other than Lord Sathya Sai.
