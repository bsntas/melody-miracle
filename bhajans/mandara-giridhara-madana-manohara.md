## Lyrics

Mandara Giridhara Madana Manohara
Meera Ke Prabhu Giridhara Naagara
Giridhari Giridhari Govardhanadhara Giridhari (2)
Madhava Madhusudhana
Murali Gaana Vilola

## Meaning

Glory to Lord Krishna who lifted the Govardhana mountain, which was earlier known as Mandara mountains, to save the people from the wrath of Indra. Glory to the Lord of Meera and to the slayer of demon Madhu. Glory to the one who loves playing the flute.
