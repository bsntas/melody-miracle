## Lyrics

Govinda Gopala Prabhu Giridhari
Govinda Gopala Hrudaya Vihari [ Govinda Gopala ... ]
Nanda Kumara Navaneetha Chora
Parthi (Hrudaya) Vihara Bada Chitha Chora

## Meaning

Glory to Lord Krishna, who is the protector of cows, the one who lifted the Govardhana hill and the one who is the indweller of our hearts. The one who is the son of Nanda and the one who stole butter in Gokula, is verily Sai, the Lord of Parthi who steals our hearts.
