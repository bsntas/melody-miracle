## Lyrics

Rama Raghuvara Rama Sita Rama Rama Ram
Sri Rama Rama Ram Sai Rama Rama Ram [ Rama Raghuvara ... ]
Hey Patitha Pavana Ram Hey Shyamala Komala Ram
Hey Vaidehi Priya Ram Sai Ram Rama Jaya Ram [ Sri Rama ... ]

## Meaning

Chant the name of Lord Rama of Raghu dynasty whose consort is Mother Sita; He is the handsome and tender Lord who liberates the penitent. He is the beloved of Vaidehi (Sita), victory to Lord Ram.
