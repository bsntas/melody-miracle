## Meaning

Pray to the Divine Preceptor, the complete Avatar, Sai. He bestows divine bliss on His devotees. He is the very embodiment of Divine Love. Lord Sathya Sai is none other than the Supreme Lord, who took the form as the perfect human. He will incarnate again as Lord Prema Sai.
