## Lyrics

Shiva Shiva Shivaya Namah Om
Bhava Bhava Bhavaya Namah Om
Shirdi (Parthi) Pureeshwara Shambho Shankara
Hara Hara Haraya Namah Om
Parthi Shivaya Namah Om
Samba Shivaya Namah Om
Parthi Pureeshwara Shambho Shankara [ Hara Hara ... ]

## Meaning

Chant the name of Lord Shiva who dispels the fear of the materialistic world, He is none other than Shirdi Sai and Parthi Sai.
