## Lyrics

Sarvatra Govinda Naama Sankeertana Govinda Hari Govinda
Govinda Hari Govinda Hari Govinda Hari Govinda
Purana Purusha Govinda Hari Punya Shloka Govinda

Sai Rama Govinda Hari Sai Krishna Govinda

## Meaning

Chant the name of Lord Govinda, the Supreme Lord who is extolled in the Vedas, the one who is dear to His devotees and to whom He is affectionate. Chant the names of Lord Rama and Krishna who are the same as Lord Sai.
