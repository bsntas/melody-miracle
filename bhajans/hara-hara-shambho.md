## Lyrics

Hara Hara Shambho Karuna Sindho
Shiva Shiva Shankara Loka Pathey
Paripalaya Maam Parthi Pureeshwara
Sai Shiva Kailasa Pathey

## Meaning

Salutations to Lord Shiva, the ocean of compassion and the Lord of the world. O Sai, Lord of Parthi, please protect me. You are verily Lord Shiva, the Lord of Kailasa.
