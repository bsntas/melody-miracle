## Lyrics

Kodanda Rama Raghava Jaya Kalyana Krishna Keshava
Govinda Madhava Gopala Keshava
Narasimha Achyutha Narayana
Dasharatha Nandana Sita Manohara Danava Samhara Daya Nidhey

## Meaning

Victory to Lord Rama, who was born in Raghu dynasty and who held the great Kodanda bow. He is the darling son of Dasharatha and the beloved of Mother Sita. Glory to Lord Krishna whose beauty is unparalleled. He is also known by different names Govinda, Madhava, Gopala, Keshava, Narasimha, Achuyta and Narayana.
