## Lyrics

Prathama Sharana Gananayaka Om
Vighna Vinashaka Siddhi Vinayaka [ Prathama Sharana ... ]
Vidya Dayaka Buddhi Pradayaka
Mooshika Vahana Sai Gajanana

## Meaning

Let us pay our foremost obeisance and surrender to the Lord of the divine forces, Lord Vinayaka. He destroys obstacles and assures success. He who rides a mouse as a vehicle, grants knowledge and pure intellect, Lord Sai is His embodiment.
