## Meaning

O Lord Shankara (Shiva), dance in rhythm with the Damaru (drum), You are the one who dwells at the peak of highest ice-laden mountain, Himachala, You perform the cosmic dance of creation, sustenance and destruction. Dance O Lord Sai Shiva.
