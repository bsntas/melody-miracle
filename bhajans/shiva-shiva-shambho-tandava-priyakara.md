## Lyrics

Shiva Shiva Shambho Tandava Priyakara
Bhava Bhava Bhairava Bhavani Shankara [ Shiva Shiva ... ]
Hara Hara Bham Bham Bhola Maheshwara
Dama Dama Damaru Natana Manohara
Sathyam Shivam Sai Sundara

## Meaning

This song extols the attributes associated with Lord Sai who is none other than Lord Shiva. He is described as the one who loves Cosmic dance (Tandava Priyakara), simple in nature (Bhola) and personification of Truth (Sathyam), Goodness (Shivam) and Beauty (Sundaram).
