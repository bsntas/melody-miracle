## Lyrics

## Meaning

Come O beautiful and charming Lord Krishna, the dark-hued one who has incarnated as Lord Sai of Parthi. You are the same Lord Krishna of Brindavan, the sweet, enchanting, lotus-faced Lord, who lifted the mountain and conquered the demonic force Mura. Chant the many names of the Lord Govinda and Lord Sai.
