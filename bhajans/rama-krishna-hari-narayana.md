## Lyrics

Rama Krishna Hari Narayana
Deena Janavana Hari Narayana
Deena Sharanya Narayana
Bhava Bhaya Harana Sai Narayana

## Meaning

Chant the name of Lord Rama, Krishna, Narayana and Hari. Lord Narayana, who is the protector and the one in whom the downtrodden takes refuge. He is none other than Lord Sai, who protects and liberates us from the bondage of life and death.
