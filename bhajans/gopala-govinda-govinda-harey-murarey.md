## Lyrics

Gopala Govinda Govinda Harey Murarey
Mayura Mukuta Peetambara Dhari
Brindavana Sanchari [ Gopala Govinda ... ]
Shankha Chakra Gadha Padmadhari
Krishna Mukunda Murari ( Sai )

## Meaning

Oh Lord Gopala, You are extolled by various names such as Govinda, Hari and Murari; You are the one who wears the peacock crown and the yellow cloth; You are the one who wanders in Brindavan; You are the one who bears the Conch, Disc, Mace and Lotus in Your hands. You are verily Sai.
