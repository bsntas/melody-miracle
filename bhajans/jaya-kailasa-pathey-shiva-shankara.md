## Meaning

Glory to the Lord of Kailasa Mountains, Lord Shiva, who is Shiva and Shakthi (Parvathi). Glory to Lord Sai, the Lord of Parthi who is the very embodiment of Lord Shiva. Salutations to the Lord of Shaila mountains, who is full of compassion, who has the Trident and who holds Mother Ganges in His matted locks. Salutations to Lord Sai who creates the Lingam (symbol of infinite proportions).
