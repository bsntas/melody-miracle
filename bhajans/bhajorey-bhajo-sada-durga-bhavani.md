## Lyrics

Bhajorey Bhajo Sada Durga Bhavani
Mangala Kaarini Sai Janani [ Bhajorey Bhajo ... ]
Mahishasura Mardhini Bhavani
Daya Sagari Hey Jagat Janani

## Meaning

Always worship the Mother of the Universe, Durga (Parvathi), the bestower of auspiciosness. She is the slayer of the demon Mahishasura. O Mother of the Universe, You are the ocean of mercy.
