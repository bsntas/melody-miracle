## Lyrics

Sadaa Bhaavayaami Sri Sathya
Sai Paripoornaavataaram
(Pallavi)

Sadaa Naadopaasita Modam
Maayaamaanusha Vesha Dhaaram
(Anupallavi)

Prema Thanumanisham Karunaa Hrudayam
(Charanam)

1. Veda Purusha Veda Poshakam
Veda Saara Roopa Veda Gaanalolam

2. Lingodbhava Bhasmaabhisheka Leelaa Vilaasa Parthi Shivam

3. Nenundaga Bhayamelananuchu Abhayamosage Anugraha Vrushti

4. Bhakthaapa Mruthyu Haranam Gathajeeva Prana Pradaayakam

5. Poovu Puttagane Parimalinchu Reethi Baala
Praayambuna Tyaaga Sheelatha nerpe

6. Nee Avathaara Paramaarthamunu Sunthaina Theliyudure
Nirvyaaja Premaathishayamutho Ninnu Neeveruka Parachani Naadu

7. Purushulalona Purushudavu Pasivaarilona Baaludavu
Mahilala Madhya Mahilavu Manaserigi Manoradhamuleederchu
Prana Vibhudavu Neevu Eakaanthambuna Vishwavibhudaveevu

8. Yuvathaku Anusaraneeyam Sathyam Samaya Paalanam
Hitha Mitha Mrudu Bhaashanam Samuchitha Veshadhaaranam
Prathi Panilo Nindaina Samskaaram Seva Paraayanathvam
Sathyam Nithya Vratham Saayi Jeevithame Jagathiki Sandesham

9. Deena Jana Sevaye Saayiki Santhushti
Sarva Jeeva Raasulapaina Kaarunya Drushti
Janabhaagadheya Easwaraamba Thanayaa
Mee Kanusannala Saagu Ee Sarva Srushti

## Meaning

I always meditate on Sri Sathya Sai, the complete and absolute incarnation of God. 

One who always delighted in music, adorning an illusionary human form. 

The embodiment of love with a compassionate heart. 

1. One who is extolled in the Vedas, the sustainer of the Vedas; His form is the very essence of the Vedas; One who enjoys the recitation of the Vedas. 

2. He is Parthi Shiva who brings forth the holy Linga, performs the miraculous Vibhuti abhisheka (the ritual of pouring Vibhuti on the idol of Shirdi Sai Baba). 

3. He is the one who showers abundant grace by declaring, “Why fear when I am here?” 

4. Who wards off the fear of death through devotion, Who brings back to life even the dead. 

5. Just like a flower spreads fragrance even as it blossoms, (Sathya Sai) taught the virtue of sacrifice in childhood itself.  

6. Who can ever comprehend even a fraction of the glory of Your incarnation! With boundless love You have revealed Yourself to all. 

7. You are a man among the men, child among the children, a woman among women; thus You become the beloved knowing each one’s heart and fulfilling their desires. When alone, You are the emperor of the Universe.  

8. You set an ideal for youth, by adhering to Truth and punctuality, through speaking that is good, limited and soft, through appropriate mannerisms. Every act is suffused with refinement and the feeling of service. Truth is Your eternal vow, Sai’s life is His message to the world. 

9. Serving the destitute alone satisfies Sai, His compassionate gaze is on all living beings. O Son of Easwaramma, the giver of all wealth, The entire creation functions by Your mere glance.
