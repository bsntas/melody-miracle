## Lyrics

Govinda Muralidhara Murahara

Govinda Govinda

## Meaning

Chant the divine names of the Lord Krishna - Govinda, Muralidhara, Murahara, Ghana Shyama and Giridhari. Chant the name of Lord Sai who is verily Lord Krishna.
