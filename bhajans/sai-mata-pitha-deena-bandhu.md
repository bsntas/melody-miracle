## Lyrics

Sai Mata Pitha Deena Bandhu Sakha
Terey Charanon Mey Sai Mera Koti Pranam
Sai Mata Pitha Deena Bandhu Sakha [ Terey Charanon ... ]
Mujhey Shakthi Do Merey Sai Shiva
Mujhey Bhakthi Do Merey Sai Shiva
Mujhey Mukthi Do Merey Sai Shiva

## Meaning

O Lord Sai, You are the mother, father, kith, kin and friend of the forlorn. A million prostrations at Your Lotus Feet. O Lord Sai Shiva, grant me strength, devotion and liberation.
