## Lyrics

Deena Sharanya Deena Natha
Karuna Sagara Sai Baba
Karuna Sagara Sai
Sita Rama Radhey Shyama
Sai Sundara Rama
Maya Manusha Vesha Dharey Tum
Jaya Leeladhara Sai Baba
Jaya Leeladhara Sai

## Meaning

Oh radiant Lord Sai, You are the protector of needy and downtrodden, the ocean of compassion, You are Lord Rama, the consort of Mother Sita. You are also Lord Krishna, the beloved of Radha. You have incarnated in these beautiful human form to be among Your devotees.
