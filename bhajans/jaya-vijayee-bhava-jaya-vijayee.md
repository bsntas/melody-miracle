## Lyrics

Jaya Vijayee Bhava Jaya Vijayee Bhava
Bhava Bhaya Nasha Hanumantha
Pavana Kumara Paavana Veera
Ati Balavantha Hanumantha
Anjani Putra Veeranjaneya
Niranjana Vijayee Bhava (Bolo) (2)

## Meaning

Victory to the Lord Hanuman, the one who removes the fear of repetitive Births. The son of Pavana and Anjana is blemishless and is of extraordinary strength.
