## Lyrics

Madhava Harey Madhusudhana Harey
Yadava Harey Yadu Nandana Harey
Keshava Harey Radha Krishna Harey
Janardhana Mana Mohana Harey
Sai Satchidananda Harey

## Meaning

Chant the different names of Lord Krishna, of Yadava lineage, Madhava, Madhusudhana, the charmer of the minds and hearts of devotees and the very embodiment of Truth, Awareness and Bliss.
