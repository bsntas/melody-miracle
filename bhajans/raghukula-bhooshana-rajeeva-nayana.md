## Lyrics

Raghukula Bhooshana Rajeeva Nayana
Eshwaramba Nandana Sathya Sai Rama
Janaki Vallabha Lavanya Rama
Nirupama Sundara Sugunabhi Rama
Prashanthi Nilaya Pavana Naama
Jaya Jaya Rama Prabhu Sai Rama

## Meaning

Glory to the jewel of the Raghu dynasty, the lotus-eyed Lord Rama. He is none other than the darling son of Mother Eshwaramba, Lord Sathya Sai. The beloved of Janaki (Sita), the exquisitely handsome Lord Rama, is beautifully-complexioned and the most virtuous. Victory to Lord Rama, who is verily our Lord Sai Rama, of the Holy abode, Prashanthi Nilayam.
