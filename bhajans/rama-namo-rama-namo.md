## Lyrics

Rama Namo Rama Namo Rama Namo Sai Rama Namo
Dasharatha Nandana Rama Namo
Vasudeva Nandana Krishna Namo [ Rama Namo … ]
Shirdi Pureeshwara Baba Namo
Parthi Pureeshwara Sai Namo
Sai Namo Sai Namo Sai Namo Sathya Sai Namo

## Meaning

Salutations to Lord Rama, son of Dasharatha and to Lord Krishna, son of Vasudeva. Salutations to Lord Sai of Shirdi and Parthi who is verily Lord Rama and Krishna.
