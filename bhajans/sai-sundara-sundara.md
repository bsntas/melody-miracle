## Lyrics

Sai Sundara Sundara
Vyagrambaradhara Harey Hari Hara [ Sai Sundara ... ]
Anantha Guna Mandira Sundara
Natana Sundara Bhuvana Sundara

## Meaning

This song praises Lord Sai as the most beautiful in the world, the one who is none other than Lord Shiva adorning tiger skin, who is highest in virtues and who is the most charming Cosmic dancer.
