## Lyrics

Prem Se Gao Mangala Naam

Prem Ke Sindho Deena Ke Bandho
Karunamaya Sai Nayanabhi Ram [ Govinda Gopala ... ]

Harey Krishna Harey Ram Sai Ram Sai Ram

## Meaning

With love and devotion chant the auspicious name of the merciful and loving Lord Sai, who is an ocean of love and companion of the meek. Chant the names Govinda, Gopala and Hari. Chant the name of the most compassionate Lord Sai. Hail to Krishna, Ram and Sai Ram.
