## Meaning

Full of surrender I bow to Your Feet; In my lifeboat, there I am, save my Lord; Praise be to Thee, king of my heart; (Sathya) Sai Baba, all glory be to Thee; Om I bow to Shiva, Shiva I bow to Thee; You warm my heart, Lord Shiva, You are making that happen; Baba I sing Your name, I sing Your name, I bow to You.
