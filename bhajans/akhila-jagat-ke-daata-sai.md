Shirdi Nivasi Bhagawan [ Darshan Do ... ]

## Meaning

O Lord Sai, the dweller of Shirdi, grace us with Your divine presence. You are the supreme reserve of compassion and kindness. Annihilate our acquired impurities. O Lord of Parthi, You are the refuge of the forlorn.
