## Lyrics

Man Mandir Mey Aao Giridhari
Nis Din Barsat Nayan Hamarey [ Man Mandir ... ]
Surdas Ke Shyama Murari
Akhiyan Terey Darsha Ke Pyaasi (2)

## Meaning

O Lord Krishna, the one who lifted the Govardhana hill, please be seated in our minds, our eyes are shedding tears everyday because of separation from You. O Lord of Surdas, our eyes are pining to have Your Darshan.
