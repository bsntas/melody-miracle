## Lyrics

Nirupama Guna Sadana Charana Neeraja Dala Nayana
Kashayambara Vesha Dharana Kali Yuga Avatara
Nithya Ranjana Nirmala Charitha Nirupama Yogendra
Maha Teja Nataraja Viraja Asha Paasha Nasha Esha

## Meaning

Glory to Lord Sathya Sai who has no parallel and who is the source of pure and auspicious traits. We take refuge at the lotus feet of the lotus-eyed Lord Sai. He represents sacrifice signified by the orange robe, which He adorns during this Kali age. He is the ever-charming one with blemishless character and is the incomparable Lord of the Yogis. He is verily Eshwara (Shiva), the majestic Lord of dance, who grants us freedom from every kind of worldly attachment and desire.
