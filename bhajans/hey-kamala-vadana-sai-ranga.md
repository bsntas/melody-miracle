## Meaning

Hail the lotus-faced Lord Ranga who is the Absolute reality. Sing in praise of Lord Gopala of the complexion of thick dark clouds, of curly hair and who is the sustainer of the three worlds. He is verily the Embodiment of the Trinity and who uses Illusion as His instrument to destroy the sinner.
