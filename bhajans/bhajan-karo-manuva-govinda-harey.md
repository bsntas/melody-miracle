## Lyrics

Bhajan Karo Manuva Govinda Harey
Govinda Murari Gopala Murari [ Bhajan Karo ... ]
Mathura Natha Hey Giridhari
Radha Madhava Hey Giridhari (2)

## Meaning

O mind, worship Lord Krishna, known by many names - Govinda, Murari, Gopala, the Lord of Mathura, Giridhari, the Lord of Radha.
