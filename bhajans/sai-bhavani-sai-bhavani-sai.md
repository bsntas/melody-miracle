## Lyrics

Sai Bhavani Sai Bhavani Sai Bhavani Ma (2)
Shubhankari Jai Abhayankari Jai Sai Bhavani Ma
Durga Lakshmi Saraswathi Jai Bhavani Sai Bhavani Ma
Gayathri Shiva Gowri Manohari Sai Bhavani Ma

## Meaning

Victory and salutations to the auspiciousness Mother Sai, who is the destroyer of the bondage of life and death and the remover of fear. Victory to the Mother as Durga, Lakshmi, Saraswathi and Gayathri. Glory to Mother Sai Bhavani, who bestows goodness and who protects us. Glory to goddess Gayathri and Gowri (Parvathi), who is the consort of Lord Shiva.
