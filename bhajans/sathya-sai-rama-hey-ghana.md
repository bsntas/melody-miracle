## Lyrics

Sathya Sai Rama Hey Ghana Shyama
Prema Sudha Baraso Shanthi Sudha Baraso [ Sathya Sai ... ]

## Meaning

O Lord Sathya Sai, the most venerable one, shower on us Your immense love and bestow peace upon us, O Lord of this Kali age.
