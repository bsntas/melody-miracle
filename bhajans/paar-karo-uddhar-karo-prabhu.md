## Lyrics

Paar Karo Uddhar Karo Prabhu Parthi Puri Bhagawan
Harey Ram Ram Ram Ghana Shyam Shyam Shyam Hey Sai Natha Bhagawan
Deena Dayakara Karuna Murthy Kali Yuga Avatar Sai Bhagawan
Allah Sai Buddha Zohrashtra Yesu Christhu Bhagawan

## Meaning

O Lord Sai, Lord of Parthi, please save us and liberate us from the cycle of birth and death. O the most compassionate and merciful Lord, You are the Incarnation of this Kali Age. Chant the name of the Lord Ram, Ghana Shyam (deep blue hued Lord Krishna), Allah, Buddha, Zohrashtra, Jesus Christ.
