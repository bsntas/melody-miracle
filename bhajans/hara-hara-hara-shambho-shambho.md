## Meaning

Glory to Lord Shiva, the resident of Kailasa who holds the Holy Ganges in His matted locks, the consort of Mother Parvathi, the Supreme Lord who destroys our sins, who disguises in human form, who is adorned with the cobra, who has the third eye in His forehead, who has the divine bull Nandi as His vehicle, who protects His devotees and who is the saviour of the downtrodden. He is verily Lord Sai who resides at Parthi.
