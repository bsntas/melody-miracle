## Lyrics

Sapney Mey Aao Shyama Murari
Savari Surath Muralidhari [ Sapney Mey ... ]
Sab Ke Saajan Prabhu Giridhari
Sadguru Sai Parthi Vihari

## Meaning

Oh dark complexioned Lord Krishna, I invite You to come in my dreams. Oh Lord Sai who strolls in Parthi, You are the beloved companion of all.
