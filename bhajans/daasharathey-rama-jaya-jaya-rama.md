## Lyrics

Daasharathey Rama Jaya Jaya Rama
Raghukula Bhooshana Raja Rama [ Daasharathey ... ]
Sita Vallabha Sundara Rama
Sri Rama Jaya Rama Sathya Sai Rama

## Meaning

Victory to Lord Rama, the Prince of King Dasharatha. Lord Rama is the Jewel of the Raghu Dynasty. Chant the name of Lord Rama, the beloved of Mother Sita. Chant the name of Lord Rama, who is verily Lord Sathya Sai.
