## Lyrics

Kamala Nayana Sai Ranga
Sab Sukh Daata Sai Bhagya Vidhata Sai
Radha Ramana Sai Ranga [ Kamala Nayana ... ]
Parama Pitha Hamey Sahara Do Prabhu
Kar Chintana (Vandana) Sai Ranga

## Meaning

O Lord Sai with lotus-petal shaped eyes, You are the bestower of happiness and auspiciousness for all. You are verily Lord Krishna, the beloved of Radha. O Supreme Father, You are our only support. O Lord, may we incessantly contemplate on You and offer obeisance to You.
