## Lyrics

Jagat Janani Shiva Sai Shankari
Mata Maheshwari Parvathi Ma
Abhaya Pradayini Gaana Vinodini
Mata Bhavani Ma (Jaya Ma)
Jaya Ma Jaya Ma
Kaali Kapalini Ma (Jaya Ma)

## Meaning

Glory be to the Mother of the Universe Shankari, Parvathi, Bhavani, the one who confers freedom from fear, one who is delighted by music and the one wearing a garland of skulls.
