## Lyrics

Shiva Shiva Sadashiva

## Meaning

Glory to Lord Shiva, the Lord of Lords, the eternally blissful one who is besmeared with sacred ash. Glory to the one who is worshipped with the Bilva leaf (Leaf of wood apple tree or Bel tree) which He likes. Glory to Lord Shiva who bears the Crescent moon on His head and who wears the Tiger skin.
