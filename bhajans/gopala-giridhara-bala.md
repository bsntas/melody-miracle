## Lyrics

Gopala Giridhara Bala
Gopi Lola Gokula Bala [ Gopala ... ]
Goparipala Narayana (2)
Narayana Narayana [ Goparipala ... ]
Govinda Govinda Narayana (Sai)

## Meaning

Chant the name of Gopala, who lifted the Govardhana mountain, who is the boy of Gokula and who gave happiness to the Gopis. Chant the name of Lord Narayana, Govinda - the protector of cows.
