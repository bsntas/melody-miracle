## Lyrics

Gopala Radha Lola
Murali Lola Nandalala [ Gopala ... ]
Keshava Madhava Janardhana
Vanamala Brindavana Bala [ Murali Lola ... ]

## Meaning

Chant the name of Gopala, who gives joy to Radha, who is the son of Nanda. He is the one who played the flute, who is also known as Keshava, Madhava and Janardhana. He is the sweet child of Brindavan who wears the garland made of jungle flowers.
