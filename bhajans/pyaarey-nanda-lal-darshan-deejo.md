## Lyrics

Pyaarey Nanda Lal Darshan Deejo
Yashoda Ke Bal Darshan Deejo [ Pyaarey Nandalal ... ]
Natavara Lal Darshan Deejo
Giridhara Bal Darshan Deejo

## Meaning

O loving Lord Krishna, kindly present Thyself. O the beloved Prince of Nanda and Mother Yashoda, give me Your Darshan, You are the divinely mischievous child and the one who lifted the Govardhan mountain. O Lord, please grant me Your Darshan!
