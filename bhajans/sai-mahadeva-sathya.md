## Lyrics

Sai Mahadeva (Sathya)
Parthi Pureeshwara Hey Sai Baba Maheshwara Hey [ Sai Mahadeva ]
Nirupama Guna Sadana Sai Neeraja Dala Nayana
Vibhuti Sundara Hey Sai Baba Maheshwara Hey

## Meaning

Praise be to our Lord Sathya Sai, who is the very embodiment of Lord Mahadeva (Shiva). Praise be to our Lord Sai Baba - the Lord of Parthi, who is the embodiment of Lord Maheshwara (Shiva). He, who is of pristine virtues, adorned with Vibhuti and whose eyes are like the tender petals of the Lotus.
