## Lyrics

Jai Jai Prabhu Giridhari Natavara Nandalala
Natavara Nandalala Hey Giridhara Gopala (Hey)
Hey Giridhara Gopala Hey Giridhara Gopala Hey Giridhara Gopala

## Meaning

Sing in praise of Lord Gopala (Krishna), the playful son of Nanda and the one who lifted the mountain Govardhana, to save His tribesmen.
