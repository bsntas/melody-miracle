## Meaning

Let us all chant the Holy mantra "Om Namah Shivaya", the name of the Supreme Lord Shiva. O Lord, let us lose ourselves meditating on You and sing Your praises ceaselessly. You are verily Lord Sathya Sai, the embodiment of compassion and the protector of the entire universe.
