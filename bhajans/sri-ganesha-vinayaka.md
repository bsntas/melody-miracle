## Lyrics

Sri Ganesha Vinayaka
Vighna Vinashaka Vinayaka

## Meaning

Oh Lord Ganesha, remover of the fear of the materialistic world, you remove obstacles and grant auspiciousness, Oh Lord Vinayaka of Parthi.
