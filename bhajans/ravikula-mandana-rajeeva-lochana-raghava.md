## Meaning

Lord Sai took the form of Lord Rama of Raghu dynasty. In Dwapara age, He was the son of Vasudeva, as Krishna, who liberates us from bondage. He is the abode of compassion, the protector of all, the one who conquered the demon Madhu and the one who enchants all with the melody from His flute.
