## Meaning

O Mind, sing the Glory of Lord Narayana also known as Hari, who has descended on Earth as the dark-complexioned and enchanting Gopala (Krishna). He is the very embodiment of Truth, Awareness and Bliss and the one who protects the cows and the Gopis.
