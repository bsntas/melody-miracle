## Meaning

Glory to Thee, O Ganesha, the elephant faced one, the bestower of boons, the one who has the mouse as His vehicle, the one who has Modaka (a sweet preparation) in His hand, the one who is supremely pure. O Lord, I offer obeisance at Your Lotus feet.
