## Lyrics

Vighneshwara Gananatha Gajanana
Parvathi Nandana Shubhanana
Mangala Murthy Sri Ganaraya
Jaya Ganaraya Sri Ganaraya (2)
Ashta (Siddhi) Vinayaka Shubhanana

## Meaning

Victory to Lord Vighneshwara (Lord Ganesha) the son of Parvathi,, having a pleasant and auspicious face, who bestows eight types of blessings. He is also known as Siddhi Vinayaka, the one who fulfills our prayers.
