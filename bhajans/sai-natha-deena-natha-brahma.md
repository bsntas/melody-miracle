## Meaning

Salutations to You, O Lord Sai, the Supreme Guru, who is the protector of the weak and the downtrodden, who is the very embodiment of Brahman. You are the Formless One that has taken the Form of Lord Sai. Glory to You O Lord, the unperturbed one, who is present in the primordial sound "Om". Glory to You, O Divine Preceptor Sai, You are our mother, father and our everything.
