## Lyrics

Prema Sudha Rasa Deejo Sai
Paramananda Sada Sukha Dayi [ Prema Sudha ... ]

Deena Dayaghana Sri Raghurayi

## Meaning

O Lord Sai, give us the nectar of divine Love and bliss of the highest order. You are the most blissful one who confers endless joy. You are the Divine Preceptor of Dwarakamayi in Shirdi and Prashanthi Nilayam at Parthi. You are verily Lord Rama of the Raghu lineage who has unending compassion for the meek.
