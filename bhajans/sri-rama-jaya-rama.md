## Lyrics

Sri Rama Jaya Rama

Shanthi Priya Prema Priya [ Hey Bhaava Priya ... ]

## Meaning

Victory to Sri Rama who responds to a devotee's yearning. You are Sai Rama of Prashanthi Nilayam, the one who is fond of truth, righteousness, peace and divine love.
