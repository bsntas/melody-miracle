## Meaning

Lord Krishna is described as the one having a dark blue complexion like that of a rain cloud, one who is very sweet in nature and one who killed the demon Mura. He has a gentle smile on His face and is adored by Radha. He is the Supreme Cowherd looking after His cows (mankind), He is the Supreme Dancer and Actor, the one who lifted the mountain Govardhana for protecting the people of Gokula from the wrath of Indra.
