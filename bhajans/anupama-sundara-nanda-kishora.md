## Lyrics

Anupama Sundara Nanda Kishora
Brindavana Ghana Shyam
Murali Madhava Radhey Govinda
Madhusudhana Ghana Shyam Sathya Sai Sundara Shyam
Brinda Vihari Mandara Giridhari
Kamala Nayana Bhagawan
Bhakthon Ke Prabhu Partheeshwara Sai
Natavara Sundara Shyam Sathya Sai Sundara Shyam

## Meaning

O Krishna, son of Nanda, You are unsurpassingly beautiful. You are dark as the clouds and is adored in Brindavan as Radhey Govinda, Madhusudhana and Giridhari. O lotus-eyed Lord Sathya Sai, You are beloved to Your devotees and adored as the same dancing Shyam (Krishna).
