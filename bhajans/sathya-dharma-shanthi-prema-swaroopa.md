## Lyrics

Sathya Dharma Shanthi Prema Swaroopa Prashanthi Nilaya Deva
Rama Ho Krishna Ho Sai Ram Deva
Shiva Shakthi Swaroopa Baba Hey Deena Palana Baba
Hey Prashanthi Nilaya Deva Hey Poornavatara Baba
Allah Ho Maulah Ho Sai Ram Deva

## Meaning

Salutations to the Lord of Prashanthi, Lord Sai, who is the embodiment of Truth, Peace, Love and Harmony, O Lord Sai, You are none other than Rama, Krishna, Shiva, Shakthi, Allah and Maulah all of whom offer solace to the weak and the downtrodden.
