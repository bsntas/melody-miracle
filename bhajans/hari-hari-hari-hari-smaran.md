## Lyrics

Hari Hari Hari Hari Smaran Karo
Hari Charana Kamal Dhyan Karo [ Hari Hari ... ]
Murali Madhava Seva Karo
Murahara Giridhari Bhajana Karo

## Meaning

Let the Mind dwell incessantly on the Lotus Feet of Hari, let the hands indulge in the service of Madhava who holds Flute in His Hands, Let the mouth sing the Glory of the One who vanquished the demon Mura and lifted the Mountain with a finger.
