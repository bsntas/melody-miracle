## Meaning

Glory to Lord Shankara (shiva), the blue-throated one who is adorned with the crescent moon on His head and who wields the Trident. He is hailed as Lord Brihadheeshwara in Tanjore and as Shaileshwara, the Lord of the mountains. He is verily our Lord Sai of Shirdi and Puttaparthi. He is the master of this universe as well as our hearts.
