## Lyrics

Gowri Nandana Gajanana (Hey)
Girija Nandana Niranjana [ Gowri Nandana ... ]
Parvathi Nandana Shubhanana
Paahi Prabho Maam Paahi Prasanna

## Meaning

Oh Lord Ganesh, son of Mother Gowri, You are blemishless and Your countenance is most auspicious. O Lord, protect us.
