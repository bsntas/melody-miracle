## Meaning

Please come from Your abode at Shirdi, O my Lord Sai, and install Thyself in the temple of our hearts. O Protector of the forlorn, You are our mother, father, kin and friend. You are the Master of the vital forces and the very basis of our lives. Please bestow Your compassion on us, O resident of Parthi.

O Lord Sai, come from the temple at Shirdi. O Lord Sathya Sai, please come from the Holy place of Puttaparthi and bestow Love and Peace on all.
