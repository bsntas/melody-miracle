## Meaning

Salutations of the Lord of dance form, Nataraja (Lord Shiva), who enacts the enchanting cosmic dance known as Tandava. He thrills the whole universe. Lord Shiva who appears in His cosmic dance form as Nataraja at Chidambaram in Southern India, has incarnated as our Lord of Parthi.
