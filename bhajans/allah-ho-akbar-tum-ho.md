## Lyrics

Allah Ho Akbar Tum Ho Sai
Tum Ho Antaryami (Baba)
Tum Hi Saar Ho Geeta Quran
Tum Ho Sab Ka Jeevan Ka Aadhaar
Daya Karo Merey Baba
Tum Ho Antaryami (Baba)

## Meaning

O Lord Sathya Sai, You are the most merciful and benevolent Allah, the supreme sovereign. O Baba, You are the indweller of our hearts. You are the quintessence of the Bhagawad Geeta and the Holy Quran. You are the saviour and sustainer of all life. O Lord, please have mercy and compassion for us.
