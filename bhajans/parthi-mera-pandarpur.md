## Lyrics

Parthi Mera Pandarpur
Partheeshwara Sai Ranga
Shiva Hari Roopa Panduranga
Shiva Shakthi Roopa Sai Ranga
Pundarika Varada Panduranga
Bhaktha Janashraya Sai Ranga
Panduranga Pandari Natha Sai Natha Vittala (Hey) (2)

## Meaning

Puttaparthi is my Pandarpur and Lord Sai is verily Lord Panduranga from whom the devotees seek solace. He is the embodiment of Lord Shiva and Shakthi (Parvathi) as well as Lord Hari, the one who blessed His devotee Pundarika. Lord Sai is the same as Lord Vitthala, the Lord of Pandarpur.
