## Lyrics

Guru Deva Guru Govinda
Mandara Giridhara Shirdi Pureeshwara
Jaya Partheeshwara Sai Shiva [ Guru Deva ... ]
Sundara Roopa Sri Sai Deva
Vaidehi Mohana Narayana (2)

## Meaning

Glory to the Lord Sai of Shirdi and Parthi. Glory to Lord Vishnu, the benevolent teacher whose beautiful form enchanted Vaidehi (Goddess Lakshmi).
