## Lyrics

Lambodhara Hey Vighneshwara
Shambhu Kumara Siddeshwara [ Lambodhara ... ]
Ambika Tanaya Omkareshwara
Heramba Sai Shirdeeshwara (Partheeshwara)

## Meaning

Sing the glory of Lord Ganesha who removes obstacles, the son of Shambho (Shiva) and Ambika (Parvathi) and who has a big stomach. He is the Lord of Yogis, the embodiment of Om, and is also known as Heramba. He is none other than Sai, the Lord of Shirdi and Parthi.
