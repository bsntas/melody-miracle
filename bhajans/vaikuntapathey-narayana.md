## Meaning

Glory to Lord Narayana, the one who resides in Vaikunta who has eyes the shape of a lotus, the one with the Kaustubha jewel on His chest and who is adorned with Shanka (Conch), Chakra (Disc) and who dons the auspicious yellow silk.
