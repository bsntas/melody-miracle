## Lyrics

Sunaada Vinodini Sai Janani
Bhavani Jaganmohini Sai Janani [ Sunaada Vinodini ... ]
Sambhavi Shiva Ranjani Sai Janani
Shankari Narayani Sai Janani
Bhagawathi Vara Dayini Sai Janani

## Meaning

Glory to Mother Sai who enjoys music and who has several forms and names - Bhavani who enchants the whole world, Sambhavi, who pleases and entertains Lord Shiva, Shankari, Narayani and Bhagawathi who bestows boons to all.
