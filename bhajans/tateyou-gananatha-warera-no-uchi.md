## Lyrics

Tateyou Gananatha Warera no Uchi ni Hikaru Kami
Iainaru Kami Shiva no Itoshigo
Eichi o Sazukeru Vinayaka
Parvathi no Miko ni Eikou Are
Warera ni Michi o Shimesu Ganesha yo

## Meaning

Praise the Lord Gananatha, the God that shines within us. Glory to the beloved son of Parvathi and Shiva. Let Your wisdom show us the way.
