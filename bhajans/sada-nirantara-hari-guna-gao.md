## Lyrics

Sada Nirantara Hari Guna Gao
Prema Bhakthi Se Bhajan Sunao [ Sada Nirantara ... ]
Sai Nath Ke Charanon Mey Aao
Man Mandir Mey Deep Jalao
Jeevan Naiya Paar Lagao

## Meaning

Always sing the glory of our Lord; Let them resound with love and devotion; Come to the lotus feet of our Lord Sai; In your heart's temple, light the lamp of love; He will take you across the ocean of life.
