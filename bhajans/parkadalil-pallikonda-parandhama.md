## Lyrics

Parkadalil Pallikonda Parandhama
Parthipuriyil Avadaritha Sai Rama
Darmam Kaatha Raman Niye Sai Rama
Neela Vanna Kannan Niye Sai Shyama
Kaliyugathu Deivam Niye Parandama
Katharulvai Karunakara Sai Rama (2)

## Meaning

Glory to Lord Vishnu reclining on the ocean of milk (consciousness), who has incarnated as Lord Sai in Puttaparthi. Lord Rama, the protector of righteousness, and Lord Krishna of blue complexion are none other than Lord Sai of the Kali Age. Bless us and protect us, O compassionate Lord Sai Rama.
