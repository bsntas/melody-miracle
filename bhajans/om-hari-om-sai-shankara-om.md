## Lyrics

Om Hari Om Sai Shankara Om
Om Hari Om Shirdi Pureeshwara Om
Parthi Vihari Shivom Trinetra Dhari Shivom
Patitha Pavana Paahi Mahesha Shiva Shiva Hara Hara Om

## Meaning

Glory to Lord Sai of Shirdi and Parthi, the very embodiment of Om. O Sai, You are verily Lord Hari and the three-eyed Lord Shiva. O uplifter of the downtrodden, grant us Your protection.
