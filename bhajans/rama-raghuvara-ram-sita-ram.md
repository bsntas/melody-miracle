## Meaning

Chant the name of Lord Rama, the consort of Mother Sita and the son of King Dasharatha. Lord Rama, the tender, handsome one is the annihilator of the demon clan. He is the one who wields the Kodanda bow, the ocean of compassion and who is dear to the forlorn. Sing, O mind, the purifying name of Lord Rama.
