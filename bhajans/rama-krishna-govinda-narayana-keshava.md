## Lyrics

Rama Krishna Govinda Narayana Keshava
Radhika Ramana Madhava Jaya Ranga Deva
Harey Rama Harey Krishna Sita Rama Raghava [ Radhika Ramana ... ]

## Meaning

Chant the names of the Lord Rama, Krishna, Narayana, Keshava and Madhava (names of Krishna), the one adored by Radhika (Radha). Victory to You, the Lord of the stage (world). Glory to Lord Rama and Lord Krishna.
