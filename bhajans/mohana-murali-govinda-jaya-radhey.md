## Lyrics

Mohana Murali Govinda Jaya Radhey Govinda
Hey Giridhari Govinda Jaya Radhey Govinda
Krishna Murari Govinda Jaya Radhey Govinda
Hrudaya Vihari (Parthi Vihari) Govinda Jaya Radhey Govinda

## Meaning

Victory to the enchanter with the flute, Lord Govinda, who lifted the mountain to protect His devotees and who is the beloved of Radha. Victory to Krishna - slayer of demon Mura (Murari). He is the one who resides in Parthi (and in devotees' hearts), He is Govinda (Vishnu).
