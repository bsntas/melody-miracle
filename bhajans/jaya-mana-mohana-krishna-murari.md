## Lyrics

Jaya Mana Mohana Krishna Murari
Jaya Brindavana Kalimala Haari [ Jaya Mana Mohana ... ]
Jaya Jaya Giridhara Parthi Vihari
Deena Bandhu Karunakar Sharan Tumhari
Jaya Partheeshwara Sai Giridhari

## Meaning

Victory to the enchanter of our minds, Lord Krishna, the vanquisher of demon Mura. Victory to the one who removes the blemishes of Kali age and the one whose abode was Brindavana. Victory to the one who held aloft the Govardhana mountain to protect devotees from torrential rain. Victory to the resident of Parthi, Lord Sai. O Lord, I surrender to You. You are the most compassionate one who is kith and kin to the downtrodden. Victory to Thee, O Lord of Parthi, You are verily Lord Krishna.
