## Lyrics

Pranam Sweekar Karo Sai Mahadeva
Sai Mahadeva Sai Mahadeva [ Pranam Sweekar ... ]
Allah Eshwar Yesu Sai
Buddha Zohrashtra Mahavira Sai
Natana Sutradhari Jagannatha Sai [ Sai Mahadeva ... ]

## Meaning

Accept our salutations O Sai, the Lord of Gods, You are verily the Supreme One, be it Allah, Eshwar, Jesus, Buddha, Zohrashtra, Mahavira. You are the supreme Director and Lord of the world.
