## Lyrics

Jai Jai Jai Mana Mohana Jai Jai Jai Madhusudhana
Madhava Keshava
Keshava Madhava
Gopala Gopalana

## Meaning

Victory to Lord Krishna who is known by many names: Gopala, Madhava and Keshava. He is the divine charmer who destroyed the demon Madhu.
