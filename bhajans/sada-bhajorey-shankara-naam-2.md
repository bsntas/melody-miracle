## Lyrics

Sada Bhajorey Shankara Naam (2)

## Meaning

Always sing and chant the name of Lord Shankara (Shiva) who holds the Holy Ganges in His matted locks. May the beautiful form of the Lord Sai, who is embodiment of happiness and prosperity, be ever in our hearts.
