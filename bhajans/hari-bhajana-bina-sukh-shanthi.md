## Meaning

O Mind, understand that there is no peace without chanting the glory of the Lord Hari, which itself is Bliss. There is no progress without loving devotion to the Lord and no liberation without service to the feet of the Guru. There is no equanimity of mind without remembering the Lord's name and meditation. There is no integrated awareness without the Lord's vision. No deed or action can be deemed good if performed bereft of righteousness and compassion. Only the Lord is one's own, and no one else. There is no realization of the Supreme Consciousness without chanting the Lord's name.
