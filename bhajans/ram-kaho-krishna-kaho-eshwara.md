## Lyrics

Ram Kaho Krishna Kaho Eshwara Allah Sai Kaho
Buddha Kaho Guru Nanak Kaho Zohrashtra Mahavir Yesu Kaho [ Ram Kaho ... ]
Yug Avatara Tum Ho Vishwa Shakthi Tum Ho
Sarva Dharma Priya Sai Mahesha
Parabrahma Tum Ho (Sai)

## Meaning

Chant the name of all the Gods - Rama, Krishna, Eshwara (Shiva) or Allah. Chant the name of Buddha, Guru Nanak or Zohrashtra. Chant either Mahavir or Jesus. O Sai, You are the incarnation of this age. You are the power behind the Universe, the one who loves all religion equally. You are the embodiment of the Supreme One.
