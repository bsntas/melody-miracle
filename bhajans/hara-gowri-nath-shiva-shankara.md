## Lyrics

Hara Gowri Nath Shiva Shankara
Bhola Harey Shambo Bhola Harey

Kailasa Vasa Mahadeva

## Meaning

Chant and celebrate the many names of Lord Shiva. He is the consort of Gowri and the auspicious one. He is the Supreme Being who resides in Mount Kailasa and remains an abode of joy for all who seek him.
