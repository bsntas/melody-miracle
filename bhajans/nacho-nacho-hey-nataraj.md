## Meaning

O Lord Shiva, perform the cosmic dance. O Lord of the Universe, Sai, the Lord of Puttaparthi, You are verily Lord Shiva who is beautifully smeared with the holy ash and the consort of Bhavani (Parvathi). O Sai Shiva, our salutations to You.
