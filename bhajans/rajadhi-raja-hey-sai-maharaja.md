## Lyrics

Rajadhi Raja Hey Sai Maharaja
Deenon Ke Nath Bhagawan

## Meaning

Chant the name of the King of Kings, the Supreme King, Lord Sai, the protector of the miserable and dejected. O the Lord of Lords, the resident of Parthi, Lord Sai, You are Lord Rama and Lord Krishna.
