## Lyrics

Jaya Jaya Krishna Yadu Nandana
Jaya Jaya Brindavana Chandana [ Jaya Jaya ... ]
Jaya Jaya Krishna Mana Mohana
Jaya Jaya Sai Gopalana (2)

## Meaning

Victory to Lord Krishna of  Yadu lineage, the shining star of Brindavan, the one who charms the mind. He is none other than our Sai Gopala.
