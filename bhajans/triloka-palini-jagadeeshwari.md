## Lyrics

Triloka Palini Jagadeeshwari
Sadananda Roopini Sayeeshwari [ Triloka Palini ... ]
Karunya Lavanya Kadambari

## Meaning

Oh Mother Sayeeshwari, the protector of the three worlds, one who is always blissful, compassionate and beautiful, the Mother of Parthi, the consort of Shiva, our salutations to Thee.
