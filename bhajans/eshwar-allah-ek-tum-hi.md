## Meaning

Eshwara (refers to God in Hinduism) and Allah (God in Islam) are both one and the same. The name refers to the same Almighty. O Lord Rama, You are the most compassionate one, You are the refuge to the destitute and the saviour of the devotees. Chant victory to Lord Sai who is verily Lord Rama and Allah.
