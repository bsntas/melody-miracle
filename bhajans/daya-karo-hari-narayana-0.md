## Lyrics

Daya Karo Hari Narayana
Krupa Karo Hey Mana Mohana [ Daya Karo ... ]
Deena Dayala Dashavathara (2)
Darshan Do Hey Sai Gopala

## Meaning

O Lord Hari Narayana, bestow on us Your mercy, You are the Lord of the destitute and the helpless who incarnated ten times for the sake of Your devotees. O Lord Gopala (Krishna), grant us Your Darshan.
