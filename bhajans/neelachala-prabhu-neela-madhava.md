## Lyrics

Neelachala Prabhu Neela Madhava
Jagannatha Swami Prabhuvara
Vasudeva Hari Mayura Mukutadhari
(Jaya) Radha Mohana Murari
Sai Krishna Murari Prabhuvara

## Meaning

Glory to Lord Jagannatha (Krishna), the blue-hued Lord of Puri, the city also known as the blue mountain. Glory to the bestower of boons, the one adorned with a peacock feather on His head and who is the divine enchanter of Radha. Glory to Lord Sai who is verily Lord Krishna, the slayer of demon Mura.
