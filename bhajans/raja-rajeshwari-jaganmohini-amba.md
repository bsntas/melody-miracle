## Lyrics

Raja Rajeshwari Jaganmohini (Amba)
Jagadambe Mangala Kaarini [ (Amba) Raja Rajeshwari ... ]
Jagadodharini Vighna Vinashini
Sthira Sukha Dayini Sai Janani

## Meaning

Effulgent and beautiful Goddess Amba enchants the world and bestows auspiciousness. She destroys obstacles and saves the world. Mother Sai grants ever-lasting happiness.
