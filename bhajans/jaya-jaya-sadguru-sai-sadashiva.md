## Lyrics

Jaya Jaya Sadguru Sai Sadashiva
Hara Hara Shankara Shambho Mahadeva [ Jaya Jaya ... ]
Neelakanta Sai Bhasma Vibhushitha [ Hara Hara ... ]

## Meaning

Let us bow down in reverence to the Sacred Preceptor Sai Sadashiva, the one who confers auspiciousness, Mahadeva, the blue-throated one, who has smeared Sacred Ash all over His Body and Maheshwara, who is none other than Sai, the Lord of Parthi.
