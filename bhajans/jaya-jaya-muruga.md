## Meaning

Victory to Lord Muruga who is worshipped by many names - Kanda, Kumara and Karthikeya. Victory to the son of Lord Shiva and Mother Gowri (Parvathi). Victory to the brother of Lord Ganesha. O the six-faced one who wields the Vel, please protect us. 

Salutations to Thee, O Lord Karthikeya who has incarnated as Lord Sai.
