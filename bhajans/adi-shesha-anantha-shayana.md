## Lyrics

Adi Shesha Anantha Shayana
Srinivasa Sri Venkatesha
Raghukula Tilaka Raghu Ramachandra
Sita Pathey Sri Ramachandra [ Adi Shesha ]
Yadu Kula Tilaka Yashoda Bala
Radha Pathey Gopala Krishna
Pannaga Bhooshana Gowri Manohara
Uma Pathey Shiva Shambho Shankara

## Meaning

Adore the Lord Srinivasa also known as Venkatesha, who reclines on the seven headed serpent. Sing the glory of Ramachandra, the supreme of Raghu dynasty who is worshipped by his consort Sita. Chant the name of Krishna, the son of mother Yashoda and the supreme of Yadu dynasty who fostered the cowherds and who is worshipped by His consort Radha. Worship Lord Shiva bestower of auspiciousness, one who wears a serpent as His ornament and who is adored by his consort Parvathi.
