## Lyrics

Radhey Govinda Radhey Gopala Sai Ram
Bansidhara Bansidhara Hey Chitha Chora
Hey Natavara Hey Natavara Nanda Kishora
Ghana Shyama Sundara Megha Shyama Sundara Sai Ram

## Meaning

Chant the name of the Lord Krishna, the Lord of Radha and the protector of all. O holder of the Flute, You are the stealer (Chitta Chora) of our awareness, mind and heart. Sing the names of Govinda and Gopala. O the handsome and mischievous son of Nanda, You have the complexion of thick black clouds, thou art verily Lord Sai.
