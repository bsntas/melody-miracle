## Lyrics

A crown of hair, an orange robe
Loving eyes, a heart of gold
He floats to us across the sand
Sacred ash pours from His hand
Millions call Him living Lord, a living Lord is He
Millions wave with tearful eyes, tearful eyes with joy inside
A million hearts in sweet devotion filled with the love of the Lord
