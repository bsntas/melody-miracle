## Lyrics

Murali Manohara Natavara Lala
Shyama Manohara Hey Nandalala [ Murali Manohara ... ]
Nanda Mukunda Goparipala
Jaya Jaya Govinda Gopala Bala

## Meaning

Victory to the enchanting Flutist, Lord Krishna, the dark-hued son of Nanda and the protector of the cowherd.
