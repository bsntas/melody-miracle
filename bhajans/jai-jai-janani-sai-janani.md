## Lyrics

Jai Jai Janani Sai Janani Ambe Bhavani Ma
Jaya Ma Jaya Ma
Sai Bhavani Ma (Sathya)
Simha Vahini Trishoola Dharini Ambe Bhavani Ma
Jai Jai Janani Sai Janani Parthi Nivasini Ma
Jaya Ma Jaya Ma [ Sai Bhavani ... ]

## Meaning

Victory to Mother Bhavani who has the lion as Her vehicle, who holds the Trident in Her hands, who is the same as Mother Sathya Sai who dwells in Parthi.
