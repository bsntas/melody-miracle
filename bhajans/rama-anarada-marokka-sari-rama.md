## Lyrics

Rama Anarada Marokka Sari Rama Anarada 
Rama Ante Ragamu Tolagunu 
Krishna Anarada Marokka Sari Krishna Anarada 
Krishna Ante Kashtamu Tolagunu 
Sai Anarada Marokka Sari Sai Anarada 
Sai Ante Soukyamu Kalugunu 
Baba Anarada Marokka Sari Baba Anarada 
Baba Ante Badhalu Theeranu

## Meaning

Chanting the name of Rama removes all diseases. Chanting the name of Krishna removes all pains. Chanting the name of Sai grants bliss to all. Chanting the name of Baba removes all difficulties.
