## Meaning

Glory to the descendant of the Raghu dynasty, Lord Rama, the Lord of Sita, the princess of Mithila. Glory to the lotus-eyed Lord Rama, the protector of the poor, the one filled with compassion and the one who is the son of Queen Kausalya.
