## Lyrics

Madhava Murahara Murali Gopala
Jaya Govinda Jaya Gopala
Parthi Purandara Parama Daya Ghana
Deena Janavana Sai Gopala [ Jaya Govinda ... ]

## Meaning

Victory to Govinda and Gopala (names of Lord Krishna), the one of unbounded compassion, dear to the forlorn and who is none other than Lord Sai, the life principle at Parthi.
