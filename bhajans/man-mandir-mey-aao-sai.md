## Lyrics

Man Mandir Mey Aao Sai
Sai Bhagawan Darsha Dikhao [ Man Mandir ... ]
Sada Nirantara Bhajo Sai Ram
Giridhara Naagara Natavara Shyam [ Sai Bhagawan ... ]

## Meaning

Come and grant me Your Vision in the altar of my mind, O Sai Bhagawan. Chant the name of Sai continuously. He is the Supreme Actor, who was the Lord of Meera.
