## Lyrics

Nandalala Yadu Nandalala
Brindavana Govinda Lala
Radha Lola Nandalala
Radha Madhava Nandalala (Jai)

## Meaning

O Lord Krishna, child of Nanda, You are the darling of the Yadava (cowherd) chieftain. You are the child of Brindavan and the protector of cows. You are the favorite of Radha, the cowherd girl. Victory to You, O Krishna.
