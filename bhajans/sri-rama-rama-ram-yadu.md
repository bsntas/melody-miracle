## Lyrics

Sri Rama Rama Ram Yadu Nandana Hey Ghana Shyam
Sri Ram Jaya Ram Jaya Jaya Ram (3)
Raghupathi Raghava Raja Ram
Patitha Pavana Sita Ram [ Sri Ram Jaya Ram ... ]

## Meaning

Sing the name of Rama, who is beloved of Sita and son of Dasharatha and star of Raghu clan. Fill the mind with the glory of Rama, who sanctifies the sinners. Repeat the name of Sai Ram.
