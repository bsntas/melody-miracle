## Lyrics

Hari Anandamaya Jaya Narayana ( Sai )
Vasudeva Nandana Radha Jeevana
Paramananda Madhava
Narayana Hari ( Sai ) Narayana (3)

## Meaning

Praise be to Lord Narayana who is Supreme Bliss personified, who incarnated as Lord Krishna, the son of Vasudeva and who is the very life of Radha.
