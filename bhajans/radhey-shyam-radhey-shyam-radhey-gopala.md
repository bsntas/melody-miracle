## Lyrics

Radhey Shyam Radhey Shyam Radhey Gopala

Parthi Pureesha Sai Deva [ Jaya Madhava ... ]

## Meaning

Chant the name of Lord Shyam (Krishna), the dark-complexioned one who is the beloved of Radha. Victory to Thee who is worshipped as Madhava and Keshava, the one who enjoys sweet music from His flute. O Lord, You are verily Lord Vittala, the Lord of Pandari, the most compassionate one and the Lord of Parthi, Lord Sai.
