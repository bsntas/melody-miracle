## Lyrics

Bhajo Madhura Hari Naam Nirantara Bhajo Madhura Hari Naam
Sarala Bhaav Se Sai Bhajo
Madhura Bhaav Se Baba Bhajo
Hari Hi Sukh Hai Hari Hi Shanthi
Hari Prashanthi Nivasi Ram

## Meaning

Worship and recite forever the enchanting and sweet name of Lord Hari. There is no happiness and peace without chanting the sweet name of the Lord Sai, the resident of Prashanthi Nilayam, the abode of everlasting peace.
