Parathpara Jagadeeshwara (Partheeshwara)

## Meaning

Salutations to Lord Shiva Shankara (also known as Mahadeva) who holds the sacred river Ganges in His matted locks, who has made Mount Kailasa His abode, who is Higher than the Highest, the Lord of the Universe and the consort of Goddess Parvathi.
