## Meaning

O Lord Sai, Lord of Shirdi and Parthi, we worship You as Shiva, Parameshwara, Shambho and Shankara. You are the remover of fear and worldly misery, Your abode is Kailasa and You are a great dancer of the Tandava (Celestial Dance).
