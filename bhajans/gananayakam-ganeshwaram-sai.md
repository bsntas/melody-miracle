## Meaning

O Lord Ganesh, You are the leader of the demigods, beloved son of Gowri (Mother Parvathi) and the one who rides the mouse. You hold the noose and hook in Your hands and is the delightful cosmic dancer having a spotless form. You are the Supreme Lord who removes all our obstacles.
