## Lyrics

Punya Naam Pavitra Naam Rama Naam Sai Ram
Harey Krishna Harey Ram Madhura (Amrutha) Naam Sai Ram [ Punya Naam ... ]
Karuna Sindhu Sita Ram Deena Bandhu Radhey Shyam [ Harey Krishna ... ]

## Meaning

Recite the pure and holy name of Lord Sai Rama. Glory to the names of the Lord Rama, Krishna and Sai Ram that are sweet and nectarine. Chant the name of Lord Krishna, the protector of the dejected and miserable. Chant the name of Lord Rama, the ocean of mercy. The names confer auspiciousness.
