## Meaning

This song praises Lord Shiva, the presiding deity in Srisailam, in Andhra Pradesh State in India, who is the Consort of Parvathi, with various names and attributes such as, Mallikarjuna, Vishweshwara, Sri Shaila Vasa, the one who resides in the heart of Bhramaramba (Parvathi's name in Srisailam).
