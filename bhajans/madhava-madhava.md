## Lyrics

Madhava Madhava
Madhusudhana Hari Madhava
Mathuradhipathey Keshava [ Madhava ... ]
Mohana Murali Mukunda Madhava
Pavana Parthi Pura Hari Madhava
Jaya Jaya Jaya Hey Jaya Hari Madhava

## Meaning

Victory to Lord Krishna, the slayer of demon Madhu. Victory to the Lord of Mathura who is attractive to behold and who plays the flute. Victory to Lord Sai, the resident of the sacred land of Parthi who is verily Lord Madhava (Krishna).
