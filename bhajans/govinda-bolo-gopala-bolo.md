## Lyrics

Govinda Bolo Gopala Bolo
Ram Ram Bolo Hari Naam Bolo
Allah Sai Yesu Nanak
Zohrashtra Mahavira Buddha Naam Bolo [ Govinda Bolo ... ]
Yeh Naam Saarey Hai Jeevan Saharey
Paramananda Ke Yeh Kholtey Hai Dwarey
Jo Naam Chaho Woh Naam Bolo
Prem Se Bolo Bhaav Se Bolo

## Meaning

Sing the glorious names of Govinda, Gopala, Rama, Hari, Allah, Sai, Yesu, Nanak, Zohrashtra, Mahavira and Buddha. When these names are chanted with love and devotion, they sustain the devotee through the journey of life.
