## Lyrics

Antaranga Sai Anatha Natha Sai
Deena Bandhu Sai Karuna Sindhu Sai [ Antaranga Sai ... ]
Rama Krishna Shiva Shakthi Prema Roopa Sai
Yuga Avatara Baba Sri Sathya Sai

## Meaning

O Lord Sai, You are love and bliss personified, repository of compassion and kindness and hope of the downtrodden. You are verily Rama, Krishna and the incarnation of Supreme Love in the modern era.
