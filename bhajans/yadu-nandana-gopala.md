## Lyrics

Yadu Nandana Gopala
Jaya Brindavana Lola
Jaya Murali Gaana Vilola
Gopala Gopala Sai Gopala

## Meaning

Chant the name of Sai Gopala, the son of Yadu dynasty, who moved around in Brindavan and who is enchanted by the sound of Flute He plays.
