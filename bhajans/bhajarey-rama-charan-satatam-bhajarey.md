## Lyrics

Bhajarey Rama Charan Satatam Bhajarey Rama Charan
Bhajarey Janaki Jeevana Ramam
Bhajarey Ramam Bhadrachala Ramam
Bhajarey Pavana Taraka Naamam
Bhajarey Sri Rama Ramethi Ramam

## Meaning

Constantly chant and pray to the Lotus feet of Lord Rama. Pray to the Lord of Janaki, Lord Rama, the resident of Bhadrachala. Chant the sacred and pious name of Lord Rama.
