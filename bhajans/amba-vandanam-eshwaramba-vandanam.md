## Lyrics

Amba Vandanam Eshwaramba Vandanam
Janani Vandanam Jagat Janani Vandanam (2)
Sai Vandanam Sai Mata Vandanam
Mata Vandanam Eshwaramba Nandanam

## Meaning

O Universal Mother Sai, we bow to You and we bow to Mother Eshwaramba who was blessed to be Your physical mother during Your advent on earth.
