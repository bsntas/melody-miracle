## Lyrics

Karuna Sagara Prema Swaroopa
Paapa Vimochana Ram
Dukha Bhanjana Sri Ram
Allah Eshwar Terey Naam
Nanak Yesu Terey Naam
Parthi Pureeshwara Deena Dayaghana [ Dukha Bhanjana ... ]

## Meaning

Lord Sai is an embodiment of Love, the ocean of compassion who removes sin and destroys sorrow. Allah, Eshwar, Nanak and Jesus are all His names. He is highly compassionate towards the distressed and is the Lord of Parthi.
