## Meaning

Glory to the Lord of the Lords, Lord Shiva. Chant the auspicious name of "Shiva" who is worshipped by the other two Gods, Brahma and Vishnu. O Lord, You are served by Your consort, the Divine Mother Uma and by Your sons, Ganesha and Sharavana (Subrahmanya).
