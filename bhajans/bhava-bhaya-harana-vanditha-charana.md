## Lyrics

Bhava Bhaya Harana Vanditha Charana
Jaya Radha Jaya Madhava (Shourie)
Mangala Charana Kalimala Dahana
Narayana Keshava (Sai) [ Jaya Radha ... ]

## Meaning

O Lord, we worship Your Holy Feet that frees us from the continuous cycle of birth and death. Glory to Radha, victory to Lord Krishna, the slayer of the demon Madhu, and the sustainer of the Universe. Thy Holy Feet dispels the sins of the Kali age. Glory to Lord Narayana, the eternal guardian and to Keshava, the slayer of demon Kesi.
