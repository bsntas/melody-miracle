## Lyrics

Anjana Sutha Anjaneya
Vayu Putra Vathathmaja
Vanarotthama Bala Bheema
Rama Dootha Ranadheera

## Meaning

Oh Anjaneya, the son of Mother Anjana and Vayu, You are the supreme among the Vanaras (monkeys) and the strongest. You are a supreme warrior and the humble emissary of Rama.
