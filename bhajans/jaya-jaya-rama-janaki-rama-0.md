## Meaning

Praise be to Lord Rama, the consort of Mother Janaki (Sita) and the Crown Jewel of Raghu dynasty. Chant the Holy name of Rama that liberates one from the cycle of birth and death and which is a source of delight for the sages and saints. Glory to Lord Rama, the wielder of the Kodanda bow and the vanquisher of the demonic race.
