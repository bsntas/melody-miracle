## Lyrics

Jaya Guru Omkara Jaya Jaya Sadguru Omkara Om
Brahma Vishnu Sadashiva
Hara Hara Hara Hara Mahadeva
Shiva Shiva Shiva Shiva Sadashiva

## Meaning

Venerable salutations to our Preceptor Lord Sai, who is the form of the primordial sound Om, the creator Brahma, protector Vishnu and destroyer Shiva. Salutations to Sai who is the embodiment of Mahadeva and Sadashiva (names of Lord Shiva).
