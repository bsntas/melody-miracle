## Lyrics

Krishna Rama Govinda Narayana
Madhava Keshava Hari Narayana [ Krishna Rama ... ]
Sri Venu Gopala Krishna
Madhava Madhusudhana Narayana

## Meaning

Glory to Lord Narayana, the one who resides in the heart of Nara (man) who is also Rama and Krishna, the one bearing the flute in His Hands and the  slayer of demon Madhu.
