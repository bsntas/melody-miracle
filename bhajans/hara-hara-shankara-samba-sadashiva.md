## Lyrics

Hara Hara Shankara Samba Sadashiva Esha Mahesha
Tandava Priyakara Chandrakala Dhara Esha Mahesha
Amba Guha Lambodhara Vanditha Esha Mahesha
Vindhya Himachala Shringa Nivasita Esha Mahesha

## Meaning

Chant the many names of the Lord Shiva - Shankara, Sadashiva and Mahesha. O Lord, You are fond of the Tandava Dance (cosmic dance). You are adorned with the moon on Your forehead. You are worshipped by the darling son of Mother Parvathi - Lord Ganesh, the pot-bellied one. You are the resident of the Himalayas. By chanting the above divine names, the Lord destroys evil and grants protection.
