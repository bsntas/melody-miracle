## Meaning

Glory to Lord Ganesha, the primeval God, the destroyer of all ill-effects. Son of Lord Shiva, You are the symbol of knowledge. It brings us immense joy when Your name resonates in all eight directions. We surrender to You, Lord Sai, who is verily Lord Ganesha and the embodiment of eternal Aum.
