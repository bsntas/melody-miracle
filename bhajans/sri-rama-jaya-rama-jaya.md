## Lyrics

Sri Rama Jaya Rama Jaya Jaya Rama
Sri Rama Jaya Rama Sundara Rama [ Sri Rama ... ]
Sri Rama Jaya Rama Mangala Naama
Sri Rama Jaya Rama Atma Rama
Sri Rama Jaya Rama Sita (Sai) Rama

## Meaning

Victory to Lord Rama, the most beautiful one whose name is auspicious, the one who is the indweller, Atma Rama (Higher conscience or Higher Self). Victory to Lord Sai who is none other than Lord Rama.
