## Lyrics

Sri Sathya Sai Shubhanana (Bolo)

## Meaning

Oh Lord Sai, You are none other than the Lord Gajanana (Ganesha), who bestows auspiciousness, removes the obstacles in life and fulfills every wish. You are the personification of the Primordial sound "Om".
