## Lyrics

Jagatpathey Hari Sai Gopala
Jagadodhara Sai Nandalala
Mathuradhipathey Krishna Gopala
Madhura Madhura Hey Gaana Vilola
Jagadodhara Sai Nandalala
Sai Nandalala Jai Jai Gopala (3)

## Meaning

Glory to Lord Gopala (Krishna), the Lord and sustainer of the Universe. Victory to Lord Krishna, the King of Mathura, the patron of sweet music and the son of Nanda. Victory to Lord Sai who is verily Lord Krishna.
