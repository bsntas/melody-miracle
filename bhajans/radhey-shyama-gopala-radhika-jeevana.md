## Lyrics

Radhey Shyama Gopala Radhika Jeevana Sri Ranga
Megha Shyama Gopala Mana Mohana Sundara Sri Ranga [ Radhey ... ]
Pandari Natha Gopala Panduranga Sri Ranga
Parthi Pureesha Gopala Sai Ranga Sri Ranga

## Meaning

Pray to Lord Krishna (Ranga) , the beautiful cowherd boy with dark blue complexion like a cloud, who enchants our minds and who is the beloved of Radha. Pray to the Lord of Pandharpur, Panduranga (Vittala) who resides in Puttaparthi as Sai.
