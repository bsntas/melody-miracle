## Meaning

Salutations to Lord Narayana who is reclining on the Celestial Serpent Adi Sesha. O Lord, You are the ever-pleasing Parijata flower for Your devotees, the indweller of all beings. You foster the Saama Veda which is the basis of music. O Lord Sathya Sai, You are none other than Lord Narayana.
