## Lyrics

Jaya Jagadeesha Harey Prabhuvara
Hara Hara Shambho Jagadeka Vibho [ Jai Jagadeesha ... ]
Chandrashekara Kailasadhipathey
Bhaava Gochara Bhavani Shankara

## Meaning

Sing the glory of the Sovereign Lord of the Universe, Shiva, the benevolent one. Lord Shankara bears the crescent moon on His head, is the Lord of Mount Kailasa, imperceptible to the senses and the consort of Mother Bhavani (Parvathi).
