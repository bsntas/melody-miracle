## Lyrics

Durga Bhavani Ma Jaya Jaya Sai Ma
Kaali Kapalini Ma Jaya Jaya Sai Ma [ Durga Bhavani ... ]
Parama Shivani Ma Jaya Jaya Sai Ma
Jagadodharini Ma Jaya Jaya Sai Ma

## Meaning

Victory to Mother Sai who is verily Mother Durga (Parvathi). Victory to the Divine Mother hailed as Bhavani, Kaali. Victory to Mother Sai, who is the embodiment of Supreme Power and who has come to save and deliver mankind.
