## Meaning

Protect me O Lord Ganesha, the one with a big belly and the son of Universal Mother Amba. Protect me, for I have surrendered to You. O the ocean of Mercy, give me true devotion to Thee, O Lord of Shiva's forces who looks after my welfare.
