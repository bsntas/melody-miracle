## Lyrics

Jaya Krishna Kunja Vihari
Jaya Radha Hrudaya Vihari
Jaya Yamuna Teera Vihari
Jaya Gopi Ke Hrudaya Vihari
Jaya Madhava Raasa Vihari
Jaya Sai Parthi Vihari

## Meaning

Victory to Lord Krishna, the one who moves about through thick shrubs, on the banks of Yamuna river, the one who dwells in the hearts of Radha and the Gopis and with whom He performed the celestial dance. Victory to Lord Sai who dwells in Parthi.
