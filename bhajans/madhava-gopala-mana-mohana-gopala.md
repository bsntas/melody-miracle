## Lyrics

Madhava Gopala Mana Mohana Gopala
Sai Mohana Gopala
Yashoda Ke Bala Yadu Nandana Gopala

Bala Gopala Giridhara Gopala
Sai Mohana Gopala

## Meaning

Glory to Lord Gopala (Krishna), the protector of cows who is hailed as Madhava. Glory to the enchanting son of Yashoda of the Yadu clan. Glory to the one who lifted the Govardana mountain to protect His devotees. The most beautiful Lord Sai is none other than Lord Gopala.
