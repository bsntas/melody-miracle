## Lyrics

Jeevan Jyothi Tum Ho Sai
Prem Ki Jyothi Jalao Sai Prem Ki Jyothi Jalao

Sai Deep Jalao Sathya Sai Deep Jalao [ Prem Ki Jyothi ... ]

## Meaning

You are the Lamp of Life O Lord Sai, light in us the lamp of Love. Enlighten our life so we may cross the dark crevices of this turbulent world.
