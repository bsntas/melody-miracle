## Lyrics

Meera Nath Harey Giridhari
Radha Pyaarey Krishna Murari [ Meera Nath ... ]
Brindavana Ke Sai Murari
Bhakthoddhari Baba Murari

## Meaning

O Lord Krishna, You are the Lord of Meera, You are the beloved of Radha and the one who resides in Brindavan. You are verily Sai, who liberates all devotees.
