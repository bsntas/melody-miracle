## Lyrics

Raghava Sundara Rama Raghuvara
Parama Pavana Hey Jaga Vandana [ Raghava Sundara ... ]
Patithodharana Bhaktha Parayana
Ravana Mardhana Vighna Bhanjana
Parthi Pureeshwara Sai Narayana

## Meaning

The beautiful Lord Rama is Supreme among the Raghu dynasty. He is worshipped by the entire creation. Liberator of the chaste, venerated by the devotees, He destroys obstacles and annihilated the demon-king Ravana. Worship Lord Narayana, who has incarnated in Parthi as Lord Sai Ram.
