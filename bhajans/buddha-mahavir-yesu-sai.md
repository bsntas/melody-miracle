## Meaning

O mind, chant the name of Vahey Guru, Buddha, Mahavir, Jesus and the Divine Preceptor Sai, who are the essence of scriptures Bagawad Gita, Quran and Bible. O Sai, You are the indweller of the body and mind and every atom of body is filled by You.
