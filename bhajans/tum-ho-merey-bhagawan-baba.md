## Lyrics

Tum Ho Merey Bhagawan (Baba)
Parthi Pureesha Sri Sai Natha
Shirdi Pureesha Hey Jagannatha [ Tum Ho ... ]
Prashanthi Vasa Sri Sai Natha
Purana Purusha Hey Jagannatha

## Meaning

Glory to Lord Sai, the Lord of Shirdi and Parthi, is also Lord of the world and the Lord who was mentioned in great Epics (Puranas) like Mahabharata, Ramayana etc. O Sai, You are my Lord.
