## Lyrics

Nandalala Nandalala
Nanda Mukunda Hari Nandalala
Murali Manohara Nandalala
Radha Manohara Nandalala

## Meaning

Oh son of Nanda, the cowherd chieftain, You are the son who grants liberation! You enchant with your music on the flute! You are the One who adorns the mind of Radha, the cowherd girl.
