## Lyrics

Govinda Sai Krishna Govinda Govinda
Gopala Sai Krishna Govinda Govinda
Mohana Bala Krishna Govinda Govinda
Parthi Nivasa Krishna Govinda Govinda

## Meaning

Chant the names of the Lord Govinda and Gopala, the handsome boy Krishna who is same as Sai, the resident of Parthi.
