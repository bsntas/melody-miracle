## Lyrics

Sita Rakshaka Rama Dootha
Lanka Dahana Ravana Mardhana
Sri Rama Daasa Daya Nidhey
Sarva Mangala Anjaneya

## Meaning

Glory to You, O Lord Hanuman, You protected Mother Sita and is the messenger of Lord Rama. You are the one who put Lanka in flames and showed the path for Lord Rama who killed Ravana. You are the one who confers auspiciousness on everyone, the son of Anjana. We offer our salutations to You, O Lord Hanuman.
