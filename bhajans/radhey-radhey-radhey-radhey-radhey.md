## Meaning

Chant continuously the name of Krishna - 'Radhey Govinda' signifying Radha (the individual soul) and Govinda (signifying the Universal soul), the beautiful shining star of Brindavana, who is the refuge of the forlorn. He is the son of Nanda. He is the one who stole butter (steals devotees' hearts), who is dear to His devotees and who takes care of them. He is the victorious one who likes to play scintillating music on His Flute. He is Panduranga, the Lord of Pandharpur. He is the one who waited at the doorstep of devotee Pundarika to grant him liberation. He is the ancient and the Supreme One.
