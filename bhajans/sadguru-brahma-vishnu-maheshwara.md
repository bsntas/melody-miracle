## Meaning

O Divine Teacher, You represent the Trinity (Creator, Preserver & Destroyer). You are the one who gave the boon of immortality. You drank the poison arising from the churning of the ocean. Help me cross the ocean of fear and sorrow. Grant me fearlessness, O ever blissful Lord Sathya Sai.
