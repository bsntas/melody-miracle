## Lyrics

Sri Rama Jaya Rama Jaya Jaya Ram
Govinda Gopala Hey Ghana Shyam
Sundara Shyam Nayanabhi Ram
Madhura Manohara Sai Bhagawan

## Meaning

Victory to the Lord Rama, the beautiful lotus-eyed one and Lord Gopala (Krishna), the beautiful dark-hued one who is the protector of cows. Victory to Lord Sai, the sweetest and most pleasing Lord.
