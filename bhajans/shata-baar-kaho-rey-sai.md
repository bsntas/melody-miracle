## Lyrics

Shata Baar Kaho Rey Sai Ram Sai Ram Sai Ram Sai Ram
Hey Mangala Nayana Ram Hey Gopi Jeevana Shyam
Hey Shirdi Ke Bhagawan Hey Prashanthi Nilaya Ram
Shata Koti Namana Sai Ram
Sai Ram Sai Ram Sai Ram

## Meaning

Our innumerable salutations to Lord Sai Ram, the one whose look is the most auspicious and the one who is the very life of the Gopikas. He is the Lord of Shirdi and the one who resides at Prashanthi Nilayam in Puttaparthi. Let us chant His name million times.
