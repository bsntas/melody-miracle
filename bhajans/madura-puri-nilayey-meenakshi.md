## Lyrics

Madura Puri Nilayey Meenakshi
Kamakoti Nilayey Kamakshi [ Madura Puri ... ]
Kashi Puradheeshwari Vishweshwari
Parthi Puradheeshwari Sayeeshwari

## Meaning

Goddess Meenakshi has Her Abode in Madurai while Kamakshi has Her Abode in Kancheepuram, Vishweshwari has Her Abode in Kashi and Sayeeshwari has Her Abode in Parthi.
