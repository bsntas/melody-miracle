## Lyrics

Nataraja Namami Shankara
Shiva Raja Shiva Shiva Shankara [ Nataraja Namami ... ]
Shirdi Pureeshwara Shiva Shankara
Parthi Pureeshwara Parameshwara
Hrudayeshwara Sayeeshwara

## Meaning

We offer our humble salutations to Lord Shankara (Shiva) who is the source of all dance forms. We pray to Shirdi Sai and Sathya Sai, incarnations of Shiva. We pray to Sai who resides in the hearts of the devoted.
