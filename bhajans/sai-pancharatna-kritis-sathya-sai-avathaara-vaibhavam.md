## Lyrics

Sathya Sai Avathaara Vaibhavam, Nithya Noothanam Sathya Sanaathanam
 (Pallavi)

Sathya Dharma Shanthi Prema
Prathihrudhi Nilipina Vishwaprema Prasthaanam
(Anupallavi)

Prema Ekaika Matham Sai Bodhaamrutha Saaram
(Charanam)

1. Udaattha Maanaveeya Sevaye Aadhyaathmika Saadhana Tholi Sopaanam

2. Graama Sevamari, Deena Jana Sevaye Karma Yogam Chakkani Raaja Maargam

3. Naada Brahma Sai Sankeerthaname Kalilo Mukthi Maargam

4. Bhaava Raaga Thaala Melana, Bhajanaanandame Bhakthi Maargam

5. Soham, Soham, Soham Yerukaye Jnaanam              
Anuvanuvuna Ninnu Kanugonute Jnaana Maargam

6. Kalushitha Kali Maanava Medhaahamkruthi Doorikaranam
Kula Matha Deshaatheeta Vishwajaneena,
Sathyapathanirdheshanam Saayi Mahitodhyamam

7. Premaakarshana Shakthiyutha Sayee    
Janaganamanamula Divyathwa Jaagruthiki Velasina Vishwa Viraat Roopaa
Nithya, Shuddha, Buddha, Chaitanya Deepthaa Apuroopa Leelaavathaara

8. Sai Siddhaanthamulu Vannemeera Saakaaramaaye
Bhuvi Dharmakshetram, Jagathi Vasudaika Kutumbam
Digdhigantha Dheedhithula Thejarille
Sathya Sai Vibhavam Apoorvam Akhandam Anantham

## Meaning

The grandeur of the Sathya Sai incarnation is ever new, true and eternal.  

You are the source of Universal Love that established Sathya (Truth), Dharma (Right-conduct), Shanthi (Peace) and Prema (Love) in every heart.  

‘Love is the only religion’ is the essence of Sai's ambrosial message.  

1. One who is extolled in the Vedas, the sustainer of the Vedas; Noble service to mankind is the first step in the practice of spirituality.  

2. Serving the villages and the downtrodden is true karma yoga (path of action) and verily the royal path.  

3. Singing the name of the Lord Sai, who is Nada Brahma (the Primordial Sound), is the pathway to liberation in the Kali age.  

4. Experiencing bhajan bliss through the harmony of the feeling (Bhaava), tune (Raaga) and rhythm (Taala) is the path of Devotion (Bhakti Marga).  

5. Realising ‘I am That’ with every breath is the True Knowledge (Jnana). The path of Knowledge (Jnana Marga) therefore lies in finding You in every atom.  

6. Warding away the intellectual arrogance of the impure minds of humanity in Kali age;  Bringing universality by transcending the caste, religion and nation; Guiding everyone on the path of Truth This is the grand mission of Sai.  

7. Sai is endowed with the magnetic power of Love. Sai is the Cosmic Being whose advent is for awakening the Divinity latent in all. Sai is a rare incarnation of divine playfulness; the effulgent Consciousness which is eternal, pure and enlightened.  

8. Sai's principles have taken shape in a grand manner turning the Earth into a land of righteousness, and the entire world into one family by spreading its effulgence in all directions.  The grandeur of Sathya Sai is unique, ceaseless and infinite.
