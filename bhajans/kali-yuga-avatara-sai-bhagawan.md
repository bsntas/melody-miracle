Parthi Pureeshwara Sai Bhagawan

## Meaning

Sing the Glory of Lord Sai, the Incarnation of the Kali Age, son of Mother Eshwaramba, the indweller of every being, the ocean of compassion, the very life of the underprivileged and the Lord of Puttaparthi.
