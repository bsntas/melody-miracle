## Lyrics

Hari Bol Hari Bol Hari Hari Bol

## Meaning

Contemplate on the name and meditate on the Lotus Feet of Lord Hari. Worship the divine Flutist, Lord Madhava (Krishna). Sing the glory of Lord Keshava.
