## Lyrics

Sita Ram Bolo Bolo Sita Ram Radhey Shyam Bolo Bolo Radhey Shyam
Sai Ram Bolo Bolo Sai Ram (4)

## Meaning

Sing the names of Lord Rama, the consort of Mother Sita. Chant the names of Lord Krishna and Lord Sai.
