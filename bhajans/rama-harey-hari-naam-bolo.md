## Meaning

Sing the many names of the Lord Rama, Hari, Govinda. Chant the name of Guru Nanak, Allah, Maulah and the all-pervasive Lord who is extremely compassionate and whose presence has kindled the light in the temple of our hearts. Chant the Name of Rama and of Hari. Light the lamp of Love in our Hearts, O Sai, the most merciful One.
