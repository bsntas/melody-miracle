## Meaning

Glory to Lord Sai, the embodiment of eternal and divine love, who fills our hearts with the nectarine wisdom (Amrut), who showers compassionate love through merciful eyes and sweet and soothing words of wisdom. Guru of gurus Sai, You are the true support and the eternal guide of my life.
