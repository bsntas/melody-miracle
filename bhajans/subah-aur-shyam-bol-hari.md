## Lyrics

Subah Aur Shyam Bol Hari Naam
Gopi Gopala Bhajo Sai Ghana Shyam [ Subah Aur ... ]
Chalo Man Chalo Chaley Baba Ke Dham
Shirdi Ke Dhaam Sathya Sai Ke Dham

## Meaning

Chant the name of Lord Hari day in and day out. Chant Gopi, Gopala, Ghana Shyam (various names of Lord Krishna) and Sai who is verily Krishna. O Mind, let us proceed with our journey to the abodes of Shirdi Sai Baba and Sathya Sai Baba.
