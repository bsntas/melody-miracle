## Lyrics

Tum Bin Pran Nahin O Sai Merey
Sab Bhakthon Ka Tum Ho Sahara
Rahim Kaho Raghu Rama Kaho [ Tum Bin ... ]
Nanak Yesu Mahavira Shiva Shankara Premavatara Sai Ram
Tum Ho Merey Pran O Sai Merey
Tum Ho Sai Bhagawan

## Meaning

O compassionate Lord Sai, You are none other than Lord Shankara(Shiva), Guru Nanak, Yesu and Mahavira. Lord Sai, we adore You as the very personification of Love, the basis of all faiths. Chant the names of Ram, Rahim, Nanak, Yesu, Mahavir. O Sai, there is no life without You.
