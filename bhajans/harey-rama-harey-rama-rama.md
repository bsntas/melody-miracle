## Meaning

Chant incessantly the holy names of Lord Hari, Lord Rama and Lord Krishna. Good company leads to blissful solitude, which further leads to freedom from infatuation, mental equanimity and finally to liberation even as one is alive. This is how an aspirant progresses by chanting the Lord's name with sincere devotion.
