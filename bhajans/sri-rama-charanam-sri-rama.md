## Lyrics

Sri Rama Charanam Sri Rama Charanam Sri Rama Charanam Bhajey (2)
Vaidehi Ramam Vaikunta Dhamam
Bhoopala Choodamani
Atmabhi Ramam Prabhu Sai Ramam
Sri (Sathya Sai) Rama Charanam Bhajey (3)

## Meaning

Worship the feet of Lord Sri Rama, Lord of Vaidehi, whose abode is Vaikunta. Like the Choodamani, which is the best among the jewels worn on the head, Sri Rama is the Crown Jewel among the kings who have ruled this world. He is the Atma (Higher self or Higher conscience) Rama, Sathya Sai Rama.
