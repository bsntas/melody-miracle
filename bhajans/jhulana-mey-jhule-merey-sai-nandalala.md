## Lyrics

Ma Eshwaramma Jhulana Jhulave 
Godi Mey Bittake Kahani Sunaye 
Hari Katha Choti Sai Ram Ko Sunaye 
Apni Hi Katha Suno Sai Nandalala 
Jhulana Mey Jhule Sai Bala Gopala

## Meaning

O young, Lord Sai, swing my Lord. Mother Eshwaramma rocks Your cradle gently. She seats You on her lap and tells You Your own story. She tells You the stories of Lord Hari. Listen to Your own story, O Lord Sai while You rock gently on the cradle.
