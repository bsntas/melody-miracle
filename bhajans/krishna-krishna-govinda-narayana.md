## Lyrics

Krishna Krishna Govinda Narayana
Krishna Rama Govinda Narayana
Sai Rama Govinda Sai Shyama Gopala
Sai Krishna Govinda Narayana (Harey)

## Meaning

Chant the Name of Krishna, Govinda, Narayana, Sai Rama, Sai Shyama and Sai Krishna to the delight of everyone.
