## Lyrics

Tum Ho Daata Sai Shankara
Hum Ko Deejo Ananda Baba [ Tum Ho Daata ... ]
Aao Shankara Anatha Natha
Deejo Darshana Deena Natha

## Meaning

O Lord Sai who is verily Lord Shiva, You are the one who bestows everything. We pray that You give us the eternal bliss. Please come and give Your Darshan. You are the Lord and savior of the helpless and the downtrodden.
