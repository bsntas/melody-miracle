## Lyrics

Ven Mi Sai Rama
Haz De Mi Mente Un Jardin, Dionde Florezca Dharma Shanthi Prema
Ven Shiva Sathya Sai, Y Baila
Baila En Mi Corazon (4)
Baila En Mi Corazon Tu Cosmica Danza

## Meaning

Come my Sai Rama, make my mind a garden where Dharma, Shanthi, and Prema bloom. Come Shiva Sathya Sai and dance. Perform Your cosmic dance in my heart.
