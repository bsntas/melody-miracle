## Lyrics

Yadu Nandana Yashoda Bala
Madhava Keshava [ Yadu Nandana ... ]
Mathura Natha Mama Sai Natha
Mandara Giridhara Madhusudhana Hey

## Meaning

Glory be to You, Oh Lord Krishna, the son of Yadu dynasty, the son of Yashoda, You are the Lord Madhava and Keshava, the one who held aloft the hillock Mandara, You are the destroyer of the demon Madhu.
