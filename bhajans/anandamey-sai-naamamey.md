## Lyrics

Anandamey Sai Naamamey
Adbhutamey Sai Geetamey

## Meaning

Oh Lord Sai! Chanting Your divine name is true bliss and singing Your glory confers ecstasy. Your infinite effulgence is all pervading and Your form is beauty personified.
