## Lyrics

Harey Murarey Sai Ram Harey Murarey Ram
Parama Sumangala Sai Ram Parama Sumangala Ram
Eshwar Allah Terey Naam Sab Tum Ho Bhagawan [ Parama Sumangala ... ]

## Meaning

Glory be to Lord Sai Ram, the one who is supremely auspicious who is glorified by many names - Rama, Allah and Eshwara (Shiva).
