## Lyrics

## Meaning

Glory to Lord Sai Rama of Puttaparthi, the Lord of Prashanthi Nilayam. Glory to the beloved Prince of King Dasharatha, Lord Rama, the saviour of Ahalya. Victory to Thee, O Lord Sai Rama of Prashanthi, salutations to You.
