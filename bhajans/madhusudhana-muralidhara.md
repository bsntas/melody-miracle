## Lyrics

Madhusudhana Muralidhara
Madhura Madhura Hey Giridhara Bala
Madhava Madhusudhana
Hrudayanta Ranga Sri Sai Ranga
Sri Ranga Ranga Puttaparthi Ranga [ Madhava ... ]

## Meaning

Glory to Lord Krishna, the slayer of demon Madhu and the one who holds the flute. The child who lifted the Govardhan hill who is the very embodiment of sweetness, lures the minds of everyone. He is also Lord Ranga residing in our hearts and is verily Lord Sai residing at Parthi.
