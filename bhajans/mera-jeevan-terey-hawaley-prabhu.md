## Meaning

I submit my life in Your care, O Lord. Please take care of it every moment. The boat of my life is wobbling in this sea of worldliness, O dear Caretaker, only You should take it across. Liberate us from the bondage of illusion, take us in Your care and shelter. Please accept this sinner, O Lord.
