Phaala Netra Tripura Haari Sai Shankara [ Chandrashekara ... ]

## Meaning

Obeisance to Lord Sai who is none other than Lord Shankara (Shiva), the one who holds the crescent moon on His head. He is also known as Neelakanta, as His throat is blue in color (due to the poison He swallowed during the conflict between Gods and demons). Salutations to the one who holds the Trident and who has the Third Eye on His forehead and who slayed the demon Tripura.
