## Lyrics

Joota Jatadhara Gangadhari
Shivaya Namah Shiva Om
Shiva Shiva Om Hara Hara Om [ Shivaya Namah ]
Gangadhara Gowri Shiva
Shivaya Namah Shiva Om

## Meaning

Prostrations to Shiva who has Ganges on His matted Locks of Hair on His Head and consort of Gowri.
