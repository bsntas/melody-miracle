## Lyrics

Giridhara Gopala Gopala
Sai Gopala Sai Gopala [ Giridhara ... ]
Shirdi Pureeshwara (Parthi Pureeshwara) Sai Shankara
Sai Shankara Sai Shankara

## Meaning

Chant the divine name of Lord Krishna, Giridhara Gopala and of Lord Sai, the resident of Shirdi and Parthi, who is verily Lord Krishna. He is also Lord Shankara (Shiva) who bestows auspiciousness.
