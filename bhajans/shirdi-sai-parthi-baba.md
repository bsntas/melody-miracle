## Meaning

Glory to Lord Shirdi Sai who is now in the form of Parthi Baba, the protector of His devotees as well as the weak and the downtrodden, He has incarnated in this Kali age to sustain and protect all beings. We offer our salutations to You, O Lord Sai, You are our divine Preceptor.
