## Lyrics

Bhaja Mana Ramam Sri Ramam
Shyamala Komala Nayanabhi Ramam [ Bhaja Mana ... ]
Bharata Lakshmana Pramudita Ramam
Maruti Sevitha Manobhi Ramam
Pankaja Lochana Prabhu Sita (Sai) Ramam

## Meaning

O Mind, chant the Holy name of Lord Rama, the one with soft, beautiful eyes, the one who was the source of joy for His brothers Bharata and Lakshmana and the one who was served by Hanuman. Glory to the lotus-eyed one, the consort of Mother Sita. Glory to Lord Sai who is none other than Lord Rama.
