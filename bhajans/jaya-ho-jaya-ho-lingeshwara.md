## Lyrics

Jaya Ho Jaya Ho Lingeshwara
Jaya Ho Jaya Ho Partheeshwara
Jaya Ho Jaya Ho Mruthyunjaya
Jaya Ho Jaya Ho Sayeeshwara

## Meaning

Victory be to Sayeeshwara, who is in the Form of a Linga (denotes without beginning or end), the One who has conquered Death and the One who resides in Parthi
