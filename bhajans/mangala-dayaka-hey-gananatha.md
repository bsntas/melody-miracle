## Lyrics

Mangala Dayaka Hey Gananatha
Pranava Swaroopa Vighna Vinasha
Hey Shiva Nandana Paahi Dayala
Parthi Pureeshwara Jagat Paripala [ Pranava Swaroopa ... ]

## Meaning

Glory to Lord Ganesha, the bestower of auspiciousness, Lord of the Ganaas, the very embodiment of Pranava (Omkar) and destroyer of obstacles. O the merciful, loving son of Lord Shiva, You are none other than the Lord of Parthi, Sai, the protector of the Universe.
