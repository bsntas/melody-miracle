## Lyrics

Mandir Mey Tum Ram Ho Sai
Masjid Mey Noor Mohammad (Allah Ho Akbar)
Gurudware Mey Tum Guru Nanak
Man Mandir Mey Sai Sai
Bolo Ram Ek Hi Naam
Sab Mil Bolo Sai Ram
Bolo Ram Ek Hi Naam [ Sab Mil ... ]

## Meaning

You are adored as Ram in the Temple, as the light of Mohammad in the mosque; You are Guru Nanak in the Gurudwara; In the temple of my mind, You are Sai. Chant the name of Ram, let us chant together 'Sai Ram'.
