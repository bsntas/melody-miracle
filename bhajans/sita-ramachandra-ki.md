## Lyrics

Sita Ramachandra Ki Jai Jai Bolo
Veera Hanuman Ki Jai Jai Bolo [ Sita Ramachandra … ]
Gnana Guna Sagar Ki Jai Jai Bolo
Maruthi Bhagawan Ki Jai Jai Bolo

## Meaning

Chant victory to Lord Rama, the consort of Mother Sita. Victory to the valorous Lord Hanuman, the ocean of wisdom and supremely good qualities. Victory to the son of wind-god.
