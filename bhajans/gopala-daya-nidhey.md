## Lyrics

(Bala) Gopala Daya Nidhey
Sri Parthi Krupa Nidhey Sai Rama [ Gopala Daya ... ]
Nanda Mukunda Govinda Nithyananda

## Meaning

Sing the glory of Lord Gopala (Krishna), the treasure house of compassion who is none other than Lord Sai Rama, the most compassionate one residing at Parthi. Glory to the son of Nanda, Lord Govinda (Krishna), who is ever blissful and who grants liberation.
