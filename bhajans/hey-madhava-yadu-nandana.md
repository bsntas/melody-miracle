## Lyrics

Hey Madhava Yadu Nandana
Mana Mohana Hey Madhusudhana
Janardhana Radha Jeevana
Gopalana Gopi Ranjana

## Meaning

Glory to Lord Krishna, the beloved son of the Yadhava clan, the one who vanquished the demon Madhu. He is the one who enchants the mind of His devotees, the one who is the very life of Radha. He is the protector of the cows and is the source of delight to the Gopis.
