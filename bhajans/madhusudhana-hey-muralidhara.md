## Lyrics

Madhusudhana Hey Muralidhara
Madhava Keshava Devadi Deva [ Madhusudhana Hey ... ]
Mana Mohana Hey Narayana
Narayana Sai Narayana [ Mana Mohana Hey ... ]
Bhuvana Bharana Vanditha Charana

## Meaning

Madhusudhana, Muralidhara, Madhava, Keshava, Mana Mohana are different names of the same Lord Krishna, the incarnation of Lord Narayana. Lord Sai is verily Lord Narayana and is the bestower of Auspiciousness, jewel of the Earth and whose Holy Feet are worthy of obeisance.
