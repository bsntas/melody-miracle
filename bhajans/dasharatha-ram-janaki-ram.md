## Lyrics

Dasharatha Ram Janaki Ram
Maruti Sevitha Manobhi Ram
Sita Ram Sri Raghu Ram
Parthi Pureeshwara Sri Sai Ram
Jaya Jaya Ram Janaki Ram
Ahalyodharaka Ananda Ram

## Meaning

O Lord Rama, Prince of King Dasharatha, O Lord of Sita, You are constantly worshipped by Maruti (Hanuman, a monkey devotee of Lord Rama). Chant the name of the Lord of Parthi, Lord Sai Ram. Victory to Lord Rama, Lord of Janaki (Sita), blissful Rama, the saviour of Ahalya.
