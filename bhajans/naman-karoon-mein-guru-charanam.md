## Lyrics

Naman Karoon Mein Guru Charanam Guru Charanam Sadguru Charanam
Pranavananda Ananda Chandha
Sai Guru Mama Guru Satchidananda [ Naman Karoon ... ]
Shanthi Vidayaka Guru Govinda
Mangala Dayaka Sadguru Natha
Eshwaramba Priya Tanaya

## Meaning

I bow down to the feet of the eternal Guru, the embodiment of Om or the Pranava. The blissful Sai is my Guru and the form of Satchidananda - symbolizing Truth, Existence and Bliss. He is the One who bestows everlasting peace, He is Guru Govinda, the great Preceptor, He is the Real Teacher who blesses with auspiciousness. He is Sai, the beloved son of Mother Eshwaramba.
