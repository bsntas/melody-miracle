## Lyrics

Kamala Netra Sayeeshwara
Kaivalya Teja Sureshwara [ Kamala Netra ... ]
Megha Shyama Ghana Gagana Shareera
Shantha Brahmamaya Abhayakara
Megha Shyama Ghana Gagana Shareera [ Shantha Brahmamaya ]

## Meaning

Salutations to Sayeeshwara the Lotus eyed One who confers Liberation, whose complexion is that of thick clouds in the sky, guards against fear and embodiment of Peace.
