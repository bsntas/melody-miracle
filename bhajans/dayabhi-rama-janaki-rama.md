## Lyrics

Dayabhi Rama Janaki Rama
Madhura Manohara Prabhu Sai Rama
Atma Rama Nayanabhi Rama
Kodanda Rama Pattabhi Rama
Raghupathi Raghava Raja Rama [ Madhura Manohara ... ]

## Meaning

O Lord Rama, who resides in the heart of devotees and holder of the Kodanda bow, You are merciful and beautiful to behold. Chant the name of the King Rama of the Raghu dynasty and the beautiful and enchanting Lord Sai who is verily Lord Rama.
