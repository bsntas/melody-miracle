## Lyrics

Nanda Kishora Navaneetha Chora
Narayana Hari Madhava
Madhusudhana Muralidhara
Gopi Jana Chitha Chora
Brindavana Sai Kanhaiya [ Narayana Hari ... ]

## Meaning

O Lord Krishna, the son of Nanda and the one who stole butter, You are the Lord in whom all beings take refuge. O Lord Vishnu, You are the Lord of Goddess Lakshmi. You play soulful music on the flute. You annihilated the demon Madhu. O Lord, You stole the hearts of the Gopis, You are the child of Brindavan and the one who has incarnated as Lord Sai.
