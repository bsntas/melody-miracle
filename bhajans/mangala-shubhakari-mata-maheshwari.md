## Lyrics

Mangala Shubhakari Mata Maheshwari
Ambe Bhavani Akhilandeshwari [ Mangala Shubhakari ... ]
Rajeeva Lochani Raja Rajeshwari
Adi Parashakthi Sri Parameshwari
Ananda Roopini Hey (Sai) Shiva Shankari

## Meaning

I propitiate Mother Maheshwari (Parvathi), who bestows auspiciousness, Mother Bhavani, the mother of the universe, the lotus-eyed Raja Rajeshwari and Shiva Shankari who is the personification of the primordial energy and eternal bliss. Glory to Lord Sai who is verily the Divine Mother.
