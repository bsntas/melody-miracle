## Lyrics

Madhava Gopala
Nanda Nandana Ghana Shyama Gopala [ Madhava ... ]
Shyama Sundara Brindavana Lola
Murali Manohara Giridhara Bala
Jaya Hari Govinda Radhe Gopala (3)

## Meaning

Sing the glory of Lord Krishna, the dark complexioned son of Nanda and dear consort of Radha. With His enchanting form with the Flute in hand, He played in Brindavan. He held aloft the Govardhana mountain to protect His devotees. Victory be to Lord Hari and Govinda.
