## Lyrics

Va Va Murugaiyya Vadivel Azhaga
Ka Va Va Kumara Thiru Karthikeya [ Va Va ... ]
Singara Vela Shiva Shakthi Bala

Va Va Muruga Vadivel Azhaga (6)

## Meaning

Come O beautiful Lord Muruga, protect us, Lord Karthikeya, the youthful son of Shiva and Shakthi, and the bearer of the divine spear. You are verily Lord Sathya Sai, who enjoys divine music.
