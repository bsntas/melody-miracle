## Lyrics

Tum Ho Vighna Vinasha Ganesha (3)
Parvathi Nandana Paahi Gajanana
Pranava Swaroopa Parthi Pureesha
Paahi Ganesha Vighna Vinasha

## Meaning

Protect me Oh Lord Ganesha, the destroyer of all obstacles, the son of Parvathi, the embodiment of primordial sound "OM" and the Lord of Parthi.
