## Lyrics

Deva Deva Mahadeva Sai Shankara
Sai Shankara Shirdeeshwara
Bhasmodbhavakara Partheeshwara
Swarna Varna Tejonmaya Jagadeeshwara
Sarva Mantra Swaroopa Sayeeshwara
Vamadeva Virupaksha Vishweshwara

## Meaning

God of gods O Mahadeva, You are Sai Shankara, the resident of Shirdi. You are Partheeshwara, who creates the sacred and divine ash. You are the effulgent God of the universe. You are Sayeeshwara, Vama Deva, Virupaksha and Vishveshwara who are the embodiment of all mantras.
