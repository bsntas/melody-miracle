## Lyrics

Jai Jai Jai Jai Ganapathi Deva (3)
Mata Parvathi Pitha Mahadeva (2) [ Jai Jai ... ]
Gajanana Gajanana
Gajanana Hey Ganapathi Deva

## Meaning

Glory to the elephant-faced Lord of Ganaas (Shiva's tribe) whose Mother is Parvathi and Father Mahadeva (Lord Shiva).
