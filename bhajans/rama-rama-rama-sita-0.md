## Lyrics

Rama Rama Rama Sita Ramachandra Paahi Maam
Rama Krishna Govinda Harey Paahi Maam
Krishna Krishna Gopala Vasudeva Paahi Maam [ Rama Krishna ... ]
Hara Hara Gangadhara Neelakanta Paahi Maam [ Rama Krishna ... ]

## Meaning

Protect me O Lord Rama, the consort of Sita. Protect me O Lord Krishna, the son of Vasudeva and the protector of cows. Protect me O Lord Shiva, the blue-throated one who holds the river Ganges on His head. Protect me O Lord Rama, Krishna and Govinda.
