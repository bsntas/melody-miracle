## Lyrics

Sarva Naama Swaroopam Sai Baba
Sarva Hrudaya Nivasam Sai Baba [ Sarva Naama ... ]
Sarva Daivatva Swaroopam Sai Baba
Sarva Dharma Virajam Sai Baba
Om Brahma Sanathana Sai Baba

## Meaning

O Lord Sai, You are the personification of all Names (of Gods), You live in everyone's hearts, You are the reflection of all pervading divinity, You are pure and You symbolize all faiths. O Lord Sai, You are the eternal Supreme Conscience.
