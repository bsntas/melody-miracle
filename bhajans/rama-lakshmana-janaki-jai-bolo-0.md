## Lyrics

Rama Lakshmana Janaki Jai Bolo Hanuman Ki (4)

## Meaning

Victory to Lord Rama, Lakshmana, Sita. Victory to Hanuman (monkey devotee of Lord Rama) and Lord Sai Rama.
