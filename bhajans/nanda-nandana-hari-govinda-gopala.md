## Lyrics

Nanda Nandana Hari Govinda Gopala
Ghana Shyama Mana Mohana (2)
Gopi Lola Gopala
Gokula Bala Gopala
Hey Nandalala Gopala [ Ghana Shyama ]

## Meaning

The son of Nanda, Oh Vishnu! You are the protector of cows. You are the blue colored Lord resembling the clouds and the One who enchants the mind. You are the favorite of the devout cowherd girls. The cowherd boy who is the beloved child of the cowherd abode Gokula and the loving child of Nanda.
