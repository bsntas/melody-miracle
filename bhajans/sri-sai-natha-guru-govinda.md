## Lyrics

Sri Sai Natha Guru Govinda
Parthi Pureeshwara Jaya Govinda
Prashanthi Nilaya Radhey Govinda
Sri Guru Jaya Guru Vittala Govinda

## Meaning

Sing the name of Lord Sai who is our Guru, the Lord Govinda who resides in Parthi/Prashanthi Nilayam, the Lord of Radha and also known by the name of Vittala.
