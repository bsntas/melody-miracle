## Lyrics

Gowri Nandana Gajanana
Gangadhara Hara Shambho Nandana
Parama Sumangala Mooshika Vahana
Gaana Vilolana Gajanana

## Meaning

O Lord Ganesha, the elephant-faced one, You are the beloved son of Mother Gowri (Parvathi) and Lord Shiva, who adorns the Ganges on His head blissfully. You are the supremely auspicious Lord who rides the mouse (Mooshika) and who loves music.
