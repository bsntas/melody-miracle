## Lyrics

Jaya Jaya Jaya Jaga Vandini Ma
Jaya Paramesha Hrudvasini Ma [ Jaya Jaya ... ]
Adi Shakthi Parabrahma Swaroopini
Jagat Janani Chathur Veda Vilasini
Samba Vinodini Mata Bhavani
Sambhavi Shankari Moksha Pradayini

## Meaning

Salutations to the Divine Mother who is adored by the entire Universe, the indweller and embodiment of Supreme Energy, the one whom the four Vedas describe and the consort of Lord Shiva. She confers liberation and is known by many names such as Bhavani, Srikari, Shankari and Sambhavi.
