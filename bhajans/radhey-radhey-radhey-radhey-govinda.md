## Lyrics

Radhey Radhey Radhey Radhey Govinda

## Meaning

Let us sing in praise of Lord Krishna and Radha. Praise be to Lord Krishna who is the protector and sustainer, whose music from the flute enchants us. He is Madhava, the Lord of Lakshmi.
