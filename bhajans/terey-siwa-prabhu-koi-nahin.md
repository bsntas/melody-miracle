## Lyrics

Terey Siwa Prabhu Koi Nahin Hai Tujko Mera Pranam
Tujko Mera Pranam O Sai Baba Tujko Mera Pranam
Murali Manohara Asha Na Todo
Dukh Bhanjana Mera Saath Na Chodo
Dwar Khada Hoon Mein Dukhiyara
Sun Ley Meri Pukar O Sai Baba Sun Ley Meri Pukar

## Meaning

O Lord Sai, I have none other than You, please accept my salutations. O beautiful Lord Krishna, do not disappoint me. O the destroyer of sorrows, do not leave me. I am standing at Your door with sorrowful mind. Please listen to my cries, O Lord Sai Baba.
