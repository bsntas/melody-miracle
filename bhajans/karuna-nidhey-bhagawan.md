## Lyrics

Karuna Nidhey Bhagawan

Mana Mohana Madhava Sai Bhagawan [ Karuna Nidhey ... ]
Meera Ke Prabhu Giridhara Bala

Parthi Puri Bhagawan [ Mana Mohana ... ]
Karuna Nidhey Bhagawan (3)

## Meaning

Glory to the enchanting Lord Gopala (Krishna), the Lord of Meera. He moves about in Brindavan and is extremely compassionate. Lord Sai who is verily Krishna, is an ocean of Mercy and His abode is at Puttaparthi.
