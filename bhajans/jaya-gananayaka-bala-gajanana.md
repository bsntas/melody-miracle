## Lyrics

Jaya Gananayaka Bala Gajanana
Hey Shiva Nandana Paahi Gajanana [ Jaya Gananayaka ... ]
Prathama Poojya Sri Natha Gajanana
Mooshika Vahana Hey Shubhanana
Yuga Yuga Vanditha Paahi Gajanana

## Meaning

Victory to Lord Ganesh, the leader of the Shiva's army (Ganas). Protect us O Lord Gajanana, the son of Lord Shiva. Riding on the Mouse, You are the auspicious one who is worshipped first and who has been worshipped for eons.
