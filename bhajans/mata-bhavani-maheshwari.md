## Meaning

Chant the name of Mother Bhavani, Maheshwari, who is none other than our Mother Sai. She is the goddess of all goddesses. She is the Ocean of Grace (Krupa Sagari), She is the Cause (Kaarini), She is the Fulfillment (Poorani), She is the one who sustains the Universe (Dharini), Mother Bhavani is the destroyer of evil.
