## Lyrics

Jaya Jaya Gajanana Gananatha
Gowri Nandana Hey Gananatha [ Jaya Jaya ... ]
Buddhi Pradayaka Hey Gananatha
Siddhi Vinayaka Hey Gananatha
Jaya Gananatha Sai Gananatha (4)

## Meaning

Sing the praises of the elephant-faced Lord, the Leader of the demigods (Ganas), son of Gowri, who confers intelligence and spiritual powers.
