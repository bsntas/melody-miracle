## Meaning

Chant the name of Lord Govinda (Krishna) who wears a garland of forest flowers, who lifted the Govardhana hill and the beloved of Radha. He is beautiful with dark-blue complexion and is none other than Lord Narayana. He is like the Mandara flower in the hearts of His devotees, effulgent like a million Suns, the son of Nanda and one among the cowherds of Brindavan.
