## Meaning

O Lord Sai, You incarnate through different ages as Rama, Krishna and as Sai Baba. The devotees worship You by different names as Allah, Eshwara (Shiva), Nanak, Buddha, Zohrashtra, Mahavira, Ram and Rahim.
