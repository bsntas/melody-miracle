## Lyrics

Jaya Sayeesha Jaya Sayeesha
Radha Madhava Jaya Partheesha [ Jaya Sayeesha ... ]
Hrudaya Sarathi Sai Kanhaiya
Radhey Govinda Prashanthi Nilaya

## Meaning

Victory to Lord Sai, who is verily Lord Krishna incarnated again on Earth, who is the charioteer of the Spiritual Heart and dwells at Prashanthi Nilayam in Parthi.
