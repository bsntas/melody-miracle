## Lyrics

Hari Guru Natha Prabhu Guru Natha Partheesha Natha 
Sri Sathya Sai Guru Natha
Talli Tandriyu Neevey Kadaa Daari Chupe Guru Devudu Kadaa
Narayana Govinda Mukunda Maaku Prapanchamu Neevey Kadaa
Nanda Sunanda Nanda Mukunda Brindavana Chandha
Sri Sathya Sai Guru Natha

## Meaning

Let us worship Sri Sathya Sai as our preceptor, the one who is both Mother and Father, the Divine Master who guides on our path of Spiritual progress. There is no other world for us except You dear Lord Sai. You are verily Lord Krishna, the dear child of Nanda. You are none other than Narayana glorified by the names Govinda and Mukunda, the pleasing child of Brindavana.
