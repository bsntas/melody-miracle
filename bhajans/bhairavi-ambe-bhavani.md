## Lyrics

Bhairavi Ambe Bhavani (Amba)
Jagadambe Ma Jagat Janani [ Amba Bhairavi ... ]
Narayani Patitha Pavani
Parthi Purandari Sai Janani

## Meaning

Glory be to Lord Sai of Puttaparthi, the Mother Goddess, who is the source, support and mother of the Universe. She is the savior of the fallen.
