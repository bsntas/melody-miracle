## Lyrics

Bhaja Mana Sita Ram

Bhajo Bhajo Parthi Baba Naam (Bhajo)

## Meaning

O mind, meditate and sing the glory of Mother Sita and Lord Rama of Raghu dynasty. O mind, chant incessantly the name of Lord Sai, the Lord of Parthi who is none other than Lord Rama.
