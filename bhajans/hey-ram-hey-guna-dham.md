## Lyrics

Hey Ram Hey Guna Dham

Jaya Jaya Ram Jaya Sai Ram

## Meaning

Glory to Lord Rama, the repository of virtues, the most beautiful one, the descendant of the Raghu dynasty and the very life of Mother Janaki (Sita). Lord Rama is the crest jewel of Ayodhya and the son of Mother Kausalya and King Dasharatha. Victory to Lord Rama and Lord Sai who is verily Lord Rama.
