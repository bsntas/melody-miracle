## Lyrics

Mata Saraswathi
Vidya Dayini Veena Paani [ (Jagan) Mata ... ]

Veda Mata Gayathri
Premamayi Sathya Sai Mayi

## Meaning

O Mother Saraswathi, playing divine music with the Veena, You are the Mother of the whole world. Adorned in white, You are the embodiment of music, full of consciousness and is permanent. You are the Mother of the Vedas and the bestower of Knowledge. You are none other than Sathya Sai who is full of Love.
