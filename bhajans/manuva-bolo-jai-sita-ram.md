## Lyrics

Manuva Bolo Jai Sita Ram Bolo Jai Radhey Shyam
Jai Sita Ram Bolo Jai Radhey Shyam (3)

## Meaning

Let us chant and meditate on Lord Rama whose consort is Mother Sita, and Lord Shyam (Krishna), the savior of Mother Radha. Victory to Lord Rama and Lord Krishna.
