## Lyrics

Shambho Mahadeva Mallikarjuna
Mangala Charana Trilochana
Pinaka Paani Parvathi Ramana [ Shambho Mahadeva ... ]
Bhava Bhaya Harana Sanathana
Parthi Pureeshwara Patitha Pavana

## Meaning

Our salutations at the sacred feet of Lord Shiva, (also known as Shambho, Mahadeva and Mallikarjuna) who wields the Bow (Pinaka), who is the consort of Parvathi, who destroys the fear of materialistic world and who is none other than Lord Sai of Parthi.
