## Lyrics

Amba Bhavani Shiva Shambhu Kumara
Sri Gananatha Shanthakara
Purana Purusha Sathya Sai Natha
Pranava Swaroopa Partheesha

## Meaning

O Lord Ganesha, beloved son of Shiva and Mother Parvathi, You are the embodiment of peace. O Lord Sathya Sai, You are the one adored in ancient Holy scriptures and You are the very form of the primordial sound Om.
