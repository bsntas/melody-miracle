## Lyrics

Bada Chitha Chora Brindavana Sanchara
Gopala Gopala Hey Murali Gopala
Govardhanodhara Gopala Bala
Gopi Manohara Radhey Gopala

## Meaning

Chant the name of Lord Gopala (Krishna), who played in Brindavan, who lifted the Govardhana mountain and who captivated the hearts of Radha and the Gopis.
