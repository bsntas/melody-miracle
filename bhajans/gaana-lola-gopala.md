## Lyrics

Gaana Lola Gopala
Sai Manohara Gopala [ Gaana Lola ... ]
Jagadoddhara Parthi Vihara
Meera Ke Prabhu Giridhara Bala

Gopala Sai Gopala (5)

## Meaning

Glory to Lord Gopala (Krishna), the beautiful one who enjoys music, the liberator of the universe, the child who lifted the Govardhana hill and the Lord of Meera. Glory to Lord Sai residing at Parthi who is verily Lord Gopala.
