## Lyrics

Pandari Nivasa Hey Panduranga
Hey Parthi Vasa Sri Sai Deva [ Pandari Nivasa ... ]
Deena Rakshaka Anatha Rakshaka ( Sai )
Bhaktha Vatsala Hey Sayeeshwara (2)

## Meaning

Sing the glory of the Lord of Pandharpur, Panduranga. He is the resident of Parthi, Lord Sai, who is the protector of the weak, the orphaned and the under privileged. He is the Lord who always takes care of His devotees.
