## Lyrics

Sri Rama Jaya Rama
Daasharathey Jaya Raghu Rama
Kalyana Rama Kodanda Rama
Sita Rama Sri Raghu Rama
Sri Ram Jaya Ram
Sri Rama Jaya Rama Sathya Sai Rama

## Meaning

Victory to Lord Rama of Raghu dynasty, the son of Dasharatha and the consort of Mother Sita. Victory to the one who wields the Kodanda bow and who bestows welfare on all. Victory to Lord Sathya Sai who is verily Lord Rama.
