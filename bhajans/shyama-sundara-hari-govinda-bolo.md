## Lyrics

Shyama Sundara Hari Govinda Bolo
Govinda Bolo Hari Gopala Bolo [ Shyama Sundara ... ]

## Meaning

Chant the various names of Lord Krishna - Hari and Govinda. Glory to the beautiful, dark-hued, enchanting Lord with lotus petal eyes and who is dear to Radha.
