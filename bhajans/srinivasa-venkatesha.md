## Meaning

Sing the names of Lord Srinivasa, Venkatesha (names of Lord Vishnu) and Lord Sayeesha who protects all those who have surrendered to Him. Glory to Lord Govinda who resides in the Tirumala hills and the one who rides on Garuda.
