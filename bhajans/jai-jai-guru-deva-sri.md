## Lyrics

Jai Jai Guru Deva Sri Sai Mahadeva (3)
Brahma Vishnu Maheshwara Sai Mahadeva
Mata Pitha Guru Deva Sri Sai Mahadeva

## Meaning

Glory to the revered preceptor Mahadeva, who is the embodiment of Mother, Father, Preceptor and the Trinity Brahma, Vishnu and Maheshwara.
