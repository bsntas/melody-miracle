## Lyrics

Vittala Jai Vittala Jai Rakumayi Vittala

## Meaning

Victory to the Lord of Pandari, Lord Vittala, who waited patiently for His devotee Pundalika. Victory to that Pundarikaksha. Recite the name of Lord Panduranga and Vittala. Victory to Vittala (Vishnu), the Lord of Rakumayi (Rukmini).
