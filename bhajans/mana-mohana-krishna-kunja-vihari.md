## Meaning

O Lord Krishna, You are the charmer of our hearts, You reside in a garden (Kunja means creepers and vine, and here, it is a reference to Brindavan), You held aloft the Govardhana hill and You destroyed the demon Mura. O Madhava, You were born in the Yadava clan, and is verily Lord Vittala and Ranga and the one who has the beautiful complexion of dark rain bearing clouds.
