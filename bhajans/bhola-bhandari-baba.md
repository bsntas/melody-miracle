## Lyrics

Bhola Bhandari Baba Shiva Shiva Shiva Sai Baba
Anatha Rakshaka Deena Dayala Patitha Pavana Sai Baba [ Bhola Bhandari ... ]
Yogeshwara Sai Murari Yogeshwara Hey Tripurari
Nithyananda Brahmananda Premananda Sai Baba

## Meaning

O Lord Sai Baba, You are the Lord who can be pleased easily, You are the treasure of mercy and prosperity, You are the supporter of the orphans, protector and the refuge to the destitute and You uplift them and make them sacred. O Lord of the Yogis, O Sai Murari, Tripurari, the destroyer of three worlds, You are the embodiment of eternal, supreme bliss and love.
