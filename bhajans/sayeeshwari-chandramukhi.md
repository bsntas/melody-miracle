## Lyrics

Sayeeshwari Chandramukhi
Sharanagatha Paripalini
Shashwata Ananda Dayini
Sharanam Sharanam Jaganmayi

## Meaning

Glory to you Oh Mother Sayeeshwari who is as resplendent and beautiful as the moon, to the one who showers on all of us eternal bliss and joy. We Offer our humble surrender to you Oh mother Sai.
