## Lyrics

Bhasma Vibhushitha Bhavani Shankara
Bhava Bhaya Nasha Bhola Shankara
Hara Hara Shankara Samba Shiva
Hara Hara Shankara Sai Shiva
Om Namah Shivaya Shivaya Namah Om (3)

## Meaning

Worship Lord Shankara, the Lord of Bhavani(Parvathi), who has sacred ashes smeared all over the body. The easy-to-please Lord Shankara destroys the fear of the cycle of birth and death. Chant the mantra 'Om Namah Shivaya'.
