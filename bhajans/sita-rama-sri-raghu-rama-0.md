## Lyrics

Sita Rama Sri Raghu Rama
Ananda Roopa Hey Guna Dhama
Dasharatha Nandana Janaki Rama
Parthi Vihara Sri Sai Rama
Madhuvana Ramana Hey Ghana Shyama
Raasa Vilola Radhey Shyama

## Meaning

Glory to the consort of Sita, Lord Rama, of Raghu dynasty, the embodiment of bliss, the abode of good qualities. the son of Dasharatha and the consort of Mother Janaki (Sita). Glory to the dark-hued Lord Krishna of Madhuvana, who danced with the Gopis and who is the Lord of Radha. Lord Sai of Parthi is verily Rama and Krishna.
