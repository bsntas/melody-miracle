## Lyrics

Naam Bhajo Hari Naam Bhajo
Narayana Sai Narayana [ Naam Bhajo ... ]
Nanda Ananda Mukunda Harey (2)
Brindavana Sai Baba Harey
Baba Harey Sai Baba Harey (3)

## Meaning

Chant the name of Lord Hari who incarnated as Lord Krishna, the adorable son of Nanda in Brindavan. Lord Sai Baba is none other than Lord Hari.
