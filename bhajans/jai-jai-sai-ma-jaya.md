## Lyrics

Jai Jai Sai Ma Jaya Sai Saraswathi Ma
Veena Vadini Ma Vidya Daayini Ma [ Jai Jai ... ]
Mukthi Pradayini Ma Shakthi Pradayini Ma [ Veena Vadini ... ]

## Meaning

Victory to Mother Sai who is Mother Saraswathi Herself, the One who bestows Knowledge and the One who plays on the Veena (stringed instrument). Mother Saraswathi gives strength and grants Liberation.
