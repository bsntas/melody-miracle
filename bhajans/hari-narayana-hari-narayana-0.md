## Lyrics

Hari Narayana Hari Narayana
Nithya Niranjana Sai Narayana
Narahari Roopa Namo Narayana
Rajeeva Lochana Sai Narayana

## Meaning

Glory to Lord Narayana, the eternally pure one who incarnated as Lord Narasimha. Salutations to the lotus-eyed Lord Sai who is verily Lord Narayana.
