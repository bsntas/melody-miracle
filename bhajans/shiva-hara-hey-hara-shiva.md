## Lyrics

Shiva Hara Hey Hara Shiva Hey
Samba Sadashiva Shiva Hara Hey
Hara Hara Hara Hara Mahadeva
Parvathi Ramana Sadashiva
Namami Shankara Tava Charanam
Bhajami Shankara Tava Charanam

## Meaning

We offer our obeisance at the feet of Lord Shiva who is worshipped as Sadashiva, Mahadeva and as the consort of Mother Parvathi.
