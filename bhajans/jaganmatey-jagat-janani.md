## Lyrics

Jaganmatey Jagat Janani
Jaya Jagadambe Devi Bhavani
Devi Saraswathi Durga Lakshmi
Mahishasura Samhaarini Mata

## Meaning

Victory to the Divine Mother who nourishes the entire Universe. You are verily Mother Saraswathi, Durga and Lakshmi and the one who removed demon Mahishasura from the face of the Earth.
