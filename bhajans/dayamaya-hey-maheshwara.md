## Lyrics

Dayamaya Hey Maheshwara
Krupamaya Hey Sadashiva
Dama Dama Damaru Maheshwara
Damaruka Natha Sadashiva
Hari Om Hari Om Sadashiva

## Meaning

Hey Maheshwara (Shiva) you are full of compassion. You are also Shiva the embodiment of auspiciousness show us your kindness who loves the sound of damaru (drum) dama, dama. . chant Hari Om.
