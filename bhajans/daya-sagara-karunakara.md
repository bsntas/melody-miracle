## Meaning

Oh Lord Sai, you are the ocean of compassion and mercy. Oh Lord of the entire creation, Oh Sai eshwara You are the Lord of the entire world, You are the master of the entire world and the entire Universe Oh Lord Sai, You are the embodiment of all virtues qualities.
