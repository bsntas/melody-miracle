## Lyrics

Raksha Raksha Jagadeeshwara
Parthi Puri Parameshwara
Paahi Paahi Parameshwara
Dehi Dehi Tava Pada Seva

## Meaning

Protect us, O Lord of the Universe, Lord Sai. Protect us, O Lord of Parthi and bless us, O Supreme Lord. We pray to Thee to grant us the opportunity to serve at Thy Lotus Feet.
