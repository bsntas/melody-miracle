## Meaning

O Lord Narayana, You are the Lord Sai who is the incarnation of Hari. You are the embodiment of peace, You recline on the serpent Adi Sesha. You have a Lotus emerging from Your navel (symbolizes creation arising from the Lord). O Lord Sai! O Lord Narayana! Salutations to You.
