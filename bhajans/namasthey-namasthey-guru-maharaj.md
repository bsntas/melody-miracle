## Lyrics

Namasthey Namasthey Guru Maharaj

Sharanam Sharanam Sri Sayeesha

## Meaning

Oh King of Preceptors! I bow down to You. You are the Eternal Preceptor Sathya Sai. I surrender to You Oh Sai! I offer reverential prostration with all the eight limbs to You, Oh Lord of the Lords!
