## Lyrics

Rama Naama Tarakam Sada Bhajorey
Sada Bhajorey Sada Japo Rey [ Rama Naama ... ]
Rama Rama Rama Jaya Kodanda Rama

Rama Rama Rama Jaya Kalyana Rama

## Meaning

Incessantly worship Lord Rama who helps attain liberation. Victory to Lord Rama, who wields the 'Kodanda' bow. Victory to Lord Rama, the crowned King. Victory to Lord Rama, the bestower of auspiciousness.
