## Lyrics

Hey Rama Dootha Hey Rama Bhaktha Hey Rama Daasa Namo Namo
Sita Mata Shoka Vinasha
Anatha Natha Namo Namo
Maruti Raya Mahanubhava
Ati Balavanta (Veera Anjaneya) Namo Namo

## Meaning

Salutations to Hanuman, the messenger, devotee and the servant of Lord Rama. Salutations to the one who destroyed Mother Sita's agony. Salutations to Maruti, the guide to the orphaned, the Supreme being and the brave one who possesses magnificent strength.
