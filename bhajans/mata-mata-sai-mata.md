## Lyrics

Mata Mata Sai Mata
Jagatha Mata Sai Vishwa Mata [ Mata Mata ... ]
Durga Bhavani Kaali Kapalini
Shanthi Dayini Sai Mata

## Meaning

Mother Sai is the mother of the Universe. She is known by names like Durga, Bhavani, and Kali. She is the bestower of peace.
