## Meaning

Chant the mantra "Om Namah Shivaya". I bow down to Lord Shiva, who confers auspiciousness, who is also Hara, who destroys the evil. He is the Great God - Mahadeva, the destroyer of evil, who plays the hand-held drum, Damaru, while the percussion instrument, Mrudanga, keeps the beat, as He dances the cosmic dance.
