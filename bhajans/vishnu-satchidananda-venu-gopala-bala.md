## Lyrics

Vishnu Satchidananda Venu Gopala Bala
Krishna Rama Govinda Hari Hari (3)

## Meaning

Chant the various names of the Lord Vishnu, namely Rama, Govinda, Hari and the sweet child Krishna holding the flute in His hand, who are all the embodiments of Being (Sath), Awareness (Chith) and Bliss (Ananda).
