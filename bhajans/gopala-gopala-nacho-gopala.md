## Lyrics

Gopala Gopala Nacho Gopala
Nacho Nacho Sai Nandalala
Rhuma Jhuma Rhuma Jhuma Nacho Gopala
Nacho Nacho Sai Nandalala

## Meaning

Please dance and give us the enjoyment of seeing you dance - Gopala and son of Nanda. While you dance, let us hear the sound of your anklets "Rhuma Jhuma".
