## Lyrics

Jaya Guru Omkara

## Meaning

Victory to Sadguru Sai, the embodiment of the sacred sound Pranava. You are the verily the Lords Allah, Jesus, Buddha, Zohrashtra and Mahavir. You are the Master and the Supreme Preceptor of all of us.
