## Lyrics

Adi Narayana Sai Narayana
Jaya Hari Narayana Jaya Jaya
Pandari Vasa Hari Narayana
Parthi Nivasa Sai Narayana
Varada Narayana Veda Narayana [ Jaya Hari ... ]

## Meaning

Victory to Lord Sai who is verily Lord Narayana, the primordial one. O Lord, You are the resident of Pandari and the Lord of Parthi. Victory to Lord Narayana who confers boons and who is the source of the Vedas.
