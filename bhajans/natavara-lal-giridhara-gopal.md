## Lyrics

Natavara Lal Giridhara Gopal
Meera Prabhu Ghana Shyama Gopal [ Natavara Lal ... ]
Veda Saara Jeevan Ka Aadhaar
Murali (Gopi) Manohara Hrudaya Vihar

## Meaning

Sing the glory of the mischievous child Lord Krishna, the protector of Gokula, the one who lifted the Govardhana mountain, the one who stole the pure hearts of Gopis. He is the Lord of Meera, whose heart was filled with pure love for the dark-hued Gopala. He is the the essence of all Vedas and the life force for us. We bow to Lord Gopala who enchants us with the flute and who always resides in the pure hearts of the cowherd, Gopis.
