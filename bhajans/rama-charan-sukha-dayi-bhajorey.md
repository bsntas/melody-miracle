## Lyrics

Rama Charan Sukha Dayi Bhajorey (3)
Ram Naam Ke Do Akshara Mey
Sab Sukh Shanthi Samayirey
Sai Nath Ke Charnon Mey Aakar
Jeevan Saphal Banaorey

## Meaning

Worshipping the Lotus Feet of Lord Ram endows one with happiness. Reciting only the two letters Ra and Ma, confers everlasting peace and happiness. Finish the mission of human birth by surrendering to the Lotus Feet of Lord Sai (by merging in Lord Sai) who is none other than Lord Rama.
