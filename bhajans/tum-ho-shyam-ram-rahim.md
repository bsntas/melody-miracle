## Lyrics

Tum Ho Shyam Ram Rahim (Baba)
Ram Rahim Shyama Rahim [ Tum Ho ... ]
Allah Yesu Buddha Mahavira
Nanak Sai Zohrashtra Bhi Ho
Sarva Dharma Priya Sai Narayana (Shankara)

## Meaning

You are the Lord of every religion, O Sai. Devotees adore You as Ram, Rahim, Allah, Yesu, Buddha, Mahavira, Nanak, Zohrashtra, Narayana and Shankara.
