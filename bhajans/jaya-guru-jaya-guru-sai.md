## Meaning

Prostrations to Lord Sathya Sai Rama, the Divine Preceptor of the Universe. He is the Mother, Father, Preceptor as well as Creator (Brahma), Sustainer (Vishnu), Destroyer (Shiva) and is the very embodiment of the Supreme One.
