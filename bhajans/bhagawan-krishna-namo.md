## Lyrics

Bhagawan Krishna Namo Bhagawan Krishna Namo
Patitha Pavana Patitha Palana
Ramachandra Namo [ Bhagawan Krishna ... ]
Kaalatheethaya Shirdi Roopaya Sathya Sai Namo
Antaryami Namo Sarvantaryami Namo

## Meaning

Salutations to Lord Krishna and to Lord Rama, who is the saviour and uplifter of the afflicted and miserable. Salutations to Lord Sathya Sai who is above and beyond time and is the very embodiment of all virtues and success. Salutations to Lord Shiva who sustains the entire creation. Salutations to the omniscient Lord.
