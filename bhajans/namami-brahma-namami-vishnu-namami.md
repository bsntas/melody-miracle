## Lyrics

Namami Brahma Namami Vishnu Namami Maheshwara
Smarami Brahma Smarami Vishnu
Smarami Sayeeshwara (2)

## Meaning

I salute Brahma, the God of Creation, prostrate to Lord Vishnu, the God of sustenance and worship Lord Shiva, the Lord of Destruction. I meditate on Brahma, contemplate on Vishnu and dwell upon Lord Sai.
