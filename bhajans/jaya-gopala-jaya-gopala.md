## Lyrics

Jaya Gopala Jaya Gopala
Jaya Radhey Govinda Gopala [ Jaya Gopala ... ]
Chinmayananda Hey Sai Gopala
Nithyananda Roopa Sai Gopala [ Jaya Radhey ... ]

## Meaning

Victory to Lord Gopala (Krishna), the consort of Radha. Victory to Lord Sai who is ever in the bliss of pure Consciousness and who is verily Lord Kirshna.
