## Lyrics

Namo Bhagawathey Rama Roopaya

Rama Rahim Krishna Karim Yesu Buddha Mahavir
Sarva Dharma Priya Sai Devaya
Sarva Dharma Priya Sai Roopaya

## Meaning

I bow down to the Lord in the form of Krishna, son of Vasudeva, I worship the Lord in the form of Rama, I prostrate the Lord in the form of Krishna, the star of Yadu dynasty or the blue-colored Lord Rama, the star of Raghu dynasty. He is Rahim and Karim (names of Allah), the merciful and compassionate one, Jesus Christ, Buddha and Mahavir. I bow down to Lord Sai who is fond of all faiths.
