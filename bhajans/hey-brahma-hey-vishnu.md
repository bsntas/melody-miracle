## Lyrics

Hey Brahma Hey Vishnu

## Meaning

Glory to Lord Sathya Sai who is the Creator, Sustainer and Destroyer and the Supreme Sovereign Lord who is full of compassion. Lord Sathya Sai is the Total Incarnation of the Supreme Energy.
