## Lyrics

Vinayaka Vinayaka
Vighna Vinashaka Vinayaka
Gowri Nandana Hey Shiva Nandana
Parvathi Nandana Gajanana
Mooshika Vahana Gajanana
Modaka Hasta Gajanana

## Meaning

Salutations to Lord Vinayaka (Lord Ganesha), the destroyer of obstacles, the elephant-faced son of Parvathi and Shiva, the one who rides on the mouse and the one who has Modaka (a type of sweet) in His hand.
