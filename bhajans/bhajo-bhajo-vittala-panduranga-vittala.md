## Lyrics

Bhajo Bhajo Vittala Panduranga Vittala
Pandari Natha Panduranga Bhajo Mana Vittala [ Bhajo Bhajo ... ]
Partheeshwara Sai Natha Vittala
Parameshwara Sathya Sai Vittala
Rakumayi Vittala Ranga Sai Vittala

## Meaning

Worship Lord Vittala of Pandharpur, Sai Vittala of Parthi who is the supreme Lord of the Universe and Lord of Rakumayi.
