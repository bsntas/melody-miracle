## Lyrics

Shaila Girishwara Uma Maheshwara Kashi Vishweshwara Sadashiva
Sadashiva Sadashiva
Sadashiva Shambho Sadashiva

## Meaning

Salutations to Lord Shiva who is the Lord of Kashi (also known as Banares) and the consort of Goddess Uma (Parvathi).
