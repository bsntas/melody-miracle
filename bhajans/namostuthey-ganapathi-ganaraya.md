## Meaning

I bow down to You, Lord Ganapathi (Ganesha), the Lord of the Ganaas. Salutations to the son of Lord Shiva and the one who has the mouse as His vehicle. I repeatedly bow down to You, O Lord Sai, You are verily the elephant-faced Lord Ganesha.
