## Lyrics

Sarva Dharma Priya Deva
Sathya Sai Deva
Allah Yesu Buddha Aur Nanak
Zohrashtra Mahavira Tum Ho
Rama Bhi Tum Ho Krishna Bhi Tum Ho
Vishwa Roopa Tum Ho (2)

## Meaning

Glory to Lord Sathya Sai who fosters all faiths and religions. He is verily the embodiment of Allah, Christ, Buddha, Nanak, Zohrashtra, Mahavira, Ram and Krishna.
