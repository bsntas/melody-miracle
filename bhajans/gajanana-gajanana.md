## Lyrics

Gajanana Gajanana
Prathama Poojana Ganaraya [ Gajanana ... ]
Prathama Prarthana Sri Ganaraya
Lambodhara Ganaraya

## Meaning

O Lord Ganesha, the elephant-faced one, You are the one whom all worship and pray. You are the one to whom the first prostrations should be offered. O Ganaraya, the controller of all the Ganaas (the Demigods), we offer You our first obeisance. O mighty one, You are the King of the Divine Forces.
