## Meaning

Salutations to Lord Shirdi Baba, the Lord of Dwarakamayi and to the Lord of Prashanthi, Sai Ram, the name so enthralling. He is none other than Allah and Eshwar (Shiva) and the Lord of Parthi. O Lord show mercy and protect us.
