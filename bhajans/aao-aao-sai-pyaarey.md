## Lyrics

Aao Aao Sai Pyaarey
Keertan Karoon Main Sai Tumharey [ Aao Aao ... ]
Tum Ho Merey Nayanon Ke Taarey

## Meaning

Welcome, O beloved Lord Sai, I sing Your glory. O Supreme Lord, You are the twinkling star of my eyes. O companion of my life, kindly grant us Your Divine Darshan.
