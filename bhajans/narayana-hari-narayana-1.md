## Meaning

Glory to Lord Narayana, the ultimate destination of all creation, O Lord, You took the form of the lion-faced Lord Narasimha. O resident of Vaikunta, prostrations to You. You were the favorite of Mother Sita when You incarnated as Rama. Salutations to You, O Lord Sai, You are the incarnation of Lord Vishnu.
