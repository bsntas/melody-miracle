## Meaning

O Sai, Lord of Parthi, You are the king of my mind (Chitha - the spiritual Heart). Your auspicious feet (when You incarnated as Lord Krishna) rambled the thick forests of Brindavan and made them auspicious. Your beautiful complexion captivates us and fills us with Love. O Lord of Parthi and King of my mind, You have a charming beautiful loving Lotus face. O holder of the Flute, You are the bestower of auspiciousness.
