## Meaning

Sing the praises of Lord Krishna, the very soul of His devotee Radha. Lord Krishna is the one adored by all, the darling son of Devaki and fostered by Nanda. He is the enchanter of the Gopikas. He has a beautiful form playing the flute. He is the slayer of the demon Madhu.
