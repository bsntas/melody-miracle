## Meaning

God of Gods Lord Ganesha, the elephant-faced one, You are the embodiment of Pranava, the primordial sound, You are the one who is worshipped by Gods and saints. We surrender at Your auspicious Feet, protect us, O Lord. We pray to You to bestow goodness and frees us from the miseries of the world.
