## Meaning

Chant the various names of Lord Krishna, the ever blissful and enchanting Flutist - Govinda, Gopala. Pray to Lord Krishna, the beautiful cowherd boy with dark-blue complexion like a cloud, the one with beautiful hair and who enchants the Gopikas. He is the Lord of Lakshmi. Victory to Lord Sai who is none other than Lord Krishna.
