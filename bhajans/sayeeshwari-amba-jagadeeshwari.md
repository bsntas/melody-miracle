## Lyrics

Sayeeshwari Amba Jagadeeshwari
Kalyani Kanchi Puri Kamakshi [ Sayeeshwari Amba ... ]
Partheeshwari Amba Parameshwari
Matangi Madura Puri Meenakshi

## Meaning

Salutations to Mother Sai whose abode is Parthi, who is the mother to this entire universe. She is also the presiding deity in Kanchi (by name Kamakshi) and Madurai (by name Meenakshi).
