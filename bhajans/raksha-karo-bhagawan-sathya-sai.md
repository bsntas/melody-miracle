## Meaning

Protect us, O Lord Sathya Sai, You are the compassionate and merciful Lord of the helpless, the bestower of truth, righteousness, peace and love, the ever - cheerful Lord who sustains all life, the embodiment of Brahma (Creator), Vishnu (Sustainer) and Maheshwara (Destroyer of evil). You are Truth, Auspiciousness and Beauty.
