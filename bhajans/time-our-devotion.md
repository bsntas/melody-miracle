## Lyrics

At this time of our devotion
Let there be no barriers
Cast them away, Lord Ganesha (2x)
Bless us with your loving grace
Oh Lord with the elephant face
May out hearts be filled with joy
Singing out in love for Sai
Bless us, bless us, Sai Ganesha (2x)
