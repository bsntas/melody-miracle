## Lyrics

Eshwar Allah Terey Naam Sai Terey Naam
Sarva Dharma Priya Sai Ram Sathya Sai Ram
Allah Sai Maulah Sai
Yesu Buddha Sai Hey Mahavir Sai
Govinda Sai Gopala Sai Nanak Sai [ Sarva Dharma ... ]

## Meaning

O Lord Sai, Eshwara is your name, so is the name Allah (the names Eshwar and Allah, both reach You, O Lord Sai). O Sathya Sai Rama, You are fond of all the faiths, You are Allah and You are Maulah. O Sai, You are Jesus Christ, Buddha, Mahavir. You are Govinda, Gopala, Guru Nanak. You are fond of all the faiths (all the Faiths lead to You).
