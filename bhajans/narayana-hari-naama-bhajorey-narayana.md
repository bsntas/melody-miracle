## Lyrics

Narayana Hari Naama Bhajorey Narayana Veda Parayana
Karuna Bharana Bhava Bhaya Harana
Pavana Charana Patitha Janavana
Narayana Hari (Sai) Narayana (3)

## Meaning

Sing the name of Lord Narayana and Hari, the ultimate shelter for all creatures. He is the Lord of the Vedas who is adorned with compassion. He removes the fear of the mundane world. His Feet are the most sacred that redeems the downtrodden. He has incarnated as Lord Sai.
