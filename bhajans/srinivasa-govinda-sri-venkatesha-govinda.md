## Meaning

Chant the divine names Srinivasa, Govinda, Venkatesha - various names of Lord Balaji of Tirupati. He is Lord Narayana, the one who is the Lord of great Epics (Puranas) and the one who has taken ten incarnations. He is verily Lord Sai, who is the same Lord who incarnated as Rama and Krishna.
