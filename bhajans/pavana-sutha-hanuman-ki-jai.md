## Lyrics

Pavana Sutha Hanuman Ki Jai
Sita Pathi (Siyavar) Ramachandra Ki Jai [ Pavana Sutha ... ]
Parakrama Anjaneya Ki Jai (2)
Parthi Puri Sai Nath Ki Jai (2)

## Meaning

Victory to Hanuman, the son of the wind God, victory to the chosen Lord of Sita, Lord Ram, victory to the mighty son of Anjana Devi, victory to the Lord of Parthi, Sai Nath.
