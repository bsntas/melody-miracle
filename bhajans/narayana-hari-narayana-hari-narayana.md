## Lyrics

Narayana Hari Narayana Hari Narayana Veda Parayana
Shambho Mahadeva Shambho Mahadeva Shambho Mahadeva Sadashiva

## Meaning

Glory to Lord Narayana, the author of Vedas. Glory to Lord Shiva who is ever blissful and who bestows welfare.
