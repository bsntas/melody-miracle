## Lyrics

Jaya Jaya Devi Girija Mata
Jaya Jagadambe Pranava Swaroopini
Ashtabhujankita Akhila Dharini
Jaya Yogeshwara Hrudaya Nivasini

## Meaning

Praises be to the Universal Mother Girija, the embodiment of the Primordial Sound Om. She is the one who has eight arms and the one who bears the universe. She is the one who resides in the heart of Lord Shiva.
