## Lyrics

Jai Jai Bhavani Ma Ambe Bhavani Ma
Ambe Bhavani Ma Sai Bhavani Ma [ Jai Jai Bhavani ... ]
Shirdi Nivasi Ma Parthi Nivasi Ma
Hrudaya Nivasi Ma Ambe (Sai) Bhavani Ma

## Meaning

Victory to Divine Mother Parvathi who is hailed as Ambe and Bhavani and who is the indweller of the heart. She is none other than Lord Sai who dwells in Shirdi and Parthi.
