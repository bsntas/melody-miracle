## Meaning

O Lord Sri Sai, You are Supreme among Gods, the protector of the afflicted. Oh Lord of all Lords, You are the embodiment of compassion, You are the embodiment of mercy, the uplifter of the fallen and the destitute, O the Supreme Lord who moves about in Parthi, the Creator of Bhasma, the sacred ash and the remover of the fear of the bondage of cycle of birth and death, glory be to Thee.
