## Lyrics

Sri Raghu Nandana Dasharatha Nandana
Bhadradeeshwara Rama
Vaidehi Priya Vaikunta Vasa
Sada Smarami Ramethi Rama

## Meaning

Chant incessantly the holy name of Rama from Raghu Dynasty. He is the son of King Dasharatha and who is the presiding deity in Bhadrachalam (a holy place). He is the beloved of Vaidehi (Sita) and the one who resides in Vaikunta (the abode of Lord Vishnu).
