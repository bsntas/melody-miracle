## Lyrics

## Meaning

O Lord Shiva, the one who holds the Holy Ganges in Your locks of hair, shower Your compassion on us. O Lord who destroyed the city of Tripura, shower Your mercy on us. Your sweet name bestows all auspiciousness. O the imperceptible and faultless one, the holder of the trident, O the easy-to-please Lord, shower Your mercy on us.
