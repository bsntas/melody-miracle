## Lyrics

Sai Deva Sri Ganesha
Vande Vighna Vinasha
Parvathi Nandana Parthi Pureesha
Hey Shiva Nandana Jaya Sayeesha (2)

## Meaning

O Lord Sai who is verily Lord Ganesha, the one who removes all obstacles from our path, the beloved child of Goddess Parvathi and Lord Shiva, our prayerful salutations to You. Victory to You, Lord Sai.
