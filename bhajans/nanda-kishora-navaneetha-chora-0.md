## Lyrics

Nanda Kishora Navaneetha Chora
Brindavana Sanchara
Hey Shesha Shayana Hey Garuda Gamana
Aravinda Nayana Gopalana

## Meaning

Sing the glory of Lord Krishna, beloved son of Nanda and stealer of butter (devotees' hearts). Glory to the one who wandered about in Brindavana, who is verily Lord Vishnu resting on the celestial serpent Adi Shesha and who rides on Garuda. Glory to the lotus-eyed Lord Gopala.
