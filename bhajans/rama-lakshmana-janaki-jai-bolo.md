Sai Ram (6) Sai Rama

## Meaning

Victory to Lord Rama, Lakshmana, Sita and Hanuman. Victory to the mighty son of Anjana and Vayu (God of wind). Victory to the valorous one of the monkey clan. Bow to the Lord of Parthi, Lord Sai Rama, the pure and blemishless one.
