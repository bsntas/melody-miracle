## Meaning

Pray to Lord Ganesha, Lord of the Ganaas (Shiva's hordes), the elephant-faced one with a single tusk. Pray to Him who removes obstacles, the beloved son of Shiva. Pray to Him who is the embodiment of the life-giving force, Pranava. Pray to Him, with whom all Holy rituals begin. Pray to the pot-bellied one, the son of Parvathi and the one who removes obstacles.
