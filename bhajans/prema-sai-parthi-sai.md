## Meaning

Sing the name of Sai who is the embodiment of Love, the inner purifier who is the Supreme Guru, who bestows auspiciousness and full of Grace. Sai is the sweetest indweller of our hearts. He is none other than Lord Krishna, the enchanter with the flute.
