## Meaning

O beloved Prince of King Dasharatha, Lord Rama, You are the crown jewel of the Solar Dynasty, radiating bliss and happiness. You are the cause of Mother Kausalya's happiness and Sage Vishwamitra's beloved treasure. Victory to You, O King Rama, consort of Mother Sita.
