## Meaning

Chant the names of the Lord Krishna, Rama, Gopala, Govinda, Madhava and Mukunda (names of Lord Krishna). Bless us O Sai, the Lord of Parthi. You are the Supreme Lord. Victory to Thee, O Sai Rama.
