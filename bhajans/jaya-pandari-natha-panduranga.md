## Lyrics

Jaya Pandari Natha Panduranga Pundalika Varada
Jaya Parthi Pureesha Pavana Naama Prashanthi Sayeesha
Jaya Vittala Vittala Paahi Jaya Vithoba Rakhumayi
Sri Nikethana Srinivasa Shritha Jana Paripala
Paramanda Vigraha Ananda Lola Pandari Pura Vasa
Jaya Parthipuri Vasa [ Jaya Vittala ... ]

## Meaning

Victory to Lord Panduranga, the Lord of Pandari, the one who blessed Pundalika. Victory to the Lord of Parthi, Lord Sai, whose very name is sacred and who resides at Prashanthi Nilayam. Protect us O Lord Vittala, the consort of Rakhumayi. O Lord, You are Lord Srinivasa (Vishnu) in whom Mother Lakshmi (Sri) resides. You are the protector of Your devotees. You are ever blissful in Your beautiful form at Pandari. Victory to Thee who is none other than Lord Sai whose abode is Parthi.
