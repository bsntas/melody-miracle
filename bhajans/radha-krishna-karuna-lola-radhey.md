## Lyrics

Radha Krishna Karuna Lola Radhey Govinda
Radhey Govinda Murali Govinda
Radhey Radhey Govinda Murali Govinda

## Meaning

Chant the name of the merciful Lord, Radha Krishna. He is Radhey Govinda and Murali Govinda, who plays the enchanting flute.
