## Meaning

O Lord Narayana, show us Your mercy. You are the ocean of compassion, the lotus-eyed one reclining on the celestial serpent Adi Sesha. O Lord, You are the indweller of all the beings. You have incarnated in this Kali Yuga as Lord Sai who resides in Prashanthi Nilayam at Puttaparthi. You destroy our sins and You are the personification of Supreme Bliss.
