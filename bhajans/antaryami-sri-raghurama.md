## Meaning

O Lord Rama, You the indweller of the heart, the supreme leader of the universe, the Atma (soul) in all and Your name is ever purifying. You foster truth, righteousness and peace in the world. You have incarnated in this Kali Age as Lord Sai who loves and fosters all faiths.
