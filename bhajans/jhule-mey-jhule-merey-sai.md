## Lyrics

Jhule Mey Jhule Merey Sai Ram
Sathya Sai Ram Merey Bhagawan [ Jhulana Jhule ... ]
Eshwaramba Nandana Jaya Jaya Ram

## Meaning

Rock the swing of our heart to the tune of this song, O Lord Sathya Sai, the son of Eshwaramba. May Lord Sai be in our minds and in our hearts. Victory to Sai Ram.
