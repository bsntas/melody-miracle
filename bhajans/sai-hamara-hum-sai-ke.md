## Lyrics

Sai Hamara Hum Sai Ke Aisa Prem Hamara
Sai Ram Hamara Sai Ram Hamara
Sathya Sai Hai Naam Tumhara Shirdi Sai Avatara [ Sai Ram ... ]
Hindu Muslim Sikh Isai Sab Ka Palanhaara [ Sai Ram ... ]

## Meaning

O Lord Sai, we belong to You and You are verily ours; this is the loving relationship that we share; Glory to You, O Lord Sai Ram, You are ours. Praise be to Lord Sathya Sai who is the Avatar of Shirdi Sai and who is the nourisher and protector of Hindus, Muslims, Sikhs and Christians.
