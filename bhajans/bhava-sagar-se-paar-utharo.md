## Lyrics

Bhava Sagar Se Paar Utharo
Sai Nath Bhagawan [ Bhava Sagar ... ]
Allah Yesu Buddha Mahavira
Sab Naamon Ki Ek Hai Mahima
Sarva Dharma Ke Palanhaarey [ Sai Nath Bhagawan ]

## Meaning

Oh Sainath Bhagawan! Help me to cross the ocean of continuous passage of life and death. You are Allah, Yesu, Buddha, Mahavira - all these names have the same magnitude of power. You are the sustainer of all the different faiths.
