## Lyrics

Govinda Harey Gopala Harey
Muralidhara Hey Ghana Shyama Harey [ Govinda ... ]
Meera Ke Prabhu Giridhara Naagara (3)
Giridhara Naagara
Charana Kamala Balihaari Rey

## Meaning

Chant the name of Govinda, who is of dark blue complexion, holding the enchanting flute in his hand. Oh Lord of Meera who is adored as Giridhara Naagara, we salute at your divine lotus feet.
