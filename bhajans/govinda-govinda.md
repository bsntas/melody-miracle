## Lyrics

Govinda Govinda
Mukunda Madhava Govinda
Gopala Sai Gopala [ Mukunda Madhava ... ]
Jai Jai Govinda Jai Jai Gopala (4)

## Meaning

Chant the name of Lord Krishna, Govinda (foster of cow herd) who gives liberation to devotees. Victory to Govinda who is also Sai Gopala.
