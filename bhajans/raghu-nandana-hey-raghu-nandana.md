## Meaning

I bow to the lotus-eyed Lord Rama of Raghu Dynasty who is beloved to me. He is the beloved Prince of King Dasharatha and is the destroyer of the ten-headed Ravana. Salutations to the one who is the very life of Mother Janaki (Sita).
