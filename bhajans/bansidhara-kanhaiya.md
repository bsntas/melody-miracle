## Lyrics

Bansidhara Kanhaiya Ghana Shyama Sundara
Giridhari Gopa Bala Brindavana Vihara [ Bansidhara ... ]
Govinda Madhava Harey Madhusudhana Mukunda
Radhey ( Sai ) Govinda Radhey ( Sai ) Gopala Radhey ( Sai ) Govinda Radhey ( Sai )

## Meaning

Chant the various names of Lord Krishna - Govinda, Madhava, Madhusudhana, Mukunda, Sai Gopala, the one who plays the enchanting flute and who resides in Brindavan. He is verily Lord Sai.
