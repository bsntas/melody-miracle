## Lyrics

Radhey Radhey Brindavana Radhey
Govinda Gopala Nachey Radhey
Radhey Radhey Madhura Murali Baajey
Nanda Kishore Ki Payal Baajey
Rhuma Jhuma Rhuma Jhuma Payal Baajey

## Meaning

O Radha of Brindavana, dance in the name of Gopala and Govinda - the names of Lord Krishna, the son of Nanda, the one who plays the Flute sweetly. May the anklets of His and all those who dance in His name ring melodiously.
