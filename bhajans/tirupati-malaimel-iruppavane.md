## Lyrics

Shirdi Sai Govinda Sri Sathya Sai Govinda

## Meaning

Glory to Lord Govinda who resides on Mount Tirupati and who is the remover of all sins. He dwells in the temple of the heart and incarnates to save the world. Residing on the seven hills, He removes all the afflictions of the devotees. He is none other than Lord Shirdi Sai and Lord Sathya Sai.
