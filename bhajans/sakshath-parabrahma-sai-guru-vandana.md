## Lyrics

Sakshath Parabrahma Sai Guru Vandana Sathya Sai

## Meaning

Our salutations at Your divine Lotus feet, Oh Lord Sai. You are our divine preceptor, the Supreme God, and the unified form of the Trinity (Brahma, Vishnu and Shiva).
