## Lyrics

Jaya Jaya Jaya Shiva Shakthi Swaroopa Mangala Dayaka Naam
Dasharatha Nandana Ram
Ananda Dayaka Shyam
Premamrutha Bhagawan
Jai Jai Jai Sai Ram (Bolo)

## Meaning

Glory to the son of Dasharatha, Lord Rama, who has a blissful and serene face and who upholds righteousness. He is the same as the son of Devaki, Lord Krishna (Shyama), who, holding the enchanting flute bestows happiness. He is verily the son of Eshwaramba, Lord Sathya Sai, who is the embodiment of love. Victory to Lord Sai, the embodiment of Shiva and Shakthi and the all powerful one whose name bestows auspiciousness. Chant victory to Sai Ram.
