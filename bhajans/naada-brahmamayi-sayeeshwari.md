## Lyrics

Naada Brahmamayi Sayeeshwari
Bhagawathi Mata Krupa Sagari [ Naada ... ]

Bhava Bhaya Haarini Sathya Sayeeshwari

## Meaning

O Mother Sai, the abode of divine music, You are the Ocean of compassion. You are adored by various names, Varahi (a form of Shakthi), Vaishnavi (the sister of Vishnu). You are the bestower of speech who is worshipped as Meenakshi, Mahamayi and Mookambike in various forms in temples. O Supreme Mother, the sister of Vishnu, You are the one who removes the fear of worldly existence. O Mother Sathya Sai, You are verily Mother Parvathi.
