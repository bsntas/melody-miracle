## Lyrics

Partheeshwara Sri Sai Prabho
Eshwaramba Nandana Sai Prabho [ Partheeshwara Sri ]

Madhava Mohana Sai Narayana
Prema Avatara Prabhu Sai Rama

## Meaning

Sing the glory of the Lord of Parthi, Sri Sai The darling son of Mother Eshwaramba He is the unifier of all religious denominations. He is the personification of Eshwar, Allah, Krishna, Zohrashtra, Buddha and Madhava. He is the incarnation of Divine Love.
