Daya Sagara Karunakara Sai Guru Deva

## Meaning

O Divine Preceptor Sai, the Lord of the destitute, You are the embodiment of love. You grant peace to the devotees. Victory unto You, O Lord, the embodiment of truth, awareness and bliss, who has a face like the moon, decorated with a crown of hair. O Sai, You are the ocean of compassion and kindness.
