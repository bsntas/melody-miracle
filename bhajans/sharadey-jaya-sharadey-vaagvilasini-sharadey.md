## Lyrics

Sharadey Jaya Sharadey Vaagvilasini Sharadey
Sharadey Jaya Sharadey Vidya Dayini Sharadey
Sharadey Jaya Sharadey Veda Mata Sharadey
Veda Mata Sharadey

## Meaning

Victory to Mother Sharada (Saraswathi) who resides in our speech, who is the embodiment of knowledge and who is the mother of all Vedas.
