## Lyrics

Rama Krishna Govinda Narayana
Narayana Hari Narayana [ Rama Krishna ... ]
Sri Lakshmi Ramana Narayana
Om Anantha Narayana (Hari) (3)

## Meaning

Worship Lord Rama, Krishna, Govinda and Narayana, the Lord of the Goddess of Prosperity (goddess Lakshmi). He is the infinite, supreme Lord Hari.
