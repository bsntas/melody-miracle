## Lyrics

Aravinda Lochana Aartha Janavana
Sai Narayana Sathya Narayana [ Aravinda Lochana ... ]
Eshwaramba Nandana Dosha Nivarana
Parama Niranjana Hey Shubhanana

## Meaning

O lotus-eyed Lord Narayana, You are the protector of the deprived and distressed. O Lord Sai, the auspicious son of Mother Eshwaramba, the blemishless one, You are the redeemer of our defects.
