## Lyrics

## Meaning

Oh Mother of the Universe, Sai. Your form is the Pranava, Mother Sai. You are the destroyer of fear and the protector of the forlorn. Mother of the Universe Sai, we offer our prayers to you who grants universal protection to the underprivileged, destitute and deprived.
