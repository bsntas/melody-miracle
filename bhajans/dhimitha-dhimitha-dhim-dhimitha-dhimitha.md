## Lyrics

Dhimitha Dhimitha Dhim Dhimitha Dhimitha Dhim Nachey Bhola Nath
Nachey Bhola Nath (4)
Mrudanga Boley Shiva Shiva Shiva Om
Damaru Boley Hara Hara Hara Om
Veena Boley Sai Ram Sai Ram
Nachey Bhola Nath (4)

## Meaning

Lord Shiva dances in ecstacy with the tune, "Dhimita Dhimita Dhim". The Mrudanga (musical instrumental) chants the auspicious name of, "Shiva Shiva Shiva". Along with it, the drum vibrates with the sound, "Hara Hara Hara Om" and the Veena (musical instrument) chants "Sai Ram".
