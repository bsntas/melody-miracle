## Lyrics

Rama Charan Sada Bhajo

## Meaning

Worship the Holy Feet of Lord Rama, the son of Kausalya and the enchanter of Vaidehi (Sita). The Holy Feet of Lord Rama confers supreme peace, were worshipped by Maruti (Hanuman) and Bharata and liberated Ahalya from her curse. Sing the glory of the Holy Feet of Lord Sai Rama whose abode is Prashanthi Nilayam.
