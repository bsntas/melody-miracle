## Lyrics

Durge Durge Devi Durgati Nashini
Kaali Kaali Maha Kaali Kapalini [ Durge Durge ... ]
Bhagawathi Bharati Devi Saraswathi
Ambe Bhavani Lakshmi Narayani
Parthi Nivasini Sai Janani

## Meaning

Chant the name of Durga, the goddess who removes poverty. Chant the name of Kaali, the goddess who adorns the garland of skulls (of evil doers). O Mother Sai, You are also adored as Bhagawathi, Bharati, Saraswathi, Bhavani and Lakshmi.
