## Lyrics

Muralidhara Hey Ghana Shyama
Partheeshwara Sai Shyama
Giridhari Parthi Vihari
Meera Ke Giridhari
Surdas Ke Krishna Murari
Ghana Shyama Megha Shyama Sai Shyama [ Giridhari ... ]

## Meaning

Salutations to You Lord Sai, resident of Parthi. You are none other than Lord Krishna, the Lord of Meera and Surdas. You held the Govardhan Mountain to save the cowherds. Your complexion is that of dark clouds.
