## Lyrics

Rama Harey Sita Ram
Shyam Harey Radhey Shyam
Sita Vallabha Sundara Ram
Murali Manohara Radhey Shyam
Parthi Pureeshwara Ram

## Meaning

Chant the name of Lord Rama, the Lord of Sita, Radhey Shyam, the Lord of Radha. Chant the name of Rama, the handsome consort of Sita, Radhey Shyam, the enchanting Flute Player. They are none other than our Lord of Parthi, Sai Ram.
