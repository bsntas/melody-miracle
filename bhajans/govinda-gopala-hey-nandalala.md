## Lyrics

Govinda Gopala Hey Nandalala
Radhey Gopala Gopi Gopala
Meera Ke Natha Prabhu Murali Gopala
Govardhanodhara Gopala Bala [ Radhey Gopala ... ]

## Meaning

Glory to Lord Krishna also known as Govinda, Gopala, and the one who is the son of Nanda. Glory to the Lord of Radha, Meera and the Gopis. Glory to the one who plays the flute and the one who lifted the Govardhana mountain.
