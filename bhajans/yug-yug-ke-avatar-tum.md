## Lyrics

Yug Yug Ke Avatar Tum Hi Ho
Merey Ram Merey Shyam Shirdi Sai Parthi Ram
Jag Ke Palanhaar Tum Hi Ho

Sab Ko Sanmati Do Bhagawan
Allah Eshwar Terey Naam Shirdi Sai Parthi Ram [ Jag Ke ... ]

## Meaning

O Lord Sai of Shirdi and Parthi, You have incarnated from age to age, You are my Lord Rama and my Lord Krishna. You are the protector of this Universe. You are hailed by many names such as Allah and Eshwar (Shiva). O Lord Sai Rama of Shirdi and Parthi, bless us all with wisdom.
