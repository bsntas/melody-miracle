## Lyrics

Govinda Harey Gopala Harey
Govinda Harey Avatar Harey [ Govinda ... ]
Treta Mey Sita Rama Baney
Dwapara Mey Radhey Shyama Baney
Kali Yug Mey Sai Naam Liye
Sai Naam Liye Sarva Naam Liye

## Meaning

Glory to Govinda, Gopala, the one who plays the flute and the one is dark complexioned. The One who was Rama in Treta yuga, and the one who was Lord Shyama the consort of Radha. In the Kali Yuga, We chant the name of Sai and Surrender to His Lotus Feet.
