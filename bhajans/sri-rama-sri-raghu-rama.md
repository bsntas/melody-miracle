## Lyrics

Sri Rama Sri Raghu Rama
Sri Rama Sita Rama [ Sri Rama ... ]
Sri Rama Megha Shyama
Sri Rama Sai Rama

## Meaning

Glory to Lord Rama of Raghu dynasty, the consort of Sita. Glory to the one who is the color of the rain-cloud. Lord Sai is none other than Lord Rama.
