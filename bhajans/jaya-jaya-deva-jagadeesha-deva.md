## Lyrics

Jaya Jaya Deva Jagadeesha Deva
Daya Sagara Sai Deva [ Jaya Jaya Deva ... ]
Neeraja Nayana Ksheerabdhi Shayana
Neela Megha Shyama
Saama Gaana Priya Sarva Dharma Priya Sathya Sai Mahadeva

## Meaning

Oh Lord Sai, You are the ocean of compassion. You are verily the lotus-eyed Narayana whose abode is that of the ocean of milk, and Krishna whose complexion is deep blue. Oh Sai Mahadeva, You are the lover of the music of Veda and the one who loves all religions equally.
