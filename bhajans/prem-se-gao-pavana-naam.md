## Lyrics

Prem Se Gao Pavana Naam
Rama Rama Raghu Nandana Rama [ Prem Se ... ]

## Meaning

With love and devotion, sing the purifying and uplifting name of Lord Rama of the Raghu Dynasty. Victory to Lord Rama. Victory to Lord Sathya Sai Baba.
