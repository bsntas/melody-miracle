## Lyrics

Ravikula Mandana Rajana Ram
Parama Pavana Mangala Dham

## Meaning

Chant the name of Lord Rama and Lord Sai of Puttaparthi. O Lord Rama, nectarine is Thy name. You are exceedingly beautiful and charming with a graceful look. You are the jewel of the Ravi Kula (descendant of the Solar dynasty) and You are the greatest among all kings. Reciting Your name confers auspiciousness.
