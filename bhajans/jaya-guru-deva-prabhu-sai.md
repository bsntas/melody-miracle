## Lyrics

Jaya Guru Deva Prabhu Sai Deva
Devadi Deva Pranamamyaham
Shiva Shiva Shambho Kailasa Pathey
Parthi Pureeshwara Palaya Maam [ Devadi Deva ... ]

## Meaning

Our Salutations to the Preceptor and Lord of Lords Sathya Sai. Protect me O Shiva, the originator of Auspiciousness, Lord of Mount Kailasa who is verily Lord Sai, Lord of Parthi.
