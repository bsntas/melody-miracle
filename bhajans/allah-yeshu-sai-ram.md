## Lyrics

Allah Yeshu Sai Ram
Buddha Mahavir Sai Ram
Treta Mey Tum Sai Ram
Dwapar Mey Tum Sai Shyam
Sarva Dharma Priya Sai Ram
Parthi Pureeshwara Sai Ram

## Meaning

O Lord Sai, You are Allah, Yeshu, Buddha and Mahavira. In Treta era, You incarnated as Lord Rama and in Dwapara as Lord Krishna. You are the patron of all religions and the Lord of Puttaparthi.
