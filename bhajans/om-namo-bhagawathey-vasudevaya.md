## Lyrics

Om Namo Bhagawathey Vasudevaya

Om Namah Shivaya Om Namo Narayanaya (2) [ Hari Om Namo ... ]

## Meaning

I bow down to Thee, O Lord Krishna, son of Vasudeva. I bow down to Lord Shiva, to Lord Narayana. Adoration to Lord Vishnu and Lord Shiva, who abide and shine in all beings and is one with the primordial sound Om. Om symbolizes the creator and creation.
