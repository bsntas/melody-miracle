## Lyrics

Giridhari Murari Govinda
Ghana Shyama Murari Gopala [ Giridhari ... ]
Radhey Krishna Murari Govinda
Sathya Sai Murari Gopala

## Meaning

Chant the name of Giridhari Govinda who is the destroyer of the demon 'Mura' chant the name of Radha Krishna and Sathya Sai Gopala.
