## Lyrics

Rama Jayam Raghu Rama Jayam
Raghava Dhyanamey Premamayam [ Rama Jayam ... ]
Rama Sita Pathi Raghava Mantram
Prema Niguna Gana Seva Niranthram
Rama Dayapara Parama Pavitram
Raghukula Dasharatha Suthamutha Mitram

## Meaning

Glory to Lord Rama of Raghu lineage. The entire world meditates on Rama, the dear son of Dasharatha of the Solar Dynasty. Chant the name of Raghava, the Lord of Sita, and immerse ourselves ever in extolling His Divine qualities and in His service. Glory be to Rama who shows immense compassion towards the pure and the good ones.
