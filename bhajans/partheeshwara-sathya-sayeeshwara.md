## Lyrics

Partheeshwara Sathya Sayeeshwara
Daya Karo Hari Narayana
Sri Sai Narayana ( Sai )
Bhava Sagaroddhara Bhuvanavana
Sharanagatha Thrana Narayana [ Daya Karo ... ]
Sri Sai Narayana ( Sai )

## Meaning

Oh Lord Sathya Sai, Lord of Puttaparthi, Oh Lord Sai Narayana, have compassion. You are the One who can help us cross the ocean of wordly existence. Hey Sai Narayana, we surrender to Thee. Please have compassion, Sai Narayana.
