## Lyrics

Neela Megha Shyama Krishna Gopala Krishna

Manusha Veshadhari Sathya Narayana
Parthipuri Nayaka Sai Narayana

## Meaning

Chant the name of the dark-hued Lord Krishna, protector of cows and the beloved of Radha. Chant the name of Lord Sathya Narayana who reclines on the Vata leaf and who incarnated in human form. He is none other than Lord Sai, Lord of Parthi.
