## Lyrics

Si Me Caigo, Me Levantas
Me Proteges Con Tu Amor

## Meaning

O Lord Sai, if I fall You lift me up. You protect me with Your love. Your smile is all heaven. I love You my Lord. O Lord, without You I could not take even one step in life.
