## Lyrics

Madhava Govinda Govinda Madhava Naam Gaate Chalo [ Hari Naam ... ]
Govinda Vittala Gopala Vittala

## Meaning

Let every moment be spent in singing and contemplating on the Lord's name Hari, Madhava, Govinda, Gopala, Vittala and Sai who dwells in Parthi.
