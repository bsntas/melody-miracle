## Lyrics

Aao Sai Narayana Darshan Deejo
Tum Ho Jagat Vidhata (2)
Tum Hi Ho Brahma Tum Hi Ho Vishnu
Tum Hi Ho Shankara Roopa (2)

## Meaning

Welcome Sai Narayana grant us Your Darshan. You are Brahma, Vishnu, Shiva, Rama, Krishna and You are the one who creates and organizes the universe.
