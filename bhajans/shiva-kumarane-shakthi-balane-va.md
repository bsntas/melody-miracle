## Lyrics

Shiva Kumarane Shakthi Balane Va Va Va

## Meaning

Grace us with Your presence, O Lord Shanmukha, son of Shiva and Shakthi (Parvathi). You are the inner meaning of Omkara. Please come, O Lord Sai, residing in Parthi.
