## Lyrics

Araj Suno Merey Parama Krupalu
Partheeshwara Sai Deva [ Araj Suno ... ]
Sathya Sanathana Antaryami
Sakala Charachara Tum Ho Swami
Janam Maran Se Paar Karo
Bhava Bandhan Se Uddhar Karo (Baba)

## Meaning

O Supremely merciful Lord of Parthi! Kindly listen to my prayer and help me come out of the bondage of the cycle of birth and death. O Lord Sai! You are omnipresent, infinite and the indweller of our heart.
