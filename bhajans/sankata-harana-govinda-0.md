## Lyrics

Sankata Harana Govinda
Pankaja Charana Govinda
Sathya Sai Deva Govinda
Saptagiri Vasa Govinda
Govinda Hari (Sai) Govinda

## Meaning

Glory to Lord Govinda (Narayana) who resides in the seven hills and has now incarnated as Lord Sathya Sai, who is the remover of all obstacles. O Lord, we offer ourselves at Your tender lotus feet.
