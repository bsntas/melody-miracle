## Lyrics

Jaya Jaya Jaya Hey Gajanana

Neelakanta Sutha Gajanana

## Meaning

Victory to the elephant-faced Lord Ganesha, the son of Parvathi and the blue-throated Shiva and the one who confers grace on His devotees continuously.
