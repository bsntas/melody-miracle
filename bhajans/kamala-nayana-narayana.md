## Meaning

Glory to Lord Narayana, the lotus-eyed one who reclines on the Ocean of Milk, the one who is dark-hued like the rain cloud, the one who has Vaikunta as His abode. He is verily Lord Sai whose abode is Prashanthi Nilayam at Puttaparthi.
