## Lyrics

Giridhara Madhava Murahara Natavara, Jaya Jaya Jaya Hey Madhusudhana 
Jaya Jaya Jaya Hey Madhusudhana
Jaya Jaya Jaya Hey Janardhana
Venu Vilolana Vedoddharana
Yashoda Nandana Bhaktha Samrakshana
Ratnakara Bhooshana Hrudaya (Parthi) Viharana  [ Jaya Jaya ... ]

## Meaning

Victory to Lord Krishna, the one who lifted the Govardhana hill, the one who vanquished the demons Mura and Madhu. Victory to the protector of mankind, the one who plays blissful music from the flute, the one who fosters the Vedas. Victory to the son of Yashoda and the one who protects His devotees. Victory to Lord Sai, the crown jewel of the Ratnakara dynasty who resides at Parthi and in the hearts of devotees and who is verily Lord Krishna.
