## Lyrics

Sai Prem Dey Shanthi Dey Ananda Dey Baba Prem Dey
Tum Ho Baba Daya Ke Sagar
Tum Ho Guna Mandira Nata Naagar
Adi Anantha Hey Sathya Sanathana
Alakh Niranjana Sai Janardhana
Satchidananda Prabho Krupa Karo
Prem Dey Shanthi Dey Anand Dey Baba Prem Dey (Sai)

## Meaning

O Lord Sai, please grant us peace, bliss and love. O Lord, You are an ocean of compassion who possesses all good and pure qualities. Your form is infinite, eternal truth, with no beginning or end. You are beyond the perception of senses, always supremely pure. O Sai, uplifter of humanity, You are Satchidananda - Existence, Consciousness and Divine bliss. Please shower Your Grace on us.
