## Lyrics

Bhavatharini Partheeshwari
Sukha Dayini Sayeeshwari [ Bhavatharini ... ]
Bandha Mochani Prashanthi Eshwari
Janani Devi Leela Vinodini
Shiva Vama Bhage Sayeeshwari
Charanam Sharanam Samrajya Dayini

## Meaning

O Mother Sai, residing in Parthi, You help us cross the worldly existence. O Sai Mother, You grant us all the pleasures and comfort. O Mother, Goddess of Parthi, You free us from bondage and bestow joy upon us. O Eshwari, the consort of Lord Shiva and the one capable of bestowing kingdom, we surrender at Your feet.
