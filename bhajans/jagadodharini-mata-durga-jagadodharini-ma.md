## Lyrics

Jagadodharini Mata Durga Jagadodharini Ma
Jago Jago Ma Jago Jago Ma Jago Jago Ma Janani
Hey Gowri Devi Rana Chandi Devi

## Meaning

Oh Universal Mother Durga! Awaken us from the slumber of ignorance. You are Mother Gowri and the valiant Chandi devi. Awaken us to the inherent Atmic reality, the charmer of Lord Shiva.
