## Lyrics

Sri Venkatesha Sayeeshwara
Tirumalai Vasa Sayeeshwara
Sayeeshwara Sathya Sayeeshwara

Venkatesha Sai Venkatesha (3) [ Balaji Govinda ... ]

## Meaning

Salutations to Lord Sai who is verily Lord Venkatesha or Balaji who resides in Tirumala as Lord Govinda. He is the Lord of the whole Universe and the embodiment of happiness.
