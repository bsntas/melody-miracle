## Lyrics

Prema Swaroopa Premavatara

Hey (Sai) Jaganmohana Krishna Krishna

## Meaning

Glory to the son of Eshwaramba, Lord Sathya Sai, who is verily Lord Krishna, the destroyer of the demon Madhu. O Lord who resides in Parthi, You have come to uplift the whole world. You are the very embodiment of Love and the incarnation of Love. You are none other than Lord Sai of Shirdi whose abode was the Dwarakamayi. O Lord Sai, You enchant the entire world.
