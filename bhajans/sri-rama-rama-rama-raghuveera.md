## Lyrics

Sri Rama Rama Rama Raghuveera Rama Ram
Sri Rama Rama Rama Bharatagraja Rama Ram
Sri Rama Rama Rama Ranadheera Rama Ram
Sri Rama Rama Rama Sharanam Tava Rama Ram

## Meaning

I surrender to Thee, O valorous Lord Rama of the Raghu Dynasty, the one who is the elder brother of Bharata.
