## Meaning

The divine names of Sai Narayana and Sathya Narayana are most auspicious. Lord Sai is verily Brahma, Vishnu, Shiva, the supremely blissful Krishna and the divine sound Om. Chant the name of Lord Sai that bestows auspiciousness.
