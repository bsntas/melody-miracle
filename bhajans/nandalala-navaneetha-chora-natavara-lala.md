## Lyrics

Nandalala Navaneetha Chora Natavara Lala Gopala
Devaki Vasudeva Kumara
Deva Deva Gopala [ Nandalala ... ]
Mohana Murali Gaana Vilola
Mohana Jaya (Sai) Gopala (Mana)

## Meaning

Glory to Lord Krishna, son of Nanda, the stealer of butter, the beautiful dancer, the protector of cows, the son of Devaki and Vasudeva, the Lord of the Lords who plays melodiously on the flute. Victory to the one with an enchanting form.
