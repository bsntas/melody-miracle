## Lyrics

Murali Manohara Shyama Murari
Gopala Sai Gopala (2)
Radha Lola Harey Giridhari
Gopala Sai Gopala (2)

## Meaning

Salutations to you Oh Lord Gopala (Krishna) you are fond of the music on flute, you are the beloved of Radha.
