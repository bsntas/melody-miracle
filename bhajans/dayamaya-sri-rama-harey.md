## Lyrics

Dayamaya Sri Rama Harey
Krupamaya Sri Sai Harey
Dharma Swaroopa Rama Harey

Sathya Dharma Shanthi Daata Sathya Sai Baba Harey

## Meaning

Chant the name of Rama, the compassionate one and Sai, who is highly merciful. Rama is the embodiment of righteousness. Sai is the embodiment of love and the one who bestows truth, righteousness and peace.
