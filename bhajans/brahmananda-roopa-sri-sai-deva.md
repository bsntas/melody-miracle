## Lyrics

Brahmananda Roopa Sri Sai Deva
Brinda Vihara Ananda Chandha [ Brahmananda Roopa ... ]
Nithyananda Atmananda Keshava Govinda
Govinda Gopala Jaya Ananda Mukunda
Govinda Gopala Jaya Ananda Mukunda (4)

## Meaning

Glory to Lord Sai, the very embodiment of Supreme Bliss. In the incarnation as Lord Krishna, he was the resident of Brindavan who bestowed bliss on all. Chant the names of Keshava and Govinda, who is ever blissful and immersed in the Self. Victory to Lord Govinda, Gopala and Mukunda.
