## Lyrics

Shiva Shambho Hara Hara Shambho
Bhava Nasha Kailasa Nivasa [ Shiva Shambho ... ]
Parvathi Pathey Harey Pashupathey
Gangadhara Hara Gowri Pathey

## Meaning

O Lord Shiva, You are the destroyer of all fear, the one who resides in the holy mountains of Kailasa, the consort of Mother Parvathi, the one who holds the mighty Ganges in Your matted locks, the consort of Mother Gowri. We pray and offer our humble salutations at Your Feet.
