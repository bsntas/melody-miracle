## Lyrics

Ram Kaho Sai Ram Kaho
Shyam Kaho Ghana Shyam Kaho
Yesu Kaho Zohrashtra Kaho
Jag Ka Vidhata Sai Kaho
Mandir Mey Kabhi Masjid Mey
Apna Hrudaya Mey Sai Bhagawan

## Meaning

Call Him by any name be it Rama, Sai Ram, Shyam, Yesu, Zohrashtra, sometimes He is in a temple or mosque, but the Creator of this Universe, Lord Sai, lives in our Heart.
