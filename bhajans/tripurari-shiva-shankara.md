## Lyrics

Tripurari Shiva Shankara
Naga Bhooshana Naga Bharana [ Tripurari Shiva ... ]
Omkareshwara Namah Shivaya
Mahakaleshwara Namah Shivaya

## Meaning

Oh Lord Shiva (also called Shankara), the slayer of the demons of Tripura, the one who wears the serpent as ornament, the personification of the primordial sound "Om", the Lord Mahakaleshwar (Shiva's another name), I offer my salutations to Thee.
