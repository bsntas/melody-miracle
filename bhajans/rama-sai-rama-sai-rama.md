## Lyrics

Rama Sai Rama Sai Rama Sai Ram (2)
Kausalya Nandana Dasharatha Ram
Janaki Vallabha Jaya Jaya Ram
Lakshmana Sevitha Lavanya Ram
Ravana Mardhana Ranadheera Ram
Ayodhya Vasi Raja Ram
Anjana Priya Sutha Atmabhi Ram

## Meaning

Chant the name of Lord Rama, who has incarnated in this Kali Age as Lord Sai Ram. O Lord Rama, the darling son of Kausalya and Dasharatha, victory to Thee, the beloved of Sita. Glory to the gentle Lord who was served by brother Lakshmana. Glory to the illustrious warrior who destroyed the demon Ravana. Glory to the resident King of Ayodhya, who is the indwelling Lord of Hanuman, the dear son of Anjana.
