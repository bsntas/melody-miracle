## Lyrics

Sri Raghu Nandana Rama Harey
Hey Yadu Nandana Krishna Harey
Vaidehi Priya Rama Harey
Radha Ramana Shyama Harey

## Meaning

Glory to Lord Rama of Raghu dynasty and the beloved of Mother Sita. Glory to Lord Krishna of Yadu dynasty and the beloved of Radha.
