## Lyrics

Mukunda Madhava Murali Manohara Govardhana Giridhari
Gopi Poojita Manasa Chora Peetambara Dhari [ Mukunda Madhava ... ]
Geeta Bodhaka Sai Sundara
Vanditha Hrudaya Vihari (2)

## Meaning

Salutations to you Oh beautiful Lord Sai Krishna. you were worshipped by Gopikas, you stole their hearts wearing the yellow colored dress, you were called by many other names such as Mukunda, Madhava, Murali Manohara, Govardhan Giridhari (All names of Krishna), you were the preceptor for the Sacred Bhagawath Geeta and you reside in the hearts of the devotees who worship you.
