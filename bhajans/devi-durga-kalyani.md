## Lyrics

Devi Durga Kalyani
Maha Shakthi Bhavani Shivani [ Devi Durga ]
Suguna Manohari Sai Bhavani
Devi Jagadambe Janani Dayamayi
Vande Bhagawathi Mata Bhavani

## Meaning

Oh goddess Durga, who confers auspiciousness. You are Bhavani with tremendous power, who is the consort of Shiva. You are the captivating Sai Bhavani with virtuous qualities. Oh divine mother of the universe, who is highly compassionate. Oh mother who is adored as Bhagawathi and Bhavani, we bow to you.
