## Lyrics

Jai Jai Jai Devi Jagadamba
Sankata Harini Mangala Karini [ Jai Jai ... ]
Gnana Shakthi Vignanamayi
Premamayi Kalyanamayi
Shubha Dey Mata Devi Jagadamba

## Meaning

Glory be to Universal Mother, destroyer of sorrows and promoter of the well being of her devotees. Confer your grace upon us Oh Mother the personification of Integrated awareness, Supreme wisdom, Supreme Love and Auspiciousness.
