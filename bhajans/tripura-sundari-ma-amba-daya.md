## Lyrics

Tripura Sundari Ma Amba Daya Sagari Ma
Sundara Vadani Ma Amba Suguna Manohari Ma
Jaya Jagat Janani Ma Amba Jagadodharini Ma
Parthi Nivasini Ma Amba Paapa Vimochani Ma

## Meaning

O Mother of Parthi, You are the most beautiful in all the three worlds, You are the ocean of compassion, full of virtues, You are the Mother who liberates the world and frees us from our sins.
