## Lyrics

Krishna Rama Govinda Gokula Nandana Gopala
Rama Krishna Govinda Jaya Brindavana Gopala
Sai Krishna Govinda Parthi Pureeshwara Gopala
Gopala Sai Gopala
Gopi Manohara Gokula Bala Sai Murari

## Meaning

Chant the names of Lord Krishna, Rama and Govinda. Chant the name of Gopala, the charming child of Gokula and Brindavan. Gopala, the favourite of the Gopikas, and the one who slayed the demon Mura, is the same as our Sai.
