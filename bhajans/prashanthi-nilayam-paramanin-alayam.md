## Meaning

Prashanthi Nilayam is the abode of Parabrahman (Supreme Lord). It is the temple of the one with the Divine Form, of the avatar of the Kali age, who showers compassion on all. It is the enchanting divine abode for all humans, the temple of Love, where the sins of His devotees are washed away at the Lotus Feet.
