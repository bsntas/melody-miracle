## Lyrics

Jaya Jaya Govinda Narayana
Jaya Hari Govinda Narayana [ Jaya Jaya ... ]
Mathuradhipathey Mandara Giridhara
Murali Manohara Narayana
Narayana Namo (Sai) Narayana (3)

## Meaning

Prostrations to Lord Narayana, who incarnated as Govinda (Krishna), who is the Lord of Mathura and the one who lifted the Mandara mountain and played soulful music with His Flute.
