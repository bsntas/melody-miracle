## Lyrics

Señor, Señor, Señor, Shiva Nataraja, Señor, Señor, Señor, ...
... Maha Sathya Sai
Shiva Nataraja, Shiva Nataraja, Shiva Nataraja, De Mi Corazon

... Llename, De Amor

## Meaning

O Lord Shiva Nataraj, who is none other than the great Lord Sathya Sai, You are dear to my heart. O Lord, dance to the beat of the drums, and fill me with Your Divine Love.
