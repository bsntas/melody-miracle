## Meaning

O Lord Sai, You are the incarnation of this Kali age. You are verily Lord Rama, the consort of Sita. O Lord of Prashanthi, You are eternally pure. Glory to Lord Rama of the Raghu dynasty who is none other than Lord Sai.
