## Lyrics

Jaya Raghu Nandana Jaya Jaya Rama
Jaya Jagat Janani Janaki Rama [ Jaya Raghu Nandana ... ]
Nava Nava Komala Megha Shyama
Bhava Harana Bhadrachala Rama

## Meaning

Victory to Rama whose consort is Janaki, the Mother of Universe. Victory to the one who is ever new and fresh, the one who is of deep blue complexion, the Lord of Bhadrachala (pilgrim city in Andhra Pradesh) who destroys the cycle of Birth.
