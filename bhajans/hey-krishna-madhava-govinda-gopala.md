## Lyrics

Hey Krishna Madhava Govinda Gopala
Mathura Natha Madana Gopala
Nanda Mukunda Shyama ( Sai ) Gopala
Krishna Harey Jaya Krishna Harey Jaya Hari Govinda Krishna Harey (2)

## Meaning

Chant the various names of Gopala, Govinda, Krishna, Madhava, the One who gives delight and the one who is the Lord of Mathura. Glory be to Him.
