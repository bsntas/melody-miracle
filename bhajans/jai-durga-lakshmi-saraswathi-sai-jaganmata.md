## Lyrics

Jai Durga Lakshmi Saraswathi Sai Jaganmata
Sai Jaganmata Maam Paahi Jaganmata (2)

## Meaning

Glory to Goddesses Durga, Lakshmi, Saraswathi and Mother Sai, the Divine Mother of the Universe, O Mother Sai, please protect me.
