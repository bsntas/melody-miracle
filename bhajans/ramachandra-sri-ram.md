## Meaning

Victory to the merciful Lord Ramachandra, the darling son of Dasharatha. Glory to Thee, the slayer of the ten-headed demon - Ravana. We bow to You, O Lord with the liberating name that enthralls the saintly and the one who liberated Ahalya, the chaste devotee.
