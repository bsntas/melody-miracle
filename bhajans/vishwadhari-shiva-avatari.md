## Lyrics

Vishwadhari Shiva Avatari
Sai Shankara Namo Namo [ Vishwadhari Shiva ... ]
Mathura Natha Narayana Hari
Sai Shankara Namo Namo (Sathya) (2)

## Meaning

Salutations to You O Lord Sai, You are the very incarnation of Lord Shiva and Lord Narayana of Mathura (Krishna).
