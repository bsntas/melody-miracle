## Lyrics

Vittala Vittala Hari Vittala
Vittala Vittala Naama Bhajo Hari Vittala
Panduranga Vittala Vittala Vittala Naam
Vittala Vittala Naama Bhajo Hari Vittala
Vittala Vittala Hari (Sai) Vittala

## Meaning

Chant the name and sing the glory of Lord Panduranga Vittala and Lord Sai who is verily Lord Vittala.
