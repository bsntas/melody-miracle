## Meaning

Worship the merciful and compassionate Son of Mother Devaki, Lord Krishna. O Lord, You are the treasure house of compassion and mercy. O Lord of Lords, Sai, the up-lifter of the forlorn, please grant us Your Darshan, the view of Your Divine Form.
