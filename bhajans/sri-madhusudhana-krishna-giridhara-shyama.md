## Lyrics

Sri Madhusudhana Krishna Giridhara Shyama Murari
Bhaktha Vatsala Prabhu Hey Giridhari
Radha Ramana Sri Hari
Keshava Madhava Sadhu Samrakshaka
Sai Krishna Murari

## Meaning

O Lord Krishna, You are adored by Your devotees as well as Your beloved Radha. O Lord who lifted the Govardhana hill, You are saviour of the holy men and ascetics.
