## Lyrics

Sarva Mangala Sai Janani
Parthi Nivasini Parama Shivani [ Sarva Mangala ... ]
Mata Maheshwari Durga Bhavani
Adi Parashakthi Sai Narayani
Jagadodharini Bhava Bhaya Harini

## Meaning

Glory to Mother Sai, the most auspicious one residing in Parthi, who is verily Mother Parvathi, the consort of Lord Shiva. She is worshipped by names like Shivani, Durga, Maheshwari, and Parashakthi. She bestows liberation and destroys the fear of birth and death.
