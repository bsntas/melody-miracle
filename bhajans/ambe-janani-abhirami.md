## Lyrics

Ambe Janani Abhirami
Abhaya Pradayini Shivakami [ Ambe Janani ]
Sambhavi Shankari Sarveshwari
Shantha Swaroopini Shivaranjani
Sathya Swaroopini Sai Mata
Shashwata Ananda Dayini Mata

## Meaning

Oh pleasing divine mother, the consort of Shiva known also as Abhirami Sambhavi, Shankari the one who grants fearlessness and is omnipresent. We chant your glorious names. You are Sai Mata the embodiment of truth and peace who grants us with eternal peace.
