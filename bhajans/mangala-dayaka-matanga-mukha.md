## Lyrics

Mangala Dayaka Matanga Mukha Jaya Jaya Jaya Gananatha
Mahadeva Shiva Gowri Nandana Jaya Jaya Jaya Gananatha
Mooshika Vahana Mukthi Pradayaka Jaya Jaya Jaya Gananatha
Mahadeva Shiva Gowri Nandana Jaya Jaya Jaya Gananatha

## Meaning

Victory to Lord Ganesha who bestows auspiciousness, who is the beloved son of Lord Shiva and Mother Gowri. Victory to the Lord of Ganaas (Shiva's army), provider of liberation who rides on the mouse (represents the temperamental mind).
