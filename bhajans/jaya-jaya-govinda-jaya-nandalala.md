## Lyrics

Jaya Jaya Govinda Jaya Nandalala
Radha Lola Raasa Vilola
Tribhuvana Paripala Deena Dayala [ Jaya Jaya Govinda ... ]
Surdas Prabhu Karuna Sagara
Meera Ke Prabhu Giridhara Naagara
Bhaktha Sakha Bhagawan Gopala

## Meaning

Victory to Lord Govinda (Krishna), the son of Nanda, and who is dear to Radha. Victory to the one who did the celestial dance with the Gopis (Raasa Leela), who nurtures the three worlds and who is compassionate towards the helpless. Lord Gopala is a friend to His devotees and is the Lord of devotees Surdas and Meera.
