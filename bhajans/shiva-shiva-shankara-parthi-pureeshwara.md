## Lyrics

Shiva Shiva Shankara Parthi Pureeshwara

Haalaahala Dhara Hey Gangadhara
Natana Manohara Chandra Kaladhara
Samba Sadashiva Hara Parameshwara

## Meaning

Lord Sai, the resident of Parthi is none other than Lord Shankara, who wears the tiger skin, who swallowed the poison for saving the gods, who holds the sacred river Ganges in His matted hair, who is fond of dance and who wears the crescent moon on the head.
