## Lyrics

Rama Krishna Vasudeva Narayana Hari Hari
Vishwa Roopa Vasudeva Narayana Hari Hari
Narayana Hari Hari Om Narayana Hari Hari [ Vishwa Roopa ... ]

## Meaning

Chant the names of Lord Rama, Krishna, Vasudeva, Hari, Narayana, the all-pervading cosmic form of the Lord of Lords, Lord Hari, who is verily Lord Sai.
