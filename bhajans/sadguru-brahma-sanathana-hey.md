## Meaning

Worship the most merciful Divine Preceptor, Lord Sai, who is the Supreme Soul (Brahman). He is the saviour of the world and is verily Lord Krishna. O Lord, You are the one who helps overcome sorrows and the fear of crossing the ocean of life and death.
