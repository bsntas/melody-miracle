## Lyrics

Gowri Nandana Bala Gajanana
Gajavadana Gananatha Gajanana [ Gowri Nandana ... ]
Maha Ganapathey Paahi Gajanana
Gajanana Sai Gajanana

## Meaning

Victory to the son of Mother Gowri (Parvathi), the little Lord Ganesha, who has the face of an elephant and who is the Master of Shiva's army. Protect us O Lord, You are none other than Lord Sai.
