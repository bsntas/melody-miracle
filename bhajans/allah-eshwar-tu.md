## Meaning

O Lord Sai, You are Allah, Eshwar, Jesus, Buddha, Zohrashtra, Mahavir, Rama, Krishna. In the Treta Age, You incarnated as Lord Rama; in the Dwapara Age as Lord Krishna and in the Kali Age as Lord Sathya Sai who fosters the unity of all religions.
