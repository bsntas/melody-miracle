## Meaning

Glory to Lord Shiva, the embodiment of Truth, Goodness and Beauty, the Supreme Lord who removes our sins. Victory to the three-eyed one who wields the Trident, the one who destroys the fear of birth and death and uplifts His devotees. Protect us, O Lord.
