## Lyrics

## Meaning

O Lord Sai, You are verily the Trinity - Brahma, Vishnu and Shiva (Creator, Sustainer and Annihilator). You are the most compassionate one and the destroyer of sins. Please shower Your grace on us and protect us, O Lord.
