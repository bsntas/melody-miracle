## Lyrics

Hey Giridhari Hrudaya Vihari

Gopi Lola Gokula Bala
Hasita Vadana Sai Gopala

## Meaning

Glory to Lord Krishna who charms the Mind, who held aloft the mountain high, who is of dark complexion, the indweller of the Heart and who moves about the thick bushes of Brindavan. Lord Sai is verily Krishna who loves music, plays the Flute, delights in playing with the Gopis and has a smiling face.
