## Lyrics

Deena Dayala Sri Sai Rama
Jagadodhara Prashanthi Rama
Anatha Rakshaka Ananda Rama
Ananda Rama Sadananda Rama [ Anatha Rakshaka ... ]
Atmabhi Rama Prabhu Sai Rama

## Meaning

O Lord Sri Rama, Sai Rama, You are merciful and kind towards the weak and the meek. O Lord of Prashanthi, You have come to uplift mankind. O Rama, the always blissful one, You are the protector of those who are orphaned. O Lord Sai Rama, You are the indweller of our hearts.
