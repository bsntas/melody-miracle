## Lyrics

Nandi Vahana Nataraja Namo

Bholey Baba Sayeesha Namo

## Meaning

Recite the name of Lord Shiva who rides on the celestial Bull Nandi, has the third eye on His forehead, who is the consort of Goddess Shakthi (Parvathi). Lord Sai of Parthi is the very embodiment of Shiva and Shakthi.
