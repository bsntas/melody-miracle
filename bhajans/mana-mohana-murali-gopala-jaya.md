## Lyrics

Mana Mohana Murali Gopala Jaya Govinda Radhey Gopala
Pandari Natha Jaya Hari Vittala
Govardhana Giridhari Gopala

## Meaning

Gopala with the flute in His hands has a bewitching form, which lures the mind, one who lifted the Govardhan hill to save His devotees is also Lord Vittala of Pandharpur.
