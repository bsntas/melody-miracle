## Lyrics

Tum Ho Ram Tum Ho Shyam
Bhakthon Ke Pran Bhagawan Sai Ram
Sita Ram Radhey Shyam
Ek Prabhu Bhagawan Sai Ram [ Bhakthon Ke ... ]

## Meaning

O Lord Sai, You are the very life of Your devotees, all forms blended in one, though known as Rama, the consort of Sita and Shyama (Krishna, the Lord of Radha).
