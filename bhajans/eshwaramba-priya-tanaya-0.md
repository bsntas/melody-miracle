## Lyrics

Eshwaramba Priya Tanaya
Esha Mahesha Sri Sayeesha
Yuga Avatara Sayeesha
Poornavatara (Premavatara) Sayeesha

## Meaning

Glory to the loving son of Mother Eshwaramba, Lord Sai, the embodiment of Lord Shiva. Sai is the incarnation of the Kali Age in His complete glory. He is the very incarnation of Love.
