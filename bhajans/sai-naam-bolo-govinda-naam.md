## Lyrics

Rama Krishna Bolo Jai Buddha Sai Bolo

Prema Sai Shanthi Dayi Sathya Sai Bolo
Govinda Bolo Gopala Bolo (3)

## Meaning

Sing the name of Lord Sai who is verily Lord Govinda (Krishna). Chant the name of Lord Mahadeva (Shiva) and Panduranga. Chant the name of Lord Rama, Krishna and Buddha. Chant the name of Lord Zohrashtra, Mahavira, Jesus Christ and Allah. Chant the name of Lord Sathya Sai who is the embodiment of Love and Peace.
