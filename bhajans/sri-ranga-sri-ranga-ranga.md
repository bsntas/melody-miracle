## Lyrics

Sri Ranga Sri Ranga Ranga Ranga Sri Ranga (2)
Kaveri Thata Vasa Kasturi Ranga
Karunya Lavanya Karunanta Ranga
Jaya Panduranga Jaya Sai Ranga (2)

## Meaning

Chant the name of Lord Sriranga (Narayana) who resides on the banks of river Kaveri and who adorns the fragrant musk. He is compassionate and His beauty is enchanting. Victory to You, O Lord Panduranga who is none other than Sai.
