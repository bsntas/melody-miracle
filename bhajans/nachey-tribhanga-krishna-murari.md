## Lyrics

Nachey Tribhanga Krishna Murari
Baajey Mrudanga Manjula Bheri [ Nachey ... ]
Karunanta Ranga Yuga Avatari
Sri Sai Ranga Bhava Bhaya Haari

## Meaning

Glory to Lord Krishna who dances in the pose of tribhanga (three bends). Let the percussion instrument Mrudanga and the temple drums beat. O Lord of Compassion, the Avatar of this age, O Sai, salutations to Thee, who removes the fear of this worldly existence.
