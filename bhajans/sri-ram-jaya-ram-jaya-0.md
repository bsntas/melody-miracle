## Lyrics

Sri Ram Jaya Ram Jaya Jaya Ram Janaki Jeevana Ram
Patitha Pavana Rama Harey Jaya Raghava Sundara Ram
Deenodharana Rama Harey Jaya Maruti Sevitha Ram
Jaya Jaya Ram Jaya Raghu Ram Janaki Jeevana Ram

## Meaning

Victory to Lord Rama, the embodiment of auspiciousness, the very life of Janaki (Sita) the uplifter of downtrodden, the beautiful one from Raghu dynasty, who sanctifies the sinners and the one who is worshipped by Maruti (Hanuman).
