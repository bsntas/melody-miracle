## Lyrics

Karunamaya Deva Krupamaya Deva
Hari Om Hari Om
Hari Mera Jeevan Ka Sahara
Hari Mera Aadhaar
Krupamaya Deva

## Meaning

We chant Your Glory, O compassionate Lord Hari. You are the most merciful one and the basis and support of my life.
