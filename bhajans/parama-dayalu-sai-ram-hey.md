## Lyrics

Parama Dayalu Sai Ram (Hey)
Bhagawan Bhagawan [ (Hey) Parama Dayalu ... ]
Treta Yug Mey Dasharatha Nandana
Dwapara Yug Mey Murali Mohana
Kali Yuga Mey Sai Narayana
Ek Prabhu Anek Naam

## Meaning

O Lord Sai, You are the most merciful Lord who incarnated as Rama, son of King Dasharatha in the Treta Age. In the age of Dwapara, You incarnated as the charming and enchanting Flutist, Krishna and in this present age of Kali, as Lord Sai Narayana. You are the One Lord assuming many names and forms.
