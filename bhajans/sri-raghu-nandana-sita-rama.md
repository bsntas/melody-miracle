## Lyrics

Sri Raghu Nandana Sita Rama
Rajeeva Lochana Raja Rama
Raja (Sai) Rama
Bhava Bhaya Bhanjana Taraka Naama
Atma Rama Sai Rama
Kalimala Bhanjana Kodanda Rama

## Meaning

Worship Lord Rama of Raghu Dynasty, the consort of Sita, the one with lotus eyes, the one whose sacred name destroys the fear of the materialistic world and grants liberation, the one who is Atma Rama (inner self or higher conscience), the one who wields the bow named "Kodanda" and the one who destroys the impurities of the age of Kali. He is verily Lord Sai.
