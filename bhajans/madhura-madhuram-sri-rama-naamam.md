## Lyrics

Madhura Madhuram Sri Rama Naamam

Shirdeesha Naamam Partheesha Naamam
Sri Rama Naamam Madhurathi Madhuram

## Meaning

Chant the name of Lord Rama which is sweetness personified. Songs on His glory are unforgettably sweet. Salutations to Lord Sai, Lord of Shirdi and Puttaparthi. The name of Sai and the name of Rama are the sweetest of all.
