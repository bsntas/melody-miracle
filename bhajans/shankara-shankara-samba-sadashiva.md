## Lyrics

Shivaya Bhavaya
Shiva Bhava Hara Chandrashekhara

## Meaning

Glory to Lord Shiva, the most auspicious one who is adorned with the crescent moon and who bears the Holy Ganges on His head. He is the resident of Mount Kailasa, wields a Trident and destroys the fear of the materialistic world.
