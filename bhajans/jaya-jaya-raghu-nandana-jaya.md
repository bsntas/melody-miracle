## Lyrics

Jaya Jaya Raghu Nandana Jaya Janaki Jeevana
Sharanagatha Palana Jaya Jagadodharana [ Jaya Jaya ... ]
Rajeeva Dala Lochana Jaya Jagadanandana
Rajadhi Raja Ramachandra Sai Jaga Vandana

## Meaning

Extol the lotus-eyed son of Raghu clan, Lord Rama, the very life of Mother Janaki, the sustainer of Universe and protector of those who surrender to Him. He is the king of kings who is the source of delight to the Universe. He is verily Lord Sai who is worshipped by all.
