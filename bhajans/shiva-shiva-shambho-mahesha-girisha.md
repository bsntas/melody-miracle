Chandrakaladhara Parvathi Ramana

## Meaning

Salutations to Lord Sai who is none other than Lord Shiva, the consort of Parvathi. Salutations to the one who resides on Mount Kailasa, who moves around on Nandi, the celestial Bull, who wears the serpent as an ornament around His neck, has a third eye on the forehead, holds the sacred river Ganga and crescent moon on His head and who slayed the demon Tripura.
