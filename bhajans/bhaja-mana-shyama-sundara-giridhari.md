## Lyrics

Murali Manohara Shyama Murari

## Meaning

O Mind, chant the name of Lord Krishna, the beautiful, dark-complexioned Lord who lifted the Govardhana mountain with His little finger, who adorns a crown of peacock feathers and is draped in yellow silk. Glory to the one who plays the Divine Flute and to the Lord of Meera.
