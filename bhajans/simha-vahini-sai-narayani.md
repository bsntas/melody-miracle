## Lyrics

Simha Vahini Sai Narayani
Mata Maheshwari Durga Bhavani [ Simha Vahini ... ]
Akhilandeshwari Jaya Jagat Janani
Abhaya Pradayini Ananda Roopini
Mangala Karini Moksha Pradayini

## Meaning

Chant the name of Sai Narayani who rides on the Lion and who is known by many names as Maheshwari, Durga Bhavani, Akhilandeshwari, Jagat Janani, Abhaya Pradayini, Ananda Roopini, Mangala Karini and Moksha Pradayini.
