## Lyrics

Shankara Samba Shiva Hara Hara

Kailasa Vasa Kashi Pathey
Karuna Sagara Gowri (Parthi) Pathey

## Meaning

Glory to Lord Shankara (Shiva), the one who holds the mighty Ganges in His Locks, the one adorned with the crescent moon, the consort of Gowri and the Lord of Kashi (the city of Banaras where He is adored as Lord Viswanath). O Sai, the Lord of Parthi, You are verily Lord Shiva, You are the ocean of compassion and mercy. We pray and offer our salutations to You.
