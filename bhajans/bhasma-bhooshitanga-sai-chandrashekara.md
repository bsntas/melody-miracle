## Meaning

Glory to Lord Shiva who is besmeared with sacred ash, who is adorned with the moon on His head, who has a third eye on His forehead and who holds the Trident. O liker of music of the Sama Veda, O King among the dancers, we bow down to You, Sai Shankara, the resident of Parthi.
