## Lyrics

Siddhi Vinayaka Mangala Daata
Buddhi Pradayaka Gananatha [ Siddhi Vinayaka ... ]
Vighna Harana Prabhu Vighneshwara Tum
Gowri Nandana Hey Jaga Vandana
Namostuthey Namostuthey

## Meaning

Oh Lord Vinayaka (Lord Ganesha), You fulfill our wishes and bestow auspiciousness. You bestow knowledge to all, Oh Lord of Ganaas, You destroy the obstacles in our lives. Oh son of Goddess Gowri (Parvathi), You are worshipped by the entire Universe. Salutations to You, Oh Lord Vighneshwara.
