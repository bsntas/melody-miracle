## Lyrics

Madhava Harey Krishna Madhura Mohana
Gokula Balana Devaki Nandana
Venu Vilolana Paapa Vinashana [ Madhava Harey ... ]
Radhika Jeevana Rajeeva Nayana
Nanda Sunandana Gopi Ramana [ Venu Vilolana ... ]

## Meaning

Sing the glory of Lord Krishna, the son of Devaki and the dear child of Gokula, who plays the flute and destroys our sins. He is the lotus-eyed lord who is the very life of Radha and the ever joyful lord of Gopis.
