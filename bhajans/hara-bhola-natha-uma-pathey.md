## Lyrics

Hara Bhola Natha Uma Pathey, Shambho Shankara Pashupathey
Kailasa Vasa Chidambaresha, Neelakanta Sadashiva
Bham Bham Hara Hara Bhola Maheshwara, Parvathi Ramana Sadhashiva

## Meaning

Let us chant the divine names of Shiva or Shambho who is the consort of Uma, the blue-throated Lord who resides in mount Kailasa and Chidambaram, whose drum resounds Bham Bham, who is the consort of Parvathi.
