## Meaning

Sing the glory of Lord Ganesha, the short-statured Lord of the Ganaas (Lord Shiva's army). Protect us, O elephant-faced son of Mother Parvathi. You are the single tusked one, the epitome of good qualities, the destroyer of Maya (illusion) and the one who rides the mouse Mooshika. You are the bestower of superior knowledge, intelligence and powers. O son of Lord Shiva, You are verily the form of Om (Pranava).
