## Lyrics

Sai Hai Jeevan Jeevan Sathya Sai
Sai Mera Jeevan Sahara [ Sai Hai ... ]
Terey Bina Sai Sab Hai Andhera
Paar Karo Merey Jeevan Naiya
Charan Lagalo Mujhey Sai Kanhaiya

## Meaning

O Lord Sathya Sai, You are my life. You are the constant companion and the support in my life. Without Thee there is darkness (ignorance) in my life. Kindly help me to cross the ocean of life and death. O Lord Sai Krishna, accept me at Your Lotus Feet.
