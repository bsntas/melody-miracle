## Lyrics

Partheeshwara Sai Baba Aao Aao Sai Nandalala
Jagadeesha Daya Sagara
Paramesha Krupa Sagara
Jai Jai Sai Ram, Bolo Jai Jai Sai Ram

## Meaning

O Sai Nandalala of Puttaparthi, come into our hearts. You are the Lord of the Universe, the ocean of compassion and mercy. All praise be unto Thee, O Lord Sai Ram.
