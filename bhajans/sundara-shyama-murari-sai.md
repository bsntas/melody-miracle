## Lyrics

Sundara Shyama Murari Sai
Gopala Sri Hari Sri Hari (2)
Parama Niranjana Madhava Sai
Sakala Charachara Vyapaka Sai [ Gopala Sri ... ]

## Meaning

Sing the praises of beautiful Lord Sai, who is none other than Lord Krishna also known as Shyama Murari, Gopala, Sri Hari and Madhava. Lord Sai is eternally pure and encompasses all the animate and inanimate things.
