## Lyrics

Jaya Rama Mohana Ram
Jaya Rama Sri Raghu Ram
Jaya Rama Sai Ram [ Jaya Rama Mohana ... ]

## Meaning

Victory to the enchanting Lord Rama of Raghu dynasty whose consort is Sita (Janaki). Victory to Lord Sai who is none other than Lord Rama.
