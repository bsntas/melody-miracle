Ghana Shyama Shyama Shyama Jaya Jaya Radhey (Sai) Gopala [ Jaya Nanda ... ]

## Meaning

Chant the divine names of Govinda and Madhava (names of Lord Krishna) who protects cowherds and who slayed the demon Keshi. Victory to the beloved of Nanda and the one who confers liberation to the devotees. O Lord You are adored as the beloved of Radha, as Giridhari, (the one who lifted the Govardhana mountain), the dark-hued one who has now incarnated as Lord Sai of Parthi.
