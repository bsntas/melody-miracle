Brahmananda Se Hrudaya Bharo

## Meaning

Meditate on the divine word 'Soham' which means 'I am That'. Meditate on Sai who is our true and Divine Mentor. Soham is none other than Brahma, Vishnu and Shiva and the Atma (Higher self or Higher Conscience). Fill your hearts with Divine Bliss.
