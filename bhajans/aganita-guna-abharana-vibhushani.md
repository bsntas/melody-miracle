## Lyrics

Aganita Guna Abharana Vibhushani
Adi Parashakthi Devi Namo
Sakala Charachara Srishti Samrakshini
Jagadeeshwari Jaganmata Namo

## Meaning

O Supreme Goddess Shakthi (Parvathi), salutations to Thee, who has immeasurable good qualities as Your ornaments and who protects and sustains both animate and inanimate things of this creation. O Goddess of the universe, salutations to Thee.
