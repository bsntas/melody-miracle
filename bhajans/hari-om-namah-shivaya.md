## Lyrics

Hari Om Namah Shivaya
Shiva Shiva Shankara Hara Parameshwara Sayeeshwaraya Namah Om (2)

## Meaning

Chant the mantra "Hari Om Namah Shivaya". Let us offer our obeisance to Lord Shiva who bestows auspiciousness and is the Lord of the Lords. He has presently incarnated as Lord Sathya Sai.
