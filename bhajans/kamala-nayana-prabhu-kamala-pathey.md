Kamaleshwara Prabhu Kamala Natha

## Meaning

Our salutations to the lotus-eyed Lord who is the consort of Kamala (Goddess Lakshmi). The Lord who is the enchanter of Kamala, seated on the lotus from whose navel arises the lotus of creation, is Lord Narayana, the Lord of Kamala (Goddess Kamala).
