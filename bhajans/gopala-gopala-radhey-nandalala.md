## Lyrics

Gopala Gopala Radhey Nandalala
Govinda Gopala Radhey Nandalala [ Gopala ... ]
Brindavana Sanchara Radhey Nandalala
Murali Manohara Radhey Nandalala

## Meaning

Chant the name of Gopala, the one who is the beloved of Radha and the darling son of Nanda, the one who resides in Brindavan, the one with an enchanting form and who holds the flute.
