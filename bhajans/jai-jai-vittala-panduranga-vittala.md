## Lyrics

Jai Jai Vittala Panduranga Vittala
Pundarika Varada Panduranga Vittala
Jai Jai Vittala Panduranga Vittala [ Pundarika ... ]

## Meaning

Sing the praises of Lord Vittala, standing on a brick. Victory to Lord Panduranga Vittala who blessed His devotee Pundarika.
