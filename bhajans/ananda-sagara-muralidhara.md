## Lyrics

Ananda Sagara Muralidhara
Meera Prabhu Radhey Shyam Venu Gopala [ Ananda Sagara ... ]
Nanda Yashoda Ananda Kishora
Jai Jai Gokula Bala Jai Venu (Sai) Gopala

## Meaning

O Lord Krishna, You are the ocean of bliss and the one who plays captivating music on the flute. You are the Lord of Meera and Radha. Glory to Thee, the blissful son of Nanda and Yashoda. Victory to the Divine child of Gokula who has incarnated as Lord Sai.
