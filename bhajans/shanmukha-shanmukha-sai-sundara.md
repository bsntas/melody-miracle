## Lyrics

Shanmukha Shanmukha Sai Sundara
Shiva (Guru) Sharavana Bhava Om
Mangala Gowri Shankara Nandana [ Shiva (Guru) ... ]
Parthi Vihari Pranavakaari [ Shiva (Guru) ... ]

## Meaning

Glory to Lord Sai who resides in Parthi and who is the personification of the primordial sound "Om", who is worshipped as Lord Shanmukha and as the Guru, who is the blessed son of Mother Gowri and Lord Shankara.
