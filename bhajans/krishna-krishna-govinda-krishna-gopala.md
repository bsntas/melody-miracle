## Lyrics

Krishna Krishna Govinda Krishna Gopala Bala Krishna
Nanda Nandana Bhaktha Chandhana Gaana Lola Krishna
Sundara Vadana Saroja Nayana Radha Priya Krishna
Yadava Krishna Yashoda Krishna Sathya Sai Krishna

## Meaning

Glory to Lord Krishna, the son of Nanda and the one who is dear to His devotees and delights in music. He is Lotus eyed, with a charming countenance and is dear to Radha. He belongs to the Yadava clan, and is the son of Yashoda who has presently incarnated as Sathya Sai Krishna.
