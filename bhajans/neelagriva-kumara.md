## Meaning

O Lord Ganesha, You are the son of Shiva who is hailed as Neelakanta (blue-throated one) and Mahadeva (Lord of the Lords). You command the army of Lord Shiva (Ganaas). You are the very basis of the universe (also the presiding deity of the Mooladhara Chakra). We offer our prayers to the Lord of Parthi, Lord Sai, who is verily Ganesha, to protect us at all times.
