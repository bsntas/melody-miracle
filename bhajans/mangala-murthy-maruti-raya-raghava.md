## Lyrics

Mangala Murthy Maruti Raya Raghava Priya Jaya Hanuman
Anjani Putra Anjaneya Vayu Kumara Jaya Hanuman
Sita Shoka Vinasha Kaaraka Rama Dootha Jaya Hanuman
Sakala Mangala Moola Kaarana Sarva Mangala Jaya Hanuman
Jai Jai Hanuman Jaya Hanuman Jai Jai Hanuman Jaya Hanuman (2)

## Meaning

Victory to Hanuman, the embodiment of auspiciousness who is also known as Maruti Raya (King of Monkeys), who adores Lord Rama and is the son of Anjani and Vayu (Wind god). He is the one who removed the sorrow of Sita, the messenger of Rama and the one who bestows auspiciousness.
