## Lyrics

Bhavani Nandana Bala Gajanana
Pranava Swaroopa Paahi
Hey Parthi Pureeshwara Sai

## Meaning

O young Lord Ganesh, the elephant-headed son of Mother Bhavani (Parvathi), please protect us, O Lord, the embodiment of the primordial sound Om (Pranava). You are verily Lord Sai of Parthi.
