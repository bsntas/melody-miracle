## Meaning

O Lord Ganesha (elephant-headed God), You are the Supreme Lord and the chief of the Ganaas (demigods), You are the remover of obstacles. You exist in the thoughtless and pure mind. You are attributeless and spotless who delights the mind and destroys the worldly miseries.
