## Lyrics

Raghupathi Raghava Raja Ram Patitha Pavana Sita Ram
Sita Ram Jaya Sita Ram Bhaja Pyaarey Tu Sita Ram
Eshwara Allah Terey Naam Sab Ko Sanmati Dey Bhagawan
(Hey Bhagawan Hey Bhagawan Sab Ko Sanmati Dey Bhagawan)
(Sri Ram Jaya Ram Jaya Jaya Ram Sita Ram Jaya Sita Ram)

## Meaning

Victory to You, Lord Rama, king of the Raghu dynasty, and the Lord of Sita, the protector of the poor and helpless. Sing praises to our beloved Lord Sita Ram, known also as Ishwara and Allah. O Lord, please endow us with intelligence.
