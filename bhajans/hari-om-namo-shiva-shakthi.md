## Lyrics

Hari Om Namo Shiva Shakthi Namo
Sadguru Sri Sathya Sai Namo
Hari Om Namo Sita Rama Namo
Jaya Sadguru Sri Sathya Sai Namo
Hari Om Namo Radhey Shyam Namo
Jaya Sadguru Sri Sathya Sai Namo

## Meaning

Let us offer our salutations to our sacred preceptor Sathya Sai who is none other than the incarnation of Shiva and Energy Principle Shakthi, Sita Rama as well as Radhey Shyam.
