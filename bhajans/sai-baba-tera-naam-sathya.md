## Meaning

You are called Sai Baba, Sathya Sai Baba! You are Brahma (Creator) and Vishnu (Sustainer). You are Guru Nanak, Jesus Christ, Buddha, Zohrashtra, Allah and Mahavir. All are Sai Bhagawan. Your name is Sai Baba; Your Name is Allah Sai & Maulah Sai; Your name is Jesus Sai & Buddha Sai; Your name is Sai Baba and Sai Rama.
