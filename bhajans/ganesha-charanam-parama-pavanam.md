## Lyrics

Ganesha Charanam Parama Pavanam Sathya Sainam Gajananam
Nithya Smaranam Parama Pavanam Sathya Sainam Gajananam
Sadguru Charanam Parama Pavanam Sathya Sainam Gajananam
Bhava Bhaya Haranam Parama Pavanam Sathya Sainam Gajananam

## Meaning

Let us bow in reverence to Lord Ganesha, let us bow in absolute purity to the name of Sai, to the elephant-faced Lord Ganesha. Constantly remember the purifying name of Sai and Lord Ganesha. The sacred feet of Sadguru are purifying, so is the name of Sai and Ganesha which removes the fear of the cycle of birth and death.
