## Lyrics

Sundara Vadani Suguna Manohari
Manda Haasa Mukha Shashi Vadani
Chandana Kumkuma Alankruta Vadani
Sai Maheshwari Raja Rajeshwari
Om Shakthi Om Om Shakthi Om Om Shakthi Om Om Shakthi Om (2)

## Meaning

Chant the name 'Om Shakthi Om' repeatedly, which extols the Divine Mother as Maheshwari (great Goddess), virtuous Goddess of Emperors, having a blissful smile with the sandal paste and vermillion on Her beautiful face and who is verily Mother Sai.
