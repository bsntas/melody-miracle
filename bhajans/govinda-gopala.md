## Meaning

Chant the name of Lord Krishna, also known as Govinda and Gopala, the one who fosters cowherds and people. Victory to the enchanting flute player who bestows liberation to devotees, the destroyer of demon Madhu and beloved of Yashoda, Nanda and the Yadu clan.
