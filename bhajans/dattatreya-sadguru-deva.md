## Lyrics

Dattatreya Sadguru Deva
Dehi Dehi Tava Sri Paada Seva

Pannaga Bhooshana Shiva Mahadeva
Dehi Dehi Tava Sri Paada Seva (2)

## Meaning

Oh God and Noble teacher Dattatreya, grant us to serve Your Feet. You are Brahma the eternal God, Vishnu, Narayana who is indweller in all. You are Shiva, Mahadeva adorned with the serpent.
