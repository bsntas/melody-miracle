## Lyrics

Janani Ma Sai Janani Ma (3)
Prema Vahini Ma Gnana Vahini Ma
Dharma Vahini Ma Dhyana Vahini Ma
Prashanthi Vahini Chira Sukha Dayini
Jaya Jaya Sai Ma

## Meaning

O Mother Sai, You are verily the free flowing rivers of Love, Wisdom, Righteousness, Meditation and Supreme Peace reaching out to the far corners of the world and conferring long lasting Happiness on Everyone. Glory to Thee, O Mother.
