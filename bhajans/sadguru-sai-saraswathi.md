## Meaning

Worship the Supreme Teacher, Lord Sai who is verily Goddess Saraswathi, the consort of Lord Brahma (the Creator). Mother Saraswathi is the auspicious one, the embodiment of all learning and wisdom,  the one who is adept in the art of producing sweet melodies on the Veena (string instrument) and the bestower of all knowledge and who has the celestial swan as Her vehicle.
