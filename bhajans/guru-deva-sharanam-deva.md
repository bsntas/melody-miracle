## Lyrics

Guru Deva Sharanam Deva
Paahi Prabho Sharanam Deva [ Guru Deva ... ]
Sundara Roopa Sri Sai Deva
Sharanam Sharanam Sadguru Deva

## Meaning

We surrender to our Guru and God, Lord Sai. Protect us O Lord, Your form is beauty itself. We surrender to You, our true guru.
