## Meaning

Salutations to Lord Shiva, also known as Maheshwara, who resides in the holy mountains of Kailasa. We sing Your glory, O Lord Sai, You are the Lord and protector of all creatures in the three worlds. (According to Hindu mythology there are three worlds, Sky, Earth and Sub-terrain.)
