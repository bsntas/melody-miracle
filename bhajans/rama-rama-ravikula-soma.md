## Lyrics

Rama Rama Ravikula Soma 
Kodanda Rama Pattabhi Rama
Sai Rama Saketha Rama
Jaya Jaya Rama Janaki Rama

## Meaning

O lotus-eyed Lord Rama, the Crown Prince of Solar dynasty, the resident of the heart of devotees and holder of the 'Kodanda' bow, You are merciful and beautiful to look at. Victory to Lord Rama consort of Mother Sita. You are none other than Lord Sai.
