## Lyrics

Jayatu Jayatu Rama
Janani Janaki Rama
Janana Marana Bhaya Klesha Vinashaka
Raghupathi Sita Rama Rama Ramethi Rama

## Meaning

Victory to Lord Rama, consort of Mother Janaki, the one who destroys the fear of birth and death, the Emperor of the Raghu dynasty.
