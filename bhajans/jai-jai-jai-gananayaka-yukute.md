## Lyrics

Jai Jai Jai Gananayaka, Yukute wo Terasu Kami yo
Tataeyo Eichi no Ganesha wo Shinri e Michibiku Kamiyo
Misakaeare Kami (Sai) no Hikari ni
Eichi to Chikara no Shiva no Mikoyo

## Meaning

Victory to Thee O Lord Ganesha, please illuminate the path before me. Praise the all-knowing Ganesha who will us guide us to truth. Under the light of God, we will prosper. Bow to the Wisdom and Power of the Son of Shiva.
