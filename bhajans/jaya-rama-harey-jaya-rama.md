## Lyrics

Jaya Rama Harey Jaya Rama Harey Jaya Sita Rama Harey
Jaya Krishna Harey Jaya Krishna Harey Jaya Radha Krishna Harey
Jaya Sai Harey Jaya Sai Harey Jaya Sai Baba Harey

## Meaning

Sing the Glory of Rama whose consort is Mother Sita, Krishna whose consort is Radha and Sai who is also known as Sai Baba.
