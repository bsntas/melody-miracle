## Lyrics

Jagadambe Devi Bhavani
Jaganmatey Shakthi Durge (2) [ Jagadambe ... ]
Kumara Gananathamba Kalyani Shyamalamba
Janani Durge Varadey Mata
(Amba) Uma Maheshwari Kalavathi [ Jaganmatey ... ]

## Meaning

Bhavani or Durga is the Goddess and Mother of the entire Universe. Chant the various names of Mother Goddess, Janani, Uma Maheshwari, Sharada, Kalyani, Shyamala, Durga and Kalavathi who confer boons to the devotees.
