## Lyrics

Rajeeva Lochana Jaya Jaya Rama
Raghukula Bhooshana Jaya Raghu Rama
Dasharatha Nandana Lavanya Rama

Janaki Vallabha Sugunabhi Rama
Jagadodhara Sathya Sai Rama

## Meaning

Victory to Lord Rama, the lotus-eyed one, the crown jewel of Raghu dynasty, the beloved son of Dasharatha and the consort of Sita. Victory to the virtuous one who protects His devotees and the one who is ever blissful. He is none other than Lord Sai who is the liberator of the entire Universe.
