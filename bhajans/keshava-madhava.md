## Lyrics

Keshava Madhava
Govinda Gopala
Hari Bol Hari Bol Hari Hari Bol
Keshava Madhava Govinda Bol

## Meaning

O mind, chant the name of Hari, Keshava, Madhava, Govinda and Gopala incessantly.
