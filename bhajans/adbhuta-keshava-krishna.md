## Meaning

Glory to Lord Krishna, the miraculous one who destroyed the demon Keshi. Glory to the enchanting Lord who resides in the lotus of our hearts. Wearing a peacock feather on His Hair, He mesmerizes us with His flute. He is the beloved son of Yashoda who steals butter and our hearts.
