## Lyrics

Muralidhara Mana Mohana Madhusudhana Krishna
Natanagara Jaya Madhava Hey Keshava Krishna Krishna Krishna [ Muralidhara ...  ]
Bansidhara Dukha Bhanjana Yadu Nandana Krishna
Radhey Govinda Sai Mukunda Gopi Jana Priya Krishna

## Meaning

O Lord Sai Krishna, son of Yadu Dynasty, the one holding the Flute, You are also known as Madhusudhana, Keshava and adored by Your devotees, whose sorrows were driven away by You.
