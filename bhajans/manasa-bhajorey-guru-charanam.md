## Meaning

O mind, adhere to the feet of the Guru by singing His glory and take refuge under His feet, which enables us to cross the unconquerable ocean of life. Glory be to the venerable Guru Sainath. Chant repeatedly 'Om Nama Shivaya', 'Arunachala Shiva'. Prostrations to Baba who is the embodiment of Om.
