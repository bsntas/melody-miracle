## Lyrics

Pranava Swaroopa Paahi Gajanana Vighna Nasha Vinayaka
Eka Dantha Hey Gunavanta Vighna Nasha Vinayaka
Shritha Jana Palana Sindhura Vadana Vighna Nasha Vinayaka
Mahadeva Shiva Gowri Nandana Vighna Nasha Vinayaka

## Meaning

Glory to the elephant-faced Lord Ganesha, who is the embodiment of the primordial sound 'Om', destroyer of impediments in the path of seekers, one without any leader, the single tusked one who is the storehouse of sacred qualities, the protector of those who seek His refuge, the son of Lord Shiva and Mother Gowri.
