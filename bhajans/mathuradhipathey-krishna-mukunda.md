## Meaning

Glory to the Lord of Mathura, Lord Krishna, also known as Mukunda and Madhusudhana (the slayer of demon Madhu). He has a dark complexion, plays enchanting music on the Flute, the crown jewel of the Yadava clan and is lotus-eyed. Victory to Lord Sai, Lord of Parthi, who fills our minds with bliss. He is verily Lord Krishna.
