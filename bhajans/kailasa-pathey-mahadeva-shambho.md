## Meaning

Praise be to Lord Shiva, the Lord of Lords, the dweller of Mount Kailasa and the bestower of auspiciousness. Chant the mantra 'Om Nama Shivaya' repeatedly. Shankara who is also Lord Sai bestows welfare, the destroyer of Tripura and who negates the fear of rebirth, the one who causes the final delusion.
