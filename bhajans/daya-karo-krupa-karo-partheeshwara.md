## Lyrics

Daya Karo Krupa Karo Partheeshwara Sai Deva
Janam Janam Tum Saath Rahorey
Sai Nath Mahadeva
Daya Karo Krupa Karo Partheeshwara Sai Deva
Shirdeeshwara Sai Deva Partheeshwara Sai Deva

## Meaning

O Lord Sai, the Lord of Parthi be compassionate and merciful towards us and accompany us from birth to birth. You are the Lord of the Lords and the resident of Shirdi and Parthi.
