## Lyrics

Allah Ho Tum Eshwar Bhi Tum Mahavir Nanak Ram
Partheesha Sai Shankara Tum Hi Ho Merey Pran [ Allah Ho ... ]
Zohrashtra Yesu Buddha Tum Ho Sab Ke Palanhaar
Satchidananda Sai Tera Brindavana Shubh Dham

## Meaning

O Sai who dwells in the sacred abode of Brindavan, You are Allah, Eshwar, Mahavira, Nanak, Ram. O Sai Shankara of Parthi, You are our very life, our protector and the embodiment of existence, knowledge and bliss.
