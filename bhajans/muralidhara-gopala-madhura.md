## Lyrics

Muralidhara Gopala Madhura
Megha Roopa Hari Manasa Chora [ Muralidhara ... ]
Radha Madhava Kunja Vihara
Ananda Ghana Roopa Naama Madhura (3)

## Meaning

O sweet Lord Gopala (Krishna), with the flute in Your hands and with the complexion of dark clouds, You steal the hearts of devotees. You are the beloved of Radha. With beautiful and sweet name and form You stroll in Brindavan.
