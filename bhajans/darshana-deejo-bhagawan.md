## Meaning

Grant me the vision of Your Divine Form O Lord, the supremely merciful Sai, You are Infinite and Your beautiful Form is unsurpassed and indescribable. Thou art the grantor of joy and happiness to those who have come to Thee for refuge. Your Form is Love and You are the bestower of Peace. O most Supreme Lord Sri Sathya Sai, You are the embodiment of Truth, Awareness and Bliss.
