## Lyrics

Ram Rahim Ko Bhajanewaale Terey Poojari Baba
Tera Naam Ek Sahara (3)
Tum Hi Ho Geeta Tum Hi Ramayan
Tum Hi Ho Veda Quran [ Tera Naam ... ]

## Meaning

O Lord Sai Baba, for the devotees of Lord Rama (Hindus) and of Rahim (Islam), Your name is the only refuge. You are the very Form of the teachings of Holy scriptures, Bhagawad Gita, Ramayana, the four Vedas and the Quran.
