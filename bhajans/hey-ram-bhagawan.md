## Meaning

Let Your benedictions be on us devotees, O Lord Rama. You are the ocean of compassion and refuge of the forlorn. Please shower Your grace on us, O merciful one. You are the very life of Mother Janaki. You are none other than Lord Sai, the Lord of Parthi.
