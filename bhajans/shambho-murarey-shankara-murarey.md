## Lyrics

Shambho Murarey Shankara Murarey
Murahara Phani Dhara Shankara Murarey
Shambho Murarey Shankara Murarey [ Murahara Phani ... ]

## Meaning

Sing the Glory of Lord Shankara (Shiva), the slayer of the three demons of Tripura and the one who adorns the serpent around His neck.
