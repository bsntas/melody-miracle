## Lyrics

Jaya Nandalala Jai Jai Gopala
Mohana Moorat Sai Gopala [ Jaya Nandalala ... ]
Jaya Brindavana Madana Gopala
Jaya Partheeshwara Sai Nandalala [ Mohana Moorat ... ]

## Meaning

Praise be to Lord Gopala (Krishna), the son of Nanda and the most charming one who moves about in Brindavan. Lord Sai, the Lord of Parthi has a captivating form and is verily Lord Krishna.
