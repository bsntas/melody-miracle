## Lyrics

Shankha Chakra Peetambara Dhari Karuna Sagara Rama Murari
Shankha Chakra Peetambara Dhari Karuna Sagara Krishna Murari
Shankha Chakra Peetambara Dhari Karuna Sagara Sai Murari

## Meaning

Glory to Lord Narayana, who bears the Conch and the Discus, who wears yellow silk and who incarnated as the ever merciful Lord Rama, Krishna and Sai.
