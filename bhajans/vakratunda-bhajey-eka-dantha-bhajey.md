## Lyrics

Vakratunda Bhajey Eka Dantha Bhajey Phaalachandra Bhajey Gananatha 
Gananatha Gananatha Maam Paahi Prabho Gananatha

Bimbadhara Sutha Gananatha 
Sai Vinayaka Gananatha

## Meaning

Chant the name of Lord Ganesha, the one with a curved trunk, a single tusk and who is adorned with the moon on His head. O the Lord of Shiva's army, please protect us. O Lord Ganesha, the beloved son of Mother Gowri and Lord Shiva who dons the crescent moon, You are none other than our Lord Sathya Sai.
