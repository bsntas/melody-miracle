## Meaning

Glory to the elephant-faced Lord Vinayaka (Ganesha) who is the master, the son of Mother Gowri and the one who has Modaka (a ball shaped sweet produced out of coconut, jaggery and rice) in His hand, the one who has a serene appearance and who destroys hurdles. He is verily Sai, the Supreme Lord residing at Puttaparthi.
