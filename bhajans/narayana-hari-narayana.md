## Lyrics

Narayana Hari Narayana

Neeraja Nayana Neeradi Shayana
Narada Sannuta Namo Narayana

## Meaning

Glory to You O Lord Narayana, You are the final refuge of all beings, the lion-faced Narasimha. Salutations to Thee, O lotus-eyed one who reclines on the ocean and who is praised by Sage Narada.
