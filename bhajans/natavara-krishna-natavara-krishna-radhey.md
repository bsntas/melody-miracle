## Lyrics

Natavara Krishna Natavara Krishna Radhey Nandalala
Harey Harey Madhava Harey Gopika Lola
Krishna Ghana Ghana Neela Krishna Gokula Bala
Harey Harey Madhava Hara Gopika Lola

## Meaning

Pray to Krishna, the little prankster, who is the soul of Radha. Hey Madhava (Lord of Lakshmi)you are the enchanter of the Gopis of Brindavan. The hue of dark blue makes you the endearing child of Gokul. Hail Krishna who showered eternal bliss on the Gopis.
