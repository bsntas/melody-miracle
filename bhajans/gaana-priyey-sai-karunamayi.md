## Lyrics

Gaana Priyey Sai Karunamayi
Kadambari Amba Katyayani [ Gaana Priyey ... ]
Hari Vallabhey Sukha Dayini

Bhuvaneshwari (Amba) Jaganmohini
Sri Chakra Vasini Sayeeshwari

## Meaning

The Merciful Mother Sai is delighted by Music. Chant the names of the Deities Kadambari, Amba and Katyayani (different names of Goddess Parvathi). O Goddess Lakshmi, the beloved of Lord Hari (Vishnu), You are the bestower of happiness and all comforts. O Goddess Narayani, You are the embodiment of the Primodial Sound Om. You are the Goddess of the entire world and the one who beautifies the world. O Sayeeshwari, You reside in the Sri Chakra.
