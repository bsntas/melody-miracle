## Lyrics

Radha Ke Natavara Lal Kanhaiya
Gokula Bala Goparipala [ Radha Ke ... ]
Nanda Kumara Raasa Vilola
Meera Ke Prabhu Nanda Lal Kanhaiya

## Meaning

Lord Krishna (Kanhaiya), the Lord of Radha and Meera (devotees), the child of Gokula (city), is the sustainer of all beings. The darling son of Nanda, Lord Krishna, is fond of the Raasa (divine dance depicting the ecstasy of the union of the individual souls with the Supreme One).
