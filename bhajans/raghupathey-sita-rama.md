## Meaning

Victory to the Lord Rama, the Lord of the Raghu dynasty, who is the life-breath of Mother Sita. Victory to the lotus-eyed one who is the nectarine essence of the Solar dynasty, the one who wields the Kodanda bow and the one who destroyed the demon Ravana.
