## Lyrics

Shambho Shankara Deva
Bholey (Sai) Baba Mahadeva
Paahi Prabho Sharanam Deva (2) [ Bholey Baba ... ]
Hara Hara Gangey Mahadeva (3)

## Meaning

Glory to Lord Shiva who bears the Holy river Ganga and who is the Lord of the Lords, Mahadeva. We offer our salutations and surrender to You, O Lord Sai Baba. You are verily the embodiment of Lord Shiva.
