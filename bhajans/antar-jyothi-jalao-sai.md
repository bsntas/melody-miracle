## Lyrics

Antar Jyothi Jalao Sai
Brahma Sanathani Daya Karo
Daya Karo Hey Poorna Jyothirmayi
Shiva Shakthimayi Krupa Karo
Deena Dayamayi Patitha Pavani
Sai Jagat Janani Raksha (Krupa) Karo

## Meaning

Oh Mother Sai! You have neither beginning nor end. Kindly illumine our soul and shower your mercy. Oh Effulgent Mother of the Universe! You are the form of Shiva and Shakthi and merciful to the deprived. Kindly protect us.
