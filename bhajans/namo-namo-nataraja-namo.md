## Meaning

I bow down to Lord Shiva who is the Lord of the cosmic dance. He is the doer of good, Shambho, who has matted locks on His head, the pure one who bears the holy river Ganga on His hair. He is one who has killed the pride of the demons. He is one who resides in Puttaparthi as Sai. He is full of infinite compassion.
