## Lyrics

Lingodhbhavakara Lingeshwara
Partheeshwara Maam Paahi Prabho
Paahi Prabho Maam Dehi Vibho

## Meaning

Protect us O Sai, the Lord of Parthi, You are Lingeshwara and the one who creates Lingam out of His own Self.
