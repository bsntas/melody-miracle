## Lyrics

Daya Karo Sadguru Sai Daya Karo
Shirdi Nivasa Sai Daya Karo
Parthi Nivasa Sai Daya Karo [ Daya Karo ... ]
Meera Ke Prabhu Giridhara Naagara
Tum Ho Brahma Eshwara
Radha Kantha Parvathi Ramana
Deena Bandhava Sai Daya Karo [ Parthi Nivasa ... ]

## Meaning

O Lord Sai, our divine Preceptor who dispells the darkness of ignorance, the resident of Shirdi and Parthi, please shower Your mercy. O Lord Krishna, the one who held aloft the mountain to save the devotees, You are the Lord of Meera. You are Brahma (creator) and Eshwara (destroyer - Lord Shiva). O Radha's beloved and the enchanter of Goddess Parvathi, You are the Saviour of the helpless and the destitute. O Lord Sai, the dweller of Parthi, shower Your compassion on us.
