## Lyrics

Krishna Radha Jaya Krishna Radha
Nanda Gopa Bala Navaneetha Chora [ Krishna Radha ... ]
Krishna Radha Jaya Krishna Radha (3) [ Nanda Gopa ... ]

## Meaning

Chant the name of Krishna who is dear to Radha, the son of Nanda and the one who stole butter (worldly desires of devotees).
