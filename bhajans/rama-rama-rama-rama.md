## Lyrics

Rama Rama Rama Rama Rama Naama Tarakam
Rama Krishna Vasudeva Bhakthi Mukthi Dayakam [ Rama Rama ... ]
Janaki Manoharam Sakala Loka Nayakam
Shankaradi Sevyamana Divya Naama Keertanam

## Meaning

Chant the sacred name of Lord Rama which liberates one from the cycle of birth and death. Chant the name of Lord Krishna who bestows devotion and liberation. Chant the name of Lord Rama, the Lord of all the worlds and the one who pleases Mother Janaki (Sita). Sing the beautiful name of Lord Rama whose name is chanted even by Lord Shankara (Shiva).
