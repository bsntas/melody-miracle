## Lyrics

Abhayankara Sai Shiva
Avinashi Naga Bhooshana [ Abhayankara ... ]
Hara Hara Shambho Haalaahala Dhara
Pinaka Vaibhava Sai Shiva

## Meaning

Chant the name of Lord Sai who destroys the fear of birth and death. He is verily Lord Shiva who is eternal and indestructible, and swallowed the haalaa-hala poison to protect the world. He is adorned with the cobra and the Pinaka bow. Glory to Sai Shiva.
