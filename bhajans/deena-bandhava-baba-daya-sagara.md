## Lyrics

Deena Bandhava Baba Daya Sagara
Patitha Pavana Sai Karunakara [ Deena Bandhava ... ]
Sathya Sai Prema Sai Shanthi Dharma Sindhu Sai
Jeevan Ka Aadhaar Sai Janam Janam Bandhu Sai [ Patitha Pavana ... ]

## Meaning

O Baba, the ocean of compassion, You are the kinsman to destitute, uplifter of afflicted. You are Sathya Sai whose form is Truth and Righteousness, refuge of all lives, father, mother and perpetual companion from birth to birth.
