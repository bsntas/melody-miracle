## Lyrics

Sai Shankara
Narayana Bhagawan
Tum Ho Merey Pran (Baba) (2)
Deejo Merey Prabhu Shanthi Mukthi
Sikhao Prabhu Mujhey Prem Aur Bhakthi
Shirdi Roopa Baba (Parthi Pureeshwara) Hey Bhagawan [ Tum Ho ... ]

## Meaning

O Lord Sai of Parthi and Shirdi, You are Lord Shankara and Lord Narayana, You are my life. O Lord, bestow me with Peace and Liberation and teach me Love and Devotion.
