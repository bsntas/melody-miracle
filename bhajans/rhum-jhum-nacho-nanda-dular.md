## Lyrics

Rhum Jhum Nacho Nanda Dular
Madhava Murahara Madana Gopal [ Rhum Jhum ... ]
Radha Manohara Natavara Lal
Govinda Giridhara Murali Gopal

## Meaning

Chant the many names of Lord Krishna - Madhava, Natavara, Gopala and Govinda. Pray to the Divine Dancer, the beloved child of Nanda. Glory to the Lord of Radha, the destroyer of demon, Mura and the mischievous enchanter with the flute who protected His devotees by lifting the mountain.
