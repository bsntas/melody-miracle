## Lyrics

Nanda Kishora Narayana
Navaneetha Chora Narayana [ Nanda Kishora ... ]
Narahari Roopa Narayana
Naada Swaroopa Narayana

## Meaning

You are the son of Nanda and are Lord Vishnu, who is the refuge for all beings, You are Krishna who stole butter, (symbolically, devotee's heart). You are the lion God Narasimha and you are the form of divine music.
