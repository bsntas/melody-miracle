## Lyrics

Panduranga Panduranga Vittala Paahey
Deena Natha Karuna Natha
Sai Natha Harey
Pandari Natha Panduranga
Vittala Paahey

## Meaning

Protect us, O Lord Panduranga. O Lord Vittala, You are the protector of the meek and helpless. You are the merciful Lord. O Sai Natha, You are verily the Lord of Pandharpur. Please protect us.
