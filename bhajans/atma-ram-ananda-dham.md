## Lyrics

Atma Ram Ananda Dham
Achyutha Ram Ayodhya Ram
Sita Ram Sathya Sai Ram
Sundara Ram Sugunabhi Ram
Sai Ram Sai Ram Sai Ram Ram (3)

## Meaning

Worship the beautiful Form of Lord Rama, the resident of Ayodhya who is indweller of our heart and who confers bliss. Chant the name of Ram, the Lord of Sita and the virtuous one. Chant the name of Lord Sai who is none other than Lord Rama.
