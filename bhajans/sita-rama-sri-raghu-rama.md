## Lyrics

Sita Rama Sri Raghu Rama
Daasharathey Hey Jaya Rama
Atma Rama Aganita Guna Dhama
Pavana Naama Parandhama

## Meaning

Salutations to Lord Rama from Raghu dynasty, who is the consort of Mother Sita and the son of King Dasharatha. Lord Rama is the one with blemishless character, whose name is sacred and the one who is the supreme Lord.
