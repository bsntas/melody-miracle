## Lyrics

Jagadeeshwari Daya Karo Ma
Shiva Shankari Krupa Karo Ma
Sayeeshwari Raksha Karo Ma
Partheeshwari Bhalaa Karo Ma [ Shiva Shankari ... ]

## Meaning

Mercy on us, O Mother Goddess of the Universe, may Your benedictions be upon us, O Shankari, consort of Lord Shiva, shower Your blessings and protect us O Sayeeshwari, the Goddess of Parthi.
