## Meaning

O Lord Sai, You are Lord Rama, Rahim, Krishna, Buddha, Zohrashtra, Jesus Christ and Mahavira. You foster all religions and is the very embodiment of Love. You are the protector of all and You preach the oneness of all faiths. Glory to Thee, the most merciful Lord Sai.
