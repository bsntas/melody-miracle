## Lyrics

Dum Dum Dum Dum Damaru Bajey
Bhola Nath Shiva Shambho Bhajey
Hara Sai Nath Shiva Shambho Bhajey
Ghana Ghana Ghana Ghana Ghanta Bajey
Gowri Nath Shiva Shambho Bhajey [ Hara Sai Nath ... ]

## Meaning

In ecstacy, the drum (Damaru) beats "Dum Dum Dum". It plays in the worship of Lord Sai and Lord Shiva, the easy-to-be-pleased Lord and the destroyer of ignorance. The gong rings out the sounds "Ghan Ghan Ghan". Worship the Lord of Goddess Parvathi (the consort of Lord Shiva).
