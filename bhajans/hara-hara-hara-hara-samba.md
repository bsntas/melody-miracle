## Lyrics

Hara Hara Hara Hara Samba Shiva
Vibhuti Sundara Sai Shiva
Gangadhara Hara Gowri Shiva
Trishoola Dhari Trinetra Shiva
Parthi Puri Paramesha Shiva [ Vibhuti Sundara ... ]

## Meaning

Chant the name of Shiva the three-eyed destroyer of evil. He bears the Ganges and the trident; chant the name of Sai Shiva of Parthi Who is adorned with holy ash.
