## Lyrics

Rama Krishna Hari Sathya Sai Murari
Sai Murari Sathya Sai Murari [ Rama Krishna ... ]
Allah Eshwar Yesu Bhi Tum Ho
Tum Ho Ram Aur Shyama Murari
Buddha Bhi Tum Ho Brahma Bhi Tum Ho [ Sai Murari ... ]

## Meaning

Oh Sai Baba, You are none other than Rama, Krishna and Hari. Victory to You! You are Jesus Christ, You are Allah. You are Eshwar, Buddha and Lord Brahma.
