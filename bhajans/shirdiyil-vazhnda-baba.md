## Lyrics

Shirdiyil Vazhnda Baba Puttaparthiyil Piranda Baba
Prashanthi Nilaya Baba Trayee Brindavana Baba
Shivashakti Vadivame Baba Brahma Sattiyame Ni Baba
Kaliyuga Deivame Va Va Ennai Kattarulvai Ni Baba (2)

## Meaning

Baba, who lived in Shirdi, born now in Parthi as incarnation of Shiva and Shakti, is the embodiment of Truth. Please grace us, O Lord of Kali age.
