## Meaning

Salutations at Your divine lotus feet Oh Lord Sai, You are the upholder of the "Eternal Laws" (Sanathana Dharma), the one who is the embodiment of all virtues, the one who is worshipped by sages and noble ones and the one whom the entire humanity worships.
