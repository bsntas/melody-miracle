## Lyrics

Sri Guru Parthi Vasa
Sai Brahma Vishnu Mahesha [ Sri Guru ... ]
Allah Bhi Tum Ho Maulah Bhi Tum Ho
Zohrashtra Mahavira Yesu Bhi Tum Ho
Ram Rahim Ho Krishna Karim Ho

## Meaning

Chant the name of Lord of Parthi who is Guru, Brahma, Vishnu, Mahesha, Allah, Zohrashtra, Mahavira and He is also none other than Rama, Krishna and Karim.
