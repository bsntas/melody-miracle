## Meaning

Chant the name of the Lord Sathya Sai Ram, The Lord's name is Sathya, the eternal, changeless truth. Chant the name of Lord Hari and Lord Narayana. O Mind, chant the names of Sathya Narayana, Rama, my Ghana Shyama and Sathya Sai Rama.
