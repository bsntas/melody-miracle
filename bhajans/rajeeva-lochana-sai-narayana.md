## Lyrics

Rajeeva Lochana Sai Narayana

## Meaning

Worship the lotus-eyed Lord Narayana, who has incarnated as Lord Sathya Sai, the saviour of devotees and towards whom He is extremely merciful and who is the embodiment of purity.
