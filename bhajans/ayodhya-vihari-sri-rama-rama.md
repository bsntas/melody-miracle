## Lyrics

Ayodhya Vihari Sri Rama Rama Rama
Dwaraka Vihari Sri Krishna Krishna Krishna
Saptagiri Vihari Govinda Govinda
Nija Bhaktha Hrudaya Vihari Sri Sai Natha Deva

## Meaning

Sing the glory of Rama who is the resident of Ayodhya, Krishna the resident of Dwaraka and Govinda the God of seven hills. O Lord Sai, You revel in the hearts of devotees.
