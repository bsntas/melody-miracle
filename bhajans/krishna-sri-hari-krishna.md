## Lyrics

Krishna Sri Hari Krishna
Govinda Gopala Krishna Krishna
Nanda Mukunda Govinda Navaneetha Chora Gopala

Krishna Sri Hari Krishna

## Meaning

Chant the Various names of the Lord, Krishna, Hari, Govinda, Gopala, Mukunda, of the son of Nanda and the One who steals butter and Sai Krishna.
