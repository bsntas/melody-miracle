## Lyrics

Sai Naam Bolo Sathya Sai Naam Bolo
Sai Madhava Sai Keshava Sri Hari Naam Bolo [ Sai Naam ... ]
Allah Eshwar Eesa Masiha
Buddha Mahavira Naam [ Sai Madhava ... ]

## Meaning

Chant the name of our dear Lord Sathya Sai. Chant the divine names Madhava, Keshava and Hari (names of Lord Vishnu). Chant the name of Allah, Eshwar, Yesu, Buddha and Mahavir.
