## Lyrics

Govinda Murahari Madhava
Govinda Gopala Keshava [ Govinda Murahari ... ]
Janardhana Sai Parthi Vihari
Govardhanodhari Madhava
Madhava Keshava Hari Narayana

## Meaning

Glory to Lord Narayana who is known by many names - Govinda, Gopala, Madhava, Keshava, Janardhana and Hari. Glory to the one who slayed demon Mura and who lifted the Govardhana mountain. Glory to Lord Sai who resides at Parthi and who is none other than Lord Narayana.
