## Lyrics

Narayan Narayan Bhaja Mana Narayan
Sri Hari Madhava Narayan Bhaja Mana Narayan
Murali Shyam Mohana Shyam Murali Mohana Shyam
Merey Ram Hey Ghana Shyam Sai Sadguru Naam

## Meaning

O mind, sing the glory of Lord Narayana, the abode of all being. He is the dark-hued Lord Krishna who is enchants us with His flute. He is my Lord Ram, who has the complexion of the rain cloud. Sing the name of Lord Sai, the Divine Preceptor who is verily Lord Narayana, Krishna and Rama.
