## Meaning

Chant the name of Lord Sai who is verily Allah. Chant with love the name of any God. Chant the name of Lord Zohrashtra, Mahavira and Buddha. Chant the name of Lord Sathya Sai who is the embodiment of Truth, Righteousness, Peace and Love.
