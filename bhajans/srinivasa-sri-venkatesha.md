## Meaning

Chant the names of Lord Srinivasa of Tirupati also hailed as Venkatesha, Balaji, Govinda. O Lord, You are none other than Lord Sai. You are the compassionate Lord residing on the hills of Tirumala accompanied by Goddess Lakshmi. You are the lotus-eyed one who destroys our sins.
