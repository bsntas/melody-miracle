## Meaning

Glory to Lord Sai, the Lord of divine Music, the very embodiment of Lord Shiva, the one reveling in the cosmic dance, the Lord of Goddess Parvathi, the Eternal Lord holding the Holy river Ganga in His hair locks! Glory to the great Lord Sai Shiva.
