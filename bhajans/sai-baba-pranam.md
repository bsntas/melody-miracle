## Lyrics

Sai Baba Pranam
Shirdi Baba Pranam
O Merey Atma Ram
Lelo Merey Pranam
Eshwar Allah Ram
Sab Ke Ghar Sai Ram
Pooran Karo Merey Kaam
Hey Parama Shanthi Priya Ram

## Meaning

Kindly accept my offering of salutations, O indweller of my heart, Lord Sai Baba of Shirdi and Parthi. Chant the name of Eshwar, Allah and Ram. Sai belongs to all. O Lord who loves eternal peace, please bless me for successful completion of all my works.
