## Lyrics

Sai Mera Tum Ho Sahara
Shirdi Avatara Sai Parthi Puri Bhagawan
Deenon Ke Tum Prana Sakha Prabhu
Merey Sai Ram
Merey Sai Ram Baba Parthipuri Bhagawan

## Meaning

My Sai is the support and sustenance of my life; Glory to You, O Lord of Parthi, the re-incarnation of Shirdi Sai. Glory to You O Sai, who is the life-mate of the poor and the downtrodden. You are our very own O dearest Sai, the Lord of Parthi.
