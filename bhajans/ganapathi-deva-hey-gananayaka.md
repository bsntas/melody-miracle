## Lyrics

Ganapathi Deva Hey Gananayaka
Pranava Swaroopa Hey Shubha Dayaka
Parvathi Nandana Hey Dukha Bhanjana
Lambodhara Hey Mooshika Vahana (2)
Prathama Poojana Hey Sai Gajanana

## Meaning

O Lord Ganesha, the Lord of the Ganas, You are the embodiment of the Primodial Sound Om. O son of Goddess Parvathi and the one who rides on Mooshika, You bestow auspiciousness and destroy wordly afflications. Our foremost obeisance to You who is none other than Sai.
