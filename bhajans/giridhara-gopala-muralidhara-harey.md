## Lyrics

Giridhara Gopala Muralidhara Harey
Govinda Gopala Damodara
Giridhara Gopala Muralidhara Harey [ Govinda Gopala ... ]

## Meaning

Chant the names of the Lord Krishna - Gopala, Giridhara. Glory to the One with the flute who is also called Damodara.
