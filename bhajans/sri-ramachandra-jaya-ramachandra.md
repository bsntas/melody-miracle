## Meaning

Victory to Lord Rama, the valiant hero of the battlefield, the son of Dasharatha. Lord Rama is the most compassionate one, the slayer of demons, the wielder of the sacred bow Kodanda, one who does good to everyone and who is very attractive to look at.
