## Meaning

The entire Universe is permeated with the sound of 'Sai Om' the primordial sound. 'Sai Om' is everywhere, it is in the body, in the conscience, in sleep, in dream, in the sky, ether and wind. Whatever and wherever, the eyes perceive, it is nothing but 'Sai Om'. It is from emergence to dissolution.
