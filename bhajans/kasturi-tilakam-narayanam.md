## Lyrics

Kasturi Tilakam Narayanam

Kali Yuga Avatara Narayanam
Govinda Govinda Narayanam (2)

## Meaning

Glory to the lotus-eyed Lord Narayana adorning the scented vermillion who is the presiding deity at Guruvayur. He is also the cowherd boy Krishna who has incarnated in this Kali age as Lord Sai.
