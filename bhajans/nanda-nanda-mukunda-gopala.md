## Meaning

Glory to Lord Krishna, the son of Nanda. Glory to the cowherd boy Krishna who grants us liberation. O Lord, You are the one who enchants us by playing the flute. O blue-colored Lord resembling the dark color of the clouds, victory be to Thee, the child of Nanda.
