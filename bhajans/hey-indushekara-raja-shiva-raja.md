## Lyrics

Hey Indushekara Raja Shiva Raja Shankara
Hey Indushekara Shambho Shiva Raja Shankara
Om Hara Hara Shiva Shiva Pinaka Vaibhava Raja Shankara
Shiva Raja Shankara

## Meaning

Sing the Glory of Sovereign Lord Shankara (Shiva) who adorns the crescent moon on His head. He is the most auspicious one who holds the Pinaka Bow in His hands.
