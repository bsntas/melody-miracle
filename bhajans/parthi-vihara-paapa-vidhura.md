## Lyrics

Parthi Vihara Paapa Vidhura
Pavana Charana Sai Narayana 
Allah Tum Ho Yeshu Tum Ho
Sab Ka Malik Sai Tum Ho
Buddha Zohrashtra Nanak Tum Ho
Tum Ho Baba Antaryami 
Sab Bhakton Ke Palanhaarey 
Kaliyuga Avatara Sai Narayana

## Meaning

O Lord Sai who resides at Parthi, the very surrender to Thy Feet destroys our sins. You are verily Narayana, Allah, Jesus, Buddha, Zoharashtra and Guru Nanak. You are the omnicient Lord who incarnated in this Kali age to protect all devotees.
