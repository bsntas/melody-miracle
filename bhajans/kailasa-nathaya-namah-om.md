## Lyrics

Kailasa Nathaya Namah Om
Vaikunta Vasaya Namah Om
Parthi Pureeshaya Namah Om
Sri Sai Devaya Namah Om

## Meaning

Salutations to Lord Sai, Lord of Puttaparthi, who is verily Lord Shiva, the Lord of Kailasa and Lord Narayana, the resident of Vaikunta.
