## Lyrics

Sai Narayana Narayana
Sathya Narayana Narayana [ Sai Narayana ... ]
Gaana Lola Prabhu Narayana
Deena Pala Jaya Narayana

## Meaning

Chant the name of the merciful and omniscient Lord Sai Narayana, who rejoices when the divine glory is sung. Victory to Lord Narayana who is the protector of the meek.
