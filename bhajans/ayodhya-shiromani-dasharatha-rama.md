## Meaning

Sing the glory of the lotus-eyed Lord Rama, the son of Dasharatha and the crown jewel of Ayodhya. O Lord Sai, You are verily Lord Rama. Free us from the bondage of life and death as You are our dearest companion and our ultimate abode.
