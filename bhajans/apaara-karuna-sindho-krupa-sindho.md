## Lyrics

Apaara Karuna Sindho Krupa Sindho Daya Sindho 
Aganita Gunagana Dhama Atma Rama Ananda Rama 
Bhaashpavaari Paripoorna Lochanam 
Maruthim Bhajey Ram Ram Ram Sita Ram Ram Ram Raja Ram Ram Ram
Bhaktha Jana Priya Sai Ram Sathya Sai Ram Parthi Sai Ram

## Meaning

Lord Sathya Sai is the boundless ocean of mercy. Lord Sai's attributes are beyond perception. Lord Sai's eyes are infinitely beautiful. As Hanuman worships the holy name of Rama, we too worship Lord Sai Rama. Lord Sathya Sai who resides at Puttaparthi is worshipped by all of His dear devotees.
