## Lyrics

Taraka Naamam Bhajarey Manasa Sai Ram Sai Ram Sai Ram
Dharma Uddharaka Taraka Naamam
Sarva Manohara Sai Naamam
Sayeeshwara Paramatma Naamam
Sai Ram Sai Ram Sai Ram Sai Ram

## Meaning

O Mind, chant the Holy Name of Lord Sai Rama that liberates one from the cycle of birth and death, the Name that uplifts and fosters righteousness and the Name of the Supreme Lord Sai that is beautiful in all aspects.
