## Lyrics

Sri Ganesha Jaya Ganesha, Sadguru Deva Sai Ganesha
Parvathi Shankara Hey Paramesha
Rama Krishna Hari Govinda Vittala
Subrahmanyam Subrahmanyam Shanmukha Natha Subrahmanyam
Subrahmanyam Subrahmanyam Sai Natha Subrahmanyam

## Meaning

Glory to Lord Ganesha, son of Mother Parvathi and Lord Shiva. O Lord Ganesha, You are none other than Lord Sai. Chant the names of Rama, Krishna, Hari, Govinda, Vittala, Subrahmanya and Lord Sai.
