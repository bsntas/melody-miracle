## Lyrics

Dhim Dhim Dhimi Dhimi Natana Shiva
Tandava Keli Vilasa Shiva
Leela Manusha Vesha Shiva
Lingodhbhavakara Sai Shiva (Atma)
Sai Shiva Hara Bala Shiva

## Meaning

Lord Shiva's rhythmical dance is referred to here as 'Dhim Dhim Dhimi'. He performs the cosmic dance, Tandava and has taken the human form, which is like a sport (leela) for Him. Glory to Sathya Sai, our young Shiva, who brings out the Lingam from within, by His Divine Will.
