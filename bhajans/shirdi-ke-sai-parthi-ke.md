## Lyrics

Shirdi Ke Sai Parthi Ke Baba
Tum Ho Hamarey Pyaarey Sai Baba [ Shirdi Ke ... ]
Premamaya Sai Prema Pradatha
Eshwaramba Nandana Jagat Paripala
Paapa Vinashaka Parama Krupala

## Meaning

O Lord Sai, Shirdi and Parthi are Your abodes. You are our loving Sai Baba. You are embodiment of Love and the bestower of Love. O the compassionate Son of Mother Eshwaramba, You are the sustainer of this entire Universe. O Lord, please annihilate our sins.
