## Lyrics

Jai Santoshi Ma Jai Jai Sai Ma (3)
Mangala Karini Ma Sai Sankata Harini Ma
Triloka Palini Ma Sai Trishoola Dharini Ma

## Meaning

Glory be to Mother Santoshi, who removes misfortune of Her devotees and promotes their welfare. She holds the mighty Trident in Her hand and sustains the three worlds.
