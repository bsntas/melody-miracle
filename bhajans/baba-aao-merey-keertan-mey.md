## Lyrics

Baba Aao Merey Keertan Mey
Antaryami Darsha Dikhao
Naiya Hamarey Paar Lagao [ Baba Aao ... ]
Antar Jyothi Jalao Sai
Jeevan Jyothi Tum Ho Sai

## Meaning

O beloved Baba, grant Your Divine Darshan while we sing Your glory and help us cross the boat of life. Kindle the lamp of wisdom in our hearts just as You illuminate our lives.
