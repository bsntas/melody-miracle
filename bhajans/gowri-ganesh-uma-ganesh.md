## Lyrics

Gowri Ganesh Uma Ganesh
Parvathi Nandana Sri Ganesh
Sharanam Ganesh Sharanam Ganesh
Shiva Nandana Ganapathi Ganesh

## Meaning

Chant the name of Lord Ganesh, the beloved son of Mother Gowri. I surrender to Thee, O Lord of all Demigods, beloved child of Lord Shiva and Goddess Parvathi.
