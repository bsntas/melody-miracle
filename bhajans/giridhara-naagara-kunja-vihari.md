## Lyrics

Madhuvana Sanchari Parthi Vihari (2)

## Meaning

Chant the name of the Lord Giridhara who roams in the garden and in the forest of Madhu. He is very beautiful with the peacock feather as His crown and lotus eyes. He is none other than our Sai who resides in Parthi.
