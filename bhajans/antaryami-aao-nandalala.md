## Lyrics

Antaryami Aao Nandalala
Sathya Dharma Premi Sai Gopala

Radha Pathey Krishna Gopala (Priya) [ Sathya Dharma ... ]

## Meaning

Wecome O Lord Krishna, the indweller of our hearts, the compassionate son of Nanda and the Lord of Radha. You are worshipped by many names such as Madhava, Govinda, Narayana and Gopala. You are verily Lord Sai who loves Truth and Righteousness.
