## Lyrics

Premamaya Bhagawan Sai Ram
Karunamaya Sai Rama Rama Rama Ram
Hey Dukha Bhanjana Palanhaarey
Hey Leelamaya Apad Saharey
Sharan Mey Lelo Sai Rama Rama Rama Ram

## Meaning

Lord Sai Rama, You are the personification of Love and compassion, who destroys our miseries, who takes care of our troubles, Oh Lord, take us in Your fold. We surrender at Your Feet.
