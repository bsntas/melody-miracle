## Lyrics

Gajavadana Gananatha Gajavadana Vinayaka
Siddhi Vidhata Shiva Tanaya
Buddhi Pradayaka Gajanana
Parvathi Nandana Bhava Bhaya Bhanjana
Yuga Yuga Vanditha Jaya Sri Ganesha

## Meaning

Worship the elephant-faced Ganesha, the Lord of the demigods, the son of Lord Shiva who can bestow powers and intellectual prowess. O Son of Mother Parvathi, You remove the fear of the cycle of birth and death and help cross it. Victory to Lord Ganesha, who is worshipped in all ages (yugas).
