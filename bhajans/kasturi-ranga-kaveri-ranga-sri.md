## Meaning

Sing the name of Lord Ranga (Narayana) with the scented vermillion on His forehead who resides in Srirangam on the banks of river Kaveri. He is the most compassionate one who bestows welfare. He has Garuda as His chariot and reclines on the Celestial Serpent Adi Sesha.
