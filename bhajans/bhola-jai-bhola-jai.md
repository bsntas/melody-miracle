## Meaning

Victory to Lord Sathya Sai who can be pleased easily. Victory to Rama, Krishna, Supreme Lord and Narayana. Victory to Lord Sai, the upholder of ancient Dharma (faith), the caretaker of the afflicted and the beloved son of Mother Eshwaramba.
