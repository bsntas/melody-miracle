## Lyrics

Jaya Govinda Jaya Gopala
Radha Vallabha Raasa Vilola [ Jaya Govinda ... ]
Nanda Mukunda Goparipala
Munijana Vanditha Murali Gopala

## Meaning

Chant the name of Gopala, the consort of Radha, who is fond of the Raasa leela (divine folk dance with Gopis). He is the son of Nanda and the protector of cows, who is worshipped by sages and who plays the flute.
