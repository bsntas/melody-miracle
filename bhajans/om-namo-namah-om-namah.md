## Meaning

Bow to Lord Narayana in reverence and humility, revere Him as He is the source of beauty, the undecaying, infinite one who is adored by the pious. He is the primal cause of creation and the indweller of all beings. Prayerful salutations to Lord Sai, the very incarnation of Lord Narayana.
