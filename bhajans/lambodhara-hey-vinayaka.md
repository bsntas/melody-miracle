## Lyrics

Lambodhara Hey Vinayaka
Vighna Harana Girija Nandana
Shambhu Kumara Kalusha Vidhura
Siddhi Sadana Hey Gananatha
Pranavakaara Pashupathi Tanaya [ Vighna Harana ... ]

## Meaning

Glory be to the Lord of the Ganas, Lord Ganesha, the remover of obstacles, son of Mother Parvathi and Lord Shiva, one who removes our impurities and bestows us with power and wisdom, the very embodiment of the Pranava (Om).
