## Lyrics

Karthikeya Karunakara

## Meaning

I surrender to You O Lord Karthikeya, the compassionate one, the son of Mother Gowri. You are the one who destroys the cycle of Birth and death and is a source of delight for Pashupathi (Lord Shiva).
