## Meaning

Chant the name of Lord Shiva, the Lord of Kailasa who is the ocean of compassion. He is the consort of Mother Parvathi and is the Lord of Animals. Lord Sai of Shirdi and Parthi is verily Lord Shiva, the one who has Ganges on His head and is the consort of Gowri. Lord Sathya Sai is the Supreme Lord of the Universe.
