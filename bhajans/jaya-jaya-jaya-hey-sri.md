## Lyrics

Jaya Jaya Jaya Hey Sri Guru Natha
Jaya Jaya Jaya Hey Sadguru Natha
Sadguru Natha Sadguru (Sri Sai) Natha
Sathya Swaroopa Shiva Guru Natha
Antaryami Vishwa Vidhata
Mukthi Pradatha Sai Guru Natha

## Meaning

Let us sing in praise of our sacred Preceptor Sathya Sai, the embodiment of Truth. He is the indweller and the Creator of the Universe who grants Liberation.
