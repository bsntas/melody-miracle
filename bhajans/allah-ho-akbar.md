## Lyrics

Allah Ho Akbar, Allah Ho Akbar, Allah Ho Akbar

## Meaning

Allah is the Supreme. He is also Jesus Christ, Shirdi Sai, Parthi Sai, Guru Nanak, Zohrashtra and Mahavir. Chant their glorious names.
