## Meaning

Worship Lord Sai, the Divine Preceptor who is the embodiment of kindness and mercy, having a delightful form and who is the resident of Prashanthi Nilayam in Puttaparthi. He is the embodiment of the Supreme Lord and is verily Lord Narayana, treading the three worlds (in the Vamana avatar). He is the Creator (Brahma), Sustainer (Vishnu) and the Destroyer (Shiva) of the Universe.
