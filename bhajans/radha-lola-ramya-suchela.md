## Lyrics

Radha Lola Ramya Suchela
Rajitha Vanamala Gopala
Venu Vilola Vijaya Gopala
Vedagama Moola Gopala

## Meaning

O Lord Gopala (Krishna), the delightful, elegant and the beautiful one who adored by Radha, You are embellished with a garland of flowers from the vines of the forest. You are the beautiful player of the flute, the victorious Gopala and You are the origin of the Vedas.
