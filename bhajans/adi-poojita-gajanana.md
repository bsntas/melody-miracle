## Meaning

O the elephant-faced Lord Ganesha, You are worshipped as the foremost deity. You are the bestower of auspiciousness and the leader of the Ganaas (group of demigods). Victory to Thee, the son of Lord Shiva and Mother Parvathi (also known as Bhavani and Ambika).
