## Lyrics

Atma Rama Ananda Naama
Ananda Mohana Sri Parandhama [ Atma Rama ... ]
Mayabhi Rama Manasa Prema
Sundara Naama Sugunabhi Rama

## Meaning

O Lord Rama, the indweller of hearts, Your form gives happiness, bliss and love. You are the embodiment of virtues who assumed an illusive form of a human being.
