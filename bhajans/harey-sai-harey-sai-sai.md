## Meaning

Chant various names of the Lord Krishna - Achyutha, Keshava, Rama, Narayana, Damodara, Vasudeva, Sridhara and Madhava. Glory to the Lord of the Gopis who has incarnated as our sacred preceptor, Lord Sathya Sai.
