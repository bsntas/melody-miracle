## Lyrics

Siddhi Vinayaka Paahi Gajanana
Gowri Nandana Hey Jagavandana [ Siddhi Vinayaka ... ]
Pashankusha Dhara Vighna Vinasha
Buddhi Pradayaka Paapa Vinasha

## Meaning

Salutations to the elephant-faced son of Gowri, Lord Ganesha, bestower of auspiciousness and knowledge. He is the master of the universe who removes obstacles and destroys sins and who carries the noose and the hook.
