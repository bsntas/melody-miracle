## Lyrics

Deena Janavani Vara Dayini
Gnana Swaroopini Narayani
Sayeeshwari Amba Jagadeeshwari

Prema Pradayini Partheeshwari
Mata Maheshwari Mana Mohini [ Sayeeshwari Amba ... ]

## Meaning

O Mother Sai, the goddess of the universe, You are the refuge to the destitute. You bestow boons and You love divine music. You are the essence of the Vedas and the Goddess of speech who bestows love. You are Partheeshwari (Goddess of Parthi) and Maheshwari (the great Goddess). You are the one who delights the mind.
