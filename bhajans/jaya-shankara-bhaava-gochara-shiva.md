## Lyrics

Jaya Shankara Bhaava Gochara Shiva Chidambara Omkara
Parthi Vihara Paapa Vidhura
Jagadodhara Brahmapara (3)

## Meaning

Glory to Shankara who is beyond senses, Lord of Chidambaram (Holy place in India), personified as Omkara, the Supreme Brahman, the Lord of Parthi who removes sin and uplifts the world.
