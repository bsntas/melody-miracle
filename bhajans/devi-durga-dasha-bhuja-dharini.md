## Lyrics

Devi Durga Dasha Bhuja Dharini
Danava Daithya Dushta Samhaarini [ Devi Durga ... ]
Bhagawathi Bharati Bhava Bhaya Haarini
Mata Maheshwari Mangala Kaarini
Raja Rajeshwari Girija Bhavani
Bhaktha Janashraya Sai Bhavani

## Meaning

Chant the name of Goddess Durga, who has ten arms. She is the destroyer of evil people and demons. Mother Maheshwari, confers auspiciousness. Mother Bhavani, the daughter of Himavan, mountain king, is also Raja Rajeshwari - the queen of queens. She is verily Sai Bhavani, who protects the devotees.
