## Lyrics

O Baba Sai Baba
Deenon Ke Dukha Harthey Baba [ O Baba ... ]
Ram Hi Baba Krishna Hi Baba
Nara Dehi Narayan Baba (2)

## Meaning

O Lord Sai Baba, please remove the miseries of the meek. You are none other than Lord Rama, Krishna and Narayana who has incarnated as Lord Sai Baba.
