## Lyrics

Kalavathi Amba Saraswathi
Naada Brahmamayi Vedavathi
Ananda Roopini Vidyavathi

Parthi Pureeshwari Amba Saraswathi [ Naada Brahma ]

## Meaning

Salutations to Mother Saraswathi the mother of all arts. She is also the Mother of Vedas, Brahman in the form of sound. Saraswathi is the mother of all knowledge and has bliss as her form, residing at Parthi.
