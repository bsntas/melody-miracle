## Meaning

The bow wielding Rama who destroys the evil effects of the Kali age, is compassionate and grants liberation. Lord Rama who loves sages has an elusive form of a human being whose name is sweet, enchanting and confers Auspiciousness. The emancipator of the downtrodden, the cosmic consciousness is presently dwelling at Parthi.
