## Lyrics

Jaya Nandalala Jaya Nandalala
Gopi Lola Gokula Bala [ Jaya Nandalala ... ]
Vishwa Vanditha Vijaya Gopala
Veda Vedantha Venu Gopala
Radha Vallabha Raasa Vilola

## Meaning

Victory to Lord Krishna, the son of Nanda and the consort of Radha, who danced the celestial dance with the Gopis. Lord Gopala who holds the flute in His hand, is worshipped by the entire Universe and He is the origin of Vedas and Upanishads.
