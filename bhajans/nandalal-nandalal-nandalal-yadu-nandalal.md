## Lyrics

Nandalal Nandalal Nandalal Yadu Nandalal
Nandalala Navaneetha Chora
Radha Pyaarey Nandalal
Maayi Meera Manasa Chora
Parthi Vihari Nandalal

## Meaning

O Lord Krishna, child of Nanda, You are the child of Yadavas, the cowherd clan. You are the one who stole butter (devotees' hearts). You are the beloved of Radha. You stole the heart and mind of the great saint Princess Meera. You are none other than Lord Sai who dwells in Puttaparthi.
