## Lyrics

O Bhagawan O Bhagawan Araj Suno Merey Sai Bhagawan
Parthipuri Ke Tum Ho Daata
Brindavan Ke Tum Ho Daata
Sab Bhakthon Ke Man Ko Bhata
Paar Utharo Naiya Mori
Sai Ghana Shyama Sai Ghana Shyama

## Meaning

O Lord Sai, hear my prayers, You are the boon bestowing Lord of Parthi and Brindavan. You are the beloved of all devotees, enchanting their minds and hearts. Please help us cross this boat safely across the river of life, O Sai. You are none other than Lord Krishna, the one with deep blue complexion.
