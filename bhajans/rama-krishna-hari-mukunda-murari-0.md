## Meaning

Chant the names of Lord Rama, Krishna, Hari, Mukunda, the one who liberates and Murari, the one who slayed the demon, Mura. He is Lord Panduranga, the Lord of Lords. He is the great God, Mahadeva (Shiva), He is the primordial Lord, the trinity of Brahma, Vishnu and Shiva. He has taken form as Lord Sathya Sai. Victory to Lord Panduranga.
