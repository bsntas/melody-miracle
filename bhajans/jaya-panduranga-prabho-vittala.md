## Meaning

Victory to Lord Panduranga Vittala, the Lord of Pandharpur, the liberator of the world. Lord Ranga dwells in the heart of His consort, Mother Lakshmi. He is auspiousness personified and is the most compassionate one. He bestows benevolence and compassion on the destitute. Chant the name of Panduranga Vittala.
