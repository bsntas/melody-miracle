## Meaning

Chant the Name Lord Narayana who reclines on the Ocean of Milk, consort of Sri Lakshmi and who Incarnated as Narasimha (half Man half Lion). Narayana who has Vaikunta as His Abode, is the charmer of Vaidehi, the One who takes complete care of those who have surrendered to Him.
