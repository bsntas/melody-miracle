## Meaning

Glory to Lord Shiva who is none other than Lord Sai residing in Puttaparthi. He is the Supreme Lord of the entire universe, the most compassionate one and the cause of all creation. He is the Lord of the mountains and this world. His abode is the mount Kailasa.
