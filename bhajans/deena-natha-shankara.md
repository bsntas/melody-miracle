Sayeeshwara Deva

## Meaning

Salutations to You O Lord Shankara (Shiva), the bestower of happiness. You are the Lord of the destitute. You are none other than Lord Sai who resides in Parthi. O Lord, You are an ocean of compassion who is easy-to-please and whose abode is Mount Kailasa. O Lord of Parthi, You are the greatest Lord.
