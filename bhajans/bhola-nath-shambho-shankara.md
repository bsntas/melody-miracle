## Lyrics

Bhola Nath Shambho Shankara
Sai Nath Bhola Maheshwara
Dama Dama Damaru Bholey Shankara
Dhimi Dhimi Dhimi Dhimi Natana Manohara
Parthi Pureeshwara Sai Shankara 
Bhola Maheshwara Sai Shankara

## Meaning

Chant the name of the easy to please Lord Shankara (Shiva), the conferrer of all prosperity. Lord Sai is the incarnate of Lord Shiva also known as Maheshwara (Greatest Lord), the destroyer of all that is transient. He is the enchanting dancer to the rhythmic beats of the Damaru (kettle-drum). He has won over death, He is the eternal one and the bestower of all auspiciousness and prosperity.
