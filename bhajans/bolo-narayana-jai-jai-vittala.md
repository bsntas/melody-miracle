## Lyrics

Bolo Narayana Jai Jai Vittala
Sai Narayana Ranga Ranga Vittala [ Bolo Narayana ... ]
Govinda Vittala Rakumayi Vittala
Gopala Vittala Panduranga Vittala
Sri Ranga Vittala Sai Ranga Vittala

## Meaning

Let us chant the many names of the Lord: Narayana, Vittala, Govinda, Gopala, Panduranga and Sai who is verily Lord Narayana.
