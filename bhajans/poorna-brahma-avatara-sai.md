## Lyrics

Poorna Brahma Avatara Sai
Sakala Jagat Aadhara Sai [ Poorna Brahma ]
Allah Yesu Rama Sai

Hum Bhakthon Ke Baba Sai

## Meaning

Chant the name of Lord Sai, the incarnation of the Supreme Lord. He is the sustainer of all the worlds. Sai is one with Allah, Jesus, Rama, Buddha, Mahavira and Guru Nanak. He is the beloved of us devotees. Lord Sai has incarnated to preach the oneness of all religions.
