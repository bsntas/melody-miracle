## Lyrics

Nanda Kishora Jaya Gopal
Navaneetha Chora Shyama Gopal
Meera Ke Nath Prabhu Giridhara Gopal
Raasa Vilola Radhey Gopal

## Meaning

Victory be to the beloved son of Nanda, the dark-hued Lord Krishna, the one who stole butter (analogy for a devotee's heart) and the one who lifted the Govardhana hill. He is the Lord of Meera and Radha and the one who performed the Raasa Leela dance with the Gopikas.
