Sayeesha Sharanam Gachchami (Sathya)

## Meaning

O Lord Sai, You are the Truth, Auspiciousness and Beauty. I take refuge in Buddha (the enlightened one); I take refuge in Dhamma (the ultimate Truth); I take refuge in Sangham (the holy community). To Lord God Sai, I surrender all.
