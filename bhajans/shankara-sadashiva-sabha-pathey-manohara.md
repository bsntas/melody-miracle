## Meaning

Salutations to Lord Shankara (Shiva) the master of stage, the one who holds the crescent moon in His matted hair. We offer our humble salutations to the consort of Goddess Uma (Parvathi). Lord Shiva is none other than Lord Sai of Puttaparthi.
