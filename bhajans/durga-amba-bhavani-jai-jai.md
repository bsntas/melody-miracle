## Lyrics

Durga Amba Bhavani Jai Jai Durga Amba Bhavani

## Meaning

Victory to goddess Durga, glorified by the names Amba and Bhavani, the annihilator of miseries, bestower of auspiciousness and the embodiment of the primordial sound Om.
