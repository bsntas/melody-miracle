## Lyrics

Ganesha Charanam Sharanam Ganesha (4)
Sayeesha Charanam Sharanam Sayeesha (4)

## Meaning

Surrender to the Lotus Feet of Lord Ganesh. Surrender to the Lotus Feet of Lord Sai.
