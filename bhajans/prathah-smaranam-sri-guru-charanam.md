## Lyrics

## Meaning

Meditate on the Lotus Feet of the Guru (Lord Sai) in the early hours of dawn. Meditate on the holy feet of the Sadguru Sayeesha, the dispeller of ignorance. Surrender to the Lotus Feet of Lord Sai, who confers infinite happiness and heavenly bliss. We take refuge at your Lotus Feet, Oh Sainatha.
