## Lyrics

Govinda Jaya Govinda
Gokula Nandana Govinda
Ghana Ghana Neela Govinda
Bala Mukunda Govinda
Hey Ghana Shyama Govinda [ Gokula Nandana ... ]

## Meaning

Glory to Lord Govinda (Krishna), the beloved child of Gokula, whose complexion is dark like the cloud. Glory to the child Mukunda, the one who grants liberation.
