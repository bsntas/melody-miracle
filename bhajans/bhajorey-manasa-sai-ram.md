## Lyrics

Bhajorey Manasa Sai Ram
Parthipuri Ke Sai Bhagawan [ Bhajorey Manasa ... ]
Eshwar Allah Terey Naam
Yesu Buddha Bhi Terey Naam
Shirdi (Parthi) Puri Ke Sai Bhagawan

## Meaning

O mind, worship and chant the name of Sai, the Lord of Parthi and Shirdi. Eshwar, Allah, Yesu, Buddha are all His names.
