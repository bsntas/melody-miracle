## Meaning

Lord Vinayaka, You are the embodiment of Om. O son of Uma and Shiva, praised by the trinity of gods, You are the Lord of Lords and  the author of the Vedas. O Lord of Parthi, Lord Sai, You remove all our sins. O Lord Vinayaka, You grant all boons and You are the true meaning of all meditation.
