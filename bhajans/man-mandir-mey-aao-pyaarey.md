## Lyrics

Man Mandir Mey Aao Pyaarey Aao Sai Hamarey

## Meaning

O dear Sai, please come into the temple of our hearts. You are our only kin and friend. Our heart pines for You, O Lord, please come. Take our boats safely across this ocean of life, O Protector.
