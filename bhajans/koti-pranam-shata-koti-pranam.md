## Lyrics

Koti Pranam Shata Koti Pranam
Hey Deena Natha Sai Ram (3)
Tum Ho Bhakthon Ke Yuga Avatar
Tum Ho Ananda Nanda Ke Lal
Parthi Puri Ke Sai Gopal [ Hey Deena Natha ... ]

## Meaning

Many repeated salutations to You Lord Sai, the refuge of the forlorn. You were born as the son of Nanda and presently the incarnation of our age, Sai Gopala residing at Parthi.
