## Lyrics

Shuklambara Dharam Ganapathi Mantram
Nithyam Nithyam Bhajo Bhajo
Vighna Vinashaka Vishwam Vyapaka
Veera Ganapathi Bhajo Bhajo

## Meaning

Chant daily the Mantra of Lord Ganapathi (Ganesha), the one who wears a white cloth, the remover of all obstacles, the bestower of knowledge and the one who is very courageous.
