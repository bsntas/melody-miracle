Prashanthi Nilaya Hara Shankara

## Meaning

Glory to Lord Sai who is none other than Lord Shiva who wears the crescent moon on His head. He is also the Lord who resides in the Heart. He is Parameshwara, the greatest Lord who grants love to all. He is our Lord Sathya Sai whose abode is Prashanthi Nilayam.
