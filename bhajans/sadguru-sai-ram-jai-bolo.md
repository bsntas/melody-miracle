## Meaning

Chant Victory to our Divine Preceptor Sai Ram who is our mother, father, lord, relative and friend. O Lord Sathya Sai, You are verily Allah, Guru Nanak, Jesus Christ, Zohrashtra and Gautama Buddha. Victory to Thee, O Lord.
