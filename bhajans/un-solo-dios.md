## Meaning

You are the One God, my Lord. You are Jesus Christ, Mahavira, Jehovah, Allah, Brahma, Vishnu, Shiva, Sai, Zoroaster, Guru Nanak, Ahura Mazda, Buddha and Narayana. Lord Sathya Sai Baba will unite us into one humanity. One God will reign, one God will love us.
