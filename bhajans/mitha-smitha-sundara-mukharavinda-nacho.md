## Lyrics

Mitha Smitha Sundara Mukharavinda Nacho Nandalala

## Meaning

Dance on in ecstacy, O the gentle, smiling, beautiful lotus-faced Lord Krishna, the darling son of Nanda and the Lord of Meera.
