Patithodharana Bhaktha Parayana
Partheeshwara Sai Ram

## Meaning

O Sai, the Lord of Parthi, we chant Your name, which is loved by Your devotees as it destroys their miseries. You bind everything in this universe by the cord of love. We chant Your name mentally all the time. You are uplifter of downtrodden and Your devotees sing Your praise.
