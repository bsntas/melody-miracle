## Lyrics

Nataraja Nataraja Om Namah Shivaya Nataraja
Shiva Raja Shiva Raja Om Namah Shivaya Shivaraja
Sai no Hikari o, Shiva no Chikara o, Ware no Kokoro ni Sazuketa Mae (2)

## Meaning

Salutations to Lord Nataraja (Shiva), the Lord of Dance. O Lord, grant us the light of Lord Sai and the power of Lord Shiva to our hearts. May the sound of Shiva's drumbeat dwells in our lives.
