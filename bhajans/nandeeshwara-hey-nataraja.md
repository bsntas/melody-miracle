## Meaning

Oh Lord of Nandi, the divine bull, You are the Lord of cosmic dance, Shiva. You are also the son of Nanda, Lord Vishnu, the ultimate refuge of all beings. Oh Sai Narayana, You are Shiva, who is adorned with the snake! You are the one who embodies divine music!
