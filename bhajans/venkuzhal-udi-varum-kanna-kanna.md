## Lyrics

Gajarajan Thuyar Theertha Adi Mulame
Kaliyugatthai Katthunirkkum Sai Deivame
Yuga Yugamai Thondri Nirkkum Avatarame
Kamalakkanna Karmega Vanna
Azhi Mazhai Kanna Maale Manivanna

## Meaning

O Sai, You are Lord Krishna playing the flute. Your divine play happens through Your Maya. You are the primal source who ended the suffering of the helpless elephant Gajendra. Lord Sai, You are the protector of Kali age and You incarnate in every age. You are Krishna with dark complexion and lotus eyes.
