## Lyrics

Partheeshwari Jagat Janani
Jaya Jaya Jaya Hey Sai Janani [ Partheeshwari Jagat ... ]
Veda Kalamayi Devi Bhavani
Naada Vidyamayi Narayani
Karuna Sadani Karunamayi Ma
Sharanam Sharanam Sai Ma [ Jaya Jaya ]

## Meaning

Divine Mother of Parthi, Sai Ma, the One who sustains the Universe, victory to you! You are the knowledge of the Vedas, You are the Goddess of Music and the Arts, You are full of Grace, verily the embodiment of Grace. We surrender at Your feet, Mother Sai.
