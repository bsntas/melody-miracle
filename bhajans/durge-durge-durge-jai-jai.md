## Lyrics

Durge Durge Durge Jai Jai Ma (2)
Karuna Sagari Ma
Kaali Kapalini Ma
Jagadodharini Ma
(Amba) Durge Jai Jai (Sai) Ma

## Meaning

Victory to Mother Durga, the ocean of compassion. Glory to Mother Kaali, who is adorned with a garland of human skulls (represents the destruction of our ego) and who uplifts the whole world. Victory to Mother Durga who is none other than our dear Mother Sai.
