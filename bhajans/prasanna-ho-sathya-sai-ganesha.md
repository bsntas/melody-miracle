## Meaning

At the outset, worship Lord Ganesha to remove all obstacles. The supremely sacred Lord uses a mouse as conveyance and carries the symbolic rope and weapon in His hands. He is the pot-bellied son of Mother Ambika (Parvathi). Lord Sathya Sai is none other than Lord Ganesha.
