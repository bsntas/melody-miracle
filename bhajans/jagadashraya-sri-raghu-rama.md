## Meaning

Glory to Lord Rama of Raghu dynasty, the sustainer of the entire Universe, whose consort is Mother Sita and who has come to uplift the entire world. Reciting the Lord's name helps cross the vicious cycle of birth and death. We surrender ourselves at Your Lotus feet with countless prostrations, O Lord Sai Rama, the indweller of our Heart.
