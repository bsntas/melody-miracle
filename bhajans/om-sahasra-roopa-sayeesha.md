## Meaning

Lord Sathya Sai is the embodiment of the primordial sound Om, who Himself takes on a thousand different forms in this universe. As He is the origin of the Vedas (holy scriptures), Sai is imbued with the best of qualities and virtues. Glory to Lord Sathya Sai.
