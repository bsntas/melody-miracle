## Lyrics

Bhaja Mana Radhey Shyama Gopala
Murali Manohara Radhey Gopala [ Bhaja Mana Radhey ... ]
Nanda Kishora Natavara Lala (2)
Parthi Vihara Sai Gopala

## Meaning

O mind, worship Lord Krishna, the beautiful one with His flute, son of Nanda. He resides in Parthi as Lord Sai Gopala.
