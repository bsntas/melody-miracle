## Lyrics

Shankara Shankara
Sai Shankara
Naada Shareera Vedodhara
Kali Yuga Avatara Sai

Chandrashekara Sai Parthi Shankara Sai

## Meaning

Glory to Lord Sai Shankara who is the embodiment of Music and the Vedas and who has taken the form of Sai in this age of Kali. O Lord Sai, we hail victory to You and offer our salutations to You.
