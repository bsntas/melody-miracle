## Lyrics

Ayodhya Ke Sita Ram Brindavan Ke Radhey Shyam
Radhey Shyam Jaya Radhey Shyam
Radha Madhava Radhey Shyam
Sai Ram Jaya Sai Ram
Parthi Pureeshwara Sai Ram

## Meaning

Chant the names of Sita Ram, the Lord of Ayodhya and Radhey Shyam who resides in Brindavan. Victory to Lord Sairam of Parthi.
