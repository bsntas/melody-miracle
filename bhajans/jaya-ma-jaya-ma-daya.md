## Lyrics

Jaya Ma Jaya Ma Daya Karo Sai Ma
Krupa Karo Sai Ma (Sab Ko)
Mukthi Pradayini Prema Pradayini Shanthi Pradayini Ma
Janani Ma Janani Jagat Janani Sai Ma
Daya Karo Sai Ma Jaya Ma [ (Sab Ko) Krupa ]

## Meaning

Glory to Mother who confers Love and Peace and grants Liberation. Shower your mercy on us Oh Mother of the Universe.
