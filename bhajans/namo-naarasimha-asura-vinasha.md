## Lyrics

Namo Naarasimha Asura Vinasha
Triloka Pala Trivikrama
Simha Mukha Dhari Hari Narayana
Prahalada Rakshaka Hari Narayana
Sri Parthi Vasaya Sathya Narayana
Bhakthoddharaka Sai Narayana

## Meaning

Salutations to Lord Narasimha, the destroyer of demons, the incarnation of Lord Vishnu. He sustains the three worlds, has the fierce face of a lion, and is the protector of Prahalada. He lives in Puttaparthi as our Lord Sathya Sai, the one who rescues all of His devotees.
