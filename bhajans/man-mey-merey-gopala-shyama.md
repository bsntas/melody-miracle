Raasa Vilola Gopala

## Meaning

O Lord Krishna, the dark-hued son of Nanda and Yashoda, You are enshrined in my heart. You are the star of Yadu clan, the beloved of Radha. You are the one who dances the Raasa Leela with the Gopikas in Brindavan.
