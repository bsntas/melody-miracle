## Lyrics

Pranava Swaroopa Hari Om Sai
Narayana Hari Om
Sayeeshwaraya Hari Om (Sathya)
Yadavaya Madhavaya Govindaya Namah Om
Paramatma Roopaya Hari Om
Hari Om Hari Om [ Sayeeshwaraya ... ]

## Meaning

O Lord Sathya Sai, You are the very embodiment of the Primordial sound 'Om'. You are Lord Narayana, the indweller of all beings. You are Lord Krishna of the Yadava clan. You are the protector of all beings, Govinda. You are the embodiment of the Supreme soul. We pay our obeisance to You, O Lord Sai.
