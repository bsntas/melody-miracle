## Lyrics

Govinda Gopal Jaya Narayana Gopal
Madhava Gopal Madhusudhana Gopal

## Meaning

Victory to You, Oh Narayana, Gopal and Govinda, You are the slayer of demon Madhu and the one who gives liberation to the deserved.
