## Lyrics

## Meaning

Sing the glory of Mother Durga, the eternal joy of Lord Shiva, the giver of life, the Mother of all worlds, the queen of queens. She is Mother Parvathi, the sister of Lord Vishnu. She is none other than Mother Sai.
